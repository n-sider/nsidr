--- title: "[E3] Reggie: PS3 price is shockingly high" date: 2006-05-10T14:35:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Kotaku.com bumped into Reggie Fils-Aime, Nintendo of America's executive vice president of sales and marketing, yesterday at the Nintendo party.

They asked him his thoughts on Sony's recent price announcement for its PlayStation 3 console (PS3 with 20GB hard drive for $499 and 60GB hard drive for $599). Fils-Aime replied oh-so-eloquently with a simple, "It's shockingly high."
