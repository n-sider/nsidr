--- title: "[E3] Pheonix Wright Sequel Returns to DS" date: 2006-05-10T16:22:07Z contributors: - James Remington ---

Capcom has announced that work has begun on the sequel to the hit Nintendo DS title Phoeonix Wright: Ace Attorney. Titled Pheonix Wright: Ace Attorney- Justice For All, the game should be launching on the Nintendo DS in early 2007.

Justice For All will feature four new cases to solve, as well as a case health meter which can impact the strength of your case based upon any wrongful evidence you may provide or underhanded ploys you may attempt.

In addition to the new features, the game also features a new rival, Franziska von Karma, who's seeking revenge for her father by smearing Phoenix Wright's reputation.
