--- title: "Google Reader offering version optimized for Wii" date: 2007-05-09T11:41:32Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Ubiquitous Internet fixture Google is now offering a version of its Google Reader service, which tracks subscriptions to sites like blogs and news, optimized for use with Wii's Internet Channel.

The service offers Wii Remote-specific control, remapping the d-pad and buttons to Google Reader actions such as d-pad left/right to navigate between items. If you'd like to check the service out, head on over to http://www.google.com/reader/wii/.
