--- title: "Neo Geo titles make debut on US Virtual Console" date: 2007-10-08T10:41:14Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo announced this morning the debut of Neo Geo titles on the North American Virtual Console service, following up on last week's debut of Japan-only releases Sin & Punishment and Super Mario Bros.: The Lost Levels.

The titles releasing today are:

  • Art of Fighting
  • Fatal Fury
  • World Heroes

All three titles go live at the usual time—9 a.m. Pacific, and each goes for 900 Wii Points.
