Breath of Fire: Warrior of Dragon

Capcom follows its original Game Boy Advance game Rockman EXE with three enhanced ports, one of them being Breath of Fire: Warrior of Dragons. This staple Capcom RPG originally appeared on the Super NES. The series has gathered a decent following and has received sequels on the SNES and PlayStation game console.

This classic turn based RPG has received some improvements on the Game Boy Advance. Capcom has completely redone the graphic engine, recomposed the soundtrack, added cinema scenes and added communication-link support in which you can trade items with other players. Capcom hopes to release this game worldwide sometime between summer and fall 2001.
