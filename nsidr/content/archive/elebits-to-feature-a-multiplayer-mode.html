--- title: "Elebits to feature a multiplayer mode" date: 2006-08-23T08:34:17Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Konami revealed its Wii title Elebits will feature a four player multiplayer mode. Using the Wii remote, players can each control a gravity gun in a race to collect the most Elebits. A new trailer of the game can be seen at its official website.
