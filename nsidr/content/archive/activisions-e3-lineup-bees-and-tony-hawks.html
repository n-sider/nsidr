--- title: "Activision's E3 lineup: Bees and Tony Hawks" date: 2007-07-10T16:09:25Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Activision has a significant Nintendo-themed lineup prepared for E3 this week. There's the usual suspects including sequels to Spider-Man and Tony Hawk. There are also a few franchises appearing for the first time including Guitar Hero and a game based on the Bee Movie.

Nintendo DS

  • Bee Movie Game
  • Call of Duty DS
  • Spider-Man: Friend or Foe
  • Tony Hawk's Proving Ground

Wii

  • Bee Movie Game
  • Guitar Hero III: Legends of Rock
  • Spider-Man: Friend or Foe
  • Tony Hawk's Proving Ground

Please note that Activision's lineup is not limited to these announced titles. It's possible the publisher will reveal additional projects in development at the show.
