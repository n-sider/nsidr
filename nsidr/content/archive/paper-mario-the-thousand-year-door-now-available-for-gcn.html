--- title: "\"Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door\" now available for GCN" date: 2004-10-11T12:52:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has announced the release of Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door for the Nintendo GameCube. Visit the official website for screens and other info on Paper Mario.

The title currently features an 85 percent average review rating at GameRankings.com. The N-Sider staff highly suggests picking up a copy of Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door.
