--- title: "Nintendo closes SNES/GBA kiosk service" date: 2007-02-08T11:52:22Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- From the impossibly-enticing headline department, CVG is reporting that Nintendo is closing Nintendo Power—only, it's not the magazine that you got when you paid up for your The Legend of Zelda: Collector's Edition disc.

In Japan, Nintendo Power was a kiosk-based download service that let owners of special flash-based cartridges download several Nintendo titles such as Super Mario World and entries in the Donkey Kong Country series. The few kiosks that remain in operation are now being decommissioned by Nintendo.
