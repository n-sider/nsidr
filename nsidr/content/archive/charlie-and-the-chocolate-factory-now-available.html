--- title: "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory now available" date: 2005-07-18T20:15:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Take-Two and Warner Bros. said Charlie and the Chocolate Factory is now available for Game Boy Advance and GameCube. The game is based on the Warner Bros. Pictures film which debuted in theaters on July 15.

Charlie and the Chocolate Factory for GameCube currently has a 50 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com (based on 1 media outlet).
