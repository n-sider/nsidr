--- title: "Operation Wolf, Columns III to hit VC today" date: 2008-02-04T11:33:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo has announced two new Virtual Console releases for today:

  • Operation Wolf (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Columns III: Revenge of Columns (Genesis, 800 Wii Points)

As usual, the games will be available for purchase in the Virtual Console section of the Wii Shop Channel at 9 a.m. Pacific time.
