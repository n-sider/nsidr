--- title: "Super Monkey Ball in development" date: 2005-09-08T01:28:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Sega will release a version of Super Monkey Ball for the Nintendo DS in Japan during December 2005, according to Japanese Famitsu magazine. The game will be similar to Namco's Pac N' Roll featuring full 3D gameplay on the top screen and a 2D representation of your character on the touch screen. The game will also feature mini-games with wireless multiplayer support. Screens of the title can be seen here, courtesy of The-MagicBox.
