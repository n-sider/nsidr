--- title: "Wild World passes three million mark in Japan" date: 2006-07-28T15:10:30Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Animal Crossing: Wild World has crossed the three million mark in total copies sold in Japan, according to Dengeki Online.COM's rankings, making it the first Nintendo DS title to do so.

According to NeoGAF poster cvxfreak, Wild World is the 20th game in history to cross this mark; the first two spots occupied by Super Mario Bros. and Super Mario Bros. 3.
