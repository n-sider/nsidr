--- title: "Nintendogs now available" date: 2005-08-22T21:39:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America said Nintendogs is now available for the Nintendo DS. The game comes in three versions: Chihuahua and Friends, Dachshund and Friends, and Labrador and Friends. More on the games can be found at their offical website.

Nintendogs currently has an 84 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com (based on 12 reviews).
