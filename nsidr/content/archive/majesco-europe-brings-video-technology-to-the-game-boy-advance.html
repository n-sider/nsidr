--- title: "Majesco Europe brings \"video technology\" to the Game Boy Advance" date: 2004-03-10T04:31:48Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Majesco Europe, operating subsidiary of ConnectivCorp, has received approval to bring its video technology to Nintendo's Game Boy Advance system and create new products for distribution throughout Europe later this year.

A wide variety of video content, including cartoons, is said to be in development. The official website is now online at gba-video.com.
