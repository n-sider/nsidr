--- title: "Geist goes \"gold\"; being readied for August" date: 2005-07-13T02:01:57Z --- n-Space, developer of the upcoming Nintendo GameCube first-person shooter Geist, has updated its homepage to note the fact that Geist has gone "Gold" and is being readied for release in stores this August. Geist provides an innovative twist on the first-person shooter genre by allowing players to "possess" inanimate objects as well as other creatures in a full-fledged one-player experience. The title also supports up to four players and contains several multiplayer modes. Check out n-Space's homepage for more details.
