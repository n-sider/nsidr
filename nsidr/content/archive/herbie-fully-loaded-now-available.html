--- title: "Herbie: Fully Loaded now available" date: 2005-06-16T19:50:46Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Disney Interactive has said that Herbie: Fully Loaded is now available for the Game Boy Advance. The title is a one-player 3D racing game based on the recent Disney movie remake featuring a Volkswagen bug. The "Story Mode" is said to tell the story of the game and includes images of Lindsay Lohan and Herbie from the movie as a reward for players' progress.
