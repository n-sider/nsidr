Japanese resident Vinnk of 4 color rebellion has done a little writeup on advertising kiosks for upcoming-in-Japan titles Super Princess Peach and the sequel to Feel the Magic: XY♥XX: Where Do Babies Come From?

Neither title has yet been announced for release outside Japan, but even if they do cross the ocean, they certainly won't come with advertising like this.
