--- title: "North American hardware and software sales for May" date: 2005-06-28T01:25:49Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- NPD Group has released North American hardware and software sales for the month of May. Pokemon Emerald for the Game Boy Advance was the biggest seller with over 880,000 in sales. This was followed by Revenge of the Sith on PS2 which reached 490,000 units and then on Xbox with 378,000 units. The rest of the list is as follows:
