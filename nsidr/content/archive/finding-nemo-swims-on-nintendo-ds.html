--- title: "Finding Nemo swims on Nintendo DS" date: 2006-02-12T00:22:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Finding Nemo: Escape to the Big Blue is now available for $29.99 for the Nintendo DS. The game was published and developed by THQ.

The title is based on the Disney/Pixar animation of the same name. The game's story is described as follows: "After years of aquarium life, the Tank Gang is finally ready to make a daring break for the deep blue sea. But it wont be easy. Luckily, Nemo, Marlin, and Dory are waiting by the pier to lend a helping fin as their buddies dive deep into the unknown on an incredible journey to find a brand new home in the coral reef."
