--- title: "Several new titles announced for GBA and DS" date: 2004-10-28T13:34:00Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The latest issue of Japanese magazine Famitsu has revealed several new Nintendo titles in development. These include Touch! Kirby: The Magic Paintbrush, Super Princess Peach and Harvest Moon for the Nintendo DS. In addition, Sennen Kazoku, The Tower SP, and Puzzle Chai-rien have been announced by Nintendo for the Game Boy Advance.
