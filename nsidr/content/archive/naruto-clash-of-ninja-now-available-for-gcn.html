--- title: "Naruto: Clash of Ninja now available for GCN" date: 2006-03-11T22:31:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Naruto: Clash of Ninja is now available for the Nintendo GameCube for $39.99. The title is published by D3. Naruto: Clash of Ninja currently has a 75 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 4 reviews).

The game fits into the fighting genre and follows the story of the Ninja Academy, where "students are instructed in powerful and deadly subjects such as summoning a doppelganger, casting illusions and throwing deadly blades known as shuriken."
