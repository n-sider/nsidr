The world of Rayman is threatened by a devastating invasion of crazed raving rabbids. Rayman has no choice but to engage in an epic battle to save the world. Rayman Raving Rabbids comes from publisher Ubisoft and is scheduled to arrive for an exclusive period of time on Wii's launch later this year. Rayman Raving Rabbids is being developed under designer Michel Ancel, known for his work on Rayman, Beyond Good & Evil, and Peter Jackson's King Kong: The Official Game of the Movie.
