--- title: "Marionation Gear announced for Japan" date: 2006-07-12T10:31:48Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

In Japan, Nintendo is set to publish a new game by developer Sandlot called Marionation Gear.

Marionation Gear has the player controlling giant robots entirely with virtual controls on the touch screen. Different sets of controls in the robots' cockpits give the player control over tasks from fighting other robots to racing and, apparently, construction work.

 

 

The game will feature over 100 different robots, each with its own unique set of controls. The game is scheduled for release on September 2, the day the Jet Black DS Lite is first available in Japan.
