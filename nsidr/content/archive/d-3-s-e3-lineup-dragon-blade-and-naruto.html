--- title: "D3's E3 lineup: Dragon Blade and Naruto" date: 2007-07-10T13:31:04Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- D3Publisher, of Puzzle Quest fame, has announced their E3 lineup for this year. D3's list mostly consists of licensed games, but they do have one original game in the works, developed specifically for Wii: Dragon Blade: Wrath of Fire.

Nintendo DS

  • Ben 10: Protector of Earth
  • Naruto: Path of the Ninja

Wii

  • Ben 10: Protector of Earth
  • Dragon Blade: Wrath of Fire
  • Naruto: Clash of Ninja Revolution

Please note that D3's lineup is not limited to these announced titles. It's possible the publisher will reveal additional projects in development at the show.
