--- title: "Metroid Prime Hunters goes Wi-Fi" date: 2005-08-26T21:16:59Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo reaffirmed at the Penny Arcade Expo that Metroid Prime: Hunters would not release until 2006, according to 1Up.com. This postponement brings good news however. Although it was not previously compatible with the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection service, this delay will now allow the company to incorporate the Wi-Fi functionality into the game. So cheers to this, and we hope to bust a cap in ya'll in by 2006.
