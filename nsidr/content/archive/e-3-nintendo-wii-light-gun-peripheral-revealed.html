--- title: "[E3] Nintendo Wii light gun peripheral revealed" date: 2006-05-10T14:24:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGN.com has been told that Nintendo will showcase a gun peripheral for the Nintendo Wii. This peripheral will be used to present the new Duck Hunt game. It is currently unknown whether the gun is a separate unit or will attach to the Wii Remote controller. Stay tuned for more details as well as images.
