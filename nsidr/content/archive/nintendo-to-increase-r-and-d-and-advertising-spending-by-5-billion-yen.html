--- title: "Nintendo to increase R&D and advertising spending by 5 billion yen" date: 2005-01-13T05:43:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo will allocate 39 percent more funds to its research and development and advertising divisions, increasing spending to a total of 22 billion yen, according to IGN and Japan's Nikkei Financial Daily. Nintendo hopes the increase in R&D spending will result in the production of new software to take advantage of its Nintendo DS hardware. It's also likely Nintendo is preparing for its next-generation home console codenamed 'Revolution'.
