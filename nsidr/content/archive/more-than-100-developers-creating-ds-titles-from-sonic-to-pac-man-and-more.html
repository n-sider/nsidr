--- title: "More than 100 developers creating DS titles - from Sonic to Pac-Man and more" date: 2004-05-11T18:44:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to Nintendo, more than 100 companies worldwide have received software development kits and are planning games for Nintendo DS. Third-party publishers have announced games in development, including a Spider-Man 2 title from Activision, a Rayman game from Ubisoft, a SpongeBob SquarePants title from THQ, a Yu-Gi-Oh! game from Konami, a Sonic title from Sega, a Bomberman game from Hudson Soft, a Need for Speed game from Electronic Arts and a Pac-Man title from Namco.
