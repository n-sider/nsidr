--- title: "EA \"Need for Speed\" design contest" date: 2004-07-07T15:03:45Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts is holding a design contest for its upcoming game Need For Speed: Underground 2. EA will choose ten designs from entrants to use in the game, with the ten chosen winners receiving a free copy of the game. Entrants must first download the custom templates and follow the other on-site directions. Find all the necessary information here. The contest ends July 11. Be sure to let N-Sider know if you enter and win.
