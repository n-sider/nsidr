--- title: "Nintendo Power: Super Paper Mario for April" date: 2007-01-23T14:47:47Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo Power has put down April as the release date for Intelligent Systems' Super Paper Mario (which N-Forums member Ferquin previewed for us), according to a report from GWN.

Nintendo Power has been wrong before, simply by virtue of having press deadlines for putting ink to paper—but with Wii's release lineup for spring presumably solidifying soon it seems likely April will see Super Paper Mario hit stores. Stay tuned.
