--- title: "IGN Live event" date: 2005-08-25T21:24:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- IGN.com said it will host its first annual IGN Live event at the Anaheim Convention Center October 22 - 23, 2005. IGN Live is said to be a gathering of industry professionals and feature panels with technologists, developers, game publishers, and entertainment personalities. IGN Live admission costs $15 online or $25 at the door. Online tickets include trial memberships to IGN Insider and GameFly. More information can be found at here.
