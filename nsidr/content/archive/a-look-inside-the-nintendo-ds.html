--- title: "A look inside the Nintendo DS" date: 2004-11-22T19:02:28Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The guys and gals over at Lik-Sang weren't satisfied with just looking and playing with their Nintendo DS and decided to take it apart. By doing this, they've discovered that the GBA battery, after a few minor alterations, is compatible with the DS. Take a look at the insides of the DS and more here.
