--- title: "Poll: controller 2nd most memorable 2005 moment" date: 2005-12-29T11:31:26Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

This is certainly no news to those of us who crammed into the N-Forums chat on that fateful night, but GameFAQs' "what will you remember most" 2005 poll has the unveiling of the Nintendo Revolution "freestyle" controller in second place with 20%, edging out all other actual news events.

The only moment beating out the unveiling is "just playing great games and having fun" which, when it comes down to it, is all that's really important in the end anyway, isn't it? With luck, and with Revolution's 2006 arrival, we'll be even more into this in the coming year.
