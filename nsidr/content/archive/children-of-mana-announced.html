--- title: "Children of Mana announced" date: 2005-09-29T10:41:25Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Weekly Jump magazine has revealed that the previously-announced Mana title in development for Nintendo DS as part of the World of Mana project will be titled "Children of Mana".

The title is currently slated for a 2006 release in Japan. No announcement has yet been made for a North American release.
