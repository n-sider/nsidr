--- title: "Q-games working with Nintendo?" date: 2005-02-15T19:47:04Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Developer Q-games and Nintendo may have a software or technology project in the works, according to a hint in a recent interview with GCAdvanced.com. Q-games is home to Dylan Cuthbert who was the main programmer of the original Star Fox for the Super Nintendo.

AMN - What was it like working with Nintendo [on Star Fox]? Was it a positive or negative experience overall? (If positive) Would you consider working with Nintendo again in the future?

Dylan: Extremely positive. I always enjoyed fiddling with stuff when making a game and Nintendo taught me how to fiddle with even more precision and self-criticism. Yes, I would definitely work with Nintendo again, and perhaps we already are... ;-)

In the past Q-games has created technical demonstrations (videos or demos) to show off the capabilities of both the Sony PlayStation 2 and PSP during their initial unveilings. Although nothing in the least has been confirmed, it's possible they may be working with Nintendo to create demos for the Revolution.
