--- title: "Megaman ZX screens galore" date: 2006-03-17T00:53:42Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper ---

Am I alone in saying that the SNES Megaman X series is among the better 16-bit franchises to ever grace a console? Don't get me wrong -- I love Capcom and the direction they've taken over the years as much as the next guy -- but these screens from Jeuxfrance seem to signal a return (at least in spirit) to what made the Megaman games so loveable.

The new adventure for DS features two playable characters -- a male named Van and a female named Eile -- whose stories will be tied together through various animated cut-scenes. By collecting "live metal", players can expand their Hunter Armor from Z to X mode as well as utilize special powers.

Megaman ZX is slated for a December, 06 release in Japan. Expect full details on the American build as they develop.
