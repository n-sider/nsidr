--- title: "Dr. Sudoku puzzles the Nintendo DS" date: 2006-05-05T17:38:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Dr. Sudoku is now available on Nintendo DS for $19.99. Dr. Sudoku currently has a 60 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on one review). More info can be found on the game's official website.

Dr. Sudoku is the video game form of this popular puzzle game where players are presented with nine three-by-three grids and must fill in the blank squares so that each column, row, and three-by-three grid contains the numbers 1 through 9. The title contains over 1,000 puzzles and a mode to create your own Sudoku puzzles.

Nintendo Power says (6/10),
"Dr. Sudoku doesn't offer any puzzle types aside from the traditional nine by nine grid. On the other hand, Dr. Sudoku does offer the much-appreciated ability to take in-game notes. If you have a yearning for pure sudoku in video game form, Dr. Sudoku will provide a cure, but more options and a sharper presentation would have gone a long way."
