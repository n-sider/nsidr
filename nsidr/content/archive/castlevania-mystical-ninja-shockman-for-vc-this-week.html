--- title: "Castlevania, Mystical Ninja, Shockman for VC this week" date: 2007-04-30T10:34:29Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- This week's update to Wii's Virtual Console service includes three new titles, about which a consensus has been drawn amongst the N-Forums chat members this morning: this is a good week for the VC. The titles are:

  • Castlevania (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • The Legend of the Mystical Ninja (Super NES, 800 Wii Points)
  • Shockman (TurboGrafx16, 1-2 players, 600 Wii Points)

As usual, you can get these titles on the Wii Shop Channel starting at 9 a.m. Pacific time. Wii Points are currently available at the rate of 100 for $1.
