--- title: "Namco Museum 50th Anniversary now available" date: 2005-08-31T00:18:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Namco said Namco Museum 50th Anniversary is now available for the Game Boy Advance and Nintendo GameCube. The game includes 14 classic titles including Pac-Man, Ms.Pac-Man, Galaga, Galaxian, Dig Dug, Pole Position, Pole Position II, Rolling Thunder, Rally X, Bosconian, Dragon Spirit, Sky Kid, Xevious and Mappy.
