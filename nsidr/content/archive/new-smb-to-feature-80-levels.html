--- title: "New SMB to feature 80 levels" date: 2006-03-20T11:26:02Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Jeux-France has posted several new scans from upcoming DS Mario revival New Super Mario Bros., some from the issue of Nintendo Power that has yet to find its way into my hands — and the big news is that the new game will feature 80 levels of Mario goodness.

Last we heard, the game's still headed for a release May 7 in the North America, just before E3 2006, and weeks before Japan gets to play it on May 25.
