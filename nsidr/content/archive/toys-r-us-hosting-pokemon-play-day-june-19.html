--- title: "Toys 'R Us hosting \"Pokemon Play Day\" June 19" date: 2004-06-10T23:39:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Toys 'R Us stores nationwide are hosting "Pokmon Play Day" June 19, from 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM. You will have the opportunity to demo the newest Pokmon-e Trading Card Game expansion, EX Hidden Legends. With the completion of the demo, attendees will receive a free promo card.

A second date of Saturday, July 24, from 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM has also been scheduled.
