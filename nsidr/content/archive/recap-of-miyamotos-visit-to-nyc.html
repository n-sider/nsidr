--- title: "Recap of Miyamoto's visit to NYC" date: 2005-09-28T10:39:38Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

This past Sunday, Shigeru Miyamoto made an appearance at Nintendo World in New York City. During his visit, Miyamoto signed numerous T-shirts, classic and current games, as well as consoles ranging from the NES to the Gamecube.

Also at the event, Nintendogs owners were able to wirelessly unlock the Jack Russell terrier in their own copies of the game by using Bark Mode and receive a voice message from actress Haylie Duff.
