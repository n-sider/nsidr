--- title: "Chibi-Robo producer working on DS project" date: 2006-04-25T10:26:56Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Via Cubed3 comes a report that Chibi-Robo producer Kenichi Nishi has left Skip (developers of not only Chibi but also the Japan-only GiFTPiA) and now wants to make a party game for Nintendo DS.

Little is known about his project, other than his concept title — "LOL DS" — and his explanation that it will be a party game for four to eight people.
