--- title: "Atlus announces Izuna: Legend of the Unemployed Ninja" date: 2006-11-14T11:25:40Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Atlus U.S.A. has announced that it will bring Izuna: Legend of the Unemployed Ninja to retail in February 2007.

Izuna is a dungeon RPG where you play as a ninja who has recently found herself unemployed. Izuna settles into a village where everyone is "actin' all weird" and decides to get to the bottom of the situation. Gameplay features randomized dungeons, talismans for spells and equipment powerups, and weapon customization and levelling.

As more information comes out about this title, stay tuned and we'll let you know.
