--- title: "Nintendo announces new Lite colors, touts numbers" date: 2006-08-24T11:48:19Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America announced this morning that on September 13, it will be bringing two new colors of the DS Lite to retail. The new colors are Onyx and Coral Pink, and will be on shelves alongside the only other color to be introduced in NOA's market—Polar White.

Nintendo also noted the sales success of New Super Mario Bros., which has crossed the 1 million mark in the U.S. Animal Crossing: Wild World has sold 800,000 copies stateside, Brain Age 4 million worldwide (note emphasis), and Big Brain Academy "nearly" 2 million worldwide.
