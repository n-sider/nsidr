--- title: "Capcom release dates" date: 2005-06-23T21:49:06Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Capcom has several titles planned for Nintendo platforms in the coming months. Release dates have been mentioned for many of them, according to GameGossip.com. These can be seen below:

  • Killer 7 (July 6, 2005: GameCube)
  • Mega Man Zero 4 (October 4, 2005: Game Boy Advance)
  • Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney (October 11, 2005: Nintendo DS)
  • Battle Network 5: Double Team DS (November 1, 2005: Nintendo DS)
  • Mega Man X Collection (November 15, 2005: GameCube)
