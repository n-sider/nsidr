--- title: "Konami's E3 lineup: Dewy and Dance Dancing" date: 2007-07-10T16:43:00Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Konami's list of E3 games doesn't contain any surprises. It covers titles we've known about for several months now including the Wii titles Dance Dance Revolution Hottest Party and Dewy's Adventure as well as the more recently announced Contra 4 for Nintendo DS.

Nintendo DS

  • Contra 4 (working title)

Wii

  • Dance Dance Revolution Hottest Party
  • Dewy's Adventure
  • Fishing Master

Please note that Konami's lineup is not limited to these announced titles. It's possible the publisher will reveal additional projects in development at the show.
