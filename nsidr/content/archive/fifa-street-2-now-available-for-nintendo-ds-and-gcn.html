--- title: "Fifa Street 2 now available for Nintendo DS and GCN" date: 2006-03-02T13:41:31Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Electronic Arts' FIFA Street 2 is now available for Nintendo DS and Nintendo GameCube, each priced at $29.99. The GameCube game has a 65 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on two reviews).

EA has included a new feature called the "Trick-Stick Weapon". With this, players can intimidate and disgrace their rivals by executing tricks including new juggling moves using the right analog Trick-Stick. The game features more than 320 pro stars representing 20 national teams. More info can be found at the game's official website.
