--- title: "[E3] Bonk's Adventure playable tomorrow on Wii" date: 2006-05-10T01:42:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Hudson Entertainment revealed a version of Bonk's Adventure will be playable for the Nintendo Wii tomorrow. The game will ultimately be available for purchase on the Virtual Console.

Bonk's Adventure is a side-scrolling action game that was originally launched in Japan in December 1989 under the title "PC Genjin" on the PC engine, and in the U.S. in 1990 on the TurboGrafx system.
