--- title: "Cinderella and Premier Manager coming to GBA" date: 2005-06-30T00:22:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Buena Vista Games and THQ said they will bring Cinderella Magical Dreams to the Game Boy Advance this September to coincide with the release of Disney's classic Cinderella "platinum" edition DVD.

In other news, Zoo Digital Publishing said it will bring its soccer title Premier Manager 2005-2006 to the Game Boy Advance this August. In the game, players can compete in 12 cup titles and play through 14 divisions across the UK and Europe.
