--- title: "Capcom releases \"Mega Man Battle Network 4\" for GBA" date: 2004-06-30T03:16:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Capcom has released Mega Man Battle Network 4 for the Game Boy Advance. Two versions are available - Red Sun and Blue Moon. Each version presents different enemy battle chips, souls and characters, and using a Game Link cable, players can exchange battle chips and compete against each other.

Battle Network Blue Moon currently has an 80 percent review ranking on GameRankings.com.
