--- title: "2K and Konami bring Power Pro Baseball with MLB license" date: 2007-08-03T13:19:35Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- 2K Sports and Konami announced today the impending publication of MLB Power Pros for Wii as well as Sony's PlayStation 2.

Based on the popular Japanese Power Pro baseball series, which had a Wii entry released last month as Jikkyou Powerful Pro Yakyuu Wii, Power Pros adds MLB characters to the mix, shown in the "Power Pro" character style: a miniature toy-like figurine model. Every MLB ballpark has been replicated for the game, and the title features over a dozen game modes including Season Mode (be a team general manager) and Success Mode (combining RPG elements with baseball scenarios).

MLB Power Pros is set for a release this fall.
