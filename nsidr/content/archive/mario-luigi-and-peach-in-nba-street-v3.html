--- title: "Mario, Luigi and Peach in NBA Street V3" date: 2005-01-05T00:26:17Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

GameSpot has acquired video of Mario, Peach and Luigi playing b-ball in Eletronic Arts' upcoming GameCube title NBA Street V3. Whether or not you've been keen on the thought of Nintendo characters in a basketball game, the video is definitely worth checking out. The clip as well as more information on the title can be found here.
