--- title: "Metroid Prime Hunters now available for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-03-20T22:57:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Metroid Prime Hunters is now available for Nintendo DS at a retail price of $34.99. More info can be found at the game's official website. Also, if you're interested Nintendo.com recently published an interview with the creator of the Metroid Prime Hunters television commercial.

Metroid Prime Hunters contains a full one-player adventure as well as an extensive multiplayer mode. The title is compatible for two-to-four players over Nintendo's Wi-Fi Connection service. Metroid Prime Hunters currently has a 90 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 1 review).
