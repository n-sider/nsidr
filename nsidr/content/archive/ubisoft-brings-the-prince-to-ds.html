--- title: "Ubisoft brings the Prince to DS" date: 2005-07-21T23:23:03Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Ubisoft has announced details regarding the upcoming Prince of Persia title for the Nintendo DS. The game utilizes a new gameplay element not commonly associated with the series. Instead of the fast-action platforming and fighting used in past games, Battles of Prince of Persia will instead be a card-based battle game. The story takes place between the two home console versions - Sands of Time and Warrior Within. In the game, players are said to command units and troops within a familiar interface and control fights via a card battle system. Check out the first screens here, courtesy of IGN.com.
