--- title: "Lizzie McGuire 3 now available" date: 2005-08-16T22:51:21Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Buena Vista Games said it has released its teen-action puzzle game Disney's Lizzie McGuire 3: Homecoming Havoc for the Game Boy Advance. The game is based on the Disney Channel's television series "Lizzie McGuire". More information including screens can be found at the game's official website.
