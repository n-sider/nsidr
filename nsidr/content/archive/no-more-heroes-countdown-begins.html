--- title: "No More Heroes countdown begins" date: 2007-06-13T23:07:18Z contributors: - Michael Foster --- Suda 51's latest project, No More Heroes for the Wii, received a countdown on its official website. At the moment, the countdown is set for nine days, though no indication is given as to what exactly will occur when the countdown ends.

Known for Gamecube's cult hit Killer 7, Grasshopper's newest development lets gamers take the role of Travis Touchdown, a guy who wins a Beam Katana and seeks to use the weapon to become a professional killer. After killing an assassin named Helter Skelter, a mysterious French woman named Silvia Christel, agent with the UAA, gives Travis a rank of 11. Travis, however, wants to be number one, so he must kill the 10 assassins above him.

No More Heroes has an expected Japanese release this summer, with no date given for the game's release elsewhere.
