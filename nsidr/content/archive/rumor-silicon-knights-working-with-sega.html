--- title: "Rumor: Silicon Knights working with Sega?" date: 2004-06-21T17:24:59Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

In the latest issue of Electronic Gaming Monthly's "rumor" section, Silicon Knights - developer of Eternal Darkness and Metal Gear Solid: Twin Snakes - is said to be working with Sega on its next development project for the Xbox. Keep in mind this is only a "rumor" at the moment.
