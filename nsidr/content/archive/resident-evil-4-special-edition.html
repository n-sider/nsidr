--- title: "Resident Evil 4 special edition" date: 2004-10-16T01:51:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Retailer GameStop.com will be offering a limited edition Resident Evil 4 Collector's Tin for its January 11, 2004 release. The collection will retail for $59.99 and include the following items in a red, tin box:

  • Mini Resident Evil 4 art book.
  • Resident Evil 4 laser cell.
  • Original motion picture soundtracks from Resident Evil and Resident Evil: Apocalypse.
