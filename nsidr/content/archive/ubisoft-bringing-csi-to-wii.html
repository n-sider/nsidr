--- title: "Ubisoft bringing CSI to Wii" date: 2007-07-24T15:08:33Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Ubisoft has announced that it will be releasing CSI 4: Hard Evidence for Wii, shipping alongside the Xbox 360 version this fall.

The title, developed by Telltale Games, has had its interface overhauled with console players in mind. Wii owners in particular will be able to navigate through a crime scene with the Remote. Cast members from the main show set in Las Vegas will appear in the game, and players will be asked to search for clues, analyze evidence, and recreate crime scenes.

Ubisoft's stewardship of the CSI license has resulted in sales of 2.4 million worldwide since the they released their first title in the franchise back in March 2003.
