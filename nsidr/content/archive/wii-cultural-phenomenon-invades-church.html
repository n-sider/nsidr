--- title: "Wii, cultural phenomenon, invades church" date: 2008-04-07T11:06:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- On my way into the office this morning, I spotted yet more evidence that Wii really is becoming a cultural phenomenon.


Between this sermon, the local watering hole proudly touting the fact that you can get your Tennis on in-between imbibes, and a little karate dojo sticking the word "Wii" in huge print in their tiny ad to let you know you might win one if your kid signs up for lessons—all sighted within the past week—it's clear that, at least in my hometown, Wii is on everyone's mind.
