--- title: "Paper Mario headlines upcoming Virtual Console releases" date: 2007-05-21T22:22:18Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America's official Nintendo Power magazine covers upcoming Virtual Console releases within its pages every month. This month, they've revealed that the Nintendo 64 classic Paper Mario, predecessor to the GameCube's Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door and Wii's Super Paper Mario will be hitting the service on an unspecified future date.

Also making an appearance among the games that have not yet been releasesd is the Genesis sequel to Toejam & Earl: Toejam & Earl in Panic on Funkotron. It's reasonable to expect both titles will hit the Virtual Console service this coming Monday.
