--- title: "The history of Nintendo's Metroid franchise" date: 2006-03-18T21:44:13Z ---

G4 television show X-Play, hosted by Adam Sessler and Morgan Webb, has been gracious enough to compile a five-minute break-down of the Metroid storyline beginning with the original Metroid and following Samus' adventures through the NES, SNES, Game Boy and GameCube series. For those of you who've ever wanted a short and sweet narrative, check it out.

  1. Metroid (NES)
  2. Metroid Prime (GCN)
  3. Metroid Prime 2: Echoes (GCN)
  4. Metroid II: The Return of Samus (GB)
  5. Super Metroid (SNES)
  6. Metroid Fusion (GBA)
  • Metroid Prime Revolution (Rev)
  • Metroid Prime Hunters (NDS)
