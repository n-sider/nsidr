--- title: "Advertisement for upcoming GameCube titles" date: 2004-08-24T00:49:47Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

If you haven't had the opportunity to view it yet, Nintendo of America's new theatrical advertisement featuring some of this holiday's biggest GameCube titles is now available for download here. The "Who are you?" themed advert shows off titles such as Baten Kaitos, Goldeneye: Rogue Agent, and Metroid Prime 2: Echoes.
