Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2

Activision's assault of franchises on the Game Boy Advance continues with Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2, Activision's highest selling property to date. One of the first announced USA launch titles, Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2 is one of the most impressive looking titles, which is definitely a surprise after the disappointing Game Boy Color editions.

What makes this game look so impressive, is that instead of going with the usual high color rainbow palette, the development team decided to use a blander, realistic color scheme while using an isometic camera to give it a similar look to the one found on the Playstation and Nintendo 64 edition. The game will feature twelve pro skaters, and a multitude of combos that made past installments so well received by game players.

No details regarding multiplayer, but Activision will have the game playable for E3 and is adamant about having the game ready for the US launch of the Game Boy Advance.
