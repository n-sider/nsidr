--- title: "New screens for Sonic Rush Adventures" date: 2007-05-10T17:01:59Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
JeuxFrance.com has posted a handful of new screenshots of upcoming Sonic Rush sequel Sonic Rush Adventure. The new shots seem to show a sort of Wild West motif to the screen decorations, interestingly.

Sonic Rush Adventure is, last we heard, still on track for a fall release.
