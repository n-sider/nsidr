--- title: "Metroid Prime-themed GameCube bundle coming in August?" date: 2004-07-02T13:14:56Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America will be releasing a Metroid Prime-themed GameCube bundle this August, according to IGNCube.com. For the price of $99.99, the bundle is said to include a GameCube and controller, a copy of the original Metroid Prime, and a demo of Metroid Prime 2.

Nintendo of America has not yet officially confirmed the bundle.
