--- title: "EA working on six titles, more on the way" date: 2006-07-13T13:51:40Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Electronic Arts revealed today that it is working on six titles for Nintendo's upcoming Wii home console, all coming out of their "dedicated Wii development team"—presumably the one in Burnaby.

The titles under development include entries in EA franchises Madden NFL, Need for Speed, Harry Potter, Tiger Woods PGA Tour, SSX, and The Godfather. In addition, EA says it is working on additional titles to be revealed in the coming months.

"Once we started to experiment with Madden on Wii, there was an explosion of innovation. Everyone in the studio is energized by the creative opportunity afforded by both the hardware and controller. By reaching into our catalogue, we are able to bring a fresh and distinctive experience to some of EA's most popular franchises. Creatively, the game play possibilities are staggering and endless," said John Schappert, Senior Vice President and General Manager, EA.
