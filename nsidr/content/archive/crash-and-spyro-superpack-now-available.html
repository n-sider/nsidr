--- title: "Crash & Spyro Superpack now available" date: 2005-10-20T19:07:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Vivendi Universal's Crash & Spyro Superpack is now available for the Game Boy Advance. The title contains two titles "Crash Bandicoot Purple: Ripto's Rampage" and "Spyro Orange: The Cortex Conspiracy" for a suggested retail price of $19.99.
