--- title: "Brawl's Subspace Emissary mode to feature local co-op" date: 2007-10-05T13:16:49Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- The official Super Smash Bros. Brawl site, purportedly manned by series creator Masahiro Sakurai himself, today announced the presence of local co-op for the title's Subspace Emissary adventure mode.

  • Two players can play simultaneously
  • Camera follows player 1; player 2 will warp to player 1's side if caught off-screen by the scroll
  • However, if player 2 is knocked off-screen, he will lose a life (as per standard Smash rules)
  • Players have a shared lives count
  • In both modes, knocked-out characters will be replaced by another from the team, which is built as the story progresses by characters joining
  • Five difficulty modes from "easy" to "intense"
