--- title: "Best Metroid music ever?" date: 2005-11-07T19:37:07Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- No disrespect to Kenji Yamamoto or Minako Hamano (the original composers of Super Metroid's fright-pop soundtrack for the SNES), but this Brinstar remix deserves a virtual high-five.

It's from a September issue of 4 Color Rebellion's mailbag (a relatively new link, actually -- the original dates back to April '04) -- board user "Rev" created the track exclusively for the site.

Dated as this (under-rated) mix is, checking it out might be the best thing you've done all day. But... you don't have to take my word for it (cue Reading Rainbow butterflies).
