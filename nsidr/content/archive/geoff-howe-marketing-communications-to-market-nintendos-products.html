--- title: "Geoff Howe Marketing Communications to market Nintendo's products" date: 2004-07-07T14:12:33Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Europe has appointed Geoff Howe Marketing Communications to handle future UK marketing campaigns, according to DMBulletin.

Geoff Howe previously worked with Nintendo on a per-project basis. Today, Nintendo has briefed the agency to create promotional activity for all its hardware and games, including sales promotions, consumer events, experiential marketing and brokering tie-ups with third parties. Nintendo and Geoff Howe are also preparing for the launch of the new Nintendo DS handheld.
