--- title: "DK King of Swing now available" date: 2005-09-21T20:37:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America said Donkey Kong King of Swing is now available for the Game Boy Advance. The title features a suggested retail price of $29.99. More information can be found at the game's official website here.

Donkey Kong King of Swing currently has an 84 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 21 reviews).
