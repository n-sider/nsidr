--- title: "Lego Star Wars coming this October?" date: 2005-08-23T22:16:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- EBGames and Amazon.com currently have Lego Star Wars scheduled for release on the Nintendo GameCube on October 4. A version of the game was released earlier in the year on both PlayStation 2 and Xbox. The price is also listed as $29.99. There is currently no word from Eidos on whether or not this information is correct. Thanks to NintendoInsider.com for the heads up.
