--- title: "New Cobalt/Black DS Lite on the way" date: 2008-02-01T14:11:23Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America announced today the impending arrival of the seventh entry in the DS Lite's palette: Cobalt/Black.


The new Lite will go on sale February 10. The DS Lite has previously been available in Polar White, Coral Pink, Onyx, Crimson/Black and the limited-edition Gold and Metallic Rose.
