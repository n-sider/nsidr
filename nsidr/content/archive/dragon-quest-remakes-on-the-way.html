--- title: "Dragon Quest remakes on the way" date: 2007-07-30T22:21:52Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- The latest Shounen Jump revealed completely unsurprising but still big news: Square-Enix has Dragon Quest remakes in the works for DS.

Dragon Quest IV is first to get a makeover and is set for a vague release period of Winter 2007 in Japan. Dragon Quest V and Dragon Quest VI will follow, completing the trilogy within the long-running, immensely popular RPG series.
