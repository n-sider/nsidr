--- title: "\"Donkey Konga\" now available in North America" date: 2004-09-29T11:13:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has announced the nationwide release of its bongo-thumping GameCube title Donkey Konga. The title includes a set of bongo drums for the suggested retail price of $49.99.

Donkey Konga currently has a 76 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com.
