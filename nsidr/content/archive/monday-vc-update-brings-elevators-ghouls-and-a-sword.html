--- title: "Monday VC update brings elevators, ghouls, and a sword" date: 2007-03-05T11:50:19Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Welcome to Monday morning—as regular readers know, this is the day when we let you know what Nintendo will be putting out on the Virtual Console today. Without any further ado, here they are:

  • Elevator Action (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Super Ghouls 'n Ghosts (Super NES, 800 Wii Points)
  • Sword of Vermilion (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)

The titles will be available for you to blow your hard-earned Wii Points on in the Wii Shop Channel as of 9 a.m. Pacific time.
