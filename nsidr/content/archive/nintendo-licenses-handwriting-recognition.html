--- title: "Nintendo licenses handwriting recognition" date: 2005-10-26T10:13:39Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Zi Corporation, creators of the Decuma handwriting recognition software seen in some PDAs, announced today that Nintendo has licensed the Decuma technology for use in future Nintendo DS software.

Decuma technology is capable of recognizing handwriting in Roman, Chinese, and Japanese alphabets. Nintendo has not yet announced software that will use the technology.
