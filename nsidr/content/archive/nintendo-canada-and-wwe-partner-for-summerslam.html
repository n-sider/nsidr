--- title: "Nintendo Canada and WWE partner for SummerSlam" date: 2004-06-07T01:22:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of Canada and World Wrestling Entertainment said they will partner for the upcoming SummerSlam, which takes place August 15, in Toronto Canada.

"Nintendo of Canada is proud to support the WWE in Canada and we're excited to be part of WWE SummerSlam in Toronto," said Ron Bertram, Nintendo of Canada's general manager. "We will continue to build on our strong partnership throughout 2004, with the fall launch of WWE: Day of Reckoning by THQ exclusively for Nintendo GameCube."

Tickets for SummerSlam are available at Air Canada Centre Box Office and Ticketmaster Canada.
