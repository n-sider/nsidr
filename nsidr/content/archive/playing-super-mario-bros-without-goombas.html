--- title: "Playing Super Mario Bros. without goombas?" date: 2004-07-05T02:41:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

An experiment was performed by Myfanwy Ashmore that involved removing all the enemies out of Super Mario Bros. to see how gamers reacted and played the game. The results are pretty interesting in that they show players coming up with their own unique way of approaching and playing the game. Be sure to check out the full article here.
