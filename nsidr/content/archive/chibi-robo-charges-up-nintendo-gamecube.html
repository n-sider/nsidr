--- title: "Chibi-Robo charges up Nintendo GameCube" date: 2006-02-12T00:23:58Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Chibi-Robo is now available for $49.99 for the Nintendo GameCube. The game is published by Nintendo of America and developed by Skip Ltd.

Chibi-Robo follows the story of a miniature robot "as he completes chores and clashes with enemies in his epic quest to become the highest-ranked Chibi-Robo out there." Action takes place within a house littered with all sorts of obstacles. Players must be sure to occasionally stop and use a power outlet to recharge before your robot runs out of power. The official website for the game can be found here.

The game currently has a 73 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 11 reviews).
