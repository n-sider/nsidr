--- title: "Manhunt 2 still banned by BBFC" date: 2007-10-08T18:34:05Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- The British Board of Film Classification has reviewed the new version of Rockstar's Manhunt 2 and once again deemed it unsuitable for consumers. The BBFC's director, David Cooke, had some words to say:

  • The changes made to the game do not go far enough to address the BBFC's concerns.
  • The visual detail of some of the execution kills is reduced, but others retain their original casually sadistic nature.
  • The bleak and callous tone has remained unchanged.
