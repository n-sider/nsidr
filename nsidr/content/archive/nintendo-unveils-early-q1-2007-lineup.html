--- title: "Nintendo unveils early Q1 2007 lineup" date: 2006-11-17T23:50:48Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America has quietly updated their release list to reflect titles on the horizon for Q1 2007.

The current outlook for that period:

Wii

  • WarioWare: Smooth Moves (January 15; see our preview)

Nintendo DS

  • Hotel Dusk: Room 215 (January 22)
  • Diddy Kong Racing DS (February 5)
  • Wario: Master of Disguise (March 5)
  • Custom Robo Arena (March 19)

Game Boy Advance

  • Final Fantasy VI Advance (February 5)
