--- title: "Rampage coming Spring '06" date: 2005-08-11T00:26:33Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Midway said it will publish Rampage: Total Destruction for the Nintendo GameCube. The game is based on the classic arcade game Rampage. The title is being readied for a Spring 2006 release at a suggested retail price of $19.99.
