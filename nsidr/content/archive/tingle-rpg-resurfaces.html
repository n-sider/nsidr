--- title: "Tingle RPG resurfaces" date: 2006-06-08T12:13:01Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Remember Tingle RPG, announced back in October? Famitsu has new screens of the title, now referred to as (depending on who you ask, something similar to) Tingle in Rupee Land.

The story appears to be following the life of a younger Tingle, though it's unclear as of yet what the adventure is about. Speculation is rife over at NeoGAF, of course. If we find out more, we'll let you know.
