--- title: "A look at Steven Spielberg's Blocks" date: 2007-07-13T02:50:12Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Electronic Arts has released a trailer for its mystery Steven Spielberg Wii game. The game previously codenamed "PQRS" is now tentatively titled Blocks. The game will feature single player, co-op, and versus modes for players to interact with, build and destroy a variety of structures made of blocks. Think Wii's version of Sony's Little Big Planet...except, you know, with blocks.
