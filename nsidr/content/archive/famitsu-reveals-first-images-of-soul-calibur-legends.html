--- title: "Famitsu reveals first images of Soul Calibur Legends" date: 2007-06-13T13:13:09Z contributors: - Cory Faller --- Courtesy of the latest issue of Japanese gaming magazine Famitsu and its titular western provider neo2046 comes the first look at Soul Calibur Legends, an action-adventure interpretation of Namco's storied Soul Calibur franchise being developed exclusively for the Wii.

Click for scan at full size

The images feature series veterans Siegfried and Taki battling against various goons in a 3D setting, with one particular image pitting Siegfried against returning villain Astaroth. Oddly the title seems not to exceed—or even meet—the graphical finesse of Soul Calibur II on the GameCube, but that could easily be attributed to a development cycle currently in its infancy.

We'll have more on Soul Calibur Legends, particularly if the second half of this scan ever shows up, as it breaks.
