--- title: "\"Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors\" now available for GBA" date: 2004-06-24T04:02:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Atari has released Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors for the Game Boy Advance. Developed by Banpresto, Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors is a fighting game that features 15 well-known characters from the Dragon Ball series.

Dragon Ball Z: Supersonic Warriors currently has an average review ranking of 73 percent on GameRankings.com.
