--- title: "Lunar developers talks Wi-Fi, DS and Revolution" date: 2005-09-15T23:45:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- NTSC-UK has conducted an interview with Harvest Moon developers Yasuhiro Wada and Junichi Kutsuzawa at the recent Games Market Europe. The two men discussed their upcoming Nintendo DS title Lunar Dragon Song and also mentioned their preliminary development for the Nintendo Revolution. An excerpt is below while the full interview can be found here.
Q: Its almost like there has been a shift in game design; before, the emphasis was new game ideas when the inputs and controllers were largely unchanged, but now theres a new fashion to redesign the controllers and games are catching up with them now?

Katsuzawa: Yes, Id agree. This is exactly what were seeing with the DS, and with Nintendos Revolution.
