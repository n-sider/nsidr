The year 2005 is coming to a close. And wow, what an interesting one it was. How did the past 365 days treat ya? We hope you, your friends, and family had a productive and healthy one.

The year was a good one for N-Sider and I would say a phenomenal one for Nintendo. In May, Nintendo fans received the first glimpse of Nintendo's Project Revolution -- and in the months that followed, we were introduced to the controller and the strategy. We've seen the software library for the Nintendo DS mature into something that's irresistible. And we've seen Nintendo step into online gaming with the launch of Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection.

Wow. And the new year is looking even brighter with the full unveiling of Revolution scheduled for May and an ever blossoming library for Nintendo DS. We look forward to providing you all the indepth N-Sider style coverage of the events that unfold.
