--- title: "Wii outsells PS2 (not a typo) in Australia for last two weeks" date: 2007-03-06T16:09:48Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Australian IT has some numbers and analysis from the Australian console war, and the most interesting note is that over the past two weeks, Wii has managed to outsell still-going-strong last-generation stalwart PlayStation 2.

Outside those two weeks, Wii has still been making a good showing in ithe country, moving 68,000 units since its launch this past December. Sony's PlayStation 3 has yet to launch in the territory.
