Did you know?

  • Namco's SRS (Street Racing Syndicate) is still in development for the Nintendo GameCube

  • Electronic Arts will publish Free Radical's TimeSplitters 3 for the Nintendo GameCube.

  • Team 17, the developer of the Worms 3D game is still open to any opportunities that would allow it to develop additional games for the Nintendo GameCube.

  • All these news stories were falsely presented earlier this week by various websites. As journalists and as readers, it's our responsiblity to question things more often.

  • The Passion of Miyamoto is now in theaters.
