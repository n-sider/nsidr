--- title: "Harvest Moon: Magic Melody available on GameCube" date: 2006-03-29T00:26:47Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Harvest Moon: Magical Melody is now available on the Nintendo GameCube for $39.99. The title was published by Natsume. Check out the game's official website for more info.

Harvest Moon: Magical Melody is a farming simulator and adventure game. Players can choose to play as either a boy or girl. Begin a new life and build your farm, raise a family, and much more. There are also mini-games in which 4 players can participate. Harvest Moon: Magical Melody currently has an 90 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 1 review).
