--- title: "Free Kirby-pink stylus to registrants" date: 2005-09-15T11:31:27Z ---

Nintendo of America is offering a free pink Kirby stylus to registered owners of Kirby: Canvas Curse, reportedly similar to the one bundled with the Japanese version of the game, Touch! Kirby.

If you're a registered My Nintendo user and have registered your copy of Kirby: Canvas Curse, you can claim your free stylus on Nintendo's stylus offer page.
