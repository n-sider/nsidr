--- title: "EA acquires comic talent for Marvel Nemesis" date: 2005-07-14T19:28:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Electronic Arts said it has signed comic writer, Mark Millar, and artist, Terry Dodson to assist in the development of Marvel Nemesis: Rise of the Imperfects. Millar is known for his work on several Marvel Comics series such as Ultimate X-Men, The Ultimates, and Ultimate Fantastic Four. He will be assisting EA in the creation of the background stories for several characters as well as the story-line for the one-player mode. Dodson has worked on Marvel's X-Force, House of M and Marvel Knights Spider-Man. He is helping EA with the character concept designs of several characters in the game including Spider-Man, Wolverine and Captain America. Marvel Nemesis: Rise of the Imperfects is scheduled for release on the Nintendo GameCube this fall. Screens and additional information can be found at the game's official website.
