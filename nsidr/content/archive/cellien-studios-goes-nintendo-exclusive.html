--- title: "Cellien Studios goes Nintendo exclusive" date: 2006-05-18T10:59:59Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Cellien Studios, a once-PC developer currently developing the massively-multiplayer online game Terra: Formations, announced yesterday it would dedicate itself to Nintendo platforms, according to IGN.

Cellien is currently working on original titles for DS, contract work for Game Boy Advance and is also negotiating to update titles from Game Boy Color to Game Boy Advance and to port Nintendo GameCube titles to Wii.
