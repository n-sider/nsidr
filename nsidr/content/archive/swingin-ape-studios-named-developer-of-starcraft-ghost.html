--- title: "Swingin' Ape Studios named developer of \"StarCraft: Ghost\"" date: 2004-07-08T14:34:28Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Blizzard has named Swingin' Ape Studios, developer of Metal Arms: Glitch in the System, as the new developer of StarCraft: Ghost. This is after Nihilistic Software was taken off the development of the game a few weeks ago.

The agreement between the companies further states that in the future Swingin' Ape may collaboratively develop multi-platform titles with Blizzard based off Blizzard licenses - such as Diablo, Warcraft, and StarCraft. No additional titles have been announced.
