--- title: "[E3] Square Enix brings Dragon Quest to Nintendo Wii launch" date: 2006-05-08T18:57:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to GameSpot.com, Square Enix will release "Dragon Quest: The Masked Queen and the Tower of Mirrors" for the launch of Nintendo Wii. The game was announced during its press conference. GameSpot describes it, "Some dude is fighting a stone golem in a forest. No real footage of the game, but still, big news."

The game is said to be inspired by the Kenshin Dragon Quest series. Stay tuned to N-Sider for more details.
