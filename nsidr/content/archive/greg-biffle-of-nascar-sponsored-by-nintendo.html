--- title: "Greg Biffle of NASCAR, sponsored by Nintendo" date: 2007-07-04T17:20:29Z contributors: - Jon Rust ---
#16, Car of Tomorrow
Nintendo has once again decided to dip its ladle in the profitable soup that is the non-gaming market. This time, they are sponsoring NASCAR driver Greg Biffle with a Wii-inspired car.

The car will make its debut August 12 at the Watkins Glen International. Nintendo and NASCAR fans can already purchase a die-cast replica, which is expected to ship in October.
