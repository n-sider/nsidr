--- title: "Former Capcom director Yoshiki Okamoto opens new studio" date: 2004-06-21T17:29:16Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Former Capcom director Yoshiki Okamoto has opened his new company called Game Republic. Okamoto was director alongside Capcom's Yoshifumi Yamashita on The Legend of Zelda: Oracle of Ages and Oracle of Seasons games for Game Boy Color, which were collaboratively developed with Nintendo. Okamoto left Capcom in July 2003 to pursue the creation of his new company.

Game Republic has not yet announced titles in development. Their website is located here.
