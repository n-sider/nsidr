--- title: "Nintendo DS said to cost less than $200" date: 2004-05-13T16:20:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to an article by the New York Times, Nintendo Co., Ltd. president Satoru Iwata has said the Nintendo DS will cost under $200. Furthermore, Shigeru Miyamoto told the Times that some of the features of the handheld were left out to keep costs down. One such feature may have been to include a tilt sensor for gyroscopic control.

Source: NYT
