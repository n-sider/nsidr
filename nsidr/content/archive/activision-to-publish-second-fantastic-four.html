--- title: "Activision to publish second Fantastic Four" date: 2005-08-16T00:27:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Activision is developing a second Fantastic Four title for a Nintendo handheld, according to NintendoInsider.com. The game currently has the tentative title of Fantastic Four: Flame On! and is under development by Torus Games. The title is said to follow a unique storyline featuring Johnny Storm, who's also known as the Human Torch. The platform and release date for the game are currently unknown.
