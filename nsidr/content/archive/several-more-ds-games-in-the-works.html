--- title: "Several more DS games in-the-works" date: 2005-02-26T14:00:55Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

It seems several obscure Nintendo DS games have popped out of the woodwork lately.

The first is Naruto: Saikyo Ninja Daikesshu 3 which is thus far only scheduled to release in Japan. Developd by Tomy and set for an April 21 release, the game is based on a Japanese anime and comic. Check out some screens for the title here, courtesy of The-MagicBox.com.

Japanese developer Starfish has a pinball game in development for the Nintendo DS called Devilish. The title is said to be an enhanced port a classic 1991 Genesis pinball game. The DS version will have touch screen and multiplayer support. Check out the first screens of the title here, courtesy of The-MagicBox.

Bigben Interactive will release its Nintendo DS racer during the second quarter (May - June) of this year. The game is called Cocoto Kart Racer. Check out a video here and screens here, courtesy of GBA.N64Europe.com.
