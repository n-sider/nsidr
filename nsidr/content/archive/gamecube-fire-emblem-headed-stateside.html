--- title: "GameCube Fire Emblem Headed Stateside" date: 2004-04-21T15:46:00Z contributors: - Cory Faller ---

N-Sider has learned that the GameCube version of Fire Emblem will in fact be released on western shores. The current US release estimate places the game in February 2005.

Supposedly, the next GBA Fire Emblem will also be released in America in a similar timeframe. The GBA game is a chronological sequel to the already released US version of Fire Emblem, but was actually the first of the two games to be released in Japan.
