--- title: "Medal of Honor European Assault now available" date: 2005-06-07T01:37:06Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts says it has released Medal of Honor European Assault in North America for the Nintendo GameCube. The game is said to feature authentic WWII vehicles and weapons, combat squad control, and more. Players will take the role of US Army Lieutenant William Holt and will be sent on missions with the task of gathering intelligence regarding the development and deployment of the deadly Nazi Tiger Tank and stopping the Nazi plan to develop and harness atomic energy. Check out the game's official website for more info and screens.

Medal of Honor European Assault currently has an 80 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com.
