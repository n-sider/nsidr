--- title: "Buena Vista announces E3 lineup for the Game Boy Advance" date: 2004-05-11T00:30:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Buena Vista has announced its lineup of licensed Game Boy Advance software to be on display during E3. The list can be seen below.

  • Power Rangers Dino Thunder
  • TRON 2.0: Killer App
  • Tim Burtons The Nightmare Before Christmas: The Pumpkin King
  • Disneys Kim Possible 2: Drakkens Demise
  • Disneys Lizzie McGuire 2
  • Disneys Thats So Raven
  • Disneys Lilo & Stitch 2: Hamsterviels Revenge
  • Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, Witch and The Wardrobe
