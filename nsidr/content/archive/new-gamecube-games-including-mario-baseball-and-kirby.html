--- title: "New GameCube games including Mario Baseball and Kirby" date: 2004-11-27T03:37:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo has released information and screens on several brand new as well as already-announced GameCube projects.

New projects include Mario Baseball, in co-development with Namco, a new traditional Kirby title, and the third game in the Donkey Konga series Donkey Konga 3. Check out screens of each of these titles below (as well as three new screens of The Legend of Zelda), courtesy of The-MagicBox.
