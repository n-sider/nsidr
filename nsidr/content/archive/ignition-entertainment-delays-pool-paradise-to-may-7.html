--- title: "Ignition Entertainment delays \"Pool Paradise\" to May 7" date: 2004-04-28T17:49:40Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ignition Entertainment's pool-simulation title Pool Paradise for the Nintendo GameCube, has been delayed to a May 7 release.

Ignition Entertainment product manager Peter Rollinson said the extra time would allow the company to finely hone the game. Pool Paradise will be released at the bargain price of $19.99.

Source: NintendoInsider.com
