--- title: "Tecmo commits to supporting Virtual Console" date: 2006-08-30T09:48:04Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

In a financial report issued Monday, Tecmo said they would support the Wii's Virtual Console "aggressively", presumably offering downloads of some of their classic titles for the service. Some of the more popular titles Tecmo has released for Nintendo consoles include the original Ninja Gaiden and Tecmo Bowl. It's probable they will make an appearance, but Tecmo is not giving specifics at this time.

Tecmo has previously committed to bring Super Swing Golf Pangya to Wii, but has been unclear on whether the title would include its popular online functionality.
