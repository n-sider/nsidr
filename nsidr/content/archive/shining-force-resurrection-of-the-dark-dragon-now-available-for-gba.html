--- title: "\"Shining Force: Resurrection of the Dark Dragon\" now available for GBA" date: 2004-06-09T00:20:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Atlus said it has released its Game Boy Advance strategy-RPG Shining Force: Resurrection of the Dark Dragon to North American retailers with an MSRP of $29.99.

For more information, including screens and video of the game, visit the official website.

Shining Force: Resurrection of the Dark Dragon has a current ranking of 77 percent on GameRankings.com.
