--- title: "\"Naruto RPG\" coming to the GBA in Japan" date: 2004-06-09T16:45:45Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Tomy said it will release Naruto RPG for the Game Boy Advance in Japan on July 22. There is currently no word on a North American release. The first screens can be seen here, courtesy of The-MagicBox.com.
