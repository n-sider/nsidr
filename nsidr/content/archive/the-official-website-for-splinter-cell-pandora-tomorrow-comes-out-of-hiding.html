--- title: "The Official Website for \"Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow\" comes out of hiding" date: 2004-02-23T22:19:01Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ubi Soft has launched the website for its Game Boy Advance and GameCube action-stealth game titled Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow. You can jump directly to the site to view screens and movies by clicking right here.

Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow is the sequel to Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell, which was released on Game Boy Advance and Nintendo GameCube in April of 2003. Players control Sam Fisher and use stealth to carry out investigations, infiltrations, and improvisations to keep out of the enemy's sight!

Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow will be available March 24, 2004, for the Game Boy Advance. The GameCube version is said to arrive in May at the earliest.
