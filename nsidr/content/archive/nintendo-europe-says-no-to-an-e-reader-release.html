--- title: "Nintendo Europe says no to an e-Reader release" date: 2004-07-08T15:57:15Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Europe said it no longer intends to release the Game Boy Advance e-Reader in Europe, according to Cube-Europe.com. Nintendo Europe feels there isn't the market for the product, however the company says it still plans to release some of the product through other means.

"We won't launch E-Reader in Europe," Shelly Pearce, head of PR stated. "We don't think the market potential is great enough to launch the product in Europe - however we will make the downloadable elements that cards provided available via different routes."
