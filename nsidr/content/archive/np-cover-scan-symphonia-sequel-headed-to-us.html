--- title: "NP cover scan: Symphonia sequel headed to US" date: 2008-02-06T17:19:55Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
Members of the GameTrailers.com forum have come up with a scan of next month's Nintendo Power cover, revealing that the previously-announced Wii sequel to GameCube's Tales of Symphonia is headed stateside with the title Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of the New World.

The magazine—these days run by Future instead of in-house by NOA—will have a 10 page feature on the game.
