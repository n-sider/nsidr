--- title: "Super Princess Peach for US release?" date: 2005-10-29T21:03:04Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

4 color rebellion has scans from Nintendo advertising indicating Super Princess Peach may be headed for a US release.

The mention says the game is still pending an ESRB rating, and does not yet have a release date. With any luck, we'll be hearing more by early next year.
