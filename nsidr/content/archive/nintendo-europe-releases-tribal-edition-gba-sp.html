--- title: "Nintendo Europe releases \"Tribal Edition\" GBA SP" date: 2004-06-20T16:41:54Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

This past week, Nintendo Europe released a newly-designed Game Boy Advance SP for 90 Euros. Called the "Tribal Edition", the system is colored silver and contains "tribal" patterned tattoos on the inside and outside.

"We feel we have created a product in the Tribal Edition that reflects the sentiments of today's youth - rebellion, attractiveness and spirituality," said Tim Freystedt, European Marketing Manager Game Boy Advance, apparently with a straight face. "The new console allows gamers to express these emotions in a fun and interactive way, enabling them to communicate their individuality."
