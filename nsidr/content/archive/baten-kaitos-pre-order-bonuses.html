--- title: "Baten Kaitos pre-order bonuses" date: 2004-10-29T19:32:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

EBGames and Toys R Us are offering pre-order bonuses for the GameCube exclusive RPG Baten Kaitos.

The pre-order bonus from ordering through EBGames is a two-by-three foot wall scroll. Meanwhile, if you pre-order through Toys R Us you will receive a Baten Kaitos soundtrack. If you're looking for another RPG to play after Paper Mario, Baten Kaitos is a highly suggested pick.
