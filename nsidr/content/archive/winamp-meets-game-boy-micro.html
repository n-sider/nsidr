--- title: "Winamp meets Game Boy Micro" date: 2006-04-05T05:00:23Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper ---

Ever wanted a music player that resembled Nintendo's smallest portable to date? Probably not, but that hasn't stopped a certain deviantART-ist from creating one. Behold -- the Game Boy Micro skin for Winamp!

Installing the skin is pretty cut-and-dry -- simply download it from this page (which also includes a cool illustration of its features), and follow the artist's instructions. He's even gone to the length of adding a "create your own" option that includes 9 color themes and 23 faceplates. Sweet!

Whether you use this skin or not, it'd be nice to drop by the artist's page and leave a comment -- people with this kind of talent and an obvious appreciation for gaming are okay in N-Sider's book.

When you're done there, let us know how it worked out.
