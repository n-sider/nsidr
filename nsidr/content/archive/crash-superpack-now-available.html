--- title: "Crash Superpack now available" date: 2005-10-20T19:06:58Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Vivendi Universal's Crash Superpack is now available for the Game Boy Advance. The title contains two titles "Crash Bandicoot 2: N-Tranced" and "Crash Nitro Kart" for a suggested retail price of $19.99.
