--- title: "Traveller's Tales developing \"Chronicles of Narnia\"" date: 2004-06-17T21:55:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Buena Vista Games will publish the Traveller's Tales developed Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, The Witch and The Wardrobe for the Nintendo GameCube, according to IGN.com.

Traveller's Tales is known for such GameCube titles as Finding Nemo and Haven: Call of the King.
