--- title: "Activision acquires rights to create games based on third Spider-Man movie" date: 2004-05-19T20:12:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Activision said it has acquired the rights to develop a game based on the third Spider-Man movie, scheduled to appear in theaters in May 2007.

Activision already released a game based on the first Spider-Man movie and is currently developing a game based on the second Spider-Man movie for GameCube and Game Boy Advance with a planned release in June.
