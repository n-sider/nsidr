--- title: "Zelda predicted to be big at Canadian retailers" date: 2005-07-05T20:40:56Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo's The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess for the Nintendo GameCube is predicted to be one of the biggest video games of 2005 by leading Canadian retailers, according to Nintendo Canada. "We're incredibly excited about the launch of The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess," said Jim Tyo, Vice President of Canadian Operations, EB Games. "This is the must have game of 2005, and we expect to sell tens of thousands of units at launch."
