--- title: "\"Caduceus Surgical Operation\" announced for the Nintendo DS" date: 2004-08-09T16:24:12Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Atlus announced a new Nintendo DS game called Caduceus Surgical Operation, according to The-MagicBox. The Nintendo DS is creating all-new genres with its touch screen. Caduceus Surgical Operation is described as a surgery action game which is played using the touch panel and microphone. You play as a young doctor and perform operations given to you on the top screen all while using your voice to keep the patient calm. The game is scheduled for a spring 2005 release in Japan.
