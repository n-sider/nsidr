--- title: "Nintendo Japan to release GBA wireless adapter on July 15" date: 2004-06-10T12:13:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo will release a standalone version of the Game Boy Advance Wireless Adapter in Japan on July 15, for 2000 yen, according to The-MagicBox.com. The Wireless Adapter is currently available as a bundle with the Japanese release of Mario Golf GBA Tour.
