--- title: "Nintendo confirms 62 titles for launch window" date: 2006-11-01T10:03:13Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America has released a list of titles that will be available for their upcoming Wii home console within five weeks after its North American launch this coming November 19. In the list is 32 new titles and 30 classic titles for the Virtual Console feature, which lets Wii owners purchase software over the Internet.

New games

These games will be available at retail on disc. Several of these titles do not yet have a committed release date, but all will be available before the end of the year.
