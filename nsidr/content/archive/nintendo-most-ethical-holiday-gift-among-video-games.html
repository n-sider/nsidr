--- title: "Nintendo \"most ethical\" holiday gift among video games" date: 2005-10-25T07:48:47Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Gamasutra is reporting that Nintendo has been named by "Ethical Consumer Magazine" as the most ethical holiday gift among a ranking of video game systems and companies.

The ranking is based on the company's ethical behavior. Nintendo scored 10.5 out of 20 compared to Nokia's 7.5, Sony's 6.5, and Microsoft's 6.5.
