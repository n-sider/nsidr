--- title: "DS leads UK with 3 million" date: 2007-03-27T14:36:40Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo UK boss David Yarnton wants you to know that the Nintendo DS is the UK's hottest handheld by a long shot, selling over three million units in the UK alone.

The dual-screened wonder was the best-selling platform in 2006 and, as Nintendo says, is already "the UK market leading hardware format again so far in 2007". Yarnton expects even more: "With many more Touch! Generations games launching in 2007, Nintendo DS and Wii will continue to be the main driving forces behind the expansion of the videogame market this year."
