--- title: "Gumby Vs. Astrobots now available" date: 2005-08-10T01:46:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Namco said Gumby Vs. The Astrobots is now available for the Game Boy Advance. More information including screens can be found at the game's official website. The game is rated E for Everyone and carries a suggested retail price of $19.99.
