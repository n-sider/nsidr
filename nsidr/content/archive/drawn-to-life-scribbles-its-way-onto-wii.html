--- title: "Drawn to Life scribbles its way onto Wii" date: 2008-02-05T22:23:31Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- At a recent conference call with investors, THQ president Brian Farrell discussed holiday quarter sales and their new DS franchise, the 5th Cell-developed Drawn to Life. Farrell says Drawn to Life has sold several hundred thousand copies worldwide and is largely responsible for a 94 percent rise in THQ's DS game sales over the past three fiscal quarters.

Farrel also informally announced that THQ will be bringing the Drawn to Life franchise to Wii, along with several other Wii-exclusive titles to be announced in the coming weeks.
