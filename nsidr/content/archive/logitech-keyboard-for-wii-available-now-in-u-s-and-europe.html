--- title: "Logitech keyboard for Wii, available now in U.S. and Europe" date: 2007-10-10T11:07:52Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Logitech today announced support for Nintendo's ever-popular Wii with the Classic Keyboard 200, available in stores now.

  • Plug-and-play, only requires 3.1 firmware update on Wii
  • Full-sized USB keyboard, compact form factor, low-profile keys, and spill-resistant design
  • Suggested retail price of $14.99
