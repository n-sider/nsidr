--- title: "BVG's Chicken Little now available" date: 2005-10-18T15:33:32Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- BVG's Chicken Little is now available for Game Boy Advance and GameCube. The title is on sale at a suggested retail price of $29.99 and $39.99, respectively.

The game marks the first release from BVG's recently acquired game development studio, Avalanche Software, based in Salt Lake City.
