--- title: "GameDaily's E3 drinking game" date: 2006-04-18T14:16:36Z ---

With the E3 2006 show rapidly coming up, GameDaily presents their E3 drinking game. You'll want to keep this handy guide by your side when you're following Nintendo's E3 preshow press conference with your fellow N-Forums members in our chat room.

Just be sure you've got a lot on-hand, because one of the rules is that you've got to take a sip whenever someone discusses market disruption.
