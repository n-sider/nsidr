--- title: "Resident Evil two-pack" date: 2004-11-11T21:40:00Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

CokeM Interactive will release the "Pure Evil 2-Pack" for GameCube on November 29, according to GameStop.com. The pack will contain the full versions of both Resident Evil and Resident Evil 0 and also a preview to Resident Evil 4. More information on the title can be found here.
