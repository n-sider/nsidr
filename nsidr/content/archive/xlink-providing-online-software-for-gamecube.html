--- title: "XLink providing online software for GameCube" date: 2004-05-25T02:04:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

XLink said it has released its tunneling software, XLink Kai: Evolution VII, which is compatible with the Xbox, GameCube and PlayStation 2.

XLink's free software allows gamers on different consoles to play online together, using a system-link/LAN enabled game and a PC or Mac.

More information can be found at XLink's website.
