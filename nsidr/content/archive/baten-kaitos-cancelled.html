--- title: "Baten Kaitos cancelled?" date: 2005-10-09T23:52:24Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Namco's DS release list, which previously listed Baten Kaitos as in development for Nintendo DS, has been updated recently, and the Baten Kaitos title has been removed.

Interestingly, the same list now confirms that Namco is working on a Tales title for the DS, which was announced recently.

Thanks to Samer from N-Sider Forums for the tip.
