--- title: "U.S. President Bush scores Brain Age for 60th" date: 2006-07-06T10:53:20Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

On the occasion of his 60th birthday today, Nintendo has sent President of the United States George W. Bush a Touch Generations-branded bag containing a Nintendo DS Lite and a copy of brain-training title Brain Age. Kotaku has a picture of the bag and the text of the letter as well as plenty of Bush jokes in the comments section.

Nintendo has not made any announcements that they plan to give free gaming systems and/or games to any non-heads-of-state turning 60 today.
