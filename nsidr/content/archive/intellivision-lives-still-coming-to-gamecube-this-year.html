--- title: "\"Intellivision Lives!\" still coming to GameCube this year" date: 2004-08-09T16:39:58Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to GamerFeed, Crave Entertainment and Realtime Associates announced that Intellivision Lives!, a compilation of more than 60 games, is still scheduled for release on the Nintendo GameCube. The title was previously cancelled. Although a specific release date was not divulged, Intellivision Lives! is being prepared for a release later this year.
