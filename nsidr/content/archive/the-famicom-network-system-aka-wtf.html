The hardware itself doesn't require any external power source, like the Famicom Disk System add-on, which had a disk drive motor to spin around. The Network System has no moving parts, so it draws its power entirely from the Famicom's cartridge slot.

It's kind of a gargantuan thing, resting atop the system and turning the whole thing into a bizarre, angular monolith that kind of resembles the Thundercats' lair.
