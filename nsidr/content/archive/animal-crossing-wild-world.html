--- title: "Animal Crossing Wild World" date: 2005-09-09T00:43:54Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Animal Crossing: Wild World, as it has been recently renamed, will arrive in North America on December 5. Nintendo UK said Animal Crossing: Wild World is currently scheduled for a European release during the first quarter of 2006. Animal Crossing: Wild World will be one of the first titles to utilize the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection service which will allow players to play with others from around the world.
