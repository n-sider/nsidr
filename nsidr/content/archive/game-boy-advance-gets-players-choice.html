--- title: "Game Boy Advance gets Player's Choice" date: 2006-03-10T17:52:48Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper ---

From IGN comes the report that Nintendo is adding a Player's Choice line to its expansive Game Boy Advance library. This moniker has previously been applied to high-selling titles as far back as the SNES and Game Boy Classic days, and is a welcome addition as they are generally re-issued at a much lower price-point.

The series will make its debut on April 2nd with Mario and Luigi: Supertar Saga, Super Mario Advance and Yoshi's Island: Super Mario Advance 3 leading the way, all priced at a generous $19.99 (MSRP).

Stay tuned to N-Sider for more as it develops.
