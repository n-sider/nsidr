--- title: "\"Mario Party 6\" and new peripheral announced" date: 2004-08-26T22:24:01Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has announced that Mario Party 6 will appear on the Nintendo GameCube December 6. But there's more. Mario Party 6 will make use of the GameCube Mic, the until-now mysterious peripheral Nintendo mentioned earlier in the year. One such way the Mic will be utilized in the game is for a "game show" mode where players must answer questions with the microphone.

Mario Party 6 will feature 75 all new minigames and six new game boards. A day and night system will be implimented which will alter the way the game is played. We'll try to have more details and pictures of the Mic device soon.
