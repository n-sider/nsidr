--- title: "Europe to get C64 games on Virtual Console" date: 2008-02-21T12:51:33Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of Europe has announced that Commodore 64 games are destined for the Virtual Console service in that territory.

Games like Uridium and International Karate have been confirmed so far, and titles will sell for 500 Wii Points apiece—the same price point as most NES games. At press time, this apparently ludicrously high price point has the Internets aflame.
