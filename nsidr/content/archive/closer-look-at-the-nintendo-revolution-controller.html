--- title: "Closer look at the Nintendo Revolution controller" date: 2006-02-08T15:39:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

It seems Nintendo has sent out a brochure in Japan for employee recruitment. In this brochure Nintendo has included several very cool images, a few of which show the progression of the Nintendo Revolution controller and nunchaku accessory.

The book has a very appealing visual style. Not something we'd previously associate with Nintendo, however something we're quickly becoming familiar with.

Thanks to the Gaming-Age Forums for the heads up.
