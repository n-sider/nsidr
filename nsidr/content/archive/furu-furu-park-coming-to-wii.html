--- title: "Furu Furu Park coming to Wii" date: 2007-07-10T00:56:47Z contributors: - Michael Foster --- Majesco Entertainment announced yet another Nintendo title with Furu Furu Park for Wii. Originally released April 19 in Japan, it is now being brought over to America.

Furu Furu Park is a collection of 30 arcade-style games. An example of some of the included games include reworkings of Arkanoid and Bubble Bobble, as well as Sonic Blastman 20XX, a continuation of the series for arcade and SNES. The games will take advantage of the Wii Remote's capabilities, such as the game Safe Cracker, where a combination must be memorized and the player tilts the remote left or right to unlock the safe.

Developers Taito and 505 Games are also including three multiplayer modes to play against a friend: Free Battle, Panel Attack, and the chance to win a girl's heart in Love Challenge. Furu Furu Park is due for a fall release.
