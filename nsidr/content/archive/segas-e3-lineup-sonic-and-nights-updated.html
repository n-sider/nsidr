--- title: "Sega's E3 lineup: Sonic and NiGHTS [updated]" date: 2007-07-10T16:31:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Sega is the latest publisher to announce a list of titles it intends to showcase during this week's E3. Sega has several exclusive games in development for the Wii and Nintendo DS platforms including Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games, which we'll have the opportunity to see for the first time.

Nintendo DS

  • Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games
  • Sonic Rush Adventure
  • The Golden Compass
  • Bleach: The Blade of Fate
  • Iron Man

Wii

  • Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games
  • Ghost Squad
  • NiGHTS: Journey of Dreams
  • The Golden Compass
  • Bleach: Shattered Blade
  • Iron Man

Please note that Sega's lineup is not limited to these announced titles. It's possible the publisher will reveal additional projects in development at the show.
