--- title: "Phoenix Wright came and went—did you notice?" date: 2006-06-21T09:55:50Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Fans hoping to get their hands on Capcom's increasingly rare Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney may have missed their opportunity recently.

Punch Jump has been watching for the new shipment Capcom was to send to stores—and they report that at least at online outlets like Walmart.com and JR.com the game sold out in days. They've also noted that eBay prices routinely go for a 30% premium over the game's $30 list price due to limited quantities.

Perhaps, if Phoenix watchers are lucky, Capcom will ship a larger restock next time.
