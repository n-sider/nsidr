--- title: "European Wii release details" date: 2006-09-15T10:52:44Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

As part of the press conference currently going on in Europe, Nintendo has revealed their release plans for Wii on that continent.

Wii will go on sale December 8 for €249, or GBP 179. The console bundle is similar to the U.S. bundle: one white console, one Remote with Nunchuck, and a copy of Wii Sports.

Software will sell for €49 to €59, or GBP 34-39.
