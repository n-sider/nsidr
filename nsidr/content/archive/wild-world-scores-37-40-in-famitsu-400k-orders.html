--- title: "Wild World scores 37/40 in Famitsu, 400k orders" date: 2005-11-18T13:17:36Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

German gaming news site Gamefront.de has two pieces of news this week concerning Nintendo's upcoming Wi-Fi Connection title, Animal Crossing: Wild World.

First, in a recent issue of Famitsu, the game has scored a very impressive 37/40, getting scores of 9, 9, 10, and 9 from the four reviewers. The second point of interest is that Japanese preorders for the game, which releases in that territory on November 23, have reached 400,000 -- 60% more than the first-week sales of 250,000 Nintendogs managed in that territory.

Wild World appears to be shaping up as Nintendo's next big DS title. North Americans will only have to wait until December 5 to get their hands on the localized version.
