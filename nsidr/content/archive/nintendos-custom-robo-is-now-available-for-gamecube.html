--- title: "Nintendo's \"Custom Robo\" is now available for GameCube" date: 2004-05-10T16:30:10Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo said its robot battle RPG Custom Robo for Gamecube is now available at retailers nationwide. The title features an MSRP of $39.99. The game features a multiplayer mode as well as a single-player RPG scenario.
