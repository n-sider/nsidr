--- title: "Sega announces two RPGs" date: 2005-09-10T14:10:30Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Sega said it will develop an RPG version of The King of Beetle Mushiking series for the NIntendo DS. The game is scheduled for a Japanese release on December 8, according to The-MagicBox.com.

Sega said it is also collaborating with Chunsoft on an RPG called Furai no Shiren for the Nintendo DS. The title is scheduled for a Spring 2006 release in Japan.
