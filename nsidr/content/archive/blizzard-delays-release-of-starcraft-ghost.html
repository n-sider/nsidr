--- title: "Blizzard delays release of \"StarCraft: Ghost\"" date: 2004-06-20T16:40:31Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Blizzard has delayed the release of StarCraft: Ghost until a tentative early 2005 release. Blizzard said it has chosen the delay to allow the company to "expand and evolve the design" of the game.

Mike Morhaime, president of Blizzard Entertainment said, "We are sorry to disappoint players with a delayed launch but are confident that we will deliver a great game with the additional development time."
