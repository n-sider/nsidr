--- title: "Fire Emblem for GameCube gets a date and story" date: 2004-04-05T21:58:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan has opened a website for the recently announced Fire Emblem title for GameCube. Below are some moderately accurate translations of that information.

Characters

Ike - with sword in hand and determined posture, he appears to be the main character
Mist - a young innocent girl who's supposedly Ike's sister
Grail - Ike's father and leader of the mercenary group, a strong man who brandishes a two-sided battle axe
Tiamat - a proud woman with flowing red hair, treats Ike and Mist like her younger brother and sister
Senerio - a priest

Story

The story takes place on the continent of Telius, which is composed of several kingdoms, each of which possess their own influence.

Near the border of these countries, a mercenary team establishes its headquarters.

Princess of the Crimea Kingdom flees from her country and requests Ike's help. The Crimea Kingdom was invaded by the Dayne Kingdom and left very weak. The Princess of the Crimea Kingdom hires Ike and his mercenary team to escort her to the Gaul Kingdom. The group encounters all sorts of monsters and beasts along the way.

A friend of the Crimea Kingdom, the Gaul Kingdom agrees to wage war with the Dayne Kingdom.

Eventually, the entire continent is sucked into war.

Developed by Intelligent Systems, Fire Emblem: Souen no Kiseki (translated literally to "the green locus of the flame") is scheduled for a winter release in Japan.
