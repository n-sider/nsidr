--- title: "Wired's pictures of Nintendo's California digs" date: 2007-12-10T10:42:21Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
Wired's Chris Kohler has been to Nintendo's new offices in Redwood Shores—called the "San Francisco office" despite it being an hour's drive from SF—and has gathered a number of pictures of the classy offices, which don't seem to have much in the way of ties to Nintendo's past on exhibit.

Chris Kohler, Wired...it seems like the decor here will be more elegant. More reflective of the "new Nintendo," the one for adults and important people in suits? Maybe.
