--- title: "Special limited edition \"Donkey Kong SP\" for Japanese gamers" date: 2004-06-10T11:58:35Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

For the release of Donkey Kong Country 2 (as well as Donkey Konga 2), Nintendo of Japan will be offering a contest that will give 1,000 gamers the chance to win a limited edition "Donkey Kong SP" Game Boy Advance SP. Japanese gamers only need to pick up one of the two previously mentioned titles to enter to the contest. An entry form is included with each game.
