--- title: "Nintendo Australia brings lower prices to some games" date: 2007-01-19T11:08:53Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Amid apparent discontent over Wii software prices in the land Down Under, IGN AU has news and words from Nintendo of Australia's Vispi Bhopti indicating the company is considering lower prices for some software in that territory.

The first title to get the treatment is the upcoming-in-that-territory Wario Ware: Smooth Moves, which will hit not at the usual price point of AU$99.95 (about US$79), but instead will be sold at AU$79.95 (about US$63). Future titles slated for the lower price point include Kororinpa and Wing Island as well as one unnamed title. Upcoming exciting truck game Excite Truck is known to remain at the traditional price point.
