--- title: "Further information on Everybody Votes Channel" date: 2007-02-15T17:56:15Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo has released more information on the Everybody Votes Channel, the arrival of which we covered yesterday.

Regional questions will be posted every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, and new global questions will be posted semimonthly. After polls close, results will be displayed in a pie chart made up of Miis, to show voters how their opinions stack up. Some poll results will also have demographic information by U.S. state or by gender.

The channel is free to download from the Wii Shop Channel right now, though you will need to get a Wii System update to use it.
