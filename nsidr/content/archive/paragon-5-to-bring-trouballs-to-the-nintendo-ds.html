--- title: "Paragon 5 to bring \"Trouballs\" to the Nintendo DS" date: 2004-07-16T01:56:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Paragon 5 is bringing Trouballs to the Nintendo DS, according to NintendoInsider.com.

"Trouballs is a highly addictive puzzle game based on the simple game mechanic of rotation," Paul Bragiel, CEO of Paragon 5 told Nintendo Insider. "This game is perfectly suited for the DS and we especially look forward to taking advantage of the wireless multiplayer."
