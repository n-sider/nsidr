--- title: "Brownie Brown developing title for the GameCube?" date: 2004-08-25T23:46:27Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Brownie Brown, Nintendo developer of Magical Vacation and Sword of Mana for Game Boy Advance, announced on its website, according to The-MagicBox, that they are recruiting new staff for a GameCube project. Although no details are known, it's possible this is another collaboration with Square Enix.
