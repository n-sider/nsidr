--- title: "AQ Interactive commits to Revolution" date: 2005-12-21T15:47:37Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

IGN is reporting that new Japanese game company AQ Interactive has committed to Revolution.

AQ announced that it is working on "an original action title and a game based off a major comic license," though no other details were forthcoming. AQ also has an adventure game and educational title in the works for the Nintendo DS.

Also of interest is that Final Fantasy creator and Mistwalker founder Hironobu Sakaguchi is a shareholder in the new company. Sakaguchi was on hand to announce the title AQ is working on with Mistwalker and Cavia for the Xbox 360.
