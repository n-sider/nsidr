--- title: "Details on Nintendo's May 9 E3 press conference" date: 2006-04-29T04:06:17Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

We've received word from Nintendo that a webcast of its E3 press conference will be available on May 9. We've also heard that those lucky few attending the show live will be assigned to different Nintendo-themed seating areas including Warioland, Mushroom Kingdom, Yoshi's Island, Hyrule, etc. We'll let you know of further details as the time approaches.

Only ten days remain until the big morning where Nintendo will finally take the veil off its revolutionary console and games. N-Sider will be in attendance to cover the press conference and the following days of the E3 expo. For a history of the event, check out N-Sider's feature The History of E3.

Third-party developers are already preparing for the overload of information that will arrive at the show with early software announcements. N-Sider has compiled a list of games officially announced for Nintendo's Wii console. Keep in mind, especially if you notice any games lacking, that this list reflects only those that have a publisher or are being created by an established developer.
