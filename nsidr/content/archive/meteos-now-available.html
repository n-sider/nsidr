--- title: "Meteos now available" date: 2005-06-27T19:41:15Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo said that Meteos is now available for the Nintendo DS. The title is developed by Bandai and Q Entertainment as well as Masahiro Sakurai, former employee of HAL Laboratory. Check out the official website for more information.

Meteos currently has an 86 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com (based on 11 reviews).
