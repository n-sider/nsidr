--- title: "Nintendo Power's Future plans" date: 2007-09-18T09:52:32Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Zentendo has unearthed a job posting for a Licensing Manager for NOA's Nintendo Power magazine, confirming earlier rumors the magazine was to be moved out of its longstanding in-house position and into the hands of a contractor, much like the also-Future-run Official Nintendo Magazine over in Europe.

The news comes on the heels of the closure of the confusingly named NSider Forums at Nintendo.com, which was announced yesterday with a message posted on their site. Nintendo of America is promising a redesign for Nintendo.com, and it seems plausible to speculate that the Future deal might be related to this action.
