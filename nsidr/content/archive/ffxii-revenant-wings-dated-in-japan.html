--- title: "FFXII: Revenant Wings dated in Japan" date: 2007-02-06T15:52:02Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- The Magic Box has brought news from Japan, including the announcement that Final Fantasy XII side-story Final Fantasy XII Revenant Wings will be released April 26.

The game can be played in its entirety with the stylus, and will sell for 5,040 yen. No North American release plans have yet been announced for the title.
