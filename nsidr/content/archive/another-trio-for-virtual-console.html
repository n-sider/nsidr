--- title: "Another trio for Virtual Console" date: 2007-01-08T12:28:22Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- It's the dawn of a new week once again and you know what that means!

VIRTUAL CONSOLE MONDAY


  • Gradius (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)
  • Soldier Blade (TurboGrafx16, 1 player, 600 Wii Points)
  • Dungeon Explorer (TurboGrafx16, 1-5 players, 600 Wii Points)

Of particular note is Dungeon Explorer's repeat appearance on the release list; emulation issues kept it from hitting the Wii Shop Channel as expected over the holidays. Aside from the Advance Wars-themed Dungeon Explorer though, Gradius and Soldier Blade are both solidly enjoyable titles for the shooters among us!
