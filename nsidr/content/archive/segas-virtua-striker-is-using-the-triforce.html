--- title: "Sega's \"Virtua Striker\" is using the Triforce" date: 2004-06-20T16:41:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sega's Amusement Vision development studio will develop a title in its Virtua Striker soccer series Virtua Striker 4 for the GameCube-powered Nintendo-Sega-Namco Triforce arcade board. The game is currently on test in Tokyo. There is currently no word on a release date nor console plans for Virtua Striker 4.
