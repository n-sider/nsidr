--- title: "Disneys Magical Quest 3 now available" date: 2005-06-15T00:29:03Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Capcom said it has released Disneys Magical Quest 3 Starring Mickey and Donald for the Game Boy Advance. Disneys Magical Quest 3 is a 2-D side-scrolling action adventure based on the popular Super Nintendo Entertainment System game. The title currently has a 90 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.
