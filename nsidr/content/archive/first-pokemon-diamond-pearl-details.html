--- title: "First Pokemon Diamond/Pearl details" date: 2005-07-12T01:58:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Pokmon Diamond & Pearl for the Nintendo DS will release in Japan during 2006, according to a recent article in Japanese Coro Coro Magazine. The new titles are said to be compatible with the GameCube and Game Boy Advance Pokemon games including Ruby, Sapphire, FireRed, LeafGreen and Emerald. The setting of the new games will occur in a new location, unique from prior iterations in the series. Finally, Diamond & Pearl have been announced to be Wi-Fi compatible for up to 16 players.
