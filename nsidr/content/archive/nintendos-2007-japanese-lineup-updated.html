--- title: "Nintendo's 2007 Japanese lineup (updated)" date: 2006-11-25T14:02:14Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- As part of their quarterly fiscal report, Nintendo Co., Ltd. has unveiled their plans for Japanese Wii releases in 2007. They are as follows:

  • Battalion Wars II
  • Big Brain Academy
  • Disaster: Day of Crisis
  • DK Kongo Blast
  • Excite Truck
  • Fire Emblem: Goddess of Dawn
  • Forever Blue
  • Mario Party 8
  • Mario Strikers Charged
  • Metroid Prime 3: Corruption
  • Project H.A.M.M.E.R.
  • Super Mario Galaxy
  • Super Paper Mario
  • Super Smash Bros. Brawl
  • Wii Health Pack
  • Wii Music

Thanks to N-Sider Forums member Teppo Holmqvist for the tip and his work deciphering the crazy moon language.

Update: The following titles are actually listed as undecided, not for 2007. Our apologies for the mistake.

  • Animal Crossing
  • Eyeshield 21
