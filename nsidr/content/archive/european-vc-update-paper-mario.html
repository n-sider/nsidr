--- title: "European VC update: Paper Mario" date: 2007-07-13T12:31:11Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Perhaps to make up for taking so long to bring Super Paper Mario to Europe, the European Virtual Console is getting the first game in the series before America.

  • Air Zonk (TurboGrafx16, 600 points)
  • Paper Mario (Nintendo 64, 1000 points)

This brings the total number of VC games up to 111 in Europe.
