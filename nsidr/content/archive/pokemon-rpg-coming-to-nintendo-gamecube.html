--- title: "Pokemon RPG coming to Nintendo GameCube" date: 2005-03-10T03:06:10Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has announced that a Pokemon RPG, tentatively titled Pokemon XD will be coming to the Nintendo GameCube later this year.

"Pokemon XD is a brand new Pokemon game for GameCube," explained Reginald Fils-Aime in an interview with IGNCube.com. "It's a true RPG Pokemon. It's isn't a sequel to Collosseum. It's a brand new build. A brand new game that really plays off the RPG aspects. So think of it this way. If you loved Ruby and Sapphire, you're going to love Pokemon XD for GameCube."

Screens and more information on the title will likely arrive closer to the May Electronic Entertainment Expo.
