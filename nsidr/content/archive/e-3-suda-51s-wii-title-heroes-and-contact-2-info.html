--- title: "[E3] Suda 51's Wii title \"Heroes\" and Contact 2 info" date: 2006-05-11T02:38:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Gamasutra spoke with Suda 51 today to discover his new Wii title is called Heroes and is being collaboratively developed with Marvelous Entertainment and Spike. The title was initially scheduled to be shown during the Expo, however it has been held back for undisclosed reasons.

Gamasutra also learned that Akira Ueda, director of Grasshopper's Nintendo DS RPG title Contact, is going to be forming a new company called Audio Inc. This new studio is already in development with Contact 2 for an unspecified Nintendo platform.
