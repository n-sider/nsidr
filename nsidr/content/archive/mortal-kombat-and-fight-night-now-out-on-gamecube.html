--- title: "Mortal Kombat and Fight Night now out on GameCube" date: 2005-03-02T02:51:56Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

If the slicing and dicing suits your fancy, Midway has released Mortal Kombat: Deception for the Nintendo GameCube. Screens and more information can be seen at the official website. The title currently has an 80 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.

If the slapping and punching suits your fancy, Electronic Arts has released Fight Night Round 2 for the Nintendo GameCube. Screens and additional info can be seen at the official website. The title currently has an 88 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.
