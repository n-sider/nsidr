--- title: "Square Enix working on the ultimate metagame" date: 2007-03-06T15:57:34Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Coming out of GDC's Serious Games Summit, where the topic is using games in education, comes word from Square Enix, who says they're working on their own serious game project: Project GB.

Project GB, in the works for Nintendo DS is designed to teach players skills necessary for game development, including programming, graphics, music, and writing. The ten-man team behind GB is led by Vagrant Story's Tadashi Tsushima.
