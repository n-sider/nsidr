--- title: "Miyamoto to keynote at GDC" date: 2007-01-22T12:10:32Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Hot on the heels of our story about Aonuma and Kondo doing lectures at the upcoming Game Developers Conference comes the news that Shigeru Miyamoto himself will be presenting a keynote.

The talk, entitled "A Creative Vision", will see Miyamoto revealing "how a singular creative vision drives his work, not only in terms of his world-renowned software like the Super Mario Bros. and Legend of Zelda series, but also in generating key technologies, including the current global phenomena, Nintendo DS and Wii."

Jamil Moledina, executive director of the GDC, pitched the keynote thusly: "The Game Developers Conference is a place for developers to learn from one another, and I think it is safe to say that every member of the community has at one time or another fantasized about having Shigeru Miyamoto as a mentor and teacher. By guiding us through his creative vision with a keynote at this year's GDC, Miyamoto is allowing all attendees this incomparable privilege."

The Game Developers Conference runs from March 5 through 9 at the Moscone Convention Center in San Francisco.
