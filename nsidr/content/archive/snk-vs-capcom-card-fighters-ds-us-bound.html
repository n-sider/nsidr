--- title: "SNK vs. Capcom Card Fighters DS US bound" date: 2006-12-16T23:45:50Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- Via an inquisitive fellow at Insert Credit comes the news that SNK vs. Capcom Card Fighters DS is indeed headed to the United States.

This comes as particularly satisfying news considering the relatively uncertain nature of the title, which was in development for some time.

Hurray!

The title will hit in the first quarter of 2007 for an MSRP of $29.99.
