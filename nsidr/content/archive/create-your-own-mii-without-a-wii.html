Looking to practice your Mii making skills before the November 19 launch? A savvy Nintendo fan from website Joystiq has heard your request and developed a Mii application. The nifty program lets you develop your very own Mii face. Check it out while pretending your mouse is a Wiimote.

When you're done, go share it in the N-Forums.
