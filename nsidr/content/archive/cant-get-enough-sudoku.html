--- title: "Can't get enough sudoku?" date: 2006-04-11T10:26:37Z ---

Not content to put a hundred sudoku puzzles into next week's Brain Age, Nintendo has announced they're bringing an all new sudoku-only title known as Sudoku Gridmaster to the Nintendo DS this summer.

Sudoku Gridmaster will be played with the touch screen and will feature over 400 puzzles selected "by the original creators of sudoku". Nintendo currently intends to release this title in the US come June 26.
