--- title: "Elebits first game to use WiiConnect24" date: 2006-11-27T11:19:04Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- According to an interview on Famitsu online, upcoming Wii furniture-tossing title Elebits will be the first title to support the console's WiiConnect24 service, which connects Wii consoles in sleep mode to each other and to publishers over the Internet.

Elebits will use this functionality to permit the exchange of player-designed levels in the game with members of their friend list, as well as in-game photos. Konami will also be able to send additional game levels to players.

Elebits releases in Japan on December 8, and sometime in December stateside—Konami has committed to getting it out in the Wii's launch window, which ends with the new year.
