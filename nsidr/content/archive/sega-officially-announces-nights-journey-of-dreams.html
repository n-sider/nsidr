--- title: "Sega officially announces NiGHTS: Journey of Dreams" date: 2007-04-02T16:57:36Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Sega of America has officially announced today that they will be bringing NiGHTS: Journey of Dreams to Wii in North America.

Playing as the titular NiGHTS, players will fly through Nightopia, controlled with the Wii Remote. Seven worlds will be available in which the player will confront "Nightmarens" from the dark world of Nightmare with acrobatics, flight, and unspecified "other special abilities".

Journey of Dreams is currently set for a fall 2007 release.
