--- title: "Howard Lincoln comments on the Nintendo DS" date: 2004-05-13T03:54:59Z contributors: - James Remington ---

The Seattle Post-Intelligencier has posted some comments former Nintendo Executive Howard Lincoln made after Nintendo's E3 media briefing.

On the Nintendo DS:

"First of all, I thought they did a marvelous job. It was a great press conference, and Reggie (Fils-Aime), our new marketing executive, I thought did a tremendous job. This Nintendo DS looks spectacular. It has so many functions, and the way it was presented today was very effective in getting all those features out -- the wireless feature, the touchpad feature, the dual-screen feature, one screen in 3D, the other in 2D. So many things that developers can focus on, and so it's not just an add-on product, it's a completely new product, a completely new category."
