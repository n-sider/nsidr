--- title: "Reggie on Revolution, possible re-design for DS" date: 2005-11-10T17:17:19Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- NOA's executive vice president of sales and marketing, Reggie Fils-Aime recently touched on the pricing strategy for Revolution, as well as a possible face-lift for Nintendo DS.

"As soon as [the DS] was launched, we started looking at ways to tweak it visually", said Fils-Aime in an interview with CNN Money. He also puts the long-argued Revolution HD rumors to rest, claiming what Nintendo offers in gameplay and approachability will "more than make up for the lack of HD".
