--- title: "Sega announces E3 lineup." date: 2004-05-10T18:04:11Z contributors: - James Remington ---

Sega has announced their official list of games that they will have on display.

  • Amazing Island (Nintendo GameCube)
  • Astro Boy: Omega Factor (Game Boy Advance)
  • Puyo Pop Fever (Nintendo GameCube)
  • Virtua Quest (Nintendo GameCube)
