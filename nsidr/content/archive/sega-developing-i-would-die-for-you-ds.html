--- title: "Sega developing \"I Would Die For You\" DS" date: 2004-09-10T12:34:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sega Japan announced a stylish new title for the Nintendo DS called "I Would Die For You". The gameplay is said to involve the touch screen in which players can interact with a couple and assist them through the adventure. Screens are courtesy of Famitsu.
