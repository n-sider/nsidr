--- title: "PaRappa creator, artist team up again for Wii title" date: 2007-12-10T15:58:30Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Majesco Entertainment announced today that PaRappa the Rapper creator Masaya Matsuura and artist Rodney Alan Greenblat, along with NanaOn-Sha, are teaming up to create a new music-based video game for Wii.

There are currently no details on the title, but Greenblat says it will be "a one-of-a-kind music game designed from the ground up for the Wii system".
