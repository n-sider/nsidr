--- title: "Metroid Prime 2: Echoes hits shelves" date: 2004-11-17T18:26:32Z contributors: - Jeffrey Van Camp ---

Metroid Prime 2: Echoes should be available in most stores across the nation today. Though the game officially launched on November 15th, several stores did not recieve or release the game until today. Echoes is the sequel to the critically acclaimed Metroid Prime, which hit shelves roughly two years ago. For basic information about the sequel, check out N-Sider's game profile page here.
