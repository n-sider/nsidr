--- title: "Nintendo's Game Boy production to change focus" date: 2004-05-30T17:49:48Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo's managing director Yoshihiro Mori said that normal production of the original Game Boy Advance will stop and instead be done on an orders received basis, according to PolygonMag.com.

Nintendo's will instead be focused on the production of its Game Boy Advance SP. In addition, Nintendo is preparing to begin production of its new handheld Nintendo DS.

Nintendo also allegedly said it plans to release the Game Boy Advance platform in China later this year. Further details are not known.
