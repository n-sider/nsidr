--- title: "Namco and Bandai merger proceeding" date: 2005-06-28T20:39:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Namco said during an investor meeting that the proposed merger between Namco and Bandai has been approved by investors, according to GameDaily.com. The new company Bandai Namco Holdings may be finalized by September 2005. Executives say there will be no layoffs as a result of the merger.
