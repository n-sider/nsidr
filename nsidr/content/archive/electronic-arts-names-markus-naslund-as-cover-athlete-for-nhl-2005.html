--- title: "Electronic Arts names Markus Naslund as cover athlete for \"NHL 2005\"" date: 2004-06-22T20:25:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts has named Vancouver Canucks left winger Markus Naslund as its cover athlete and spokesman for its EA Sports NHL 2005 game.

NHL 2005 will be coming to Game Boy Advance and Nintendo GameCube this fall. For more information, visit the official website here.
