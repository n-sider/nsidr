--- title: "Konami hopes to sell a quarter of a million copies of \"Boktai 2\"" date: 2004-07-06T17:43:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Konami said it hopes to sell between 200,000 and 300,000 copies worldwide of its sun-sensitive Game Boy Advance title Boktai 2: Solar Boy Django, according to The-MagicBox. The game is set for a Japanese release on July 22.
