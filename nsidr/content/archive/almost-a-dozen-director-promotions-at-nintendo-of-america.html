--- title: "Almost a dozen director promotions at Nintendo of America" date: 2004-08-24T02:29:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has announced several promotions for its corporate positions in preparation for the launch of the Nintendo DS and other products. Of course, N-Sider has updated their respective employee profiles to reflect these changes. The titles next to their names below are their new positions. N-Sider would like to congratulate them all.

Employees

  • Beth Llewelyn (Senior Director of Corporate Communications)
  • Robert Matthews (Senior Director of Consumer Marketing)
  • Jon Pederson (Senior Director of Technical Services)
  • Carrie Schnelker (Senior Director and Deputy General Counsel)
  • Steve Singer (Senior Director of Sales and Marketing, Latin America)
  • Murray Weir (Senior Director of Supply Chain Planning)
  • Kevan Wilson (Senior Director of Sales)
  • Anka Dolecki (Director of Public Relations)
  • Casey Pelkey (Business Director of Publications)
