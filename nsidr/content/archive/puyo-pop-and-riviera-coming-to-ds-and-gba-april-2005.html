--- title: "Puyo Pop and Riviera coming to DS and GBA April 2005" date: 2005-02-23T22:00:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Atlus U.S.A. has announced two titles scheduled for release on Nintendo handhelds this April. Puyo Pop Fever is coming to the Nintendo DS while Riviera: The Promised Land is coming to the Game Boy Advance.

Puyo Pop Fever is a rendition of the puzzle game we all know and love. The game will feature 8-player simultaneous puzzle play with a single Game Card. Riviera: The Promised Land is described as an RPG with 30 hours of gameplay and multiple endings.
