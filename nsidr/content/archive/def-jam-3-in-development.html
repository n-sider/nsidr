--- title: "Def Jam 3 in development" date: 2005-08-17T23:54:12Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Electronic Arts and Def Jam Enterprises said they will release Def Jam 3 for next-generation consoles. Def Jam 3 is currently in development at EA Chicago.

"This partnership continues to solidify the Def Jam Enterprises and EA brands as catalysts of cultural product innovation in all facets of entertainment," said Kevin Liles, President and Chief Executive Officer of Def Jam Enterprises in a press release. "The next generation platform opens unlimited possibilities in gaming and we are confident that along with EA, we will guarantee ground-breaking titles."
