--- title: "Worms Open Warfare now available for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-03-23T02:04:16Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Worms: Open Warfare is now available for Nintendo DS at a retail price of $29.99. The title comes to DS via publisher THQ and developer Team 17. More info can be found at the game's official website.

Worms: Open Warfare contains the familiar style of gameplay established in the series. New weapons are at your disposal including bazookas, exploding sheep, homing missiles, grenades and banana bouncing bombs of death. A multiplayer mode also means that four players can connect and battle each other locally. The game currently has a 70 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 2 reviews).
