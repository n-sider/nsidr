--- title: "[E3] Mario playable; New Duck Hunt and Resident Evil" date: 2006-05-10T14:04:12Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGN.com has learned of several new titles coming to the Nintendo Wii. The first is a remake of Duck Hunt. The game will be on display within Nintendo's E3 booth. It's suspected the title will make full use of the pointing ability of the Wii Remote controller.

The second title uncovered is an exclusive edition of Capcom's survival-horror series, Resident Evil. "The next-generation console Wii is garnering much attention with its groundbreaking new controller," noted Capcom in an official statement. "The new Resident Evil game for the console will combine the series' intense gaming experience and atmosphere with the revolutionary control system of the Wii."

Finally, it was learned that Super Mario Galaxy will in fact be playable on the show floor today.

Stay tuned for more details on these games and from the N-Sider staff.
