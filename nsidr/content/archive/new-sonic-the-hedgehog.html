--- title: "New Sonic the Hedgehog" date: 2005-03-09T02:14:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Although no platforms have been specified at this time, Sega has announced a new Sonic the Hedgehog game, according to 1Up.com. The game will be starring Sonic's nemesis Shadow. The title is scheduled to release later this year. Check out the first trailer for the game here.
