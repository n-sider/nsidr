--- title: "North American lineup through October" date: 2006-05-30T14:52:57Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America today put down dates for its upcoming current-generation software. Many of these we heard immediately after E3, but some were merely pencilled in under "Q4 2006". No more!

Nintendo DS

  • Big Brain Academy (June 5)
  • Puzzloop (June 5)
  • Sudoku Gridmaster (June 26)
  • Star Fox DS (August 28)
  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Blue Rescue Team (September 18)
  • Mario vs. Donkey Kong 2: March of the Minis (September 25)
  • Clubhouse Games (October 9)
  • Pokémon Ranger (October 30)
  • Children of Mana (October 30)

Nintendo GameCube

  • Baten Kaitos Origins (September 25)
  • Super Paper Mario (October 9)

Game Boy Advance

  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Red Rescue Team (September 18)

Astute readers will note this list doesn't cover all the titles Nintendo said they plannedon releasing this year — these titles are still (barring new information) slated for Q4 2006. These include Custom Robo Arena, DK Bongo Blast, Elite Beat Agents, Hotel Dusk: Room 215, The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass, The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess, Magical Vacation, and Yoshi's Island 2.
