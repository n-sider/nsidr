--- title: "Tales of Phantasia now available for GBA" date: 2006-03-11T22:30:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Tales of Phantasia is now available for the Game Boy Advance for $34.99. The title is published by Nintendo of America and developed by Namco. Check out the game's official website here. Tales of Phantasia currently has a 78 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 16 reviews).

The story goes like this: "When young swordsman Cress Albane finds his home village destroyed and his parents killed, Cress sets off on an adventure to find those responsible. In his efforts to uncover those behind this evil deed, Cress soon finds himself on a mission that will take him back and forth through time."

The game is said to use a unique "Linear Motion Battle System" that allows players to battle in real time and attack enemies without the use of menus. Nintendo.com offers further gameplay advice to those of you who decide to pick up this latest RPG from Namco.
