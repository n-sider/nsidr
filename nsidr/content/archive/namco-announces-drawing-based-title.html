--- title: "Namco announces drawing-based title" date: 2006-01-06T11:28:56Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

By way of Jeux-France comes news of a very unique title from Namco slated for release on the Nintendo DS in Japan.

Kaitou Ruso DS finds the game's protagonist, Ruso, with a canvas for a face that the player can draw on to influence how the game plays out. In one shot, the player has drawn Ruso a face that permits him to blend in with a window in the background, making him invisible to the policemen.

The game is currently in the works for a yet-undated Japanese release.
