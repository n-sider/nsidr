--- title: "\"F-Zero GX/AX\" soundtrack coming to Japan on July 22" date: 2004-07-06T03:56:54Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan will release a soundtrack for F-Zero GX/AX, according to NinGC.com. The soundtrack will feature 82 songs on 2 CDs, with a special remix of "Big Blue". The soundtrack will go on sale July 22 for 3,360 Yen. There are currently no plans for a release outside Japan.
