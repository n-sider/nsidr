--- title: "Notorious \"Nintendo On\" Hoax Dissected" date: 2005-07-08T05:33:20Z contributors: - Cory Faller ---

Shortly before this year's E3, an amazingly well-produced CG video began circulating the 'net that promoted a supposedly upcoming helmet-based gaming device from Nintendo called the "Nintendo On". Though later revealed to be a hoax, the video captivated the minds of many Nintendo fans with its high quality production and radical concept.

1up.com recently managed to find the creator of the video, and sat down with them to discuss some behind-the-scenes action. What was the motivation behind making the video? How was it made? These answers and more, along with a soul-crushing misspelling of N-Sider staffer Jeffrey Van Camp's name in what should have been his precious five minutes of fame, can be found at the above link.
