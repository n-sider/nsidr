--- title: "\"Pikmin 2\" now available for GameCube in North America" date: 2004-08-31T00:46:59Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America said it has released Pikmin 2 to stores nationwide. The title features a battle mode and two new Pikmin to help you on your journey. More information can be found at the game's official website here.

Pikmin 2 currently has a 90 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com.
