--- title: "GameSpot to have video feed of New York conference" date: 2006-09-14T01:57:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

GameSpot.com said it will have a non-live video feed of Nintendo's North American press conference tomorrow morning. Unfortunately, their feed won't begin until noon EST tomorrow. N-Sider will keep its eyes open for a site that will offer a live feed of the conference that begins at 9AM EST; if we find one we assure you'll be the first to know.
