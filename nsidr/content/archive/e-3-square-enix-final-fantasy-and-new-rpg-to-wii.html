--- title: "[E3] Square Enix: Final Fantasy and new RPG to Wii" date: 2006-05-08T17:42:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGN.com reports that Square Enix has two titles in development for Nintendo Wii. The titles include the already announced sequel to Final Fantasy: Crystal Chronicles and another unnamed RPG. The games will be revealed at today's Square Enix press conference at the Electronic Entertainment Expo. IGN's sources say the unnamed title will "make Japanese RPG fans very, very happy." No other word on the games at the moment. Stay tuned.
