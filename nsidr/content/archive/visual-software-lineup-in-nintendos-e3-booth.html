--- title: "Visual software lineup in Nintendo's E3 booth" date: 2004-05-10T23:58:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Below is a list of games that have been visually confirmed today to appear in Nintendo's e3 booth. The full list will be confirmed tomorrow morning at Nintendo's pre-show.

GameCube

  • Advance Wars: Under Fire
  • Donkey Kong Jungle Beat
  • Donkey Konga
  • Metroid Prime 2: Echoes
  • Star Fox
  • Paper Mario 2

Game Boy Advance

  • Donkey Kong: King of Swing
  • Gundam Seed Battle Assault
  • Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap
  • Racing Gears Advance
