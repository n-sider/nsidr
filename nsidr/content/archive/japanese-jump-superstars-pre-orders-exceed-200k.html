--- title: "Japanese Jump Superstars pre-orders exceed 200k" date: 2005-07-14T01:02:55Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo announced that over 200,000 pre-orders have been made for Jump Superstars, the upcoming Nintendo DS brawler, according to Gamefront.de. The best performing Nintendo DS release to-date ocurred earlier this year when Nintendogs sold a combined 180,674 copies upon release. Jump Superstars will debut on the Nintendo DS in Japan on August 8 along with the red Nintendo DS handheld.
