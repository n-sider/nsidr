--- title: "Nintendo Canada sponsors \"NBA Hoop It Up\"" date: 2005-07-07T20:22:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo Canada said it will sponsor "NBA Hoop It Up", a three-on-three basketball tournament that allows participants of all ages to compete in an inviting environment. Nintendo is promoting released and upcoming Nintendo GameCube and Nintendo DS software at the events. There are three locations remaining that the tournament will visit including July 9 - 10 in Montral, July 16 - 17 in Halifax and July 23 - 24 in Toronto. More detailed information on locations and registration can be found at NBA Canada's website.
