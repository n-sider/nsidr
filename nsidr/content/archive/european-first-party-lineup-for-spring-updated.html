--- title: "European first-party lineup for spring updated" date: 2006-04-06T10:01:42Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo Europe has updated their spring lineup, which we previously got a look at back in January. Without any further ado, here's what our friends in the Land of Five-Languaged Games can expect before summer hits.

Nintendo DS

  • Megaman Battle Network 5 Double Team DS (April 14)
  • Tetris DS (April 21)
  • Trauma Centre: Under the Knife (April 28)
  • Pokémon Link! (May 5)
  • Metroid Prime Hunters (May 5)
  • Super Princess Peach (May 26)
  • Dr Kawashima's Brain Training: How Old Is Your Brain? (June 9)
  • New Super Mario Bros. (June 30)

(Pokémon Link! is known as Pokémon Trozei in the States. Likewise, Brain Training is Brain Age over here.)

Game Boy Advance

  • Polarium Advance (April 14)
  • Final Fantasy IV (June 2)

Nintendo GameCube

  • Chibi-Robo! (May 26)
