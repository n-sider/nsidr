--- title: "THQ signs on to create games with Nintendo IP" date: 2007-10-17T16:50:12Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- THQ's October 12 8-K, filed with the SEC, reveals that the company has signed a license with Nintendo to use their IP in upcoming games.

  • License covers THQ-developed games as well as contracts to develop or manufacture games
  • Applies to Wii platform only
  • Lasts until October 13, 2009
  • Nintendo is charging a royalty and manufacturing fees
  • Nintendo reserves the right to approve the games' names, packaging, printed material accompanying the game, and marketing
  • Unknown what property this agreement actually covers
