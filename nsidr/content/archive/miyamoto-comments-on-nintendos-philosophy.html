--- title: "Miyamoto comments on Nintendo's philosophy" date: 2005-06-07T01:32:18Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Shigeru Miyamoto recently spoke with CNNMoney regarding Nintendo's plans with the Nintendo Revolution. Nintendo hopes to expand the game market by offering titles to gamers and non-gamers of all types.

"The Revolution will use cutting edge technology, but it's ultimately about how that technology is used," he told CNNMoney. "We asked ourselves 'why would a family need or want to have a gaming console?' The answer is what's driving development of the Revolution."
