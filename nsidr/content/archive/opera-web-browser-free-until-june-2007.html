--- title: "Opera Web browser free until June 2007" date: 2006-09-26T19:37:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Opera Software issued a press release today confirming the Opera Web browser for Wii will be available as a free download until June 2007. After that date, the browser can be purchased over the Wii Shop Channel.

The Opera Web browser will support Flash and AJAX-based content meaning sites such as Google Maps will be accessible on your television. Wii will arrive in North America on November 19 for $249.99.
