--- title: "Nintendo of Europe reveals Q3 schedule: Tingle confirmed" date: 2007-06-21T11:05:28Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of Europe today issued the Q3 release calendar for Wii and Nintendo DS. Notable on the list is perennial "will they bring it?" contender Freshly Picked: Tingle's Rosy Rupeeland (formerly Tingle RPG), slated for a September 14 release. Time will tell if the game actually makes it to North America or not—Nintendo of America did run a survey to gauge interest but nothing has been heard since.

Also of interest for long-suffering Europeans: Wii owners in that territory will finally be able to get their hands on Atlus' wunderkind Trauma Center: Second Opinion come August 10, and popular platformer/RPG Super Paper Mario September 14.
