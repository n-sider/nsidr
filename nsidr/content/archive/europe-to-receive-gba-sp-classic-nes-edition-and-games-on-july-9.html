--- title: "Europe to receive GBA SP \"Classic NES Edition\" and games on July 9" date: 2004-05-10T16:54:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Europe has been confirmed to receive the Game Boy Advance SP "Classic NES Edition" and eight original NES Classic games on July 9, 2004. The eight games will include Super Mario Bros., Donkey Kong, Ice Climber, Excitebike, The Legend of Zelda, Pac-Man, Xevious, and Bomberman. The special edition GBA SP will retail for 129 Euros while the games will retail individually for 19.99 Euros.
