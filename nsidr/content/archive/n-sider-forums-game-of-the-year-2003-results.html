N-Sider Forums Game of the Year 2003

So, after all this time, the results for the N-Sider Forums Game of the Year 2003 are in. And just so you know, they're real and they're spectacular!

It was a close race between Legend of Zelda: Wind Waker and Viewtiful Joe, two games that endeared themselves to the majority of the forum community, and two games deserving of their places in these lists, so it's only natural to have them sitting where they sit.

After a false deadline, I present you with the results from all the votes cast in the forum thread. Take a look-see, see where your votes counted (or didn't count, damn you! :P), and debate all you want, but do it in this thread and be sure to get the discussion going.

So, without further ado, here are the final results for all systems:
