--- title: "Video Game Exhibition showcases past through present" date: 2004-07-20T01:51:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The National Science Museum, Tokyo Broadcasting System and Yomiko Advertising have partnered to open the "Video Game and Digital Science Exhibition".

The exhibition chronicles the history of computer entertainment and is split eight zones, starting with the birth of the computer and moving to the present day. The Famicom, the Virtual Boy and practically every other Nintendo console makes an appearance. Check out some screens from the event here, courtesy of Game Watch.
