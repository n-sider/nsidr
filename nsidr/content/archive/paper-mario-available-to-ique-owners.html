--- title: "\"Paper Mario\" available to iQue owners" date: 2004-06-10T22:26:30Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Paper Mario is now available for the iQue in China, according to Nintendo subsidiary iQue Ltd. This is the same Paper Mario released for the Nintendo 64, however it has been translated to the Chinese language.

Nintendo also released the Game Boy Advance with games Super Mario Advance and Wario Land 4 in China earlier this week under the iQue brand. It appears Nintendo is slowly but surely working its way into the market.
