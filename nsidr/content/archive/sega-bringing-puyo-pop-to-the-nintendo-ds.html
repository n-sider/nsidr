--- title: "Sega bringing \"Puyo Pop\" to the Nintendo DS?" date: 2004-09-17T13:58:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sega of America said it will release its puzzle title Puyo Pop Fever for the Nintendo DS. The game will take advantage of the either the d-pad or touch screen and therefore allow players to have full control over the falling beans. Release date and other details will be announced soon.
