--- title: "Tony Hawk's American Wasteland now available" date: 2005-10-20T19:05:45Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Activision's Tony Hawk's American Wasteland is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. The title is on sale for a suggested retail price of $49.99. The game was developed by Neversoft.

Tony Hawk's American Wasteland currently has an 84 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 4 reviews).
