--- title: "Telltale in contact with Nintendo" date: 2006-09-18T18:10:42Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Telltale Games, recently best-known for their downloadable adventure games based on the Bone comic and the reviving of Sam & Max, posted back on September 14 a plea for fans to let Nintendo know they were interested in possibly bringing their games to Wii.

According to a story at 1UP, the pestering seems to have paid off. Telltale's Emily Morganti says "They took notice! We got a phone call from Nintendo this afternoon. We'll take it from here—please don't email them anymore!"

This is clearly just the beginnings and may not even result in Telltale becoming a licensed Nintendo developer, but nonetheless, it's an encouraging start. If we hear more, we'll definitely let you know.
