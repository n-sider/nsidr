--- title: "Visual Impact to support the Nintendo DS" date: 2004-07-08T18:05:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Belgian developer Visual Impact has become an officially licensed developer for the Nintendo DS, according to Nintendojo. The company is said to be creating a 3D title for the Nintendo DS as well as an action-3D title for the Game Boy Advance. Visual Impact is also a licensed developer for the Nintendo GameCube, however no titles have been announced.
