--- title: "Mario & Luigi 2: Partners in Time details and screens" date: 2005-10-25T15:03:06Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- New details regarding Mario and Luigi 2: Partners in Time for Nintendo DS have emerged, including several new screenshots.

The title will feature Mario RPG-style mechanics (such as dodging or inflicting attacks with well-timed button presses), and will utilize the DS touch screen in unique ways -- players can locate lost team-mates and execute combo moves during battle, for instance.

Mario and Luigi 2 is scheduled for a late-November release in the US.
