--- title: "ScanSoft Gaming Speech Dev Software now available" date: 2005-06-20T23:47:59Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

ScanSoft said its VoCon Gaming Speech Software Developer Kit is now available for qualified game developers to download and evaluate, according to GameDaily.com. This technology has been used in games such as SOCOM: U.S. Navy SEALs for the PlayStation 2 and Mario Party 6 for the Nintendo GameCube. More information can be found at the company's website.
