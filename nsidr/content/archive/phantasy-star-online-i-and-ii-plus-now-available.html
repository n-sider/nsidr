--- title: "\"Phantasy Star Online I & II Plus\" now available" date: 2004-09-15T19:46:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sega of America said today Phantasy Star Online I & II Plus is available at North American retailers nationwide. The title is an updated version of the game which released in 2002. It contains new weapons, levels, the ability to play online-downloaded quests in the off-line single-player mode, and much more. The first Phantasy Star Online I & II garnered an 84 percent review ranking on GameRankings.com.
