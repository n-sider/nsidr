--- title: "Madden NFL 06 garners 1.7 million in sales" date: 2005-08-18T22:18:46Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Electronic Arts said its multiplatform Madden NFL 06 game has sold more than 1.7 million copies during its first week on sale in North America. This is said to have been the biggest week one launch in the history of the franchise.
