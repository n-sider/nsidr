--- title: "Nintendo Japan conference set for October 13 and 14" date: 2004-09-30T10:43:35Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan announced it will hold a conference on October 13 and 14 to display upcoming 2004 software releases for Game Boy and GameCube. This likely coordinates with Nintendo of America's conference. The Nintendo DS will also be on display. The GameCube titles thus far include Mario Party 6, Mario Tennis, Donkey Kong Jungle Beat, and Kururin Squash. Wario Ware 2 and Yoshi's Universal Gravitation for the Game Boy Advance will be on display as well. According to Nintendo Japan, several other titles will be unveiled for the first time.
