--- title: "Banpresto to give away Micro face plates" date: 2005-08-18T22:19:39Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Banpresto said it will give away 10,000 free Game Boy Micro faceplates as a promotional contest for its upcoming Game Boy Advance game Super Robot Taisen J, according to GameSpot.com. By purchasing both the Game Boy Micro and Super Robot Taisen J during September 13 through October 31, users can enter their product serial numbers into the Club Nintendo website. About 10,000 of those entrants will receive the limited edition Super Robot-themed face plate.
