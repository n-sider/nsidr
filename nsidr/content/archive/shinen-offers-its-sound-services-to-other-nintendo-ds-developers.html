--- title: "Shin'en offers its sound services to other Nintendo DS developers" date: 2004-07-08T18:09:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Shin'en said it will be offering its DSX Sound Engine services to other licensed Nintendo DS developers. Shin'en became an official licensed Nintendo DS developer back in April.

In the past, Shin'en also developed sound engines for the Game Boy called the GHX Sound Engine and the Game Boy Advance called the GAX Sound Engine. Shin'en is responsible for such Game Boy Advance games as Iridion and Maya the Bee.
