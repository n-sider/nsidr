--- title: "Konami sets sights on Mary Kay territory" date: 2007-06-14T12:45:00Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
Konami has their sights set on a particularly novel market among the throngs of Nintendo DS owners who eschew traditional games: they're creating skin care sofware for the platform.

Chizu Saeki Formula: Dreaming of Beautiful Skin ~Dream Skincare~ aims to hitch a ride on the increasingly woman-oriented Nintendo DS bandwagon in Japan, branding with Japanese skin care tycoon Chizu Saeki much like Brain Age attached itself to Dr. Ryuta Kawashima. The software can create a personalized regimen for users seeking to have lovely skin for a certain occasion, and features videos of Saeki doing her skin care thing.

The title will hit Japan on October 18. Needless to say, it's unlikely it'll see the light of day in any other country.
