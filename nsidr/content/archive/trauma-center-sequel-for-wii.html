--- title: "Trauma Center sequel for Wii" date: 2007-07-10T11:26:08Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Famitsu revealed today that a new game in the Trauma Center series, titled Trauma Center: New Blood, will be shown at E3. The series began on the DS with Trauma Center: Under the Knife, but the Wii remake, Second Opinion, fared better in retail. New Blood will follow the money on Wii.

The revelation also comes with some screenshots, amazing character artwork, and a few juicy details. New Blood features a brand new 2-player co-op mode, online rankings, will display in 16:9.

Nothing else was revealed, but expect to see more of New Blood at E3, which begins tomorrow.
