--- title: "7th Annual Interactive Achievement Awards finalists announced" date: 2004-02-18T13:47:23Z contributors: - Jeffrey Van Camp ---

The AIAS (Academy of Interactive Arts & Sciences) has revealed the nominees for its 7th Annual Interactive Achievement Awards, which will be held in Las Vegas, Nevada on March 3rd. Hailed as one of the top award ceremonies in the videogame industry, the Achievement Awards will cover 36 catagories this year as well as present the Hall of Fame award. The following is a complete listing of the nominees. Titles by Nintendo (plus Viewtiful Joe) are marked in italics.
