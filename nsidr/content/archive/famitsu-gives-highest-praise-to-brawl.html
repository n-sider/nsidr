--- title: "Famitsu gives highest praise to Brawl" date: 2008-01-16T13:33:33Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- In a move that drove thousands of die hard Nintendo fans to insanity, Super Smash Bros. Brawl was delayed again yesterday, as we noted—it will finally hit North American stores March 9, 2008. Japan gets it only a week later than originally planned, on January 31. Europeans are still left in the dark, sadly.

Good news is not far off, however! Famitsu has given to SSBB one of its rare perfect scores, a 40/40. The reviewers seem to especially enjoy the single-player mode, Subspace Emissary, and praise the high production values and polish of the game. So, fret not, you crazy Smash fans. The wait will not last forever!
