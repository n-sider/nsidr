--- title: "New Tales games confirmed for Wii, DS" date: 2007-07-20T16:59:55Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Bandai Namco announced a raft of new Tales games today, across several different platforms; two in particular are of interest to our readers.

Tales of Innocence, for Nintendo DS, is an Alfa Systems-developed title that is slated for a release near the end of this year. Wii owners will get Tales of Symphonia: Ratatoskr no Kishi, a direct sequel to GameCube exclusive Tales of Symphonia, sometime before spring 2008.

The remainder of the announcements are for Sony's PlayStation 2 and PlayStation Portable and neither is a new game—one is a "director's cut" and the other a port. None of these games have been announced for American release, but if we hear they are, we'll let you know.
