--- title: "Nintendo once again a no-show at TGS" date: 2007-07-31T11:41:07Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- The Tokyo Game Show 2007 is coming up—and once again, Nintendo has opted to not be an exhibitor at the four-day event, held from September 20 through 23.

TGS is bigger than it has ever been, surpassing last year's size. This year, 168 exhibitors will have 1,708 booths. The fourth day is new to the show this year, probably in response to the scaled-down nature of E3.

The full list of exhibitors is available at the TGS site.
