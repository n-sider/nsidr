--- title: "Nintendo of America looking to expand" date: 2006-01-17T17:27:52Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

The Seattle Times has the story that Nintendo of America is looking to expand its building on its property by 550,000 square feet.

The city of Redmond is looking to work with Nintendo on an agreement concerning this expansion, and would like Nintendo to commit to a pedestrian path through its campus as part of an agreement permitting the expansion. Redmond also hopes that in the future, such a path could also be used for transit routes.

NOA PR director Beth Llewelyn also put in the official line on the project: plans have not yet been completed, and it's too early for the company to discuss specifics.
