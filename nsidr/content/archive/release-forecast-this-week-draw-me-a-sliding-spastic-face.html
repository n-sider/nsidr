Europe

A pretty unremarkable week for Europe, though it appears Nintendo was able to quickly replace the "spastic edition" of Mario Party 8—the latest in what seems to be the beginnings of an epidemic of the word appearing in European games.

Japan

As usual, Japan's release list looks longer than everyone else's. The oft-sniggered-about "Face Training" title, featuring a camera attachment, hits shelves today alongside another peripheral-based game, sliding-based Mag Kid. Forever Blue, debuting here eventually as Endless Ocean, rounds out the interesting titles.
