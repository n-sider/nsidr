--- title: "Band Brothers Request Selection shipped" date: 2005-09-28T17:24:10Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

The very first Nintendo DS option pack, Daigasso! Band Brothers Request Selection, was shipped directly this week to Japanese customers who ordered the expansion from Nintendo's website.

The option pack takes the form of a Game Boy Advance cartridge which is inserted into the Nintendo DS's slot 2 at the same time the original DS game is in slot 1. When the game is started, the option pack makes 31 new songs available within the game.
