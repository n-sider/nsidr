--- title: "Shape-puzzle game Neves announced for US release" date: 2007-07-25T16:34:34Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Yuke's Company of America has announced that it will be bringing puzzle game Neves to the U.S. this coming November.

Neves is based on the classic Japanese game Hayanama's Lucky Puzzle. Your job as player is to assemble seven shapes to match the silhouette given. (We have 11 new screenshots to illustrate.) The title has 500 different puzzles, a trophy room for boasting about your victories, four different game modes (Silhouettes, 7 Steps, Time, and Pressure) and also supports Versus with DS Download Play.

Yuke's expects the game to get an E rating, though nothing has yet been handed down by the ESRB.
