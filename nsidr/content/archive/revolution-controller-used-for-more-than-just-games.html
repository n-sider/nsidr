--- title: "Revolution controller used for more than just games?" date: 2006-01-29T06:01:17Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Hmm. I'm not one to place 'sex' and 'video games' within the same arena of thought. Yet, it appears there are people out there who do. The Revolution controller is being considered for what? Well, if you dare, check out the article at Wired.com.

What does Nintendo think of this? Perrin Kaplan, Nintendo of America's vice president of marketing and corporate affairs, responded to Wired's question noting, "Just seeing the words 'Nintendo' and 'sex' in the same sentence made me laugh. Clearly, the two subjects have no connection to each other."
