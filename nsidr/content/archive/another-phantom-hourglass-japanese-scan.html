--- title: "Another Phantom Hourglass Japanese scan" date: 2007-05-10T17:35:47Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
The Japanese magazine scans for The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass just keep coming, as the game's release date approaches.

The latest scan shows a few previously-unseen game elements, such as pirate leader Tetra, what is presumably the titular hourglass apparently being used to give Link a timed challenge, and treasure hunting.

Phantom Hourglass hits Japan on June 23, and on some unspecified "holiday" date will come stateside.
