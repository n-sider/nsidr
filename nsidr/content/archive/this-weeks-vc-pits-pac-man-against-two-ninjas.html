--- title: "This week's VC pits Pac-Man against two ninjas" date: 2007-05-14T13:16:24Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Three titles grace the Wii Shop Channel's Virtual Console service this week, with an overwhelming ninja theme permeating the list.

  • Pac-Man (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Ninja Gaiden (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Ninja Spirit (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

As usual, the titles go up at 9 a.m. Pacific Time, and Wii Points are still available at an exchange rate of 100 points for one of your American dollars.
