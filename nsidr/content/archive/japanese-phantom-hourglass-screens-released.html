--- title: "Japanese Phantom Hourglass screens released" date: 2007-05-02T11:12:12Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
Famitsu's online site delivers again today with a batch of screens from the upcoming Nintendo DS entry in the Zelda series: The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass.

The new screens show an assortment of gameplay, from Link having conversations with folks in town to several dungeon scenarios. Phantom Hourglass is coming out soon in Japan—June 23—while the North American release is currently simply targeted for this year's holiday season.
