--- title: "Riviera: The Promised Land now available" date: 2005-06-28T20:40:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Atlus U.S.A. has said Riviera: The Promised Land is now available for the Game Boy Advance. Riviera is a role-playing game that is said to contain over 30 hours of gameplay. It's currently on sale nationwide for $29.99 with a rating of 'Teen'. Visit the official website for screens, video and more.

Riviera: The Promised Land currently has a 65 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 1 review).
