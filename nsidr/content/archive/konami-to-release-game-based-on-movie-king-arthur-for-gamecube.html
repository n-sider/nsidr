--- title: "Konami to release game based on movie \"King Arthur\" for GameCube" date: 2004-05-10T22:26:18Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Konami will publish a GameCube game developed by Krome Studios based on the upcoming summer movie "King Arthur". The game will feature footage from the actual movie and the likenesses of the characters. The title will of course be on display this week within Konami's e3 booth.
