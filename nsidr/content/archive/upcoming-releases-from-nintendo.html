--- title: "Upcoming releases from Nintendo" date: 2005-11-03T20:51:43Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

PlanetGameCube is reporting on new releases headed our way courtesy Nintendo of America come Winter 2006.

Nintendo DS

  • Jan 9: Electroplankton
  • Jan 23: True Swing Golf
  • Feb 27: Super Princess Peach
  • Mar 20: Metroid Prime Hunters

True Swing Golf might possibly be Touch Golf, but we have not yet confirmed this.

Nintendo GameCube

  • Feb 6: Chibi Robo
  • Mar 6: Odama

Game Boy Advance

  • Feb 6: Drill Dozer
  • Mar 6: Tales of Phantasia

In addition to these great releases, Nintendo has also said they will be bringing the Japanese hit Brain series games as Brain Training and Brain Flex. No release date has yet been announced for these titles, but seeing their stateside performance should be very interesting.
