--- title: "Smash Bros. blog reveals control scheme" date: 2007-06-08T11:14:01Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- The Super Smash Bros. Brawl blog, maintained by SSB creator Masahiro Sakurai, has seen many updates since it began anew, including character profiles, items, stages, a song, and descriptions of game mechanics. This morning, though, is perhaps the most interesting update yet: Sakurai has finally revealed the controller for SSBB.

Fans of both Super Smash Bros. and Wii have been arguing for some time now over which control scheme would be better: classic controls or Wii remote controls? Well, proponents of all control schemes can rest assured that they won't feel left out or disappointed. Four types of control were revealed today:

  • Wii Remote (held sideways)
  • Wii Remote + Nunchuk
  • Classic Controller
  • GameCube Controller

No specifics were given regarding any of the control schemes, but they will likely be revealed through the blog at a later date.
