--- title: "Nintendo Europe holding the second \"Nintendo Convent\" in June" date: 2004-06-14T17:33:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Europe will be holding its second "Nintendo Convent" at the hilltop castle Dobczyce, near Krakw, Poland from June 26 to June 28, according to Cube-Europe. The first Nintendo Convent was held in January earlier this year.

The event will feature a presentation of the newest Nintendo games, a discussion about the future of game consoles in Poland, appearances from well-known faces in the game industry, game tournaments and a fireworks display. If you live in the area, it sounds like it will be an enjoyable time.
