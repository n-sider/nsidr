--- title: "Release forecast this week: Dancing without the stars" date: 2007-09-24T15:31:21Z contributors: - Glen Bayer - Mattie Behrens --- Below is a tentative list of games available this week of September 24-30. Please note that all releases are subject to change.

North America

Coming off the tail end of an incomprehensibly long series of ports, Mercury Meltdown Revolution is slated to drop this week—in other territories; it's already earned respect from several of our forum-goers, and for $20 you probably can't go wrong. Konami's also putting Dance Dance Revolution: Hottest Party—disturbing-looking characters and all—on shelves for $70.
