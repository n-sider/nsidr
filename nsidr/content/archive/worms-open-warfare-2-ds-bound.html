--- title: "Worms: Open Warfare 2 DS-bound" date: 2007-04-20T13:51:11Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- THQ has announced that Worms: Open Warfare 2 will be headed to Nintendo DS, as well as Sony's PlayStation Portable and the always-popular mobile phones.

The sequel to Worms: Open Warfare adds new single and multiplayer modes such as Rope Race, Fort, Puzzle, Campaign, and Firing Range. 11 new weapons will join old classics, and the game reportedly features an online mode, though THQ didn't specify whether this mode will be present in the Nintendo DS version.

Worms: Open Warfare 2 is slated for release this summer.
