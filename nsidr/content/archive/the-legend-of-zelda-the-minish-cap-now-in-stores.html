--- title: "The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap now in stores" date: 2005-01-11T00:31:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has released The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap for the Game Boy Advance to North American retailers nationwide. Screens and other details on the title can be found at the official website.

The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap currently has a 90 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.
