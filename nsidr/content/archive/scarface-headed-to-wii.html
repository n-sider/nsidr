--- title: "Scarface headed to Wii" date: 2007-01-29T12:28:42Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- In its latest issue, British gaming magazine NGamer is featuring the upcoming Wii port of last year's Scarface: The World is Yours.

According to the meta-report from CVG, the Wii-enhanced port will feature the obvious Remote-aimed gun scenario, as well as allowing the player to fist-fight enemies and random passers-by, and use the Remote as a chainsaw to mangle folks while the sound emanates from the speaker.

Scarface is currently pegged for "later this year", pending a more accurate release date at a later time.
