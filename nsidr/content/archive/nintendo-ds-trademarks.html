--- title: "Nintendo DS trademarks" date: 2004-06-10T22:36:56Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

GamerFeed reports that Nintendo Co., Ltd. submitted nine different trademark applications on March 15 for the initials/model numbers for the upcoming Nintendo DS handheld. It's unknown what the acronyms stand for, nor which one Nintendo will ultimately go with. Check them out below:

  • DGB
  • EGB
  • GBD
  • GBE
  • GBN
  • GBY
  • TGB
  • WGB
  • XGB

For your interest, here are the existing model numbers for the other Game Boy handhelds:

AGS = Game Boy Advance SP
AGB = Game Boy Advance
CGB = Game Boy Color
MGB = Game Boy Pocket
DMG = Game Boy
