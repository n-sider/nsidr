--- title: "Templar's 'Ology books reimagined as games for Wii, DS" date: 2007-10-08T09:33:55Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Codemasters has inked a deal with Templar Publishing to make games out of the 14-million-seller 'Ology series of books, with Dragonology, Pirateology, and Wizardology headed for both Nintendo DS and Wii.

  • The books are encyclopedic journals with textured content and paper-engineered extras
  • Each title will be its own game
  • Wii titles will be of the action genre; DS titles will take the form of an
    interactive book and have explorative gameplay
  • Dragonology to hit first in the second half of 2008
