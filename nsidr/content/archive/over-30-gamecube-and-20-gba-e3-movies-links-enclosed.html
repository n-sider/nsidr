--- title: "Over 30 GameCube and 20 GBA E3 movies (links enclosed)" date: 2004-05-12T19:39:03Z contributors: - Jeffrey Van Camp ---

Nintendo has been good enough to update its E3 site (www.nintendo.com/e3_2004) this year with a plethora of movies from titles on the show floor. Below are movies of nearly every big first party title and a number of upcoming third party offerings. They are in no particular order except, perhaps, that of my own anticipation.

Clicking on any of the links below should take you to that game's corresponding movie page on Nintendo's website, where you can choose to download either a low quality or high quality quicktime movie file, depending on your connection speed. It should also be noted that Nintendo's website has been swarmed due to the success of its Pre-Show Conference yesterday, but it appears to be much more stable than yesterday. If you experience any problems with the site I can only suggest you try again later or search for other sites that have the videos. Enjoy.
