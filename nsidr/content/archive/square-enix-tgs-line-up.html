--- title: "Square-Enix TGS line-up" date: 2005-09-07T01:10:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Square-Enix announced its lineup of software for the Tokyo Games Show which begins September 16. The company will showcase over 12 titles across PlayStation 2, Nintendo DS, and PC. The following are titles announced for the Nintendo DS or an unspecified platform.
  • Slime Dragon Quest 2 - for Nintendo DS
  • The World of Mana - for Nintendo DS
  • Front Mission 2089 - for Platform Unknown
  • Dragon Quest II - for Platform Unknown
  • Musashiden Episode Zero - for Platform Unknown
