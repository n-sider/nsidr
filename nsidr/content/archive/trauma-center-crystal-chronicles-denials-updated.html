--- title: "Trauma Center, Crystal Chronicles denials (updated)" date: 2006-03-07T17:20:43Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Recent word (some even credible) had an entry in the Trauma Center series effectively confirmed for Nintendo's Revolution as well as a 2007 release date for an entry in the Final Fantasy Crystal Chronicles series.

However, it seems that since then, Atlus has denied Trauma Center is in the works for Revolution, and likewise Square-Enix with Crystal Chronicles' rumored release date. Time will tell whether and when we will see these franchises come to Revolution, but for right now, neither company is saying.

Update: An earlier version of this story read as if Crystal Chronicles for Revolution had no confirmation at all, when in fact it was announced for the platform last May. I apologize for the confusion.
