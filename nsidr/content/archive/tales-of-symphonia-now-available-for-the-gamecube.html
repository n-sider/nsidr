--- title: "\"Tales of Symphonia\" now available for the GameCube" date: 2004-07-14T03:47:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

"Tales of Symphonia", the GameCube-exclusive RPG from Namco, is now available in North America. The title promises and follows through with several days worth of gameplay.

Tales of Symphonia currently features an 84 percent review ranking on GameRankings.com. Don't pay much attention to that score however. If you're at all a fan of RPGs, you must pick up this game.
