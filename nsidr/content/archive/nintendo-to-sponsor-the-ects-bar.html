--- title: "Nintendo to sponsor the ECTS Bar" date: 2004-07-21T22:51:32Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

ECTS, the European equivalent of E3, will be held September 1 - 4 and Nintendo announced today it would be sponsoring the show's Bar. Nintendo will have several GameCube and Game Boy Advance video game displays in the bar for the attendees to play as well as staff to answer questions about the games. Online registration for ECTS is available at www.ects.com.
