--- title: "Wi-Fi Connection Canadian hotspot availability" date: 2005-10-19T07:41:56Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of Canada has announced that it will be partnering with FatPort to bring free access to Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection to Canadian Nintendo DS owners.

FatPort service is available in several hotel, store and restaurant chains throughout the country as well as a myriad other locations.
