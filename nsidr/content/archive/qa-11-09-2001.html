Question:
Hey guys,

I enjoy RPG games, so I was wondering what your prognosis is for the genre on GameCube, or at least what the future of RPG looks like in the coming year for GameCube. On a related note, do you think there will be any chance Square will hook up with GameCube, or is there still some soreness between Square & Nintendo over the whole N64 / PSX thing? In any case, I'm still going do the 'cube with my free time. Just curious.

S. Wong

Answer:
Brenden: I should remind you that there's a lot more to RPGs than what Square has. There's been talk of DQVIII for GameCube, we've got PSO:V2, Skies of Arcadia 1 and 2, and there's bound to be some Atlus support down the road. We can't forget about Nintendo's internal teams either. So basically, I would give the GameCube a much better prognosis than the N64 for the RPG genre.
