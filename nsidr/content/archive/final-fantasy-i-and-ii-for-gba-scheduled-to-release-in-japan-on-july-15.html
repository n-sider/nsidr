--- title: "\"Final Fantasy I & II\" for GBA scheduled to release in Japan on July 15" date: 2004-05-20T15:50:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Square Enix will release Final Fantasy I & II Advance for the Game Boy Advance in Japan on July 15, according to The-MagicBox.com. Both games will feature additional dungeons, side-quests as well as story elements not contained in previous versions of the games.
