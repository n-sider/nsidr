--- title: "Japanese release of \"Pikmin 2\" can be played in English" date: 2004-05-10T17:03:01Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Datel said that its Action Replay device for GameCube can unlock an English language option in the Japanese release of Pikmin 2.

Pikmin 2 Action Replay codes, including those needed to unlock the English-language cut scenes, can be found at www.codejunkies.com.
