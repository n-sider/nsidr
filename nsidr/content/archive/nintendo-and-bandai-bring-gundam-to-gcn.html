--- title: "Nintendo and Bandai bring Gundam to GCN" date: 2005-09-12T23:22:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo and Bandai have entered a partnership to bring SD Gundam Gatchapon Wars to the Nintendo GameCube, according to Next-Gen.biz. Nintendo is developing the title which is described as a mix of simulation and action with cel-shaded graphics.
