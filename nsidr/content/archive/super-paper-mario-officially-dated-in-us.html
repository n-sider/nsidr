--- title: "Super Paper Mario officially dated in US" date: 2007-02-12T13:21:22Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- From Nintendo comes official word that the oft-anticipated Super Paper Mario is due out in North America on April 9, 2007, as (partially) previously rumored.

Be sure to check out our preview of the game from last May, when it was still slated for a GameCube release. The release promises to help fill out the somewhat sparingly-peppered first-party Wii release schedule, and should provide April with the slam-bang double-shot of both Mario and Pokemon.


Nintendo has also chosen to creatively release a few more details through their press release:

Nintendo of America Requests the Pleasure of Your Company at the Marriage of

Peach
to
Bowser

on the 9th Day of April 2007

The nuptials will take place at the beginning of Super Paper Mario, which will be available for Nintendo's hot new Wii home video game system. Those who wish to attend - or thwart - the ceremony will need to switch between 2-D and 3-D.

No word yet on garter-tossing or awkward drunken proclamations.
