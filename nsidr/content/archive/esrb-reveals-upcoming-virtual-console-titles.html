--- title: "ESRB reveals upcoming Virtual Console titles" date: 2006-11-08T10:47:19Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- The Entertainment Software Ratings Board's website has revealed, by way of posting of its ratings of upcoming titles, eight new titles headed for Wii's Virtual Console service, through which Wii owners can purchase software for their console over the Internet—primarily, at this point, titles released for other systems running under emulation.

These titles are in addition to the previously-announced 30 Nintendo revealed a week ago. All eight releases are rated E for Everyone. The titles are:

  • Duck Hunt
  • Hogan's Alley
  • Kid Icarus
  • Kirby's Adventure
  • Pilotwings
  • Pro Wrestling
  • Punch-Out!!
  • Wild Gunman

Because the titles have not yet been officially announced by Nintendo, we currently have no way of telling when they will be released. We'll be sure to let you know when that happens.
