--- title: "Nintendo of America commits a few more releases" date: 2006-07-25T10:24:47Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America has issued their latest update to their list of upcoming game release dates, finally committing dates to releases that, until now, have had a gigantic question mark hanging over their heads. The list is as follows (Magical Starsign is not an update):

Nintendo DS

  • Clubhouse Games (October 9)
  • Nintendogs Dalmatian (October 16)
  • Magical Starsign (October 23)
  • Pokémon Ranger (October 30)
  • Children of Mana (October 3; see also our preview)
  • Elite Beat Agents (November 6; see also our preview and interview)
  • Yoshi's Island 2 (November 13; see also our preview)
  • Custom Robo Arena (December 4)
  • Kirby Squeak Squad (December 4)

Game Boy Advance

  • Final Fantasy V Advance (November 6)

Nintendo also took the opportunity to reconfirm that The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess is still headed to both Wii and Nintendo GameCube on a yet-unspecified date. Wii will hit stores in the fourth quarter of this year, with announcements to come in September.

Notably absent from the list is the recently-released-in-Japan Nintendo DS Browser, which Europe has committed to releasing on October 6.
