--- title: "E3 2005: Mario Kart DS Impressions" date: 2005-05-18T20:53:34Z contributors: - Mark Medina ---

I got the chance to play the game about 10 times, each race is an 8-player wireless race of 3 laps.

Character selection is not possible, you are automatically assigned a random character. So far the characters present in the game are:

  • Mario
  • Princess Peach
  • Wario
  • Bowser
  • Donkey Kong
  • Yoshi
  • Luigi
  • Toad

Gameplay-wise, it plays very similarly to a mix between Super Mario Kart, Mario Kart 64, and Mario Kart: Double Dash. Powerslides are possible, as well as the mini-turbo feature as seen in Double Dash.

So far Ive seen at least 5 different tracks, based loosely on previous games. Theres a track akin to Double Dashs beach circuit, one based almost exactly from the Super Mario Karts first track (Mushroom Cup, my memory is failing me at present), one based on a Ghost town, and another similar to Luigis Circuit.

Winners of each race are given a prize, a radio-controlled Mario or Yoshi kart! Ive won 4 times already! :)

The framerate is very smooth, if a little on the slow side, Im sure it will be improved by the time it launches.

No launch date or timeframe has been given, but I will be probing further into this in the next two days.

More as it develops. Watch this space.
