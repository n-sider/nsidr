--- title: "Mario Party 6 available for GameCube" date: 2004-12-07T01:54:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has released Mario Party 6 for the Nintendo GameCube. Check out the official website featuring screens and more here. Mario Party 6 includes 80 new mini-games and comes bundled with the new GameCube Mic accessory, which allows players to control some mini-games using their voice.

Mario Party 6 currently has an 80 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.
