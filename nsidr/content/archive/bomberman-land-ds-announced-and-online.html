--- title: "Bomberman Land DS announced and online" date: 2006-04-26T18:11:49Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Cubed3 has picked up a bit of news from Japanese site Rakuten: Hudson has announced Bomberman Land DS. Much to the appreciation of fans of the franchise, the title has also been confirmed to be online via Nintendo's Wi-Fi Connection service.

Bomberman Land DS is currently slated for a release this year.
