--- title: "New Super Mario Bros. box-art" date: 2006-03-07T20:42:53Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- 4 Color Rebellion has a first-look at the North American New Super Mario Brothers box-art for Nintendo DS.

The title is slated for a May 7th release, and will combine old-school Mario mechanics with brand-new ones (such as the ability to compete head-to-head for goals within a stage). Stay tuned to N-Sider for more info as the release date approaches.
