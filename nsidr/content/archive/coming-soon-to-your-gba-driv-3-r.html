--- title: "Coming soon to your GBA: DRIV3R" date: 2005-07-26T18:36:08Z contributors: - Colin Castro ---

Atari announced today that DRIV3R is heading to the GBA. The game is said to consist of 25 missions that tell the tale of Undercover cop Tanner, who will travel to two locale's in this iteration of the series: Miami and the French City of Nice.

According to Atari, each city will feature over 30 miles to explore and will allow the player to take a break from missions and take a drive around town. There will be more than 25 vehicles available in the final version and will include several driving mini-games.

Atari is targetting a Fall 2005 release.

Source: Atari
