--- title: "One Piece now available" date: 2005-09-07T01:10:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Bandai said One Piece is now available for the Game Boy Advance. Furthermore, One Piece: Grand Battle! is availabel for the Nintendo GameCube. More information on the games can be found at their official website.

One Piece: Grand Battle! currently has a 70 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on one review).
