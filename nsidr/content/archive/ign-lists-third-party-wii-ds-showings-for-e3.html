--- title: "IGN lists third-party Wii, DS showings for E3" date: 2007-06-04T10:30:49Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- E3 is coming, and publishers are starting to detail some of the titles they plan to show off. IGN has compiled a list of titles those publishers have listed, though they readily admit that "E3 is the home for videogame surprises and no company is going to let us know everything ahead of time".

As of press time, IGN had the following down for Wii and Nintendo DS.

Capcom

  • Mega Man Star Force: Dragon (DS)
  • Mega Man Star Force: Leo (DS)
  • Mega Man Star Force: Pegasus (DS)
  • Mega Man ZX Advent (DS)
  • Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney Trials and Tribulations (DS)
  • Resident Evil: The Umbrella Chronicles (Wii)
  • Zack & Wiki: Quest for Barbaros' Treasure (Wii)

D3 Publisher

  • Naruto: Clash of Ninja Revolution (Wii)
  • Naruto: Path of the Ninja (DS)

Disney Interactive Studios

  • High School Musical: Sing It! (Wii; also on PS2)
  • High School Musical: Makin' the Cut (DS)

Electronic Arts

  • Boogie (Wii)
  • NBA Live 08 (Wii; also on PS3, 360)

LucasArts

  • LEGO Star Wars: The Complete Saga (DS, Wii; also on PS3, 360)
  • Star Wars: The Force Unleashed (DS; also on PS2, PS3, PSP, 360)
