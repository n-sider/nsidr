--- title: "Tomy developing \"Zoids Vs. 3\" for GCN" date: 2004-05-29T19:27:43Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Tomy said it is in development with the third title in its mech-shooting series "Zoids Vs." for the Nintendo GameCube, according to PolygonMag.com. Zoids Vs. 3 is scheduled for a Japanese release this winter.
