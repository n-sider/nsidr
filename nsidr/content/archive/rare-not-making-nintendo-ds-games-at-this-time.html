--- title: "Rare not making Nintendo DS games at this time?" date: 2004-08-10T22:27:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Microsoft said that Rare is not currently developing software for the Nintendo DS handheld despite the company noting interest in creating games for the handheld during a recent Q&A session published on its website, Rareware.com. A Microsoft representative told GameIndustry.biz that "at this time, there are no plans to support the DS."
