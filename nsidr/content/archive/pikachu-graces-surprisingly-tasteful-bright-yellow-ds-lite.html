--- title: "Pikachu graces surprisingly tasteful bright yellow DS Lite" date: 2007-06-02T12:38:53Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- How can you have multimillion-selling Pokémon games without a limited-edition Pokémon-branded system to play them on? If you're in Japan, you don't have to worry about this question any longer: a special edition Pikachu DS Lite has been unveiled which, despite the bright yellow color, is actually pretty tasteful.


Unlike other special edition DS Lites, this one is actually limited-edition and will be sold through a lottery system. Lottery winners will be able to pick up their system in the purchase period—July 20 through August 19—for 16,800 yen (about US$138 at today's exchange rates). Again, it's limited to Japan; so if you live anywhere else, you'll have to pull some serious strings to get your hands on one of these.
