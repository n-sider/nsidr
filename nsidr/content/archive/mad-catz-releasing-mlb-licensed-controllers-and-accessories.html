--- title: "Mad Catz releasing MLB licensed controllers and accessories" date: 2007-06-07T00:01:24Z contributors: - Michael Foster --- Mad Catz, a third-party video game accessory provider, announced it is renewing its licensing agreement with the MLB to provide official customized controllers and accessories.

New controllers, faceplates, and Console Skinz will be available featuring all 30 MLB teams and their colors and logos. Mad Catz is making these products for all major platforms, including the DS Lite and Wii.

The announcement comes shortly after Mad Catz revealed similar licensing agreements with the NFL and NBA.
