--- title: "Shipping 250,000 GBMicro units" date: 2005-09-09T00:43:31Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo will ship 250,000 Game Boy Micro units in its initial shipment for the device's Japanese release on September 13, according to Nintendo INSIDE. There is currently no word on North American shipments numbers for the Game Boy Micro, which is scheduled to debut on September 19.
