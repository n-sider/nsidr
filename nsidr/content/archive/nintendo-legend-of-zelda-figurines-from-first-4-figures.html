--- title: "Nintendo Legend of Zelda figurines from First 4 Figures" date: 2006-03-23T13:36:46Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

First 4 Figures acquired a three-year licensing agreement with Nintendo to create statues based on The Legend of Zelda franchise. The company plans to release single statues, mixed media statues, and VS dioramas that will range in the prices of $80 to $200. The figurines will be on display at the E3 Expo in May and available for sale at specialty retailers or on the First 4 Figures website sometime between July and September of this year.

The company currently plans to create figurines spanning several games including The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time (Young Link, Sheik, Ganondorf VS Link Diorama, Dark Link), The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask (Fierce Deity Link, Skull Kid), and Nintendo's latest entry in the franchise The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess (Zelda, Link). Keep an eye out for more updates on the figurines at First 4 Figures' official website.
