--- title: "Three new VC titles released today" date: 2007-04-09T12:17:06Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America has announced three new Virtual Console releases today. They are:

  • Galaga (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Bravoman (Turbografx16, 600 Wii Points)
  • Alex Kidd in the Enchanted Castle (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)

As usual, the games go live at 9 a.m. Pacific time and can be obtained from the Wii Shop Channel. Wii Points are available for about $1 for 100.
