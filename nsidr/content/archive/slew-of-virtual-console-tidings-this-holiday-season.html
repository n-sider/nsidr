--- title: "Slew of Virtual Console tidings this holiday season" date: 2006-12-21T18:27:45Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- Nintendo was eager to tell us that they've got some nice stocking-stuffing treats on the way for Christmas and the New Year.

Christmas day brings the arrival of some sweet yuletide treasures indeed!


  • Super Mario Bros. (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)
  • Street Fighter II: The World Warrior (Super NES, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • Super Castlevania IV (Super NES, 1 player, 800 Wii Points)
  • Toe Jam & Earl (Sega Genesis, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • R-TYPE (TurboGrafx16, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)

Then on New Year's Day a couple more:

  • Baseball (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)
  • Urban Champion (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)

You can hardly go wrong with anything from the Christmas batch. Get ready to fire up those consoles and spend some Wii Points!
