--- title: "\"Baten Kaitos: Eternal Wings\" coming November 2004" date: 2004-07-13T01:59:25Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Namco announced that its exclusive GameCube RPG Baten Kaitos will release with the subtitle Eternal Wings in North America within the month of November this year.

Meanwhile, Baten Kaitos is scheduled for an early 2005 release in Europe.
