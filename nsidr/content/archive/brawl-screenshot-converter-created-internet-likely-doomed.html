--- title: "Brawl screenshot converter created; Internet likely doomed" date: 2008-02-28T15:53:45Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Super Smash Bros. Brawl isn't out just yet in the West; but it's been in Japan for several weeks now. An enterprising fan has been hard at work trying to figure out how to share in-game screenshots on the Internet, as we found out via 4 color rebellion this morning.

The result is a unimaginatively named tool called bin2jpg. Its sole purpose is to take the .bin files off SD cards and transform them into Internet-digestible JPEG images, so you can show them off

The net impact on the Internet has yet to be determined, but judging by some of the early efforts, it's probably going to be disastrous. Download it if you must.
