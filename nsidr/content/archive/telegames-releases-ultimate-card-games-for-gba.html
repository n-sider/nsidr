--- title: "Telegames releases \"Ultimate Card Games\" for GBA" date: 2004-05-25T02:09:18Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Telegames said it has released its Ultimate Card Games title for the Game Boy Advance. The title contains such games as Hearts, Spades, Bridge, Euchre, Cribbage, Canasta, Gin Rummy, Go Fish, Crazy Eights, Poker, Blackjack, 15 Solitaire games, and five Video Poker versions.

More information can be found at Telegame's website.
