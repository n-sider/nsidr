--- title: "European release update—Opera in October" date: 2006-07-18T10:26:59Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of Europe has posted another update to their upcoming release list. Nintendo DS, Game Boy Advance, and Nintendo GameCube are all represented. (Until we get more information on Wii's launch, we probably won't be seeing dates for Wii titles.)

Most notable is the Opera-based Nintendo DS Browser hitting European shelves October 6. With Europe making their plans known for this software, we can perhaps expect Nintendo of America to announce plans in the near future as well.

Without any further ado, the list:

Nintendo DS

  • Big Brain Academy (July 7)
  • Electroplankton (July 7)
  • 42 All-Time Classics (September 29)
  • Nintendo DS Browser (October 6)
  • Sudoku Master (October 27)
  • Actionloop (October 27)
  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Blue Rescue Team (November 10)
  • Mario Slam Basketball (November 10)
  • Hotel Dusk: Room 215 (tentative name; November 24)
  • Yoshi's Island 2 (tentative name; December 1)
  • Harvest Moon DS (Q4)

Game Boy Advance

  • Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Red Rescue Team (November 10)

Nintendo GameCube

  • Harvest Moon: Magical Melody (Q4)
  • The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess (Q4)
