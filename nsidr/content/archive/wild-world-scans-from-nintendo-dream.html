--- title: "Wild World scans from Nintendo Dream" date: 2005-11-02T10:18:22Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Jeux-France has scans from an article in Nintendo Dream about Nintendo's upcoming Wi-Fi Connection title, Animal Crossing: Wild World.

The scans don't reveal much that we don't already know about the game, but inventory shots show that players will have a new tool in their inventory: a slingshot, presumably for shooting down Pete the pelican to make him drop his mail among many other things.

In addition, Wild World news-followers who read our E3 2005 coverage of the game and wondered how they were going to conveniently sell multiple items to resident capitalist Tom Nook can now breathe easier: apparently, the touch screen splits in two, and you can drag items you wish to sell out of your inventory on the bottom and into a holding area on the top, enabling you to sell as many items as you wish all at once. Seeing this interface in action gives hope that many chores from Animal Crossing for GameCube made tedious by the interface will be much more streamlined in this Nintendo DS sequel.

Animal Crossing: Wild World is still on-target for release December 5 in North America — barely over a month away.
