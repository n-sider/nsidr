--- title: "Yoshi's Island 2 to support Wi-Fi Connection" date: 2006-08-12T21:59:29Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

The box art for Yoshi's Island 2 (which we previewed at E3) has been revealed, and with it a previously-unannounced game feature revealed.

It seems the game will support online play of some sort via the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection—though at this point, nobody's really sure how it will work. More details should be forthcoming between now and the game's scheduled release on November 13.
