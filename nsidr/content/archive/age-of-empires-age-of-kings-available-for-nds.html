--- title: "Age of Empires: Age of Kings available for NDS" date: 2006-02-20T19:39:30Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Age of Empires: Age of Kings is now available for the Nintendo DS for $29.99. The title was published by Majesco and developed by Mecca Electronics. Age of Empires: Age of Kings currently has an 82 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 10 reviews).

The game is based on Ensemble Studio's PC series however the Nintendo DS version takes a new approach. Instead of a real-time gameplay, Age of Empires: The Age of Kings allows players to use a turn-based strategy system. The game includes five civilizations and 50 unique types of units. Players construct buildings and manage their army with a goal of conquering civilization through the ages. Multiplayer scenarios allow up to 4 players to battle each other wirelessly.
