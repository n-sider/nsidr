--- title: "Lik-Sang plays with the Lite" date: 2006-02-28T12:01:39Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Video game exporter Lik-Sang recently received one of the first DS Lite units, and true to form, took lots of pictures and answered practically every question anyone would ever think of asking.

Some of the more commonly asked questions are answered:

  • The charger is not the same as the Game Boy micro's, which was speculated based on the FCC application.
  • The battery is entirely new and rated at 1000 mAh instead of the old 850 mAh.
  • Currently marketed DS screen protectors will fit, though the top screen protector is a little larger than the actual screen area.

The DS Lite hits Japan in a matter of days, and colors other than white will follow just over a week later.
