--- title: "Nintendo's Camp Hyrule coming August 15" date: 2005-07-05T20:38:30Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo has posted more information regarding its annual online "Camp Hyrule" on the event's official website. Camp Hyrule is scheduled to take place the week of August 15 at CampHyrule.com. Users must register on the Camp Hyrule website beginning July 26 through July 28. Space fills up quickly so we'll post a reminder closer to the event. More information can be found on the official website.
