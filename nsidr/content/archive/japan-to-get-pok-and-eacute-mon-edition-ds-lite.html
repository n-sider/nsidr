--- title: "Japan to get Pokémon-edition DS Lite" date: 2006-09-01T11:06:38Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Famitsu has pictures today of the special edition DS Lite that will accompany the release of Pokémon Diamond and Pokémon Pearl.

Famitsu says the interior surfaces are coated with a special metallic black paint unique to this edition. The special-edition hardware will be available through the Pokemon Center store and via mail through the Pokemon fan club.

The Pokemon Center is conducting a special campaign for the release of the Diamond and Pearl games, which includes this special-edition hardware and a 10% off sale which begins the day of the games' release.
