--- title: "VC gets ActRaiser, Kid Chameleon, J.J. & Jeff" date: 2007-05-29T10:17:22Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Another week, another batch of Virtual Console games now available to the Wii Shop Channel. In a stunning defeat for my powers of prognostication, Paper Mario didn't make it, but some other noteworthies—and not-so-noteworthies—did:

  • ActRaiser (SNES, 800 Wii Points)
  • Kid Chameleon (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)
  • J.J. & Jeff (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

The above-named games actually went live at 9 a.m. yesterday, but you'll perhaps forgive us for taking the day off. You can get the games now in exchange for some of your stash of Wii Points, which are still available at the rate of roughly $1 for 100.
