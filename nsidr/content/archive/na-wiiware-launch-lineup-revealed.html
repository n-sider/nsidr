--- title: "NA WiiWare launch lineup revealed" date: 2008-05-12T00:40:02Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Six games will be released for the launch of the WiiWare service tomorrow, ranging from 500 to 1500 Wii Points.

  • Defend Your Castle (500 Wii Points)
  • Final Fantasy Crystal Chronicles: My Life as a King (1500 Wii Points)
  • LostWinds (1000 Wii Points)
  • Pop (700 Wii Points)
  • TV Show King (1000 Wii Points)
  • V.I.P. Casino: Blackjack (700 Wii Points)

There will be no Virtual Console releases this week; they will come back again next week. WiiWare is expected to follow VC's pattern of only a few releases a week.
