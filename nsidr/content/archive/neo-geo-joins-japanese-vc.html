--- title: "Neo Geo joins Japanese VC" date: 2007-08-31T11:52:23Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- The Japanese Virtual Console will soon welcome a new member to its ever-growing family. Papa Neo Geo makes a VC debut September 11, with two more games to follow later that month.

  • Fatal Fury (September 11)
  • Magician Lord (September)
  • World Heroes (September)

They will be priced at 900 points each. There is no news, yet, on when Neo Geo games will show up on Virtual Consoles outside of Japan.
