--- title: "Custom Robo GX sequel cancelled" date: 2005-10-09T21:53:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The Game Boy Advance sequel to Custom Robo GX has been cancelled, according to IGN.com. As announced at the recent Tokyo Game Show, Nintendo and its developer Noise are currently in development with a Nintendo DS version of the series. Noise said the title will not be a simple port of the cancelled game and instead contain different as well as new content. More details will be announced soon.
