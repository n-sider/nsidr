--- title: "PC Mag: Revolution failure spells Nintendo doom" date: 2005-12-28T11:29:47Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Via ABC News comes PC Magazine's 2006 predictions, where editor-in-chief Jim Louderback says "Expect Nintendo's delayed Zelda title to be the must-play game next spring, but unless the Revolution is a home run, Nintendo will fade by the fall."

Still, even after making such a doom-and-gloom prediction, Louderback goes on to predict Nintendo will run away with the handheld market in 2006 over, thanks to its original content and continued support of the GBA platform with the micro and compelling original content on the DS.
