--- title: "Europe's VC: Kirby plays golf" date: 2007-06-30T22:38:32Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Yesterday, the European Virtual Console received two more games. After rescuing the president in Bloody Wolf, players can relax with a nice game of hazardous golf with everyone's favorite pink hero, Kirby.

  • Kirby's Dream Course (Super Nintendo, 800 points)
  • Bloody Wolf (TurboGrafx-16, 600 points)

This brings the total number of available on Europe's VC up to 105.
