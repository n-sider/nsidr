--- title: "Nintendo says Revolution might be shown at E3 2005" date: 2004-05-27T14:37:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo said during a media briefing in Osaka that it may be able to unveil its next-generation game console codenamed Revolution at the 2005 Electronic Entertainment Expo, according to Reuters.

Nintendo president Satoru Iwata first announced the console at the 2004 E3 Expo held earlier this month. While Iwata kept quiet about the console's specs and features, he said the console would create a gaming revolution since it would redefine how people play video games.
