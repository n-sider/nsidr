--- title: "Mario Kart battle mode not online" date: 2005-08-20T16:34:58Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- According to an interview with P-Nintendo.com, Mario Kart DS producer Hideki Konno revealed that only the racing modes will be available for play over Wi-Fi.

Puissance Nintendo: Will the Battle mode be playable in Wifi?

Hideki Konno: Unfortunately, the Battle mode will not be accessible in Wifi. It will be thus only one mode of race to 4 players.(local wireless)
