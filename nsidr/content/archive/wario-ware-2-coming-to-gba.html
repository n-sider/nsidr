--- title: "Wario Ware 2 Coming to GBA!" date: 2004-07-31T01:02:54Z contributors: - Kenneth Kyle Wade ---

Famitsu Magazine has revealed that Wario Ware is getting a sequel on the Game Boy Advancein addition to the sequel that is coming to the Nintendo DS later this year.

Mawaru Made in Wario is scheduled for October 16 in Japan. For those unfamiliar with Japanese, Mawaru is a term that means rotating, turning, or revolving around objects.

More information is expected sometime next month, so stay tuned!

Source: Famitsu
