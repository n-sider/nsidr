--- title: "Blitz Games to bring Possession to Revolution?" date: 2005-07-07T20:59:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Blitz Games said it will bring Possession to the Nintendo Revolution, according to GamesRadar.uk. The game is currently under development and is said to be designed to allow both on and offline play. Players will take control of a zombie who leads an undead horde against a tyrannical capitalist corporation. The title currently lacks a publisher so release intentions may change.
