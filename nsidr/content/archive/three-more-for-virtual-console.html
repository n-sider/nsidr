--- title: "Three more for Virtual Console" date: 2006-12-18T14:21:45Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- It's Monday once again and you all know what that means!

VIRTUAL CONSOLE MONDAY


  • Tennis (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)
  • Space Harrier II (Sega Genesis, 1 player, 800 Wii Points)
  • Military Madness (TurboGrafx16, 1-2 players, 600 Wii Points)

This week brings a brazen assortment of marginally un-exciting titles, chief among them the relatively lackluster Genesis port of the Sega classic Space Harrier. With Wii Sports bundled into every Wii box, NES Tennis is less than a value proposition as well. Perhaps here the shining star is Military Madness, a hex-grid, turn-based strategy game which takes place on the moon. After all, it did win the prestigious "Best Military Strategy Game" award from Video Games & Computer Entertainment magazine in 1990!

Updates to Virtual Console occur at 9 a.m. Pacific time, which works out to 10 a.m. Mountain time, 11 a.m. Central time, and noon Eastern time.
