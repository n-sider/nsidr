--- title: "Pre E3 2005: Game Boy Micro" date: 2005-05-17T17:36:56Z contributors: - James Remington ---

At Nintendo's press conference earlier this morning, Executive Vice President, Sales and Marketing, Reggie, revealed Nintendo's newest redesign of the Game Boy Advanced - The Game Boy Micro. Not much is known about the Game Boy Micro, other than Nintendo's claim that it looks to be brightest screen in a Nintendo portable to date.


Reggie also said that it would be "a hair bigger and two-thirds the weight of an iPod Mini." and is scheduled to be released in North America sometime in the Fall.
