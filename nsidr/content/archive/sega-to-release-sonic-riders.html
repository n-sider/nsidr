--- title: "Sega to release Sonic Riders" date: 2005-09-09T00:46:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Sega said it is developing a new Sonic racing game called Sonic Riders for the Nintendo GameCube. The title will support four-player multiplayer and is scheduled for release this winter. Screens of the game can be seen here, courtesy of The-MagicBox.
