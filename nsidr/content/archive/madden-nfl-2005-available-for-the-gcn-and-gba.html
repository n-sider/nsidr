--- title: "\"Madden NFL 2005\" available for the GCN and GBA" date: 2004-08-09T16:46:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts has announced the North American release of Madden NFL 2005 for the GameCube and Game Boy Advance. The title marks the 15th installment in the series.

Madden NFL 2005 currently has a 92 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com.
