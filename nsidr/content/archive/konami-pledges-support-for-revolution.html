--- title: "Konami pledges support for Revolution" date: 2005-06-30T00:02:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Konami spoke with Japanese publication Toyo Keizai and said it would support all three next-generation consoles including the Nintendo Revolution, according to Nintendojo.com. Konami said it intends on releasing one to two "flagship" titles on each system. Metal Gear Solid 4 was mentioned for the PlayStation 3, however no specific titles were announced for Revolution.
