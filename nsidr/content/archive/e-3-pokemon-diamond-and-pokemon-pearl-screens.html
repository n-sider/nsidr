--- title: "[E3] Pokemon Diamond and Pokemon Pearl screens" date: 2006-05-11T19:45:47Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo unveiled the first screens for the latest in the Pokemon series for Nintendo DS. As previously announced, the game duo is named Pokemon Diamond and Pokemon Pearl. In July, Japanese Coro Coro Magazine said the games would be Wi-Fi compatible for up to 16 players and also compatible with prior games in the series. No word on whether these features have been retained in the games. Also, no release date was specified. Stay tuned for more details.
