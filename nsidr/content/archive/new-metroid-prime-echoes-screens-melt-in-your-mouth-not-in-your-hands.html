--- title: "New \"Metroid Prime: Echoes\" screens melt in your mouth, not in your hands" date: 2004-04-24T03:27:48Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has revealed Metroid Prime: Echoes to be the name of the sequel to the 2002 original hit. Nintendo describes the game's story as follows:


In this highly anticipated sequel to Metroid Prime, become the bounty hunter behind the visor once more and travel to a planet torn into light and darkness. Hunted by a mysterious entity and a warring race called the Ing, Samus Aran must explore the light and dark worlds of this doomed planet to discover secrets and augment her suit's weapons and abilities.

And even better? Nintendo has released crisp new screens.

Thanks to N-Forum member "The Blue Yoshi", for pointing these out.
