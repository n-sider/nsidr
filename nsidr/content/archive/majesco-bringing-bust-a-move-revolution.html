--- title: "Majesco bringing Bust-A-Move Revolution" date: 2006-05-17T11:23:58Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Majesco Entertainment announced yesterday that its first Wii title will be Bust-A-Move Revolution.

Majesco's Ken Gold, VP of marketing, said that Bust-A-Move will "maximize the intuitive nature of the Wii controller to create a revolutionary offering of the highly popular Bust-A-Move franchise." The title is currently targeted for release in early 2007, after Wii's fourth-quarter 2006 launch.
