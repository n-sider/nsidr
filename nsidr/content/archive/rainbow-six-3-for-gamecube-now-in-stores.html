--- title: "\"Rainbow Six 3\" for GameCube now in stores" date: 2004-06-15T22:31:45Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six 3 is now available at retailers nationwide for the Nintendo GameCube, according to publisher Ubisoft.

In Rainbow Six 3, players are part of a counter-terrorist group who must traverse 15 missions to neutralize enemies. A split-screen mode for offline 2-player collaboration is available.

For screens and other information on the title, visit Ubisoft's official website.
