--- title: "Star Fox Arwing in Zelda: Ocarina of Time" date: 2005-10-09T14:33:39Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Apparently someone found GameShark codes that allow you to change one of the characters in the The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time's Kokiri Village into any object from the game. In particular, there's an Arwing from the Star Fox series hidden in the game's code. The Arwing flies around, attacks, and is destroyable. The GameShark codes are below for those of you interested in trying this out.
