--- title: "Atlus bringing Luminous Arc to North America" date: 2007-03-12T10:41:46Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Atlus USA has announced that they have acquired the North American publishing rights to Luminous Arc.

Luminous Arc is a strategy RPG for the Nintendo DS. Developed by Image Epoch and produced/published by Marvelous, the title features weapon/equipment creation through the Vitae Imbuing system, character friendship affecting gameplay, voiced dialogue, and perhaps most interestingly, Wi-Fi multiplayer.

Atlus intends to release Luminous Arc later this year. No ESRB rating is yet available.
