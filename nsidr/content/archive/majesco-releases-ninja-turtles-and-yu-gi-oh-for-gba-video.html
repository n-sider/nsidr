--- title: "Majesco releases Ninja Turtles and Yu-Gi-Oh! for GBA Video" date: 2004-07-21T21:34:33Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Majesco has released two new videos in its line of GBA Video software. The new videos include Yu-Gi-Oh! Vol. 1 and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Vol. 1. Each volume costs $19.99 and contains two episodes of their respective shows. More information on the GBA Video line of software can be found at the official website.
