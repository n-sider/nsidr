--- title: "Japanese \"Nintendo World Touch DS!\" tour" date: 2004-09-10T12:29:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan announced it will publicly showcase the upcoming launch lineup and future titles for the Nintendo DS beginning November 3. The "Nintendo World Touch DS!" tour, as it's dubbed, will stop in Tokyo from November 13 to November 14. A wealth of new information regarding games and the system itself will likely arrive shortly before and during the tour.
