--- title: "Analysts: Zack & Wiki, RE: UC to be boosted by Brawl delay" date: 2007-10-15T15:56:33Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Disappointed with the Super Smash Bros. Brawl delay? According to analysts at GamerMetrics, Capcom shouldn't be—the delay should be good news for Zack & Wiki and Resident Evil: Umbrella Chronicles.

  • GamerMetrics finds Brawl players also interested in Super Mario Galaxy as well as Zack and RE: UC
  • Though Galaxy is still the most closely correlated title, Capcom's offerings follow close behind
  • Zack should appeal to "the younger, casual segment" while RE aims for "older, more mature Nintendo enthusiasts"
