--- title: "Yoshi Topsy Turvy now available" date: 2005-06-14T00:59:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America said Yoshi Topsy Turvy for the Game Boy Advance is now available nationwide. For screens and other info visit the game's official website.

Yoshi Topsy Turvy currently has a 58 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.
