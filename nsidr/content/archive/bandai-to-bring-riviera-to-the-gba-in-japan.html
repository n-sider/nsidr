--- title: "Bandai to bring \"Riviera\" to the GBA in Japan" date: 2004-06-14T18:09:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Under publisher Bandai, Japanese developer Sting will bring a new RPG called Riviera to the Game Boy Advance. According to VGPro, Riviera is said to be a remake of a game Bandai released in 2002 for WonderSwan Color. At the moment, that's all the information known.
