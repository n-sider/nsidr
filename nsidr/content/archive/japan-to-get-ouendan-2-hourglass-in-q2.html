--- title: "Japan to get Ouendan 2, Hourglass in Q2" date: 2007-04-11T08:27:11Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Courtesy NeoGAF's jonnyram comes a handful of rather important release dates for Japan.

  • Osu! Tatakae! Ouendan 2 (May 17)
  • The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass (June 23)
  • DK Bongo Blast ((June 28)

Though Ouendan 2 is most likely not going to make an appearance on these shores, Phantom Hourglass most certainly is, and Bongo Blast is highly likely—when we hear what NOA's plans are regarding those titles, we'll certainly let you know.
