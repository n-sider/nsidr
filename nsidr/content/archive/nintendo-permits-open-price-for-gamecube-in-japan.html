--- title: "Nintendo permits \"open price\" for GameCube in Japan" date: 2005-10-14T10:27:33Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Gamasutra is reporting that Nintendo is now permitting "open price" for GameCube in Japan, according to an official Nintendo Co. report in that country.

The policy permits retailers to sell the GameCube at any price they see fit, rather than selling it at a price Nintendo dictates. The practice has also been used by Sony since May 2005 for the PlayStation 2.
