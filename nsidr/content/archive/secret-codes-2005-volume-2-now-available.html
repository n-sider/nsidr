--- title: "Secret Codes 2005, Volume 2 now available" date: 2005-07-08T21:27:46Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- BradyGames said it has released Secret Codes 2005, Volume 2 for Game Boy Advance, GameCube and Nintendo DS to North American retailers nationwide.

Secret Codes 2005, Volume 2 is described as a collection of codes and cheats for a variety of games released this generation. Some of the titles covered in this pocket guide include Duel Masters, GoldenEye: Rogue Agent, NFL Street 2, The Incredibles, Yu-Gi-Oh! 7 Trails to Glory: World Championship Tournament 2005, Ping Pals, and more. More information can be found at the BradyGames website.
