--- title: "Screens for \"Custom Robo: Battle Revolution\" and \"WarioWare, Inc.: Mega Party Game$\"" date: 2004-03-10T03:55:39Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

I freely admit my bias towards the following two titles. I want them, and therefore you must look at them. Fortunately, the screens are somewhat new.

First up, we have Custom Robo: Battle Revolution, which is scheduled for a North American release on May 10, 2004. The game sports both an extensive one player RPG mode and multiplayer battle.

Next up are screens for WarioWare, Inc.: Mega Party Game$, which is scheduled for a North American release on April 5, 2004. With over 200 mini-games, four-player gaming has never looked better.
