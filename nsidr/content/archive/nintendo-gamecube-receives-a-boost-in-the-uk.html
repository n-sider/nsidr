--- title: "Nintendo GameCube receives a boost in the UK" date: 2004-05-21T15:16:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo UK experienced a 60 percent increase in GameCube hardware sales after the launch of the Pokmon Colosseum Mega Pack, according to C&VG.com.

The bundle released on May 14 and contained a GameCube console, a copy of Pokmon Colosseum, a Pokdex organizer, a GameCube-to-GBA link cable and a Memory Card 251.
