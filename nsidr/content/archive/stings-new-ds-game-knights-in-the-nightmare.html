--- title: "Sting's new DS game: Knights in the Nightmare" date: 2008-02-13T13:36:26Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- The latest Famitsu revealed a new DS game from Sting, the developer of Riviera: The Promised Land and Yggdra Union: We'll Never Fight Alone, two GBA games previously brought to America by Atlus and since ported to PSP. Sting's new game, Knights in the Nightmare, is currently planned for a 2008 release in Japan. An American release is currently unconfirmed. The game features the following:

  • The main character is a wisp, who takes control of knights by charming them and giving orders.
  • It is a "turn-based RTS"—each turn (both offensive and defensive) is time based and plays out in real time, but it controls like a turn-based trategy game.
  • The top screen shows the gameplay while the bottom screen shows a wire-map of the battlefield.
  • Players control the wisp with the stylus and use it to give knights orders.
  • Demons will aim for the wisp. Players have to avoid the demons, like in a shooting game.
  • The heroine is a female knight named Maria.
  • It is the fourth entry in the "Department Heaven Episode" series—the first being Riviera, the second being Yggdra Union, and the third being unknown.
  • Shinichi Itou is the game director. He directed previous entries in the series.
  • Satoku Kiyuzuki is responsible for design creation. He was the main character designer for Yggdra Union.
  • Sunaho Tobe is handling illustration. He was the main character designer for Riviera and illustrated the cards in Yggdra Union.
