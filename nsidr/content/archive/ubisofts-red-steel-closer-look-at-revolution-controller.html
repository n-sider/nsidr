--- title: "Ubisoft's Red Steel; closer look at Revolution controller" date: 2006-04-13T01:21:43Z ---

It seems Ubisoft isn't content being the first developer to showcase a game for Nintendo's next-gen console currently dubbed Revolution. They're also determined to rub in our face the fact that they get to make funny expressions while intimately touching the controller.

The team has posted a collection of images taken with the Revolution controller in a variety of poses -- some frightful, others delightful -- on image sharing website Flickr. These images were also used in Game Informer Magazine's coverage of the game. It's hard to argue; seeing the controller in a person's hand really gives one a new perspective on the hardware's modest size.

Ubisoft is currently preparing Red Steel, a samurai-fighter/first-person shooter, for release alongside the launch of Nintendo Revolution later this year. More information on the game will come in May at the Electronic Entertainment Expo.
