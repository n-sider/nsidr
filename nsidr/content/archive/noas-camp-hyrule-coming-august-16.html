--- title: "NOA's Camp Hyrule coming August 16" date: 2004-07-02T12:54:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America will be hosting its online 2004 "Camp Hyrule" event during the week of August 16, 2004. The event is completely free however participants are required to register in advance. Registration will take place on CampHyrule.com from July 27 - July 29.

Each registered "camper" is assigned to a "virtual cabin", where he or she can meet other gamers and interact with Nintendo's gameplay experts in exclusive Camp Hyrule chat rooms and message boards. Daily events include live chat shows, games, art activities and more.
