--- title: "Director of \"Super Mario Sunshine\" to demonstrate previously unannounced title" date: 2004-05-03T16:45:10Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Yoshiaki Koizumi, director of Super Mario Sunshine and The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, and Takao Shimizu will be demonstrating a new, previously unannounced title at this years E3, according to 1up.com.

Further details on the title and its platform are not known and will likely be kept quiet until E3.
