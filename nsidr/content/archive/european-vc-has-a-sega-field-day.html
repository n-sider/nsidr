--- title: "European VC has a Sega field day" date: 2007-07-06T15:55:27Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- This week, Europe gets four new VC releases—three Mega Drive games and one TurboGrafx-16 game—bringing the total up to 109.

  • Sonic the Hedgehog 2 (Mega Drive, 800 points)
  • Ecco: The Tides of Time (Mega Drive, 800 points)
  • Golden Axe II (Mega Drive, 800 points)
  • Dragon Sprit (TurboGrafx-16, 600 points)

Between taking control of a dolphin, a blue hedgehog, a dragon, and magic-wielding warriors, there's plenty to do this week.
