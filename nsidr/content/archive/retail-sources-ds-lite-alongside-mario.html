--- title: "Retail sources: DS Lite alongside Mario" date: 2006-02-24T11:58:33Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

I don't want to say I told you so (OK, maybe I do), but IGN's got word from retail sources that they should expect DS Lite May 7, alongside New Super Mario Bros., already confirmed for a release that date.

Of course, this is all rumor at this point; no confirmation has yet come from NOA, and there may indeed yet be issues considering the half-delayed Japanese launch. But wouldn't it be great, if true?
