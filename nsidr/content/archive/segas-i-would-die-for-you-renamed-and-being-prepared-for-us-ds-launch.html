--- title: "Sega's \"I Would Die For You\" renamed and being prepared for US DS launch" date: 2004-10-05T21:06:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sega of America will release its first Nintendo DS title for the console's launch, according to IGN.com. The title, previously named "Project Rub" and "I Would Die for You", will release under the name "Feel the Magic: XY-XX". The title is said to contain several mini-games of which IGN goes into further detail here.
