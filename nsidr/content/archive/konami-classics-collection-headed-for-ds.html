--- title: "Konami classics collection headed for DS" date: 2007-02-05T09:27:09Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Konami hasn't forgotten the DS this weekend: they're also bringing a collection of 15 classic Konami games to the platform.

Konami Arcade Classics is a bit nebulous at this point, but we do know that it will include shooter Gradius, other shooter Time Pilot, and beat-em-up Yie-Ar Kung Fu. Both original-styled ports and remixed versions will be part of the title.

The collection is slated to drop this fall.
