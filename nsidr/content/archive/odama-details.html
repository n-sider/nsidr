--- title: "Odama Details" date: 2004-05-13T17:39:29Z contributors: - James Remington ---
  • Odama is a tentative title.
  • Odama takes place durring the Japanese feudal wars.
  • Gamers use the flippers to send the pinball called Odama to destory enemy barracks and turrets. Hitting enemy soldiers captures them and makes them join your army.
  • If you're not careful you'll crush your own troops.
  • Players progress to the next level by gaining more territory and assaulting the enemies gate.
  • Your actions can result in your troops losing confidence in you and your abilities, they will then become complacent.
  • A second player can use the DK Bongo controllers to speed up and urge soldiers onwards. The troops will lose confidence in the 1st player if this occurs.
  • A second player can step in and control your troops instead of allowing them to make their own decisions.
  • As you progress your troops will need to liberate additional flippers, dam rivers and clear the way to the enemies gate.
  • Odama is being developed by VIVARIUM Inc.
