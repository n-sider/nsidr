--- title: "Vivendi releases Crash and Spyro titles for Game Boy Advance" date: 2004-06-02T01:10:01Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Vivendi Universal Games said today that Crash Bandicoot Purple: Ripto's Rampage and Spyro Orange: The Cortex Conspiracy are available on the Game Boy Advance. Vivendi describes the titles as follows:

In Crash Bandicoot Purple: Ripto's Rampage and Spyro Orange: The Cortex Conspiracy, Dr. Cortex (Crash's nemesis) and Ripto (Spyro's nemesis) have accidentally met up through underground portals and are set on taking over the entire universe. In an attempt to share resources and expand their territories, Cortex and Ripto are using the portals to flood their two worlds with their evil minions. Crash and Spyro must switch worlds with each other to discover the identity of the new menace and save the universe from this evil plot!

Connecting both titles gives gamers the option of unlocking special content including additional multiplayer games, more playable characters and the full sets of Crash Bandicoot Purple and Spyro Orange collector cards.
