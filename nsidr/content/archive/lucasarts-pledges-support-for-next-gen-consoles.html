--- title: "LucasArts pledges support for next-gen consoles" date: 2005-06-07T00:53:03Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Boomtown.com recently interviewed LucasArts president Jim Ward. He speaks about the company's intended support of the next-generation consoles.

"We celebrate all new platforms, we will support all new platforms, and we will be on all platforms. When you watch a game, we will do it on all of them as best as we can. You can surely talk about all those pros and cons but to the degree that consumers buy a certain platform, we want to make sure that there is a LucasArts game for it."

Questions posed to Ward also cover a wide range of topics including storytelling in games and his thoughts on the potential of online gaming. Be sure to check out the full interview here.
