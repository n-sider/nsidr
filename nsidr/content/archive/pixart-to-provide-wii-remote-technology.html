--- title: "Pixart to provide Wii remote technology" date: 2006-05-14T15:26:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo has announced its partnership with PixArt Imaging to provide object tracking technology for use in the Nintendo Wii remote controller. PixArt's Multi-Object Tracking engine technology can track multiple objects "in an unbelievably quick and responsive way."

"PixArt's technology enables a quick, dynamic play experience," commented Genyo Takeda, Senior Managing Director/General Manager, Integrated Research & Development Division, Nintendo Co., Ltd. "With PixArt's technology, Nintendo will usher in a new era of video games."
