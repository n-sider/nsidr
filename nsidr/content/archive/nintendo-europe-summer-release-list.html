--- title: "Nintendo Europe summer release list" date: 2006-05-18T10:44:53Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

It's that time again. Nintendo Europe has published a new batch of release dates for upcoming titles on all three current Nintendo platforms, and we're here to bring you the Nintendo-published selections from that list. So without any further ado, the list:

Nintendo DS

  • Super Princess Peach (May 26)
  • Dr. Kawashima's Brain Training: How Old Is Your Brain? (June 9)
  • New Super Mario Bros. (June 30)
  • Big Brain Academy (July 7)
  • Electroplankton (July 7)
  • Tenchu Dark Secret (September)
  • Club House Games (temporary name) (September)
  • English Training (temporary name) (September)

Nintendo GameCube

  • Chibi-Robo! (May 26)

Game Boy Advance

  • Final Fantasy IV Advance (June 2)
