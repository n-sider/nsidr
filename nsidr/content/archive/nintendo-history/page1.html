N-Sider Presents...
.: Nintendo - A Legendary Tale :.

The staff of N-Sider has spent the last half decade of our lives chronicling the exciting and emotional history of Nintendo Co., Ltd. It may be difficult for some to understand just why we place so much dedication and time into what is assumed-to-be a corporate money-fueled being. Nintendo however, is not a faceless entity. Nintendo's vibrant soul can be seen in the talent and creativity it unleashes with nearly everything it makes. We wish to extend thanks to Nintendo for all the joy and comfort it has provided us through the years we grew up and continues to do today. From this article (and furthermore, the N-Sider site itself) we hope you'll grow to appreciate Nintendo's past, present, and as a consequence, its future too!

Our goal when we started creating this was to provide a paradise for Nintendo fans as well a resource for those who require research or for those who simply wish expand their intellect and become the ultimate Nintendo expert. Whatever your need or goal might be, this is the most organized and comprehensive gathering of Nintendo related info on the world wide web and possibly, on the world itself.

Time has a tendency to deteriorate the things we most cherish. We hope to preserve Nintendo's rich history and archive it for the years to come. There are many "Thank You's" that must be given. That list is by no means comprehensive. As you can understand, this would not have been possible without the help and inspiration of so many people. We sincerely thank you all.

Now join on us our journey documenting over 100 years of Nintendo Co., Ltd.

1889 - 1967: The Lucky Birth
Nintendo begins its conception within the Yamauchi heart and legacy. Read about how Nintendo was born as a playing-card manufacturer and its turbulent journey as a small company in a ballooning industry.

1968 - 1974: The Electronic Revolution
Nintendo realizes the future. Read about how Nintendo makes its first move into the interactive electronic industry and about the men, such as Shigeru Miyamoto, who would make this vision a reality.

1975 - 1978: A Future Fueled By Imagination
Nintendo utilizes the talent of its personnel to create the gadgets and devices that bring excitement to children. Read about the visionary who establishes Nintendo's first video game development division.

1979 - 1983: Family Computer Becomes Realized
Nintendo's army of visionaries grows. Read about how Nintendo prepares its monumental entrance into the Japanese interactive video game industry at a time when critics said there was no interest.

1984 - 1989: Nintendo Entertainment System
Nintendo's home entertainment console takes the world by storm. Read about one of the most groundbreaking hardware launches in the history of electronics and the many obstacles and barriers that were overcome.

1990 - 1994: The Portable Wonder-Boy
Nintendo puts gaming into player's hands. Read about a device that will rule the gaming world for two decades. Also read about Nintendo's Super-sequel home console that helps further Nintendo's dominance.

1995 - 1996: The Third-Dimension is Born

1997 - 2000: Gaming Becomes Mainstream

2001 - 2003: An Uncertain Future

2004 - present: The Revolution of Gaming
