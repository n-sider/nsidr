--- title: "Pocketeers becomes an official Nintendo DS licensee" date: 2004-04-28T17:40:15Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Pocketeers has become an official Nintendo DS licensee, according to Pocketeers president Mat Hopwood in a conversation with N-Philes.com.

Hopwood said he could not speak further about Nintendo DS development after having signed a non-disclosure agreement.

Pocketeers is currently in development with a Game Boy Advance sequel to EA's Need for Speed Underground. The game will be using an upgraded engine from the original.
