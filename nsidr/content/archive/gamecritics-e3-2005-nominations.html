--- title: "GameCritics' E3 2005 nominations" date: 2005-05-31T22:44:04Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The GameCritics have announced their nominations for the 2005 Electronic Entertainment Expo. Winners will be announced on June 7.

Best of Show*
- F.E.A.R. (Monolith Productions/Vivendi Universal Games for PC)
- Gears of War (Epic Games/Microsoft Games Studio for Xbox 360)
- Spore (Maxis/Electronic Arts for PC)
- The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess (Nintendo for GameCube)
- Xbox 360 (Microsoft)

* IMPORTANT NOTE ON HARDWARE ELIGIBILITY The PlayStation 3 and Nintendo Revolution were ineligible for Best of Show because judges did not experience "hands-on" play with the systems. PlayStation 3 was, however, eligible for Best Hardware/Peripheral because real-time hardware demos were shown.

Best Original Game
- Indigo Prophecy (Quantic Dream/Atari for PC, PlayStation 2, Xbox)
- Nintendogs (Nintendo for Nintendo DS)
- Okami (Clover Studios/Capcom for PlayStation 2)
- Shadow of the Colossus (SCEI/SCEA for PlayStation 2)
- Spore (Maxis/Electronic Arts for PC)

- Prey (Human Head Studios/3D Realms/2K Games for PC)
- Spore (Maxis/Electronic Arts for PC)
