--- title: "VideoGame.Net Experience 2004 starts June 21" date: 2004-06-17T03:59:41Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

VideoGame.Net will be hosting the VideoGame.Net Experience 2004, which is described as a summer camp for kids in 6th through 12th grade to learn how to develop and design video games.

The first program starts on June 21, and new sessions follow every week thereafter, through August. More information on the program can be found here.

The event first started as a summer camp at Penn State Great Valley in 2003, and has grown into a year-round program at six university campuses.
