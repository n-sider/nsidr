--- title: "Nintendo DS Wi-Fi Theatrical Trailer" date: 2005-07-30T01:25:10Z contributors: - Colin Castro ---

On an otherwise slow news day, I was able to discover this fascinating little item. Starting this weekend, Nintendo is launching an ad campaign to promote public awareness on the Nintendo DS' Wi-Fi capability. The trailer itself is a minute long and is titled "The Sad, Agonizing Tale of Bhuvan Ganguly."

For all interested, you may view the trailer over at IGN.
