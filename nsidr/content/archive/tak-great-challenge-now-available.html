--- title: "Tak: Great Challenge now available" date: 2005-09-21T20:37:48Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- THQ said Tak: The Great Juju Challenge is now available for the Game Boy Advance, Nintendo DS, and Nintendo GameCube. The portable versions of the title feature a suggested retail price of $29.99 while the GameCube version is listed at $39.99. The title was developed by Avalanche Software.
