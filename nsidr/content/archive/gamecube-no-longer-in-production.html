--- title: "GameCube no longer in production" date: 2007-02-22T16:42:11Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- In an interview with GameDaily, NOA VP of marketing and corporate affairs Perrin Kaplan has sounded what is the final death knell for the Nintendo GameCube.

Perrin KaplanAre we producing any more GameCubes? No. But do we have the inventory there for people to still purchase? Yes.

Kaplan also said that the GameCube, despite no longer being manufactured, is "a great starter system" due to its low cost of entry and selection of inexpensive titles.
