--- title: "Super Black Bass Fishing available on Nintendo DS" date: 2006-03-29T00:26:18Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Super Black Bass Fishing is now available on the Nintendo DS for $19.99. The title comes to us from Majesco. More info can be found at the game's official website.

Super Black Bass Fishing lets players travel the world to lakes and what every fisherman loves most...fish! The Nintendo DS brings players closer to the game with the touch screen that is used to reel in fish and trail your lure in different ways.
