--- title: "Alien Hominid sequel in development" date: 2005-07-18T20:47:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The Behemoth said it will develop a multi-platform sequel to Alien Hominid, according to Nintendojo.com. Alien Hominid started as an online Flash game and the studio hopes to expand the gameplay to a four-player co-op adventure. Nintendojo was told the game will feature sidescrolling brawling action much like Golden Axe and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. The next Alien Hominid game is expected to release in 2006.
