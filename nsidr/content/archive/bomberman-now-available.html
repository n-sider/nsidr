--- title: "Bomberman now available" date: 2005-06-22T00:02:03Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ubisoft has said that Bomberman is now available for the Nintendo DS. A single card allows several players to play multiplayer at the same time. A full list of features and screenshots can be found at the game's official website.

Bomberman currently has an 80 percent average review ranking from GameRankings.com (based on one review).
