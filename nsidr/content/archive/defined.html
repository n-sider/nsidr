we (w) pron. - inclusive, indicates a group of people.

wee (w) n. - refers to something small in stature or size.

whee (w) v. - expression of excitement or jubilation.

wee wee (w w) v. - tinkle.

oui (w) v. - French word for 'yes'.

Wii (w) n. - innovative next-generation console from Nintendo.
