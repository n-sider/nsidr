--- title: "Charlie and Chocolate Factory now available" date: 2005-07-12T01:57:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Global Star said Charlie & the Chocolate Factory is now available on Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance via developer High Voltage Software. The title is being sold for $29.99. Charlie & the Chocolate Factory is based on the feature film directed by Tim Burton and the classic Roald Dahl book. The videogame allows players to explore deep inside the chocolate factory and play as Charlie Bucket.
