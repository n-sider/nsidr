--- title: "Nintendo DS now at $130" date: 2005-08-16T11:05:46Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

In an official release, Nintendo of America has confirmed that it will reduce the price of its Nintendo DS handheld by $20. The Nintendo DS will now be priced at $130. The price will be effective August 21, a day before the release of the anticipated title Nintendogs.

Later this year Nintendo will launch its "Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection", a service that will allow other DS players to challenge each other via Wi-Fi.
