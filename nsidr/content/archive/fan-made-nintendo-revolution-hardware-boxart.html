--- title: "Fan-made Nintendo Revolution hardware boxart" date: 2006-02-20T20:47:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Crafty photoshop-wielding fans have taken to the task of envisioning the boxart for the Nintendo Revolution hardware. I'm not sure where they're going with the handle. Stay away. The images come via LiveJournal.com. Overall, they've managed to take the simplicity of the console design and apply it to the boxart well enough. Hats off, although I'm still confident we'll be more impressed with what Nintendo's designers ultimately come up with.
