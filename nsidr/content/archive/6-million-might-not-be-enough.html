--- title: "6 million might not be enough" date: 2006-10-27T10:34:14Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- A story at Virgin.net quotes Nintendo's Yoshihiro Mori suggesting the 6 million Wii consoles Nintendo is expected to manufacture between launch and the end of their fiscal year on March 31st may not be enough to meet demand.

Nintendo execs have previously said they expect to have a million units available for the console's launch in North America on November 19—potentially making the notion of preorders for launch day obsolete. The console will sell for MSRPs of around $250 in the United States and $280 in Canada.
