--- title: "Sega Sammy brings the games — but what are they?" date: 2006-05-22T10:09:15Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

The Magic Box has the scoop on Sega Sammy's recent announcement of their plans for FY 2006 releases for the multitude of game platforms available today. Without any further ado:

  • Nintendo DS: 17 titles
  • Wii: 7 titles
  • Game Boy Advance: 2 titles

Speculation is rife about the number of Wii games, since Sega only showed Super Monkey Ball Banana Blitz and Sonic Wild Fire at the recent E3 2006 show, and Sega told us that the latter was not even a launch title. Similarly, Sega had only one Nintendo DS title at the show, the movie-license Charlotte's Web. With a scant ten months left in Sega Sammy's FY 2007, it should be interesting to see what they'll pull out of their hat between now and then.
