--- title: "Star Fox 64, Turtles, and Dragon's Curse hit VC" date: 2007-04-02T15:04:41Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo has announced their Wii Virtual Console releases for the week. They're as follows:

  • Star Fox 64 (Nintendo 64, 1,000 Wii Points)
  • Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (NES, 600 Wii Points)
  • Dragon's Curse (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

The titles, as usual, went up at 9 a.m. Pacific time, so if anything on today's list interests you, head on over to the Wii Shop Channel and download away. Wii Points go for approximately $1 for 100.
