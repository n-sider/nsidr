--- title: "Even more upcoming Virtual Console titles" date: 2006-11-09T11:38:15Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Hot on the heels of the last revelation of upcoming Virtual Console titles comes another batch, courtesy the Entertainment Software Ratings Board, who is responsible for assigning ratings to games.

The additional titles are as follows—and again, all are rated E for Everyone. (Unlike other lists floating around the Internet, ours does not include Nintendo's own previously-announced titles.)

  • Dr. Mario
  • Excitebike
  • Ice Climber
  • Kirby's Dream Land 3
  • Kirby Super Star
  • The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
  • Mario Kart 64
  • Star Fox 64
  • Super Mario RPG: Legend of the Seven Stars
  • Super Metroid

Again, no dates have been set for these yet, but it's reasonably safe to expect they'll be available on Wii's Virtual Console sometime next year.
