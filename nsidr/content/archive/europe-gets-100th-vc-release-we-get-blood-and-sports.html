--- title: "Europe gets 100th VC release, we get blood and sports" date: 2007-06-18T11:47:11Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Last Friday, Europe reached its 100th Virtual Console release—less than three weeks after North America achieved the same milestone.

Europe's new games are as follows:

  • F-Zero X (N64, 1000 Wii Points)
  • J.J. & Jeff (TurboGrafx-16, 600 Wii Points)

North America will see these three new additions to the Virtual Console:

  • NES Open Tournament Golf (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • World Sports Competition (TurboGrafx-16, 600 Wii Points)
  • Bloody Wolf (TurboGrafx-16, 600 Wii Points)

Europe's new releases have been available for purchase all weekend, and North America's will be officially available later this morning at 9 a.m. PST.
