--- title: "[E3] Ubisoft Director Commends Wii, Shuns PS3" date: 2006-05-10T16:22:57Z contributors: - Brenden Petracek ---

Nintendojo has posted an interesting little bit on Ubisoft Montreal's (the development house working on the Wii title, Red Steel) creative director, Clint Hocking.

During a workshop held yesterday focusing on next-generation game design, he was very critical of Sony's choice to "mimic" Nintendo's Wii remote, among other things. Head over to Nintendojo for the details.

It should be noted that Clint was not one of the Ubisoft Montreal personel giving the Red Steel demonstration at Nintendo's pre-show conference. That was Xavier Poix and Roman Campos.
