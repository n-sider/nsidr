--- title: "EGM says Hudson planning 30 Virtual Console games" date: 2006-09-13T01:18:58Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The latest EGM Live podcast includes a short discussion about Nintendo Wii's Virtual Console. EGM executive editor Shane Bettenhausen said Hudson is working on nearly 30 titles for the online distribution service. "Hudson told me they're working on 30 games right now," said Bettenhausen. Not all are expected to arrive at launch.

In May 2006, Hudson revealed it intended on releasing over a 100 TurboGrafx titles on the Virtual Console. Bonk's Adventure is currently Hudson's only officially announced Virtual Console game and was shown at the May E3 Expo. More details soon.
