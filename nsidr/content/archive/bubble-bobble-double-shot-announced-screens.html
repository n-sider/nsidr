--- title: "Bubble Bobble: Double Shot announced, screens" date: 2006-11-30T02:24:49Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- Via The Magic Box comes word that Rising Star Games will be releasing Bubble Bobble: Double Shot, a four-player DS Bubble Bobble adventure, in the first quarter of 2007.

           

In addition to namesakes Bub and Bob, a third character, creatively named Bubu, will also be featured. In addition, the title will contain a variety of small, stylus-controlled mini-games. 100 levels are anticipated.

Theoretically, this game will be bug-free, unlike this year's Bubble Bobble Revolution, which notoriously contained a progress-halting glitch on one of the later boss levels.
