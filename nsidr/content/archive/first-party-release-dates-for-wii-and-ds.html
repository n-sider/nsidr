--- title: "First-party release dates for Wii and DS" date: 2007-07-12T02:41:00Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Nintendo has announced quite a few release dates for Wii and DS games. Some names are new, some are old, and some have changed.

Nintendo DS

  • Brain Age 2: More Training in Minutes a Day (Aug. 20, 2007)
  • Chibi-Robo: Park Patrol (Oct. 2, 2007)
  • DK Jungle Climber (Sept. 10, 2007)
  • Flash Focus: Vision Training in Minutes a Day (Oct. 15, 2007)
  • The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass (Oct. 1, 2007)
  • Mario Party DS (Nov. 19, 2007)
  • Nintendo Crossword (working title) (TBA)
  • Nintendo Magic (working title) (Nov. 26, 2007)
  • Picross DS (July 30, 2007)
  • Professor Layton and the Curious Village (Dec. 3, 2007)

Wii

  • Battalion Wars 2 (Oct. 29, 2007)
  • Donkey Kong Barrel Blast (Oct. 8, 2007)
  • Endless Ocean (Oct. 29, 2007)
  • Fire Emblem (Nov. 5, 2007)
  • Mario Kart Wii (2008)
  • Mario Strikers Charged (July 30, 2007)
  • Metroid Prime 3: Corruption (Aug. 27, 2007)
  • Super Mario Galaxy (Nov. 12, 2007)
  • Super Smash Bros. Brawl (Dec. 3, 2007)
  • Wii Fit (TBA)

DK Jet has received yet another new title; now it's called Donkey Kong Barrel Blast. Forever Blue has also been renamed to Endless Ocean for American audiences. Finally, it looks like the eye training game from Japan is getting localized as Flash Focus. The rest of the games are par for the course.
