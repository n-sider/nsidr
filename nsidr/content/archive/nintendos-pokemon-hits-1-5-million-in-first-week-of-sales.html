--- title: "Nintendo's Pokemon hits 1.5 million in first week of sales" date: 2006-10-03T03:14:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Media Create has released Japanese sales figures for the recent release of the Nintendo DS Pokemon duo -- Pokemon Diamond and Pokemon Pearl. Diamond has attained sales of 813,237 units while Pearl has reached sales of 762,029 since their September 28 release less than a week ago. This amounts to 1,575,266 in combined sales, which eclipses the first week sales of all prior Pokemon releases.
