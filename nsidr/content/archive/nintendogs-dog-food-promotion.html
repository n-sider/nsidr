--- title: "Nintendogs dog food promotion" date: 2005-08-27T16:13:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo UK said it has partnered with Bakers Complete, the maker of dry dog food, for the launch of Nintendogs. As part of the promotion, Nintendogs will be featured on over 1.5 million packs of Bakers Complete from October this year. Money off vouchers for Bakers products will also be included in copies of Nintendogs for anyone who picks up the game at the UK launch on 7 October. There will also be an online competition to win one of 100 Nintendo DS Nintendogs prize packs. More information can be found at the official website.
