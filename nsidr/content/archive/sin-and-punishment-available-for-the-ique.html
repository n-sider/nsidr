--- title: "\"Sin & Punishment\" available for the iQue" date: 2004-09-30T23:33:59Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Co., Ltd. subsidiary iQue Ltd. has confirmed the next title available on September 25 for its Chinese iQue console is Sin and Punishment: Successor of Earth. The title originally released only in Japan for the Nintendo 64 on November 21, 2000. It never found release outside of Japan.
