--- title: "Namco Museum Remixes a few classics" date: 2007-05-21T15:08:36Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Five Namco classics will be getting a Wii update with Namco Museum Remix. The game will feature remakes of Pac'n Roll, originally released on the Nintendo DS; Galaga, originally released in the arcades; Rally-X, also from the arcades; Pac-Motos; and Gator Panic. Pac-Man himself appears to be a prominent character in most of the remixes.

Along with the five updates, the game also boasts nine original classics,
including Galaxian, Dig Dug, Mappy, Xevious, Gaplus, Super Pac-Man, Pac & Pal, Pac-Mania and Cute Q.

The game is currently scheduled for a tentative release this coming fall.
