--- title: "Winners of Nintendo's \"Ultimate Summer Job\" announced and more" date: 2004-07-08T16:07:39Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo has announced the 48 winners of its "Ultimate Summer Job" contest. Check them out at www.nintendostreetteam.com. This week, Nintendo will post the 90-second video clips that helped each member secure a spot on the Nintendo Street Team. Winners were chosen based on their enthusiasm for Nintendo as well as their people skills. A calendar of events is posted on the website as well.

The group of 48 will be split into six teams of eight from Chicago, Atlanta, Dallas, New York, Los Angeles and Seattle. They will be paid $100 a day and will compete for a $40,000 team bonus. The teams will be travelling the country to showcase the latest GameCube and Game Boy Advance titles while also competiting against each other to be the best Nintendo Street Team.
