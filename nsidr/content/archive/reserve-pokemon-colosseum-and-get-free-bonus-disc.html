--- title: "Reserve Pokemon Colosseum and get free bonus disc" date: 2004-02-10T15:23:14Z contributors: - Jeffrey Van Camp ---

Nintendo of America has announced that anyone who reserves a copy of Pokemon Colosseum before it hits shelves March 24th, will immediately recieve a bonus disc containing the following:

  • Pokemon Colosseum Preview
  • New Movie Sneak Peek
  • A special download which allows you to add Jirachi to your Pokemon Ruby / Saffire collection.

For those unaware, Pokemon Colosseum is an upcoming game for the GameCube, in the same vein as Pokemon Stadium for the Nintendo 64. A notable difference, however, is that the game will actually contain a fully 3D single player campaign.

Source: Nintendo of America
