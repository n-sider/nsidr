--- title: "Virtual Console gets another three" date: 2007-05-07T16:42:56Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- As is the Monday custom, new retro games now grace the Wii Shop Channel's Virtual Console section. Today's offerings, which went live at 9 a.m. Pacific, are as follows:

  • Mighty Bomb Jack (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Final Fight (Super NES, 800 Wii Points)
  • Ordyne (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

The titles can be acquired via the Wii Shop Channel in exchange for the noted sums of Wii Points, which are available in various denominations at a price of about 100 for $1.
