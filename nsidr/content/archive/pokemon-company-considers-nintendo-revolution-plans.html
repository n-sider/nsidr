--- title: "Pokemon Company considers Nintendo Revolution plans" date: 2006-03-18T21:53:25Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Famitsu magazine recently interviewed Tsunekazu Ishihara, president of The Pokemon Company, according to IGN. He was questioned about many things relating to Pokemon including the manner in which the series might continue on Nintendo's next-generation console Revolution.

"We're considering many plans for the Revolution," Ishihara admits. "However, there are still areas of the Revolution itself that have not been seen, so we'd like to coordinate with Nintendo's movements."
