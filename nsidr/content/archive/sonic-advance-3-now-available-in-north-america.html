--- title: "\"Sonic Advance 3\" now available in North America" date: 2004-06-07T18:47:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

THQ and Sega said Sonic Advance 3 is now available at North American retailers nationwide at an MSRP of $29.99.

The title features a new up to four-player Game Boy Game Link multiplayer and battle mode. There are several playable characters including Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, Cream and Amy.

The Sonic Advance franchise has reached sales of more than 1.5 million units, according to NPD.
