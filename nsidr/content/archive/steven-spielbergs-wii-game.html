--- title: "Steven Spielberg's Wii game" date: 2007-07-10T17:17:48Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- In collaboration with Steven Spielberg, EA is currently working on three brand new intellectual properties. Two of those three were revealed in a press release today.

One of those two games is an action-adventure title with an emphasis on emotional interaction for the 360, PS3, and PC. The other is a puzzle game for all ages inspired by Jenga.

The Jenga-inspired game features a physics engine that allegedly brings Wii remote interactivity to a new level. Move the remote as you would a block to perform that motion on screen. There are single-player, co-op, and versus gameplay modes, and players can even use the Wii remote to build their own games and structures, or bring out the inner King Kong and tear them down.

No release date was given for either game. It is possible the games may be shown at E3 in some form, but there is no confirmation one way or the other.
