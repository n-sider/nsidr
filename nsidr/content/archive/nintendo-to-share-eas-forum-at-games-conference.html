--- title: "Nintendo to share EA's forum at Games Conference" date: 2006-08-02T23:36:44Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

An update to the Games Conference site shows Nintendo and Electronic Arts are to share a press conference at 2:00 p.m. in the Forum. It's not currently known what they will be discussing, but it's likely EA's commitment to Wii, especially with their dedicated Wii development group in Burnaby, will be trumpeted.

Both Nintendo and EA also have their own, separate conferences earlier in the day.
