--- title: "Mother 3 GB micro faceplate and new game screens" date: 2006-02-21T04:19:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

More presents have arrived at the Mother 3 website. The website is being updated each week in preparation for the game's anticipated Japanese launch for Game Boy Advance on April 20. The latest update includes three screens from the game, news that a special edition Game Boy micro faceplate will be released and also a look at the game's boxart. Still no word from Nintendo on a North American release. We'll keep you posted.
