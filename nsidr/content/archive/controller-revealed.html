--- title: "Controller revealed" date: 2005-09-16T01:04:31Z ---

IGN has just posted images of the long-awaited Revolution controller. Feast your eyes on these pictures:

  • Revolution and controller
  • Revolution controller from front, back, side, and both ends
  • Revolution controller in hand, in front of screen -- the controller can be used to point to objects on the screen.
  • Revolution controller with attached analog stick -- the controller supports various attachments as well as the capability to dock into future controllers.
  • Revolution controller in different colors
