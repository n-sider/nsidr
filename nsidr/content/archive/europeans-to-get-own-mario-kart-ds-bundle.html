--- title: "Europeans to get own Mario Kart DS bundle" date: 2005-11-03T09:59:46Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo Europe has announced today that it will be offering its customers a bundle featuring a Nintendo DS system and the Mario Kart DS game.

Unlike the bundle releasing in North America, which includes the ber-elite "racing red" DS, decals, and a special wrist strap, the European bundle comes with a standard-issue silver-colored Nintendo DS.

The bundle will release on November 25, 2005, the same day the game itself releases. The bundle will be available for GBP 100.00, or Euro 150.00.
