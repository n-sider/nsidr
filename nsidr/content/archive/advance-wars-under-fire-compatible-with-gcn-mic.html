--- title: "\"Advance Wars: Under Fire\" compatible with GCN mic?" date: 2004-09-14T12:36:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to Puissance Nintendo, Advance Wars: Under Fire for the Nintendo GameCube will be compatible with the microphone accessory that is being released this holiday season with Mario Party 6. Under Fire is scheduled for a 2005 release and is being developed by UK-based Kuju Entertainment. Neither Kuju nor Nintendo have confirmed the microphone compatibility at this time.
