--- title: "Video: The Life of a Space Invader" date: 2006-06-16T18:11:10Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Space Invaders is a celebrated game from the days of quarter-hungry arcades. It was manufactured by Taito in 1978 and influenced all games that followed. Tokyo DJ Ken Ishii has put together a music video that pays homage to this classic title. We follow the life of an alien, from conception to marriage to an unfortunate but inevitable death.
