--- title: "Dr. Mario & Puzzle League announced" date: 2005-09-30T11:24:59Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America announced that it will be releasing a two-game pak including Dr. Mario and Puzzle League (a.k.a. Panel de Pon) on December 5, 2005. The title is rated E by the ESRB.
