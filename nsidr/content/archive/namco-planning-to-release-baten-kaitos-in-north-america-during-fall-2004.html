--- title: "Namco planning to release \"Baten Kaitos\" in North America during Fall 2004" date: 2004-05-07T18:07:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Namco spokesman Nao Higo said in an interview with GCAdvanced that Baten Kaitos, the GameCube exclusive card-RPG, is currently scheduled for a North American release this October or November.

Higo also said that there are over 1000 cards in the game and that players can carry more cards as they gain more experience. The game is said to feature between 40-80 hours of gameplay.
