Australian Launch Centre

Keep your browser locked on to the Australian Launch Centre. Here, you will find continual updates leading up to the May 17 launch of GameCube in Australia.

Over the coming weeks, you'll see launch impressions, player's impressions of the console and its games as well as any other related news/information. Below, you can find a complete list of all playable GameCube units in the country.

The links bar on the right will lead you to the latest articles regarding the launch. Keep checking this space for updates.
