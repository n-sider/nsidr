--- title: "Nintendo Pennant Chase Baseball & Geist Delayed" date: 2005-05-21T17:52:08Z contributors: - James Remington ---

Sad news for those looking forward to Giest or Nintendo Pennant Chase Baseball. Nintendo has just pushed back the release date for both titles, which were scheduled for a June release. Nintendo Pennant Chase Baseball is now scheduled for a July 5th, 2005 release and Geist has been pushed back to some time in August 2005.
