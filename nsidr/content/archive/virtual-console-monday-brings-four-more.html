--- title: "Virtual Console Monday brings four more" date: 2006-12-11T13:55:02Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- It's Monday and you know what that means!

VIRTUAL CONSOLE MONDAY


  • Ice Hockey (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)
  • Gunstar Heroes (Sega Genesis, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • Dr. Robotnik's Mean Bean Machine (Sega Genesis, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • Alien Crush (TurboGrafx16, 1 player, 600 Wii Points)

This week's additions bring us an assortment of primarily non-Nintendo titles, including a few quite lauded fan favorites like Gunstar Heroes and the timeless classic Ice Hockey. Alien Crush is also a fun space-futuristic pinballer. A pretty good assortment this week!

Updates occur at 9 a.m. Pacific time, which works out to 10 a.m. Mountain time, 11 a.m. Central time, and noon Eastern time.
