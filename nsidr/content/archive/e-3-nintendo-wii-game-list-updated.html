--- title: "[E3] Nintendo Wii game list [updated]" date: 2006-05-09T02:28:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

This list grows by the day. N-Sider has compiled a roster of games officially announced for Nintendo's Wii console. Keep in mind, especially if you notice any games lacking, that this list reflects only those that were accompanied by an official announcement.

The latest additions come from Activision with Call of Duty 3, Namco Bandai with One Piece Unlimited Adventure and Snoopy vs. The Red Baron, Atari with Godzilla, Buena Vista with Disney's Chicken Little: Ace in Action and Disney's Meet The Robinsons, Square Enix with Dragon Quest Swords and Crystal Chronicles: The Crystal Bearers, and Nintendo with Wario Ware.
