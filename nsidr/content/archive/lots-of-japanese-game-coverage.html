--- title: "Lots of Japanese game coverage" date: 2005-11-15T11:00:18Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

DS-x2 has posted scans from Famitsu, CoroCoro, and a "yet unknown" magazine for several upcoming, released, and never-to-see-our-shores Nintendo DS games. Included in the list:

  • Animal Crossing: Wild World
  • Bleach DS
  • Mario Kart DS
  • Mario & Luigi: Partners in Time
  • Super Princess Peach
  • Pokemon Mysterious Dungeon
  • Pokemon Ranger
  • Resident Evil: Deadly Silence
