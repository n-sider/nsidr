--- title: "Australia gets Nintendogs Best Friends bundles" date: 2006-07-06T10:45:11Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Not-so-hot on the heels of September's release of Nintendogs Best Friends Edition in the US is Nintendo of Australia, doing the same thing with Mystic Pink and Cosmic Blue paint jobs on the systems bundled with the games.

The Best Friends release for Australia comes with the same starter breeds as the American release did: Labrador retriever, golden retriever, German shepherd dog, beagle, Yorkshire terrier and miniature dachshund.

The bundles are available today for a suggested retail price of $199.95. Nintendogs is classified G by the Office of Film and Literature Classification.
