--- title: "EA releases \"FIFA Soccer 2005\" for GCN" date: 2004-10-14T14:17:28Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts announced FIFA Soccer 2005 is available at North American retailers nationwide. The title will introduce several new gameplay features including "First Touch Control", which is said to allow greater freedom of movement during gameplay. More info and screens can be found at the game's official website.

FIFA Soccer 2005 currently has an 86 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.
