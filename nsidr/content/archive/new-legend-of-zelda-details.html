--- title: "New Legend of Zelda details" date: 2004-05-12T13:01:56Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGNCube has learned several new details about The Legend of Zelda for GameCube.

  • The title will run on a modified version of the Wind Waker engine.
  • It's not a remake, but rather an original story and game.
  • Link will feature the same eye-tracking system used in Wind Waker.
  • A new dynamic camera system - when Link explores dungeons the camera will pan back and shoot the action from a top-down angle but when the character encounters an enemy the camera will change to the normal behind-the-back perspective.
  • Neither Shigeru Miyamoto nor Eiji Aonuma will be directing the Legend of Zelda title. Both serve as producers. The new director is not known.
