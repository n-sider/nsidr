--- title: "Man Plays Super Mario Bros. For First Time Ever" date: 2006-06-27T16:13:09Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker ---

Some Kotaku dude posted a link to this YouTube video of Japanese comedian Shigenori Yamashita playing Super Mario Bros. for the very first time ever. Aside from the total hilarity, it's actually a very interesting case study on how even games lauded as being the most played and the most accessible ever are still just... a little confusing to the average Joe Sixpack.

I am reminded of innocent times.
