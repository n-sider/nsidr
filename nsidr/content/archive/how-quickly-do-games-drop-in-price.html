--- title: "How quickly do games drop in price?" date: 2004-06-22T20:32:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Over at Curmudgeon Gamer, they've posed the question "...how long do frugal gamers have to wait for a $50 game to drop to $40 or $30 or lower?"

It's a study that has been on-going since December 2003 and covers perhaps too small of a selection of games to make it statistically accurate. Nevertheless, the article is still interesting to check out here.
