--- title: "New DS colors and Play-Yan details" date: 2005-08-17T23:54:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo Europe said it will release two additional Nintendo DS hardware colors on October 7. These will include a Pink and Blue edition of the Nintendo DS. The new colored units will launch alongside the release of the puppy-simulator Nintendogs.

In related news, Nintendo said it will release its Play-Yan peripheral although no date nor price was revealed. Play-Yan gives the Game Boy Advance and Nintendo DS the ability to play MP3 and MPG4 files.
