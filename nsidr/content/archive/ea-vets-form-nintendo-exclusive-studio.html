--- title: "EA vets form Nintendo-exclusive studio" date: 2007-04-18T10:46:58Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- A group of veterans of the EA Canada studios responsible for FIFA, Need for Speed, and James Bond titles have announced the formation of a new studio, Jet Black Studios, that will focus solely on Wii and Nintendo DS development.

Co-founder and CEO Roger Freddi seems especially enthused about both platforms' online capabilities, saying "Both the Nintendo DS and the Wii's Wi-Fi capabilities provide an advanced medium for Jet Black to bring already popular franchises to an even broader worldwide audience."

Jet Black is currently working with business management firm Flashman Studios, who has worked on licensed projects for Disney/Pixar, Ignition, WB, and SOE.
