--- title: "VC gets 100th game with Zelda II" date: 2007-06-04T10:57:23Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Four games were released on the Virtual Console today, bringing Nintendo to a new milestone of 100 releases. The 100th game itself should please quite a few Nintendo fans.

  • Milon's Secret Castle (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • ToeJam & Earl in Panic on Funkotron (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)
  • Dead Moon (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)
  • Zelda II - The Adventure of Link (NES, 500 Wii Points)

All titles are will be available for purchase come 9am PST on the North American Virtual Console via those wonderful Wii Points.
