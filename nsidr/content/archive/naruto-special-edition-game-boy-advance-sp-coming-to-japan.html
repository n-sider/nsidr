--- title: "\"Naruto\" special edition Game Boy Advance SP coming to Japan" date: 2004-06-28T13:51:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan will be releasing a limited edition Game Boy Advance SP alongside the release of a new Naruto RPG title, according to VGPro.

The special edition Game Boy Advance SP is colored a flourescent orange and is stamped with a symbol of "Kohona", which Naruto has on his forehead. It will release on July 22 in a bundle also containing the game and retail for 17,541 Yen.
