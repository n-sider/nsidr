--- title: "1 million units for North American launch" date: 2006-09-24T13:19:14Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

In an article posted at Mackenzie Investments, Nintendo of Canada VP and general manager Ron Bertram said he expects North America to receive a million Wiis in time for the console's premier launch November 19.

When Wii drops to retail on the 19th, it will sell for an MSRP of $249.99 in the United States and $279.95 in Canada.
