--- title: "Interview with creator of DS Training For Adults" date: 2005-10-26T14:02:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

1Up.com user Adam Gothelf has conducted an interview with Professor Ryuta Kawashima, the man behind the concept used in Nintendo's DS Training For Adults: Work Your Brain game. The full interview can be read here while an excerpt is below:

Q: How did the development first occur to make DS Training for Adults? Was it a "eureka!" moment or did Nintendo first approach you to develop the game, or something different altogether?

A: Nintendo has approached me. I have been introducing our research activities towards industries in Japan. I have been suggesting that, using brain imaging techniques, companies would be able to know how their products effect on human brain. Then Nintendo wishes to create videogames that can activate human prefrontal cortex.
