--- title: "Survey of Japanese DS owners" date: 2005-10-23T20:21:35Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

TotalVideoGames.com has posted a survey of Japanese DS owners done by C-News.

The survey reveals, among other things, that the majority of male respondents wanted lengthy games, the same opinion did not appear in the majority of female respondents. Interest in the Revolution among DS owners is also high.
