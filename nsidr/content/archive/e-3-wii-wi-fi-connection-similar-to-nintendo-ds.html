--- title: "[E3] Wii Wi-Fi Connection similar to Nintendo DS" date: 2006-05-11T18:32:04Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGN.com spoke with Nintendo EAD general manager Takashi Tezuka to learn several details about how the Wi-Fi Connection online service will be handled on Wii. It was learned that the service will be nearly identical to that used for the Nintendo DS. The service will emphasize the same three qualities of being easy, safe and free. The Wi-Fi Connection interface will also be unique to each piece of software. Finally, the Wii system will likewise use the idea of Friend Codes to manage a unique list of friends for each game.
