--- title: "Naruto Contest Winners!" date: 2006-05-02T00:12:03Z contributors: - Cory Faller --- We're proud to announce the winners of our Naruto Giveaway Extravaganza. David Orr, of Hazel Crest, Illinois, walked away with Naruto: Clash of Ninja for the Nintendo GameCube by submitting the following Haiku:

Red haired Sakura...
Wants Sasuke's affection?
Or his butt sex - su?

...a little unorthodox, but amusing enough to tickle our fancy. Naruto: Ninja Council for the GBA went to the lucky Rebecca Burks of Toronto, Ontario.

Update: Just to clear up any confusion, we counted "Sasuke" in the second line as three syllables, not two, due to its pronunciation in Japanese as "Sa-su-ke" and the Japanese origin of the Haiku itself.

Congratulations to the winners, and better luck to everyone else on our next contest!
