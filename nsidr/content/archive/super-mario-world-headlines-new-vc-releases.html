--- title: "Super Mario World headlines new VC releases" date: 2007-02-05T11:37:09Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- It's that time of the week, and Nintendo has put three new old games up on the Wii's Virtual Console. The titles are:

  • Super Mario World (Super NES, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • VIGILANTE (TurboGrafx-16, 1 player, 600 Wii Points)
  • Gain Ground (Sega Genesis, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)

As usual, all games are emulated versions of the originals with no changes. The titles will be posted at 9 a.m. Pacific Time. In North America, Wii Points are available for roughly $1 per 100.
