--- title: "Neversoft working on new title under publisher Activision" date: 2004-07-21T22:38:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Activision said it wants to get the word out early about a new game the company intends on publishing. According to the company, the developers of the Tony Hawk series Neversoft is working on a new video game in an unnamed genre. The title is scheduled for release in 2006 alongside another Tony Hawk sequel. Platforms were not specified. And well, that's all the info they've given.
