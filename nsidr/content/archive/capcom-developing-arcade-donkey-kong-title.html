--- title: "Capcom developing arcade Donkey Kong title" date: 2005-07-14T01:06:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Capcom said it will release a new arcade game called Donkey Kong Jungle Fever, according to Gamefront.de. There is no word on whether it is a port of the GameCube title Donkey Kong: Jungle Beat or a new iteration in the series. More information is said to be revealed at an event at its Osaka headquarters on July 27.
