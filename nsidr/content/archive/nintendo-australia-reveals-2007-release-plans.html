--- title: "Nintendo Australia reveals 2007 release plans" date: 2006-12-13T11:56:41Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo Australia has announced their upcoming first-quarter 2007 releases. They're as follows:

Wii

  • WarioWare: Smooth Moves (January 25; see our preview)
  • Excite Truck (February 22)

Nintendo DS

  • Mario vs. Donkey Kong 2: March of the Minis (January 18)
  • Nintendo DS Browser (January 18)
  • Hotel Dusk: Room 215 (February 22)
  • Sudoku Gridmaster (February 22)
  • Kirby Squeak Squad (March 1)
  • Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney (March 8)

Of particular note is the release of the Opera Software-developed Nintendo DS Browser in this territory, currently only available in Japan and Europe. GameSpot is reporting that only the DS Lite version of the browser will be available (the browser comes with a memory expansion pak).
