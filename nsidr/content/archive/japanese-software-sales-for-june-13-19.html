--- title: "Japanese software sales for June 13 - 19" date: 2005-06-23T21:51:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Media Create has released the top ten Japanese software sales for the week of June 13 - June 19. The first number indicates sales for the week while the second shows sales since release.

  1. Drag-On Dragoon 2: Love Red, Ambivalence Black (PS2, Square Enix) - 125,000 units
  2. Touhokudai...Kyoudoukenkyuu Center (NDS, Nintendo) - 34,000 / 187,000 units
  3. Kenka Banchou (PS2, Spike) - 21,000 / 60,000 units
  4. Soccer Life 2 (PS2, Banpresto) - 16,000 / 52,000 units
  5. Metal Saga: Sajin no Kusari (PS2, Success) - 10,000 / 73,000 units
  6. Nintendogs: Shiba & Friends (NDS, Nintendo) - 9,000 / 206,000 units
  7. Rakuhiki Jiten (NDS, Nintendo) - 7,000 units
  8. Inu Yasha: Ougi Ranbu (PS2, Bandai) - 7,000 units
  9. Nintendogs: Dachshund & Friends (NDS, Nintendo) - 7,000 / 137,000 units
  10. TearRing Saga Series: Berwick Saga (PS2, Enterbrain) - 7,000 / 128,000 units
