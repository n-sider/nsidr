--- title: "Dr. Mario Classic NES to feature wireless multiplay" date: 2004-08-13T14:09:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Dr. Mario, the Classic NES title in the second release of the Game Boy Advance series, will feature both wireless and wired-link play that will only require one copy of the game for the two systems. The second round of Classic NES titles is scheduled for release in North America October 25.
