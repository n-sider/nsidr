--- title: "Pokemon theme parks and ringtones" date: 2006-02-20T01:42:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The Pokémon Company and mobile media provider Zingy, Inc. have launched "Pokémon Mobile" in North American markets. Pokémon Mobile offers over 30 Pokemon-themed ringtones, wallpapers, and voicetones on most of the major domestic cell phone carriers. Content is available at pokemon.com/mobile. The downloads are offered for the price of around $1.99 per item and will be included on users' monthly mobile phone bill.

In related Poke news, Nintendo will be operating a traveling Pokémon-themed amusement park. The park will begin in Taipei, Taiwan, and run for three months in which time Nintendo expects it to draw over 1 million visitors. Depending on the park's reception, it may eventually arrive in the United States, Europe, or Shanghai. The park was originally setup in Nagoya, Japan, for the World Exposition last September where it drew 4.5 million visitors during its initial six months of operation.
