--- title: "Nintendo to provide both wired and wireless online" date: 2006-10-04T23:31:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

C&VG reports that Nintendo will release an Ethernet Kit for Wii so players who lack a wireless connection can still go online. Pricing and a release date are not known at this time.

The Wii has built-in Wi-Fi technology. It will be compatible with most wireless routers including the Nintendo Wi-Fi USB Connector released for the Nintendo DS.
