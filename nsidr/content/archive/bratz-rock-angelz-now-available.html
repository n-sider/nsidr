--- title: "Bratz: Rock Angelz now available" date: 2005-10-11T00:29:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- THQ's Bratz: Rock Angelz is now available for the Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance. The GameCube title is on sale for a suggested retail price of $39.99 while the portable version is available for $29.99. The game was developed by Blitz Games.
