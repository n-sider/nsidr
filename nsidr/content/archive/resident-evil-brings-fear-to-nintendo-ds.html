--- title: "Resident Evil brings fear to Nintendo DS" date: 2006-02-12T00:21:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Resident Evil Deadly Silence is now available for $34.99 for the Nintendo DS. The game was developed and published by Capcom.

The title is a remake of the original Resident Evil released on the PlayStation in 1996. This remake however is said to place more emphasis on action by containing more enemies and ammo. New puzzles can be interacted with using the touchpad. The game also features a 2-4 player wireless mode. The official website for the game can be found here.

The game currently has a 74 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 8 reviews).
