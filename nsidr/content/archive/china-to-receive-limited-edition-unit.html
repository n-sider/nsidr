--- title: "China to receive limited edition unit" date: 2005-08-24T11:15:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo's Chinese subsidiary iQue will release a special Nintendo DS unit through a promotion with the 7Up Company. The promotion began on August 15 and runs for 15 weeks. Consumers of the soda can collect bottle caps to win one of 15 limited edition Nintendo DS systems featuring a 7Up logo.
