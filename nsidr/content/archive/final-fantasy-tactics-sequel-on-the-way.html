--- title: "Final Fantasy Tactics sequel on the way" date: 2006-12-13T15:40:55Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- Scans of the newest Jump are in courtesy of the never-asleepers over at GAF and they bring some exciting news:

In addition to the previously announced Final Fantasy XII: Revenant Wings for the Nintendo DS, a new game called Final Fantasy Tactics A2: The Sealed Grimoire is on the way. Though a target platform has not been officially announced, speculation is heavy toward the DS or perhaps even the Game Boy Advance, due to the "A2" part of the title, which might indicate it is a sequel to the Nintendo-exclusive Final Fantasy Tactics Advance series.


Along with a PSP port of the original FFT, these three games make up what Square-Enix is calling the "Ivalice Alliance." No screens or many details on Grimoire are available yet, just this portion of the scan announcing the game:
