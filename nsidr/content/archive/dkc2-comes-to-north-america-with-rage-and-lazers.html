--- title: "DKC2 comes to North America with Rage and Lazers" date: 2007-05-21T11:37:36Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- After appearing last week in Europe, Donkey Kong Country 2: Diddy Kong's Quest will hit the North American Virtual Console today, accompanied by two other retro titles. The full list:

  • Donkey Kong Country 2: Diddy's Kong Quest (SNES, 800 Wii Points)
  • Streets of Rage 2 (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)
  • Blazing Lazers (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

All titles will be available at 9 a.m. Pacific time from the Wii Shop Channel's Virtual Console section. As is typically the case, Wii Points are available at the exchange rate of roughly 100 Points for US$1.
