It really is difficult to understand the game if you haven't played it. The aim of this feature is to give people the best representation of what it's like to actually play the game. Some other little tidbits have been added as well that most should find interesting. There aren't many visuals to go on in this feature, but since we all know how beautiful the game is, it seemed logical to show some of the finer details. So, why bother extend this?
