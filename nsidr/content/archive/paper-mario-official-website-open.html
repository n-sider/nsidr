--- title: "\"Paper Mario\" official website open" date: 2004-10-12T20:01:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has opened the official website for Paper Mario: The Thousand Year-Door for GameCube. The title is already out in stores nationwide. The flash website sticks with the creative paper Mario theme. Screens and other information are available. Don't hesitate to click on the Goombas here. Just beware of the one named Brandon. He likes to steal and hoard all the Heart Containers.
