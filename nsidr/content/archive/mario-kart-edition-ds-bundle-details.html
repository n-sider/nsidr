--- title: "Mario Kart edition DS bundle details" date: 2005-10-13T09:44:32Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America has released images of the upcoming Mario Kart edition DS, which will go on sale November 28 bundled with a copy of the game at a suggested retail price of $149.99.

The bundle's box art features the Wi-Fi Connection logo and indicates the inclusion of a checkered wrist strap and a decal set.
