--- title: "Two million copies of \"Spider-Man 2\" shipped" date: 2004-07-08T16:13:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Activision said it has shipped over two million units of its Spider-Man 2 video game in North America. The game is available for consoles including the Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance.

Spider-Man 2 for GameCube currently has an 82 percent review ranking at GameRankings.com.
