--- title: "Cover athlete selected for EA's \"NASCAR 2005: Chase for the Cup\"" date: 2004-07-23T17:51:48Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts said today that Kevin Harvick, driver of the No. 29 GM Goodwrench Chevrolet racecar, has been selected as the cover athlete for NASCAR 2005: Chase for the Cup. The title is being developed by EA Tiburon and will be available on GameCube on August 31.
