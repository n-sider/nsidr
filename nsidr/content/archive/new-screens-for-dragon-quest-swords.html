--- title: "New screens for Dragon Quest Swords" date: 2007-05-01T13:07:22Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
Famitsu's online site has a set of new screens for upcoming Wii exclusive Dragon Quest Swords: The Masked Queen and the Tower of Mirrors, showing off game features including shops, the overworld, battle, and companion characters.

Dragon Quest Swords will hit Japanese stores July 12. An American release date has not yet been announced.
