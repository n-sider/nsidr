--- title: "Nintendo Japan releases new GameCube bundle" date: 2004-07-07T03:11:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan has released the Enjoy Plus Pack GameCube bundle. Only 50,000 units will be available at a price of 19,800 Yen each. The bundle includes a Platinum GameCube, a Game Boy Advance Player, Memory Card 251 and a limited edition clear controller.
