--- title: "\"Final Fantasy I & II Advance\" gets a new release date" date: 2004-07-06T17:38:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Square Enix delayed the Japanese release of Final Fantasy I & II Advance last week due to a programming error. It appears the error was not that severe and Square Enix said today the Japanese release has only been postponed for two weeks, scheduled now for July 29.
