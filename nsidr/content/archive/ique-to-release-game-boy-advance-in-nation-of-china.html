--- title: "iQue to release Game Boy Advance in nation of China" date: 2004-06-09T16:07:10Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

On June 8, Nintendo released the Game Boy Advance handheld in the nation of China, according to iQue.com. The system is being distributed by the iQue company and is retailing for 9,248 Yen, or the approximate US equivalent of $84.

Several games will be translated to Chinese and released, with the first titles being Super Mario Advance and Warioland 4.
