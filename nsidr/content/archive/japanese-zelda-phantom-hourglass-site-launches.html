--- title: "Japanese Zelda: Phantom Hourglass site launches" date: 2007-06-13T13:36:23Z contributors: - Cory Faller --- Nintendo has launched the official Japanese website for The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass, and with it come a bevy of gameplay videos.

  • Gameplay trailer (embedded in homepage)
  • Basic movement
  • Sword combat
  • Boomerang control

There are three other sections of the site that are still locked away, sure to be opened progressively as the title's Japanese release date approaches.

Phantom Hourglass hits Japanese shores in a scant 10 days, and is set to arrive in North America later this year.
