--- title: "Naruto RPG coming to DS" date: 2007-06-03T17:14:11Z contributors: - Michael Foster --- Naruto is coming to the DS again with Naruto: Path of the Ninja (tentative title), the first Naruto RPG to hit the U.S., based on the popular anime series. Choosing from one of six different characters, players will use the time-based combat system to fight through, and explore, the Village Hidden in the Leaves and other sites from the show. The game is based on the Japanese game Naruto RPG 2: Chidori vs Rasengan.

The game is being developed by TOMY Corporation and D3Publisher of America, which has handled Naruto game releases as of late in the United States. Naruto: Path of the Ninja is anticipated to release this fall.
