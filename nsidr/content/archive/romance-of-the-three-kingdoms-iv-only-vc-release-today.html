--- title: "Romance of the Three Kingdoms IV only VC release today" date: 2007-03-26T12:14:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Only one title is slated to be released for Wii's Virtual Console Service today: Romance of the Three Kingdoms IV: Wall of Fire—a tactical simulation game where the objective is to unite three Chinese kingdoms.

The title was originally released on the Super NES and will be available in the Wii Shop Channel for 800 Wii Points come 9 a.m. Pacific time.
