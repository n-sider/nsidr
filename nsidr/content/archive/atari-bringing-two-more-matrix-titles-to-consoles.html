--- title: "Atari bringing two more Matrix titles to consoles" date: 2004-06-14T17:34:54Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

CEO Bruno Bonnell said at the Piper Jaffray investor conference that Atari is developing two games based on the Matrix license, according to 1Up.com. He said the first title will be a sequel to Enter the Matrix while the second title in development will appear on a next-generation console.
