--- title: "O~3 to publish Chaos Field" date: 2005-08-15T20:11:47Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- O~3 Entertainment said it will publish Chaos Field for the Nintendo GameCube this holiday season. Chaos Field is a vertical shooter containing five levels, with three stages each. The game also includes a variety of playable characters and weapons.
