--- title: "Two-tone DS Lite bundled with Brain Age 2, now in US" date: 2007-08-07T20:41:19Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- The latest in fashion for the DS Lite is a two-tone color scheme—the top half crimson and the bottom half black. This new color will hit stores August 21 and come bundled with a brand new copy of Brain Age 2: More Training in Minutes a Day for the price of $149.99.

DS Lite, Brain Age 2 bundle

The bundle is already available to Canadians for $159.99, and it comes with a snazzy carrying case, which is as of yet unconfirmed for the US bundle.
