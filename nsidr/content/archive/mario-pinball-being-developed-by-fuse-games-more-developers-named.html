--- title: "\"Mario Pinball\" being developed by Fuse Games - more developers named" date: 2004-05-12T01:34:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

N-Sider has learned that Donkey Konga is being developed by Namco while Donkey Kong Jungle Beat is being developed by Nintendo.

In addition, we've found that DK: King of Swing for the GBA is being developed by PAON Corporation. Mario Pinball, also for GBA, is being developed by FUSE GAMES Ltd.

We'll have more information soon.
