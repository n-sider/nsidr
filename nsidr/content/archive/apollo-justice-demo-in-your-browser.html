--- title: "Apollo Justice demo in your browser" date: 2008-01-16T12:04:48Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Can't wait for Apollo Justice: Ace Attorney? Capcom has the first part of the first case of the as-of-yet unreleased game playable online in Flash-enabled browsers. You can even play it on Wii's Internet Channel.

Apollo Justice heads for retail shelves on February 12. It's a new story arc, so you don't have to play the Phoenix Wright games before you get it, but if you like what you see, you might want to consider checking out the previous titles in the series.
