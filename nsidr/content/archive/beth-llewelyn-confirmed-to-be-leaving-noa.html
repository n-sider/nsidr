--- title: "Beth Llewelyn confirmed to be leaving NOA" date: 2007-09-27T10:51:47Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Number two in the rumored three bigshots (George Harrison, Beth Llewelyn, and Perrin Kaplan) leaving Nintendo of America has confirmed her departure—Beth Llewelyn is on her way out, saying she's going to take "some time off to be a full-time mom for a bit".

Harrison already confirmed his departure a couple weeks ago, saying that he wouldn't be making the move to California with other NOA employees. Kaplan has yet to say anything about her plans.
