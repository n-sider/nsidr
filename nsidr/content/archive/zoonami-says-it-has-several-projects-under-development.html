--- title: "Zoonami says it has several projects under development" date: 2004-04-26T17:05:03Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Zoonami, developer of the recently announced multiplatform Funkydilla music-mix title, said it is still in development with Game Zero, according to a report by IGNCube.com.

"We have projects in the works apart from Funkydilla, some are very long-term, and we can't talk about them," Zoonami president Martin Hollis told IGNCube. "'Game Zero' is a working title we don't use anymore, because it collides with an online gaming magazine."

When asked if the company had plans to debut any titles at E3, Hollis replied, "We don't plan to show any games at E3. It is a bit of a cattle-market really."
