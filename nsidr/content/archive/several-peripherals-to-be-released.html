--- title: "Several peripherals to be released" date: 2005-10-20T16:54:50Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- In addition to the recently unveiled DS Rumble Pak, the platform will see "a host of different peripherals", according to Nintendo Representatives at last week's Digital Life Expo in New York City. Each add-on will utilize the machine's already-existing Gameboy Advance slot.

Stay tuned to N-Sider for more details as they become available.
