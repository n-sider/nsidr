--- title: "Taito's TGS line-up" date: 2005-09-10T14:09:28Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Taito has released its list of software that it will display at the upcoming Japanese Tokyo Game Show beginning September 16. The line-up includes:
  • Lost Magic (NDS)
  • Touch Game Party (NDS)
  • Bubble Bobble Revolution (NDS)
  • Kyouryuu Taisen Dino Champ (NDS)
