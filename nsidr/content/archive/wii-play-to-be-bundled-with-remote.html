--- title: "Wii Play to be bundled with Remote" date: 2006-11-20T19:19:21Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Speaking to GameSpot in New York today, Nintendo of America president Reggie Fils-Aime confirmed that Wii Play, previously slated to release January 15, will in fact be sold as a bundle with a controller (presumably a Wii Remote, as it is to be sold in Japan and Europe).

Pricing was not announced but rumors have pegged the bundle at $50.
