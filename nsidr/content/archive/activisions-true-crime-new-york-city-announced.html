--- title: "Activision's True Crime: New York City announced" date: 2005-06-22T21:24:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Activision has opened a website for the next iteration in the True Crime series. The website reveals the new name and setting of the game for GameCube, PlayStation 2 and Xbox with the title True Crime: New York City. Take a look at the website yourself here.
