--- title: "\"Wario Ware Twisted!\" now in stores" date: 2005-05-26T20:16:32Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Wario Ware Twisted is now available for the Game Boy Advance. The title expands gameplay by utilizing a gyro sensor that detects motion. Over 200 new microgames and many unlockables are available. Wario Ware Twisted is retailing for $34.99 and currently has an 88 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com. More information and screens can be found at the official website.
