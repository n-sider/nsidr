--- title: "Metroid Prime: Hunters not Retro?" date: 2004-05-12T15:43:38Z contributors: - James Remington ---

In somewhat of a shock, N-Philes is reporting that Retro Studios is not behind the new Nintendo DS title Metroid Prime: Hunters. Even more surprising is the claim that Retro was completely unaware of Hunters existence until yesterday. Of course this begs the question as to who is behind Metroid Prime: Hunters.
