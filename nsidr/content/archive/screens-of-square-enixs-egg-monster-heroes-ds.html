--- title: "Screens of Square Enix's \"Egg Monster Heroes\" DS" date: 2004-09-10T14:32:01Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Square Enix has released the first screens of its Nintendo DS RPG titled Egg Monster Heroes. The game is said to make full use of the dual screen capabilities with the top screen displaying the main adventure and battles while the bottom screen displays the field map and battle formation.
