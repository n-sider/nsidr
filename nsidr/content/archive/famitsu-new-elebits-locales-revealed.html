--- title: "Famitsu: New Elebits Locales Revealed" date: 2006-10-13T19:06:40Z contributors: - Cory Faller --- The latest issue of the popular Japanese gaming magazine Famitsu has finally shown off some Elebits screens that feature locales other than "kitchen" and "back yard". It seems that the entire game may take place on a single individual's property, as all of the new screens showcase other rooms in the house. There's a hallway, what appears to be a study, a children's room, and even a bathroom.

The screens also illustrate some uses for your laser other than "wreckin' the place", such as placing building blocks in alphabetical order and reorganizing books on a shelf into numerical order.
