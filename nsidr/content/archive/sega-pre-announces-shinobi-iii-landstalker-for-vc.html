--- title: "Sega pre-announces Shinobi III, Landstalker for VC" date: 2007-08-16T20:49:20Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Without pinpointing a date, Sega of America announced today their plans to release two titles from the Genesis' library on Wii's Virtual Console service. The titles are:

  • Shinobi III: Return of the Ninja Master
  • Landstalker

Shinobi III has already made appearances on Japan's and Europe's Virtual Console; Landstalker, on the other hand, is entirely new to the service. Sega notes that Genesis titles "start at" 800 Wii Points, but isn't pegging a specific price on these titles. Of course, if experience is any guide, they will in fact land on the service at 800 Wii Points apiece.
