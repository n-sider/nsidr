--- title: "Blizzard asks readers about StarCraft and Diablo" date: 2005-07-18T20:46:12Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Blizzard's most recent newsletter asked readers if they would be interested in buying the strategy games Diablo II and StarCraft on the Nintendo DS if they featured wireless multiplaying options, according to GBA.N64Europe.com. Neither game has been announced to be in development and Blizzard has made no comment on any intention to create the titles.
