--- title: "Lost in Blue now available" date: 2005-09-28T11:36:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Konami's Lost in Blue is now available for the Nintendo DS. The title features a suggested retail price of $29.99. More information can be found at the game's official website.

Lost in Blue currently has a 75 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on two reviews).
