--- title: "Nintendo gets Publisher of the Year and more" date: 2005-11-07T11:29:35Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- November 4th saw the prestigious London Hilton playing host to an exciting event in the gaming industry - The Golden Joystick Awards. In addition to scoring Publisher of the Year (a prestigous award in itself), Nintendo took home Portable Game of the Year for Mario 64 DS.

Other highlights included Resident Evil 4 (as many would suspect) scooping Game of the Year for GCN, while Zelda: Twilight Princess was honored with... The One to Watch for Xmas?

Your guess is as good as ours.
