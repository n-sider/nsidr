--- title: "Wii in laptop form" date: 2007-01-19T17:22:18Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---
Portable glory (courtesy Engadget)
Hardware homebrewer Ben Heckerdorn has taken the already fairly-portable Wii one step further, creating the Wii Laptop for electronics blog Engadget.

The Wii Laptop has its own built-in 16:9 7" LCD screen with its own sensor bar and has ports for connecting an external sensor bar and monitor as well as a single GameCube controller. It does not appear to have a battery, but does have the power supply built-in as well as storage space for the power cord.

Heckerdorn plans to run a multi-part How-To series on Engadget starting next Tuesday for those who'd like to build their own.
