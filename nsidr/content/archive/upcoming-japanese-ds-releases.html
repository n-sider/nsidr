--- title: "Upcoming Japanese DS releases" date: 2005-06-07T01:13:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Tomy will release the RPG Naruto RPG 2: Chidori vs Rasengan in Japan on July 14, according to The-MagicBox.com. There is currently no word on a North American release.

Marvelous Interactive will release its action-RPG Lunar Genesis in Japan on August 25. The North American version of the game will be released later this year by Ubisoft under the name Lunar: Dragon Song.

Namco will release the its action title Pac n' Roll in Japan on July 28. The title is scheduled to arrive in North America on August 16.
