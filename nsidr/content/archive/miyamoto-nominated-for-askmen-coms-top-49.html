--- title: "Miyamoto nominated for AskMen.com's Top 49" date: 2007-08-24T11:04:18Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- It is now the time of year to vote in AskMen.com's Top 49 Men of 2007. It's as simple as rating the nominees from 1 to 10, and at the end, the man with the highest average ratings is the big number one.

Last year's top man of 2006 was George Clooney, but this year, one of the nominees is a man gamers everywhere recognize as the creator of some of Nintendo's most popular franchises: Shigeru Miyamoto. Surely, all others pale in comparison.

To put in your vote for the father of Mario, find your way to the Arts & Culture tab and scroll down a bit. Don't hesitate to put in your vote for any of the other nominees you may find worthy.
