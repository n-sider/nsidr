--- title: "Nintendo DS shipment numbers?" date: 2004-10-27T19:03:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Over 300,000 Nintendo DS units will be available for the Japanese launch on December 2, according to Nintendo Japan. Shipments will follow every week with a total shipment by year's end being 1,000,000 units. While not yet announced, shipments for North America will likely be very similar. Nintendo has said that only two million units will be available worldwide by year's end.
