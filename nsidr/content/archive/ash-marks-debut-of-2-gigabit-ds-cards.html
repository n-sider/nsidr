--- title: "ASH marks debut of 2 gigabit DS cards" date: 2007-10-02T13:05:00Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- This week's Japanese release of ASH: Archaic Sealed Heat marks a first for DS releases. Mistwalker's FMV-heavy RPG comes on an unprecedentedly large 2 gigabit card—twice the size Nintendo themselves said was the maximum for the DS when it debuted at E3 2004.

Game systems generally measure in bits, not bytes; a 2 gigabit card is actually 256 megabytes.
