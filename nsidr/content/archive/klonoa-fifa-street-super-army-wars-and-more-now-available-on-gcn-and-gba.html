--- title: "Klonoa, FIFA Street, Super Army Wars and more now available on GCN and GBA" date: 2005-02-22T20:45:18Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

It's a big day for releases. There are several titles arriving at North American stores today for GameCube and Game Boy Advance.

Vivendi Universal has released Robots for the Nintendo GameCube, Nintendo DS and Game Boy Advance. Robots is based on the Twentieth Century Fox Film of the same name. For screens and additional information, check out the game's official website.

Electronic Arts has released FIFA Street and MVP Baseball 2005 for the Nintendo GameCube. The official websites are located respectively at fifastreet.com and easports.com. FIFA Street currently has a 60 percent average review ranking while MVP Baseball 2005 has an 85 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.

Atlus Software has released Super Army Wars for the Game Boy Advance. Check out the official website for screenshots, video and more.

Namco has released Klonoa 2 Dream Champ Tournament for the Game Boy Advance. The title currently has a 64 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com. Check out the game's official website for screens and info.

Namco also released Ace Combat for the Game Boy Advance. Ace Combat currently has a 61 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com. As always, check out the official website for more information on the game.
