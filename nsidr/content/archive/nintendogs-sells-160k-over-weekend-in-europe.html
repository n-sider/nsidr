--- title: "Nintendogs sells 160K over weekend in Europe" date: 2005-10-13T09:52:22Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo DS killer app Nintendogs sold 160,000 copies over the weekend since its release on Friday, October 7. Combined with a system price drop to GBP 89.99 (EUR 129.99), the Nintendo DS also saw a 400-700% increase in system sales across Europe, according to Nintendo of Europe Senior Director of Marketing Jim Merrick.
