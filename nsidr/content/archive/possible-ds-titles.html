--- title: "Possible DS titles?" date: 2004-05-11T14:58:38Z contributors: - James Remington ---

GameCube Europe is reporting that GameFAQs had updated their Nintendo DS page with the following titles.

  • Metroid Prime Hunters
  • Picto-Chat
  • Super Mario 4 x 4
  • WarioWare Inc. DS

Although the page seems to have been taken down, the titles can still be found in their database by doing a search. Whether or not this is just speculation on GameFAQs part or the titles are just place holders remains to be seen.
