--- title: "Toys R Us to retail gold GameCubes in Japan" date: 2004-07-21T01:56:41Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Japanese Toys R Us' will sell a limited edition gold GameCube in Japan for 13,799 Yen, according to IGNCube. The system is called "Starlight Gold" and comes with the standard controller and AC adapter.

There's currently no word on a North American release of the Starlight Gold console.
