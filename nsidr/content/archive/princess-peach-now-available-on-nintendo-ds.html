--- title: "Princess Peach now available on Nintendo DS" date: 2006-02-27T22:31:00Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Super Princess Peach is now available for the Nintendo DS for $34.99. The game currently has an 81 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 10 reviews).

Mario has been kidnapped and Peach must go to his rescue. Players take control of Princess Peach and with the assistance of her magical umbrella, she must traverse a variety of 2D side scrolling stages. Check out the game's official website for more info.
