--- title: "Kojima: Revolution would be \"foolish\" to pass up" date: 2005-12-29T05:23:34Z contributors: - Ryan Cooper --- Japan's top game developers were recently reviewed (by themselves, no less) in Dorimaga, a publication from the land of the rising sun. Among them was, of course, Hideo Kojima -- his credits include the Metal Gear franchise and Policenauts. When touching on the subject of content for next generation platforms, Kojima seemed adamant in his gravitation toward Revolution, saying that (as a designer) it would be "foolish" to pass up. He added that advancing in areas other than pure technology were imperitive -- a contradiction to what Kojima believes he's known for; a lover of movies.
