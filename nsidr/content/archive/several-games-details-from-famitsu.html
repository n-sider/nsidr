--- title: "Several games, details from Famitsu" date: 2006-01-16T22:30:39Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Courtesy 4ColorRebellion comes a collection of new scans from Famitsu revealing the shape of the 2006 gaming world to come — in Japan, at least.

First off, Polarium developer Mitchell has been hard at work at a new puzzle game for the Nintendo DS called PuzzLoop, while Taito will be bringing Cooking Mama — you guessed it, a cooking game.

Taito's action RPG Lost Magic is also looking pretty good, and will be offering online play via the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. Finally, Baten Kaitos 2 — rumored to be released stateside this year — makes an appearance sporting a lot of new cards and several pretty new screenshots.
