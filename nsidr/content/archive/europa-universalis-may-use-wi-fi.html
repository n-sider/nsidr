--- title: "Europa Universalis may use Wi-Fi" date: 2005-08-16T22:52:25Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- GamersMark interviewed G.R Moore of Pocket PC Studios regarding the company's upcoming Nintendo DS title Europa Universalis II. Moore was questioned in particular about whether or not the title would take advantage of Nintendo's upcoming Wi-Fi Connection service. The full interview can be found here.

GM: One thing DS enthusiasts have been wondering about is if whether or not Europa Universalis II will utilize Nintendos coming Wi-Fi online multiplayer game service. If it isnt, why was it decided not to include the feature? Can we expect some form of wireless multiplayer?

GR: We intend to provide wireless support on the Nintendo DS version, permitting the DS to attach to a desktop PC acting as the game host. This support will also be available on the PDA versions, with all platforms interacting seamlessly.
