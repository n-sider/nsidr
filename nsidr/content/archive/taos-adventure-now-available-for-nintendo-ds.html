--- title: "Tao's Adventure now available for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-03-21T21:59:56Z ---

Tao's Adventure: Curse of the Demon Seal is now available for the Nintendo DS at a retail price of $29.99. The title comes to us via Konami. For more info, check out the game's official website.

This RPG follows the adventure of a student trying to master his magical arts. After a curse befalls the people of your hometown, along with your family, it's left to you to use your magical powers and find a way to lift the curse. The game currently has a 55 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 1 review).
