--- title: "Mario Kart: Double Dash!! GameCube bundle coming in November" date: 2004-10-25T18:29:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America announced that starting November 14, it will be offering a Mario Kart: Double Dash!! GameCube bundle. The bundle will include a Nintendo GameCube, a GameCube controller, and a copy of Mario Kart: Double Dash!! for $99.99.
