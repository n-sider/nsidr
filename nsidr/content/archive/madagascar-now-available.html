--- title: "Madagascar now available" date: 2005-05-26T20:01:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Activistion has said that Madagascar for the Game Boy Advance, Nintendo DS and Nintendo GameCube is now available in North American retailers. The game is based on the DreamWork's Animation that follows the zany antics of four New York zoo animals. All four characters are playable in the game and the title features several modes of play and mini-games. More information on the game can be found at its official website.

The Nintendo DS version allows players to play wireless multiplayer minigames and use the touch screen to switch characters in the middle of levels. The Nintendo DS version currently has a 78 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com.

The GameCube and Nintendo DS versions are on sale for $39.99 while the Game Boy Advance version is $29.99.
