--- title: "Penny Arcade Expo" date: 2005-08-25T21:25:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The Penny Arcade Expo will be held August 26 - 28 at the Meydenbauer Center in downtown Bellevue, WA. Nintendo as well as several other software publishers will be exhibiting upcoming software at the event. Various musical acts will also be on hand including MC Frontalot, Optimus Rhyme, and MC Chris.
