--- title: "Video Games Live concert coming to cities across US" date: 2005-06-23T01:30:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Its been announced that Video Games Live will be brought to cities across the United States. The videogame music concert will feature music from such videogames franchises as Mario, Zelda, Halo, Metal Gear Solid, Warcraft, Myst, Castlevania, Medal of Honor, Sonic, Tron, Tomb Raider, Advent Rising, Headhunter, Beyond Good & Evil, Splinter Cell, Ghost Recon, Rainbow Six, EverQuest II, Pong and Donkey Kong. Dates and locations are below while more information can be found at the official website.
