--- title: "Nintendo Exceeding Wii Production Estimates" date: 2006-10-11T05:10:37Z contributors: - James Remington --- Briefing.com (subscription required) is reporting that Nintendo has been exceeding their original Wii production estimates and is now predicting to have an estimated 2 million units at launch, twice the quantity of Nintendo's initial estimate.

If by some chance you're unlucky enough not to have a Wii preordered, Nintendo is predicting to have at least 7 million and upwards to 9 million Wii's available by the end of the 4th quarter.
