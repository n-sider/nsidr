--- title: "VC Monday brings Zelda, more easily-ignored titles" date: 2007-02-26T21:59:29Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- It's that time of the week again, and Nintendo of America has loaded the Wii Shop Channel with three new Virtual Console games.

Leading off is perennial fan-favorite The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time for 1,000 Wii points. Of course, fans will also note that they've had a few opportunities to own this title already on the Nintendo GameCube, via the Wind Waker preorder bonus with The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time: Master Quest as well as the original, and The Legend of Zelda: Collector's Edition, so thankfully, those not keen on buying the game again have alternatives as well. The complete release list:

  • The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time (Nintendo 64, 1,000 Wii Points)
  • Bio-Hazard Battle (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)
  • Chew Man Fu (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

To snag any one of these titles, head over to your Wii Shop Channel and buy, buy, buy!
