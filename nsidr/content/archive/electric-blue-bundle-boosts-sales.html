--- title: "Electric Blue bundle boosts sales" date: 2005-07-01T23:06:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- According to Nintendo, major retailers have reported that sales of the Nintendo DS have increased 86 percent over the weekly average. This increase is attributed to the bundle featuring the new Electric Blue version or standard silver Nintendo DS and a free copy of Super Mario 64 DS. Super Mario 64 DS has sold more than one million copies since launch. The bundle offer is available at select retailers until July 31.
