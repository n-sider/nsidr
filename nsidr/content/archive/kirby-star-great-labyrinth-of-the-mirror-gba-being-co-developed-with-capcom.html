--- title: "\"Kirby Star: Great Labyrinth of the Mirror\" (GBA) being co-developed with Capcom" date: 2004-02-10T01:24:16Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to Nintendo of Japan, the upcoming Game Boy Advance title Kirby Star: Great Labyrinth of the Mirror is being co-developed with Capcom's subsidiary company Flagship. Capcom has worked with Nintendo in the past with its The Legend of Zelda series for the Game Boy platform.

Source: The-MagicBox.com
