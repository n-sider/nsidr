--- title: "Hideo Kojima developing title" date: 2005-08-20T16:35:45Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- At the German Games Convention, Metal Gear Solid producer Hideo Kojima told Jeux-France.com that he was interested in Nintendo DS development. He said he is very interested in the capabilities of the dual screen and hopes to create an innovative game for the handheld. No other details were offered but that more might be revealed at the upcoming Tokyo Game Show.
