--- title: "Dr. Mario & Puzzle League, Animal Crossing at DigitalLife" date: 2005-10-14T10:06:32Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America said today it is bringing upcoming titles Dr. Mario & Puzzle League as well as Animal Crossing: Wild World to the DigitalLife consumer technology and entertainment show. Both titles are currently scheduled for a December 5 release.

Nintendo will also be exhibiting Nintendogs, Mario Kart DS, Mario & Luigi: Partners in Time, Dance Dance Revolution: Mario Mix, Super Mario Strikers, Donkey Kong Country 3, and Mario Tennis: Power Tour at the show.

The trade show runs from today through October 16 at the Javits Convention Center in New York City and is run by Ziff-Davis Media.
