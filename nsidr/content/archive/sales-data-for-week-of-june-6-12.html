In other news, Nintendo will release a Pokemon XD + GameCube Bundle Pack in Japan on August 4 for 16800 yen, according to The-MagicBox. The bundle will include a Pokemon XD Edition of the GameCube console, along with the Pokemon XD video game and a Memory Card 59.
