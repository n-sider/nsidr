--- title: "THQ brings \"Sabrewulf\" to the Game Boy Advance" date: 2004-06-09T15:55:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

THQ has released Rare's Game Boy Advance action title Sabrewulf to North American retailers nationwide.

Rare began development of the title while it was still under Nintendo. You play as Sabreman, and must acquire items and treasure from the Sabrewulf, who will then chase after you. You must do your best to escape, avoiding obstacles and setting traps.

Sabrewulf currently features an 82 percent review rating on GameRankings.com.
