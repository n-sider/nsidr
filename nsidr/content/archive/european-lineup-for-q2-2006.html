--- title: "European lineup for Q2 2006" date: 2006-01-25T11:28:54Z ---

Nintendo of Europe has taken the wraps off its 2006 spring lineup of titles. Of particular interest is the appearance of New Super Mario Bros. for the Nintendo DS, though it does not yet have an official title or a date — but it seems Nintendo wants to get it out the door in Q2.

Nintendo also seems to be picking up the pace on publishing several exclusive titles that have garnered positive reception in other territories. For example, the Nintendo DS title Trauma Center: Under the Knife will be published by Nintendo in Europe, having been published (and become somewhat rare, according to reports) by Atlus in the States.

We've only reproduced the Nintendo-published portion of the list here; for the full scoop, including third-party titles and a rehash of the already-announced Q1 release schedule, you'll want to check Nintendo's release list yourself.

Nintendo DS

  • Prof. Kawashima's Brain Training: How Old Is Your Brain? (Q2)
  • Game Fortune Bag (tentative title; Q2)
  • Megaman Battle Network 5 Double Team DS (April 13)
  • Electroplankton (April 21)
  • Tetris DS (April 21)
  • Pokmon Link! (April 28)
  • Trauma Center: Under the Knife (April 28)
  • Harvest Moon DS (Spring)
  • Metroid Prime Hunters (May 5)
  • Super Princess Peach (May 26)
  • Metroid Prime Pinball (June)
  • Puzzloop (June)
  • New Super Mario Bros. (TBC)
  • Big Brain Academy (TBC)

Nintendo GameCube

  • Harvest Moon: Magical Melody (Spring)
  • Chibi-Robo (tentative title; May 26)

Game Boy Advance

  • Polarium Advance (April 7)
  • Drill Dozer (tentative title; May 26)
  • Final Fantasy IV (June 2)
  • Wario Ware Twisted! (TBC)
