--- title: "Capcom's \"Phoenix Wright\" contains English option" date: 2005-08-15T09:59:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Capcom's upcoming Nintendo DS courtroom simulator title, Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney, will contain an English language option, according to the latest Famitsu magazine. This option will make it easier for anxious importers. Capcom is currently preparing the North American release of the game.
