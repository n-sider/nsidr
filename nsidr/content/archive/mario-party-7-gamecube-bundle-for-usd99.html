--- title: "Mario Party 7 GameCube bundle for $99" date: 2005-10-11T00:45:47Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America will offer a special bundle this holiday season that includes a Nintendo GameCube console, two controllers and a copy of Mario Party 7 (along with the Nintendo GameCube Mic) for the suggested retail price of $99.99.

Mario Party 7 is scheduled for release on November 7 and alone will be on sale for $49.99. Nintendo has not specified the release date for the bundle.
