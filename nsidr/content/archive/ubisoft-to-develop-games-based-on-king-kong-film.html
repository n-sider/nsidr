--- title: "Ubisoft to develop games based on \"King Kong\" film" date: 2004-10-14T14:14:43Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ubisoft announced it has gained the rights to develop games based on the upcoming Universal Pictures film King Kong. The game is said to be available worldwide on all platforms, including GameCube and Game Boy Advance, at the beginning of the holiday season. For reference, the movie will come to theaters December 14, 2005.
