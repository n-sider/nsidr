--- title: "Frogger now available" date: 2005-09-28T11:36:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Konami's Frogger: Ancient Shadow is now available for the Nintendo GameCube and Nintendo DS. Both versions are on sale for the suggested retail price of $29.99.

The GameCube version currently has a 54 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on two reviews) while the Nintendo DS version has a 50 percent average review ranking (based on one review).
