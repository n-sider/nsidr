--- title: "Driver: Parallel Lines headed to Wii" date: 2007-01-29T12:35:52Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Atari has dropped a handful of screens confirming the existence of a port of Driver: Parallel Lines for Wii. Unfortunately, according to the report from Jeux-France, the company is apparently not currently saying how the title will actually play on Wii.

The Wii port of Driver: Parallel Lines looks to be hitting European shelves in March.
