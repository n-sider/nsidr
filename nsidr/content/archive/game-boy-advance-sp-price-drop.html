--- title: "Game Boy Advance SP price drop?" date: 2004-08-31T02:33:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

N-Sider has learned that Nintendo of America will be lowering the price of the Game Boy Advance SP system in September. The exact decrease is not yet known, however it will likely be in the $10 - $20 range. The drop is likely in preparation for the release of the Nintendo DS. Please wait for an official announcement from Nintendo of America.
