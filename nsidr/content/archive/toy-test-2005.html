--- title: "Toy Test 2005" date: 2005-08-10T18:55:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The Toy Test 2005 is being held in Seattle, WA, at the Pacific Science Center from August 8 - 21. A list of GameCube titles will be playable at the event including:
  • Disney's Chicken Little
  • Mario Party 7
  • Shadow the Hedgehog
  • The Incredibles: Rise of the Underminer
  • The Incredible Hulk: Ultimate Destruction
  • Ty 3
If you live in the area, swing by and check it out. More information can be found here.
