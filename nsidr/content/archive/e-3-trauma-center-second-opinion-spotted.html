--- title: "[E3] Trauma Center: Second Opinion spotted" date: 2006-05-08T18:27:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Game Informer has found that a new Trauma Center title will be on display at the Electronic Entertainment Expo. Although the platform is currently unknown, the game will be titled Trauma Center: Second Opinion. Stay tuned for more details.

The original game, Trauma Center: Under the Knife, was released in 2005 by Atlus Software for the Nintendo DS. The game placed you in the role of a doctor. With your stylus in hand, you were pushed into stressful medical situations where you had to perform surgery and save the lives of your patients.
