--- title: "Pac-Pix coming to DS in April" date: 2005-03-09T18:27:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Namco Hometek said it is preparing Pac-Pix on the Nintendo DS for an April release. In the game, the player must draw Pac-Man as well as draw obstacles in order to eat the ghosts. You can browse some screens of the Japanese version here.
