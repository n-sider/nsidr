--- title: "Nintendo Europe preparing for the holiday?" date: 2004-09-10T14:35:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to MCVUK, Nintendo Europe is considering cutting the price of the Game Boy Advance SP to 69.99. Moreover, Nintendo Europe may also be planning a GameCube bundle for release that will include Mario Kart: Double Dash for 79.99.

Nintendo Europe's PR director Shelly Pearce told MCV, "We have a number of initiatives planned this Christmas and we will be announcing them in the coming weeks."
