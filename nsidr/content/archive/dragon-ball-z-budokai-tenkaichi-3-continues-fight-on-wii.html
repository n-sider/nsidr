--- title: "Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi 3 continues fight on Wii" date: 2007-05-21T23:08:07Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Atari will publish Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi 3 on Wii sometime this holiday. The game, which is said to feature over 20 new characters, is being developed by Namco Bandai and Spike.

Budokai Tenkaichi 3, the follow-up to the November 2006 release of Budokai Tenkaichi 2, will arrive with several graphical and gameplay enhancements. The stages for example, will include both day and night versions. At night, players will use the power of the moon to transform their character.
