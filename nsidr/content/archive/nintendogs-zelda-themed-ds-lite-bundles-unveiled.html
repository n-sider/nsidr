--- title: "Nintendogs, Zelda-themed DS Lite bundles unveiled" date: 2007-11-21T11:53:31Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo has unveiled two $150-MSRP bundles for the Nintendo DS Lite featuring themed systems, slated to hit shelves on famed retail holiday Black Friday—this week, on November 23.



Depending on which bundle you get, you'll get a copy of either Nintendogs: Best Friends Edtion or Zelda: Phantom Hourglass. The bundles have been rumored for some time, with the Zelda-themed Lite showing up at the Nintendo World Store and leaks from retailers' Black Friday ads showing them off.
