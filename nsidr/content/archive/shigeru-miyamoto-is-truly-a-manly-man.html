--- title: "Shigeru Miyamoto is truly a manly man" date: 2007-10-23T17:12:32Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Shigeru Miyamoto, definitely a man, and a manly one at that, has been voted the #19 Manliest Man of 2007 in AskMen.com's annual Manliest Man competition. Just how manly is our hero?

Miyamoto's manly moments of 2007, according to AskMen.com:

  • Listed in Time Asia's 60 Years of Asian Heroes edition, along with Mahatma Gandhi, Bruce Lee and the Dalai Lama.
  • Selected as one of Time magazine's 100 Most Influential People of the Year.
  • Received the Lifetime Achievement Award at the Game Developers' Choice Awards ceremony.
  • Was a keynote speaker at the 2007 Game Developers conference.
  • Unveiled a trailer for the Wii's Super Mario Galaxy.
  • Introduced Wii Fit.
  • Developed Mario and Sonic at the Olympic Games for the Wii and Nintendo DS.
