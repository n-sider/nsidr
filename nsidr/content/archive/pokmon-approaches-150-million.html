--- title: "Pokmon approaches 150 million" date: 2005-10-05T10:14:07Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America announced today that the Pokmon franchise is approaching 150 million games sold, having accounted for $15 billion in worldwide retail sales. This news comes on the heels of the recent release of Pokmon XD: Gale of Darkness, which shipped to retailers this past Monday.
