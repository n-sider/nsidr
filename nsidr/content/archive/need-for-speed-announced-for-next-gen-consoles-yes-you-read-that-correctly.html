--- title: "Need for Speed announced for \"next-gen consoles\" (yes, you read that correctly)" date: 2004-05-27T21:38:02Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts has announced the development of a Need for Speed title for next-generation consoles, despite none of the consoles yet being formally announced, according to CV&G.com.

Need for Speed Underground 2 producer Chuck Osieja told CV&G, "I think what we're working on is going to revolutionize the way people look at driving games."
