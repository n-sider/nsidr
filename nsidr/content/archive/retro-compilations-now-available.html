--- title: "Retro compilations now available" date: 2005-08-23T22:16:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Destination Software said Chess/Checkers/Backgammon Compilation, Paperboy/Rampage Compilation, Risk/Battleship/Clue Compilation, Sorry/Aggravation/Scrabble Jr Compilation as well as Millipede/Super Breakout/Lunar Lander Compilation are now available for the Game Boy Advance. Each title is on sale for the suggested retail price of $19.99.
