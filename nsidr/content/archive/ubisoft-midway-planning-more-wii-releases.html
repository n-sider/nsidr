--- title: "Ubisoft, Midway planning more Wii releases" date: 2006-08-02T10:23:47Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

On a panel at the Ziff Davis Electronic Games Summit, Ubisoft's Laurent Detoc revealed his company is working on seven titles for Nintendo's upcoming Wii home console, in addition to previously-announced Rayman Raving Rabbids and Red Steel. At the same panel, Midway's David Zucker said his company is working on six titles.

Detoc had high praise for Wii as well, expecting it to have "a large audience and market." Of playing it at E3, he added, "The first time I picked up that sucker I couldn't stop playing it."
