--- title: "Release forecast this week: Zack & Wiki and some other stuff" date: 2007-10-23T09:32:18Z contributors: - Glen Bayer - Mattie Behrens --- Below is a tentative list of games available this week of October 22-28. Please note that all releases are subject to change.

North America

This week is just too ridiculously full of good games for anyone's good. With the third installment of Phoenix Wright launching alongside Square-Enix's remake of Front Mission for DS and the point-and-click awesome of Zack & Wiki as well as the mainstream appeal of Guitar Hero III hitting Wii, it's likely most of the rest are just going to get ignored at retail—perhaps a shame, as there's potential goodness coming from corners like Lifesigns: Surgical Unit.

Europe

Metroid Prime 3 launches officially in Europe this week (for those who haven't already imported an American copy out of protest of later release dates and exorbitant pricing), which is pretty much all people need to know. Konami's dropping Dewy's Adventure as well for those interested; though if it ends up launched like it did in the U.S., it might just be too hard to find...
