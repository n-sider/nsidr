--- title: "Players to play \"GoldenEye: Rogue Agent\" from the view of the villain" date: 2004-05-05T17:37:27Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

EA has released some of the storyline for the upcoming GoldenEye: Rogue Agent first-person shooter for GameCube. Instead of playing as James Bond, players will instead take the role of the villains.

As an aspiring 00 agent dismissed from MI6 for reckless brutality, the player is hired as an enforcer by Auric Goldfinger, the wealthy super-villain with a lust for all things gold. Goldfinger is locked in a ruthless war against his archenemy, the brilliant scientist Dr. No. The prize is control of the world's greatest criminal organization. A brutal encounter with Dr. No costs the player an eye, but Goldfinger's technicians replace it with a gold-hued, synthetic eye, earning the player the name 'GoldenEye'.
