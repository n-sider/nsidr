--- title: "Atlus to publish Conspiracy's Panzer Tactics DS" date: 2007-07-25T11:11:48Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Atlus U.S.A. and Conspiracy Entertainment have announced an agreement for Atlus to publish Panzer Tactics DS for, as you might have guessed, the Nintendo DS.

The game will feature a 100-hours-plus single-player campaign, the ability to play as the German Wehrmacht, the Red Army, or the Western Allies in unique campaigns, and both local and online multiplayer—the latter featuring an online ladder mode.

Panzer Tactics DS is slated to drop to retail in the fourth quarter of this year.
