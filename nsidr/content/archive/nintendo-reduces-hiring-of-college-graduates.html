--- title: "Nintendo reduces hiring of college graduates" date: 2004-02-23T14:03:27Z contributors: - Jeffrey Van Camp ---

Polygon Mag has reported that, starting this spring, Nintendo plans to lower the number of college graduates it hires. The lagging Japanese video game market is said to be a reason behind the reduction.

"According to a recent survey of companies based in Kyoto, Japan, Nintendo is one of eighteen companies which plan to reduce the number of new employees they hire." - Dennis Day, News Editor at Polygon

Source: Polygon
