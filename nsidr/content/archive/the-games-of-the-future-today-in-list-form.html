--- title: "The games of the future... Today! (in list form)" date: 2010-06-18T17:00:00Z contributors: - Brenden Petracek series: - E3 2010 Coverage --- Every year during E3, Nintendo publishes a list of games in development for their various platforms and—where available—the release dates associated with them. We've compiled all of the Nintendo published titles and included links to pretty screen shots. We've also included a selection of third party offerings. The lists were quite long, so most of it was a judgment call on what we felt would interest our readers. Our apologies if you disagree that LITTLEST PET SHOP 3 Biggest Stars wasn't worth mentioning.

All dates are 2010, unless otherwise noted.
