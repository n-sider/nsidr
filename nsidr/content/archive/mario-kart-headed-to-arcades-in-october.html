--- title: "Mario Kart headed to arcades in October" date: 2005-09-26T09:58:19Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo and Namco America announced today that Mario Kart Arcade GP will ship to American arcades this October in limited quantities. The game will offer 24 courses, 57 objects (40 exclusive to the game), and 11 characters, including Namco characters Pac-Man, Ms. Pac-Man, and Blinky.

Also featured is a NamCam 2, which will allow players to have their mugshot taken and appear alongside their kart in-game.
