--- title: "The Spiderwick Chronicles coming to DS, Wii" date: 2007-08-10T14:00:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer - Jon Rust --- Sierra Entertainment has announced The Spiderwick Chronicles, a video game based on the 2008 film adaptation of the book series by Holly Black and Tony DiTerlizzi. The game is in development for Wii, DS, PC, Playstation 2, and Xbox 360.

The story follows twin brothers Jared and Simon and their sister Malory as they embark on a perilous but fantastic journey. Fans of the series can look forward to exploring a magical world filled with sprites, ogres, goblins and more as they take control of multiple characters from the books.

The Spiderwick Chronicles will hit store shelves in February 2008. In the meantime, check out the game's official website to pass the time.
