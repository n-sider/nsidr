--- title: "New Revolution images from the GDC show floor" date: 2006-03-22T19:44:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Revolution Report has hit the floor at the Game Developers Conference to snap some photos of Nintendo's next-generation console codenamed Revolution. The unit as well as the controller are on display within a glass case in Nintendo's booth. The hardware appears to be somewhat thicker than that first shown at the 2005 E3 Expo. In addition, the case lacks the "Revolution" logo and instead sports a simple Nintendo decal. Hit the link to see the images yourself.
