--- title: "Kojima working on game with Metal Gear Acid team" date: 2005-10-20T10:03:18Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Aussie-Nintendo.com is reporting Hideo Kojima is at work on an unspecified Revolution title with the team from the PSP title Metal Gear Acid.

Their source, an article in Game Informer magazine, reveals that the game is not part of the Metal Gear series but an entirely new game.
