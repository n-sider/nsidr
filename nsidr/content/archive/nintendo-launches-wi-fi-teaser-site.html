--- title: "Nintendo launches Wi-Fi teaser site" date: 2005-09-30T08:08:23Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America has launched a teaser site that Nintendo-watchers will have their eyes on for some time. NintendoWiFi.com currently consists of a spinning Wi-Fi logo, the words "Coming in November", and a button to take you to their main site at Nintendo.com, but will likely be a part of their bigger Wi-Fi advertising push in the near future.
