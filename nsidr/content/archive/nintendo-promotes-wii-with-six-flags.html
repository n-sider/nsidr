--- title: "Nintendo promotes Wii with Six Flags" date: 2007-01-23T14:36:15Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo and Six Flags today announced a cross-promotional partnership that sees Wii christened the "Official Gaming Console of Six Flags parks".

With the agreement, select Six Flags parks will have Wii Gaming Stations installed where park-goers can experience complimentary playtime on the system. Additional promotion between the companies is forthcoming as well, including a Six Flags sweepstakes on Nintendo.com.
