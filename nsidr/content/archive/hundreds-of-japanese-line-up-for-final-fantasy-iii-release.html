--- title: "Hundreds of Japanese line up for Final Fantasy III release" date: 2006-08-24T02:48:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Final Fantasy mania has arrived in the land of the rising sun. For those unaware, Final Fantasy III was launched for the Nintendo DS this morning in Japan. It was accompanied by lines of 80-150 or more outside most game retailers.

The game was also sold in a Crystal White Nintendo DS Lite bundle. Famitsu reports that most stores sold out of the bundle within the first hour. Square Enix will release Final Fantasy III in North America on November 14.
