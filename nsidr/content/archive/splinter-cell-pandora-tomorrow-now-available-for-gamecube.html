--- title: "\"Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow\" now available for GameCube" date: 2004-07-07T03:24:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ubisoft's Splinter Cell Pandora Tomorrow is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. The GameCube version is said to contain an extra "Jungle Mission".

Splinter Cell Pandora Tomorrow currently has an 88 percent review rating on GameRankings.com.
