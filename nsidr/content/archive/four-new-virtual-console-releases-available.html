--- title: "Four new Virtual Console releases available" date: 2007-01-29T12:19:11Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- It's Monday morning, and if you've been following us at all, you know what that means—Nintendo has dropped some new titles to the Virtual Console, ready for you to blow some Wii Points on. Today's titles are as follows:

  • Mario Kart 64 (Nintendo 64, 1-4 players, 1,000 Wii Points)
  • Contra III: The Alien Wars (Super NES, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • Bonanza Bros. (Sega Genesis, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • Comix Zone (Sega Genesis, 1 player, 800 Wii Points)

These titles will go up at 9 a.m. PST (12 p.m. EST), so if any interest you, head on over then and give them a spin.
