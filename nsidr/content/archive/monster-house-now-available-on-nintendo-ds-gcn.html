--- title: "Monster House now available on Nintendo DS, GCN" date: 2006-07-18T21:23:43Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

THQ's Monster House is now available on Nintendo DS and Game Boy Advance for $29.99 and Nintendo GameCube for $39.99. Monster House is based on the Sony Pictures film of the same name starring animated character DJ and his two friends Chowder and Jenny.

Like the movie, the game follows the three of you as you're confronted with an evil house that gobbles up all who approach it. Each character is equipped with a snazzy water gun and has their own "unique actions and abilities" to combat this architectural foe.
