--- title: "Nintendogs wins awards at DICE" date: 2006-02-11T23:39:49Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo was awarded two Interactive Achievement Awards at the Academy of Interactive Arts & Sciences' D.I.C.E. conference. Nintendogs was named the hand-held game of the year and for achievement in game play engineering.

In a related note, the March issue of Electronic Gaming Monthly named Nintendo DS the 2005 System of the Year.
