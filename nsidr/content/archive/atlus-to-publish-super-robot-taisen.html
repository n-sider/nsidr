--- title: "Atlus to publish Super Robot Taisen" date: 2006-03-06T15:49:23Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Hot on the heels of announcing they'd publish Contact for the Nintendo DS, Atlus would also like to let everyone know that they're bringing strategy RPG Super Robot Taisen: Original Generation and Super Robot Taisen: Original Generation 2 to the Game Boy Advance in the summer and fall of 2006, respectively.

Atlus' releases mark the first time Taisen has been seen outside Japan since the franchise's inception over 15 years ago. The games bring customizable mechs to strategy RPGs boasting 40 hours of gameplay.
