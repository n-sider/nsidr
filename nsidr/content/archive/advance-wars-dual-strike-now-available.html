--- title: "Advance Wars: Dual Strike now available" date: 2005-08-22T21:39:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America said Advance Wars: Dual Strike is now available for the Nintendo DS. More information can be found at the game's official website.

Advance Wars: Dual Strike currently has a 91 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com (based on 11 reviews).
