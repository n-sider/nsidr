--- title: "Q1 2007 releases updated; Wii Play delayed" date: 2006-12-19T11:30:43Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America has updated their release schedule for the upcoming first quarter of 2007. The list is identical to its previous incarnation except for one key change: Wii Play has been moved from January 15 to February 12.

With that in mind, the updated release schedule is as follows:

Wii

  • WarioWare: Smooth Moves (January 15; see our preview)
  • Wii Play (February 12)
  • Mario Party 8 (March 5)

Nintendo DS

  • Hotel Dusk: Room 215 (January 22)
  • Diddy Kong Racing DS (February 5)
  • Wario: Master of Disguise (March 5)
  • Custom Robo Arena (March 19)

Game Boy Advance

  • Final Fantasy VI Advance (February 5)
