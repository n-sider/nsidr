--- title: "Nintendo lists third-party Q1 offerings" date: 2007-01-23T18:00:39Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Building on their previously-revealed Q1 2007 first-party release list, Nintendo of America today is additionally calling attention to third-party releases that are Wii-bound in the current quarter. These are:

  • Disney's Meet the Robinsons (Buena Vista, March 27)
  • Bust-A-Move Bash (Majesco, March)
  • Prince of Persia Rival Swords (Ubisoft, March)
  • TMNT (Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles) (Ubisoft, March)
  • Bionicle Heroes (Eidos, March)
  • Blazing Angels Squadrons of WWII (Ubisoft, March)
  • Tiger Woods PGA Tour 07 (EA, Q1)
  • SSX Blur (EA, Q1)
  • The Godfather: Blackhand Edition (EA, Q1)
  • Medal of Honor Vanguard (EA, Q1)
  • Sonic and the Secret Rings (Sega, Q1)

In addition, Nintendo also indicated that next week, they will be bringing their second Nintendo 64 release to the Virtual Console: Mario Kart 64. This Saturday, January 27, will also see the release of the Wii News Channel, backed by content from the Associated Press.

Still no solid word on what's going on with Wii in Q2 and beyond, but stay tuned. We'll let you know as soon as we do.
