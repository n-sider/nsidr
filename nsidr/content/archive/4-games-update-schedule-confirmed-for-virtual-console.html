--- title: "4 games, update schedule confirmed for Virtual Console" date: 2006-12-02T20:21:35Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- Via Nintendo finally comes official confirmation of the Virtual Console update schedule.


Starting December 4, every Monday will signal the addition of new classic titles to the Wii Shop channel. Virtual Console Monday kicks off this week with a handful of titles:

  • Donkey Kong Jr. (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)
  • Victory Run (TurboGrafx16, 1 player, 600 Wii Points)
  • Columns (Sega Genesis, 1-2 players, 800 Wii Points)
  • Ristar (Sega Genesis, 1 player, 800 Wii Points)

The updates will occur at 9 a.m. Pacific time, which works out to 10 a.m. Mountain time, 11 a.m. Central time, and noon Eastern time.
