--- title: "NPD July software sales preview" date: 2005-08-15T09:56:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- NPD has released the North American software sales for the month of July. Nintendo came in second, behind Electronic Arts, with a 10 percent marketshare in game sales. Here's the top five in both Nintendo GameCube and handheld sales.
    GameCube
  1. Fantastic Four - 37,741
  2. Mario Party 6 w/ Mic - 17,507
  3. Super Smash Bros. Melee - 28,967
  4. Madagascar - 21,937
  5. Killer 7 - 16,350
    Handheld
  1. Pokemon Emerald (GBA) - 131,537
  2. Midnight Club 3: Dub Edition (PSP) - 78,207
  3. Coded Arms (PSP) - 74,014
  4. Fantastic 4 (GBA) - 57,837
  5. Kirby: Canvas Curse (DS) - 49,058
