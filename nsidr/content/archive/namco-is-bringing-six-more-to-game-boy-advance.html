--- title: "Namco is bringing six more to Game Boy Advance" date: 2004-05-15T20:33:21Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Namco has the following Game Boy Advance titles in development:

  • Pac-Man World
  • Ms. Pac-Man Maze Madness
  • Smashing Drive
  • Dead to Rights
  • I-Ninja
  • kill.switch

Expect the titles to arrive throughout 2004 and into 2005.
