--- title: "Namco's GameCube-exclusive RPG \"Tales of Symphonia\" goes gold" date: 2004-06-21T17:08:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Namco Hometek said that its GameCube-exclusive RPG Tales of Symphonia has gone gold, which means development has been completed. Tales of Symphonia will be arriving in retailers on July 12, 2004. The title is said to contain a unique real-time battle system and feature more than 80 hours of game play.
