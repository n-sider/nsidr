--- title: "Three new releases for Virtual Console" date: 2007-03-19T17:03:12Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- It's a bit late in the day, sure, but we have three new releases for the Virtual Console that went live today at 9 a.m. Pacific. They are:

  • Excitebike (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Beyond Oasis (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)
  • Splatterhouse (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

If any of the above titles interest you, by all means head on over to your Wii Shop Channel and spend away.
