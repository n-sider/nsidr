--- title: "Nintendo UK launches WAP portal" date: 2005-12-05T10:48:16Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Virgin.net has the story that Nintendo UK has launched a WAP portal for official Nintendo information and videos. The portal is available at wap.nintendo.co.uk on mobile phone browsers.

Nintendo UK has also begun offering ringtones and wallpapers in exchange for "stars" earned since December 5 via their VIP 24:7 club.
