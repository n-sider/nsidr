--- title: "Meteos meets Mickey Mouse" date: 2006-04-26T18:07:53Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Buena Vista Games is saying today that they intend to publish touch-screen puzzler Meteos with a twist: as Meteos: Disney Edition.

This isn't just for Japan, either — in fact, a publisher has not yet been lined up for Asian territories — Buena Vista intends to publish the game in all territories. No word on whether the gameplay is actually changed: all Buena Vista is saying is that it will feature Disney characters.

Meteos: Disney Edition is currently headed for stores in fall of this year.
