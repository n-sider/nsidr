--- title: "Keep an eye on Reggie at DICE" date: 2006-01-27T20:46:49Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The latest issue of Nintendo Power reminds readers to keep an eye on Ninendo.com on February 9. Reggie Fils-Aime, Nintendo of America's executive vice president of marketing, will be giving a speech about "Expanding the Market for Interactive Entertainment" at the DICE Summit in Henderson, Nevada. Reggie's speech will be 45 minutes long, starting at 10:30AM and ending at 11:15AM. In the speech, it's likely Reggie will be highlighting Nintendo's strategy for its Nintendo DS, Wi-Fi Connection, and Nintendo Revolution.

Anyone up for a Reggie Fiesta in the N-Forums chat? We'll be sure to throw out a reminder as the date approaches.
