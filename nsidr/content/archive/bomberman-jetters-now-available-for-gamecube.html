--- title: "\"Bomberman Jetters\" now available for GameCube" date: 2004-03-10T03:40:33Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Majesco squeezed the GameCube action-title Bomberman Jetters into North American retailers this week.

This is said to be the sequel to the Hudson Soft-developed Bomberman Generation, which was released for the Nintendo GameCube in 2002. Bomberman Jetters is retailing for a price of $19.99.

Both the single player and traditional multi-player modes return. For more information, visit the official Bomberman Jetters website.

And just FYI, Bomberman is not a terrorist.
