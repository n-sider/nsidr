--- title: "Sakurai forms new company" date: 2005-09-26T22:11:18Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Gamasutra is reporting that Masahiro Sakurai, creator of Kirby's Dream Land, Super Smash Bros., and most recently, Meteos (with Q Entertainment) is forming his own company, called Sora. The new venture is working on two projects, but no information is available on them as of yet.
