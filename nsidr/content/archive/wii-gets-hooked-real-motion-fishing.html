--- title: "Wii gets Hooked! Real Motion Fishing" date: 2007-06-26T09:57:41Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Bass Fishing Wii, recently announced for Japan, now has an American release as well, thanks to Aksys Games. The official English title is Hooked! Real Motion Fishing.

The announcement brings with it some clarification on the game's features. There are three self-explanatory single-player game modes: Practice, Time Attack, and Tournament. Unfortunately, details abotut he online mode are still scarce. The controls are simple; use the remote to pull the fishing pole in different directions while reeling the nunchuk.

Hooked! Real Motion Fishing will hit North American stores this fall.
