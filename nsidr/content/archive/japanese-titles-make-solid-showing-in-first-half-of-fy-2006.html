--- title: "Japanese titles make solid showing in first half of FY 2006" date: 2005-10-14T10:34:56Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

GameSpot is reporting on DS game sales in the first half of FY 2006, by way of Famitsu. Although PlayStation 2 claimed the top five spots exclusively for itself as well as the top spot, four Nintendo DS titles and one Game Boy Advance title split the top ten fifty-fifty.

Nintendogs took the second-place spot, selling 697,940 copies, followed by Nou o Kitaeru Otona no DS Training at 615,074 copies. Yawaraka Atama-Juku and Jump Super Stars also made showings in the lower five spots.
