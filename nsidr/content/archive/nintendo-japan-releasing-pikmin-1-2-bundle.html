--- title: "Nintendo Japan releasing \"Pikmin 1 + 2\" bundle" date: 2004-07-10T18:31:06Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan will release a box set of Pikmin 1, Pikmin 2, and several collectible Pikmin figurines. The bundle is scheduled for release on July 23 for 8,900 Yen. There is currently no word on a North American release.
