--- title: "Tokyo Game Show NDS and GBA games" date: 2005-08-11T00:26:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The Tokyo Game Show will take place at the Makuhari Convention Center in Tokyo, Japan's Chiba prefecture from September 16 to 18. Over 130 exhibitors will be showcasing games and product. Although Nintendo will not be attending the event, several third-party publishers will have Game Boy Advance and Nintendo DS titles on display. These include:
    Atlus Co.
  • GBA - Kuniokun Nekketsu Collection 1
  • GBA - Miracle! Panzou
    Climax
  • NDS - Action RPG (working title)
    Koei
  • NDS - Romance Of The Three Kingdoms DS
  • NDS - Dynasty Warriors DS
    Konami
  • GBA - Boktai3
  • NDS - Castlevania: Dawn Of Sorrow
  • NDS - Azure Dreams
  • GBA - Animal Yokocho Doki Doki Kyushutsu Daisakusen! Nomaki
  • NDS - Croket! Ds Tenku No Yushatachi
  • NDS - Iron Feather
    Square Enix
