--- title: "Steve Singer now head of licensing for NOA" date: 2007-01-16T12:08:37Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America has announced a little rejiggering in its corporate org charts this weekend, moving Steve Singer, previously VP and general manager of NOA's Latin America territory, to the position of VP of licensing for the entirety of the NOA organization.

Steve replaces outgoing 22-year veteran Juana Tingdale, who is retiring March 5. Tingdale was responsible for managing relationships with publishers.

Replacing Steve, taking over as director and GM of the Latin America territory, is Melissa Wright. Wright has worked with Singer since 1999 developing the Latin America business within NOA.
