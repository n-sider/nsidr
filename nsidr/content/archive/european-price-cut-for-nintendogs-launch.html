--- title: "European price cut for Nintendogs launch" date: 2005-09-23T10:18:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo Europe announced today that it would follow Nintendo of America and Nintendo Australia in dropping the price of the Nintendo DS console. The new suggested retail price is GBP 89.99 and will take effect October 7, the same day Nintendogs is scheduled to release in European territories.

Nintendo Europe will also offer on that day Nintendo DS consoles bundled with Nintendogs for GBP 99.99. The Dachshund edition of the game will be bundled with a pink DS console, while the Labrador edition will be bundled with a blue DS console.
