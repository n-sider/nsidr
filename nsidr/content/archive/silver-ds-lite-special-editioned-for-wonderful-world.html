--- title: "Silver DS Lite special editioned for Wonderful World" date: 2007-05-31T16:08:24Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- It's not even on shelves yet, but plans are already afoot to slap the recently-announced Gloss Silver DS Lite into a special edition bundle, alongside Square-Enix's upcoming It's a Wonderful World.

Picture from Famitsu.com

The bundle lands in stores the same day Wonderful World does: July 27. As with all things "wonderful", this bundle is only available in Japan. (Thanks to N-Sider Forums' Samer for the tip.)
