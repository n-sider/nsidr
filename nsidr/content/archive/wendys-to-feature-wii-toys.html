--- title: "Wendy's to feature \"Wii Toys\"" date: 2007-05-14T18:51:30Z contributors: - Dean Bergmann ---

Starting this week, Wendy's International (all Wendy's restaurants in North America) will be featuring "Wii Toys" with their Kid's Meals.

With every Kid's Meal purchase, you'll be able to choose from one of five toys:

  • A Wii disc shooter
  • A Two Sided puzzle
  • A Miniature Excite Truck
  • A Miniature Wii-Baseball game
  • A Mario on Shell w/ Wii Remote Toy

So get to Wendy's and in the spirit of Nintendo: Catch 'em All.
