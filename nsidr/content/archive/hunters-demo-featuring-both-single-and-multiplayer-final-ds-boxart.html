--- title: "Hunters demo featuring both single and multiplayer; final DS boxart?" date: 2004-09-29T19:07:02Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has confirmed that the Nintendo DS will release with a playable demo of Metroid Prime: Hunters containing several single-player "modes" and a multiplayer "mode". The number of levels were not specified. The demo will come on a seperate Nintendo DS Game Card.

This comes in addition to the previously announced built-in instant-messaging PictoChat feature. Nintendo DS is scheduled to release November 21 in North America for $149.99. The final boxart is below.
