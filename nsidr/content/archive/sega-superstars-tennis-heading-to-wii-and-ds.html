--- title: "Sega Superstars Tennis heading to Wii and DS" date: 2007-10-23T16:23:56Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Sega Superstars Tennis was announced for a multitude of systems last week, including both the Nintendo DS and Wii.

  • Over 15 playable Sega characters, including Sonic the Hedgehog, AiAi from Super Monkey Ball, Ulala from Space Channel 5, and Amigo from Samba de Amigo
  • Both singles and doubles modes
  • Exhibition mode where players can challenge the character of their choice on one of many courts, including Sonic's Green Hill Zone and Amigo's Carnival Park
  • Tournament mode where players prove their merit by collecting silverware and unlocking a host of extras
