--- title: "Codemasters readies Heatseeker for March 2007" date: 2006-10-05T01:11:50Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Publisher Codemasters revealed a new Wii title in development called Heatseeker in an interview with IGN.com. The game is scheduled for a March 2007 release.

Heatseeker is described as a flight action game that features over 30 playable fighter jets. Take a look at more details and the first screens of the game here.
