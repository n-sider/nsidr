--- title: "Nintendo forecast up 27%, stock up 1.9%" date: 2006-04-04T09:40:18Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

The Mainichi Daily News reports this morning that Nintendo has raised its net profit forecast by almost 27% for the fiscal year ending in March, following brisk sales of the Nintendo DS and foreign-exchange gains on a weaker yen.

The news drove Nintendo stocks up 330 points, or about 1.9%, on the Osaka exchange. The gains come as the Nikkei went flat on profit-taking and bargain-hunting.
