--- title: "Advance Wars coming to DS on September 30" date: 2005-07-12T01:56:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo Europe said the turn-based strategy game Advance Wars: Dual Strike for the Nintendo DS will arrive on September 30. Players will take control of the Allied Armies of Orange Star, Blue Moon, Yellow Comet and Green Earth to defeat the Black Hole Army. The title makes use of the Nintendo DS touch screen to allow players to make movements with ease. Advance Wars: Dual Strike offers five different single player game modes. There is also a wireless function that allows up to eight players to play head to head at any one time.
