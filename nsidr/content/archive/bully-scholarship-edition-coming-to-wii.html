--- title: "Bully: Scholarship Edition coming to Wii" date: 2007-07-19T22:53:21Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Rockstar Games revealed it will release Bully: Scholarship Edition on Wii and Xbox 360 this holiday. The game is an enhanced port of the PlayStation 2 version—it will contain additional unspecified new content.

Bully: Scholarship Edition is set in a New England boarding school where 15-year-old Jimmy Hopkins must experience the tumultuous reality of growing up and doling out wedgies. More info can be found at the game's official website.
