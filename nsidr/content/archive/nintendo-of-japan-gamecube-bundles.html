--- title: "Nintendo of Japan GameCube bundles" date: 2004-06-10T23:17:46Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of Japan is prepping two bundled software and hardware packages for the Japanese market.

The first package, scheduled for release July 1 and retailing at 7,000 yen (~$64), will consist of the Donkey Konga titles. More specifically, it will include Donkey Konga, Donkey Konga 2: Hit Song Parade and the conga drum controller.

The second package is more hardware oriented and will retail for 19,800 yen. Dubbed the "Enjoy Plus Pack", it will consist of the GameCube console, a standard controller, a Limited Edition Clear Color controller, the Game Boy Player, and a Memory Card 251.

Also of note, the official Japanese website for Super Donkey Kong 2 has been opened.
