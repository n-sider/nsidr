--- title: "Four titles drop to European Virtual Console" date: 2007-08-03T13:11:36Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- This week's Virtual Console update for Europe features four new titles, almost all originally released on the Mega Drive. They are:

  • Shinobi III (Mega Drive, 800 Wii Points)
  • Dynamite Headdy (Mega Drive, 800 Wii Points)
  • Shining Force (Mega Drive, 800 Wii Points)
  • Drop Off (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

Stay tuned next Friday for the next European batch.
