--- title: "Zoonmai hints at \"Game Zero\"?" date: 2004-07-30T01:08:14Z contributors: - Kenneth Kyle Wade ---

UK-development studio Zoonami has posted a new image their website teasing gamers of a site relaunch very soon.

What is of most interested to viewers about this image is the prospect that this maybe our first inside look at Zoonamis secret project formerly codenamed Game Zero.

Very little is known about Game Zero; however, former Rare employee Martin Hollis is the founder of Zoonami and worked on such hit titles as GoldenEye 007 and Perfect Dark, which is reason enough to sit-up and pay attention to this game.
