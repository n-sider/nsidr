--- title: "Using \"thought\" alone to play video games?" date: 2004-06-15T20:12:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Four volunteers had electrodes attached to the surface of their brains in an experiment that showed they were able to play a simple video game using thought alone, according to a report by Reuters. Merely by thinking the word "move", the volunteers were able to play the video game. For more details on the experiment, check out the full article here.
