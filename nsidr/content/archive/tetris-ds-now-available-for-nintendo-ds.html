--- title: "Tetris DS now available for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-03-20T22:57:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Tetris DS is now available for the Nintendo DS at a retail price of $34.99. The title comes to us via Nintendo and Blue Planet Software. More info can be found at Nintendo's official website.

Tetris DS features six modes of play based on classic Nintendo games such as Super Mario Bros., The Legend of Zelda, and Metroid. The title is compatible for two-to-four players over Nintendo's Wi-Fi Connection service. Meanwhile, a local wireless link-up mode allows for up to ten player battles. Tetris DS currently has a 90 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 6 reviews).
