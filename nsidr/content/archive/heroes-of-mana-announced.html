--- title: "Heroes of Mana announced" date: 2006-09-13T11:32:35Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Via Cubed³: the latest Famitsu Weekly carries word that Heroes of Mana is planned to be released for the Nintendo DS by Square-Enix.

Working on the title are Mastato Kato of Chrono Trigger and Seiken Densetsu 4 fame, providing scenario and plot; Yoko Shimumura (Mana, Parasite Eve) providing music; Ryoma Ito (Final Fantasy Tactics Advance) and Akira Oguo (Final Fantasy X) doing character design; and Koichi Ishii producing. The title will include RTS and RPG elements.
