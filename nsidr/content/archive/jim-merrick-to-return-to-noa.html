--- title: "Jim Merrick to return to NOA" date: 2005-12-06T11:54:39Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

GamesIndustry.biz is reporting on NOE's announcement Jim Merrick is returning to NOA.

Three years ago, Merrick left NOA for NOE on a short-term contract. Merrick has been senior marketing director at NOE since, and has been overseeing the launch of the Nintendo DS and Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection in Europe.

NOE plans to announce Merrick's successor "in the coming weeks".
