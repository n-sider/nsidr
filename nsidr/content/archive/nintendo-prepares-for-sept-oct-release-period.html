--- title: "Nintendo prepares for Sept. - Oct. release period" date: 2005-07-14T19:42:25Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo will hold a "Nintendo Dealer Fair" in Osaka, Japan, on July 27 through 28 to exhibit new game titles for the September to October release period, according to The-MagicBox.com. It is expected Nintendo will reveal its release intentions for the Nintendo GameCube, Game Boy Advance as well as Nintendo DS. It is also possible Nintendo will announce Japanese pricing and release information for the Game Boy micro.
