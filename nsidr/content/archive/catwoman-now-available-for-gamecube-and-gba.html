--- title: "\"Catwoman\" now available for GameCube and GBA" date: 2004-07-23T17:44:49Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts has released Catwoman, based on the Warner Bros. film, for the Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance. The title will retail in North America for $39.95 on GameCube and $29.95 on the Game Boy Advance.

Screens and other information can be found at the game's official website. The title currently has a 52 percent average review rating at GameRankings.com.
