--- title: "\"Tens of thousands\" of McDonald's connections" date: 2006-01-05T11:01:57Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Wayport, Inc., provider of Wi-Fi to Big Mac types everywhere and now partner with Nintendo providing free Wi-Fi Connection access to Nintendo DS owners gracing the Golden Arches, announced today that "tens of thousands" of gaming connections were offered through its network since the Wi-Fi Connection launched November 14.

Wayport has experienced record-breaking paid customer connections this year: 10 million worldwide across 12,000 locations — putting the WFC connections at some single-digit percentage of all Wayport connections. Of those locations, 6,300 are McDonald's restaurants with WFC access inside; the rest are hotels and other locations inside and outside the US.

Also recall that WFC has seen 10 million unique connections worldwide since its mid-November launch with Mario Kart DS; "tens of thousands" begins to look like a very small number indeed.
