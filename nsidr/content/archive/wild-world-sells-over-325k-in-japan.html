--- title: "Wild World sells over 325k in Japan" date: 2005-12-01T23:26:34Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

The latest Media Create charts show the Japanese version of Animal Crossing: Wild World (which we are currently covering in our unique live coverage, N-Sider Goes Wild) has sold 325,466 copies in Japan, making it the top-selling software title for the week of November 21-27.

During the same period, the Nintendo DS sold 130,012 units. Nintendo of America will be releasing the game stateside on December 5th.
