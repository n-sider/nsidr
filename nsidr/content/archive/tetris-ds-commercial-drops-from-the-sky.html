--- title: "Tetris DS commercial drops from the sky" date: 2006-03-15T02:24:27Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Have you been watching television lately? Chances are you've caught the latest commercial advertising the Wi-Fi compatible Tetris DS. This highly-anticipated Nintendo DS title arrives in North America on March 20. The commercial does a decent job getting that message across. We watch as two planes unload their cargo of colored-parachuters. Like-colored parachuters join together in a creative homage to Tetris. Enough of the descriptions -- see it for yourself courtesy of YouTube and AdvancedMN.com.

Also take a look at the Metroid Prime Hunters advert if you haven't seen it already. Metroid is also coming to Nintendo DS on March 20.
