--- title: "Bright and colorful" date: 2006-02-20T11:45:02Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

We all knew the DS Lite was brighter than its ancestor, but did you know that — unlike the unfortunately washed-out picture you got playing Game Boy Advance games on a Game Boy Player or on a classic DS — the DS Lite has wonderful color too? Don't believe me? Check out these pictures from Jeux France.

We've heard from many folks that these are not empty shells with stickers on them: this is, in fact, actually what we can expect from the Lite's screen. And that should please folks who are particularly picky about their screen quality.
