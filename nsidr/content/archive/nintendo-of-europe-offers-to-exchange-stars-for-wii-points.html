--- title: "Nintendo of Europe offers to exchange Stars for Wii Points" date: 2007-12-10T10:53:08Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of Europe has unveiled a new purchasable in their "Stars" customer-loyalty program, giving their best customers the ability to buy Wii Points at an exchange rate of 4 Star Points to 1 Wii Point. At 250 points per game, one N64-era Virtual Console title can be earned with the points from 16 modern titles.

Wii Points are part of the new online Stars Catalog, in turn a part of the new European Nintendo site. UK members can swap their Wii Points at wiipointscard.nintendo.co.uk.
