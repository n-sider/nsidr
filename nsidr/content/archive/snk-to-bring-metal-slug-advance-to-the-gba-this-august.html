--- title: "SNK to bring \"Metal Slug Advance\" to the GBA this August" date: 2004-04-30T01:34:43Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

SNK said it will release Metal Slug Advance for the Game Boy Advance in August.

The game is said to feature five diverse locales and four mini-game sections. Alongside Metal Slug Advance, SNK will also release Metal Slug e-Cards which will enhance player's abilities and assist them in the completion of their missions.
