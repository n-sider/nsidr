--- title: "Pardon our hiatus" date: 2008-08-11T17:42:18Z contributors: - Cory Faller --- If you'd told me a month ago that E3 would end and there'd be absolutely nothing worth writing about, I'd have called you a liar.

But here we are.

Rest assured: you haven't actually missed out on any breaking news, as the typical post-E3 information lull has been especially deafening for Nintendo fans this year.

Give us another week or two, and we'll be back on our feet. We've got a few interesting pieces in production, and I'll be rolling out some of the new content indexes I promised back during the show.

Until then, why not poke around in some of our more popular forum threads, including rants, holograms, and lists that will totally never reach 2000.
