--- title: "Nintendo: Release announcement to come in September" date: 2006-07-07T13:24:59Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Addressing the rampant and plentiful speculation about its release plans for its upcoming Wii home console, Nintendo said through spokesman Yasuhiro Minagawa that its plans remained unchanged to release the console in the fourth quarter of 2006. Separately, an unnamed spokesperson speaking to Bloomberg said announcements would come in September of this year.
