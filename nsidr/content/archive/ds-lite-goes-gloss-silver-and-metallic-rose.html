--- title: "DS Lite goes Gloss Silver and Metallic Rose" date: 2007-05-30T11:41:32Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Earlier today, Nintendo held a conference for Japanese retailers, revealing two more colors for the Nintendo DS Lite: Metallic Rose and Gloss Silver.

Metallic Rose

Gloss Silver

Both colors will hit stores in Japan on June 23. There is no word yet on if the colors will see the light of day outside of the land of the rising sun.
