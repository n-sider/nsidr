--- title: "Square's DS plans?" date: 2004-07-30T01:09:01Z contributors: - Kenneth Kyle Wade ---

Square is asking buyers of Final Fantasy I & II for the Game Boy Advance, which previously released Square games they would like to see on the Nintendo DS.

Among the list of titles Square has listed are: Final Fantasy III, Final Fantasy IV, Final Fantasy V, Final Fantasy VI, the Seiken Densetsu (Secret of Mana) series, the Saga series, Chrono Trigger, Hanjuku Heroes and Chocobo.

Currently, the only known Square-Enix title confirmed for the Nintendo DS is a sequel to Final Fantasy: Crystal Chronicles for the Nintendo GameCube.

Source: GCA
