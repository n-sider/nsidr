--- title: "Electronic Arts reveals its intentions of supporting the Nintendo DS launch" date: 2004-04-30T13:26:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts said during its "Fourth Quarter Fiscal 2004 Results Conference Call" on Thursday, that it would support the Nintendo DS launch with several titles. EA also said it would support the Sony PSP however no specific titles were announced.

During the conference, Electronic Arts also revealed the official names to the GameCube sequels to Def Jam Vendetta and Timesplitters 2 would be named respectively Def Jam Fight For New York and TimeSplitters: Future Perfect. Both titles are scheduled to release this Fall.
