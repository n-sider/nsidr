--- title: "Namco announces Dig Dug" date: 2005-08-17T23:54:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Namco Hometek said it will release Dig Dug Digging Strike for the Nintendo DS fall 2005. The game is described as a 3D adventure that combines the classic arcade digging action with new baddies, environments, weapons, mini-games and strategies.
