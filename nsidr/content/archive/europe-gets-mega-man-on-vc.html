--- title: "Europe gets Mega Man on VC" date: 2007-06-22T13:13:52Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Two new games have found their way onto the European Virtual Console, including the original debut of everyone's favorite Blue Bomber.

Europe's new games are as follows:

  • Mega Man (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • China Warrior (TurboGrafx-16, 600 Wii Points)

These two games bring the total number of VC titles in Europe up to 103. North America will, as always, see its new releases on Monday.
