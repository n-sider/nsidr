--- title: "Atlus week for the Game Boy Advance" date: 2006-07-26T22:41:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Among the titles of import releasing this week are two published by Atlus U.S.A.

First off is Battle B-Daman, based on the anime and manga of the same name. The game follows Yamato Delgado, aspiring B-Daman player, as he fights in B-Daman tournaments trying to defeat the evil B-Daplayers of the Shadow Alliance with his new toy, the Cobalt Blade. The title features a dozen battlefields, 18 characters, three modes of play and a two-player simultaneous mode. GameRankings has it at 65% on 10 reviews.

Summon Night: Swordcraft Story is an adventure game featuring the RPG-standard top-down view and a Tales-like real-time combat system. Oddly enough, this game also at least starts off with a tournament, but hints at a deeper story. Link mode for battles and item trades with friends is included. Swordcraft Story has 75% on 10 reviews at GameRankings.

Both titles released Monday and should be available now.
