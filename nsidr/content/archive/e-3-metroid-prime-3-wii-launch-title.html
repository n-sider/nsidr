--- title: "[E3] Metroid Prime 3 Wii launch title" date: 2006-05-11T00:20:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGN.com learned from an interview with Retro Studios that Metroid Prime 3: Corruption will debut alongside The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess for the Nintendo Wii launch later this year. Retro Studios also confirmed that Metroid Prime 3 will feature large areas to explore on multiple worlds.
