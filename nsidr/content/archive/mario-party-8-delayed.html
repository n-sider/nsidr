--- title: "Mario Party 8 delayed" date: 2007-01-23T19:55:54Z contributors: - Dean Bergmann --- GAF member Kobun Heat points out that Nintendo has silently updated their press site indicating a delay of Mario Party 8 towards a vague "Q2" release.


Originally slated for March, Mario Party 8 will now likely end up releasing closer to summer, or even the second half of 2007, joining the ranks of Metroid Prime 3, Super Mario Galaxy, and Super Smash Brothers Brawl.

Reasons for the delay are unclear, but many hope it is a result of possible WiiConnect or WiFi features.
