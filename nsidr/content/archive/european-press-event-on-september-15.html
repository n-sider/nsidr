--- title: "European press event on September 15" date: 2006-08-30T11:18:01Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Hot on the heels of invitations to a New York press event to be held September 14, where NOA intends to hold a media hands-on session with DS and Wii, comes news that a similar press event will be held in Europe on September 15.

It looks like the pieces of the puzzle may be falling into place for Nintendo's much-anticipated announcements of Wii release information—though, of course, nothing to that end has been confirmed to be happening at these events. All we know is that Nintendo has committed to announcing this information in September. Time will tell. Stay tuned.
