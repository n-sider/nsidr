--- title: "Xenosaga and Quiz games coming from Namco" date: 2005-08-31T23:29:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Namco announced a new Nintendo DS title and re-confirmed another in the latest Famitsu magazine, according to The-MagicBox.com. The new title is called Kono Quiz Yaroo and is a quiz game. The other is Xenosaga DS, a familiar RPG from the Xenosaga series.
