--- title: "Developer of \"Advance Wars Under Fire\" also working on a racing title for GCN" date: 2004-05-26T22:22:35Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Kuju, developer of Nintendo's Advance Wars: Under Fire for GameCube are working on a second GameCube title under publisher Ignition Entertainment called Lotus Extreme.

Lotus Extreme is described as a racing game and is said to feature 38 cars and five modes of play. For more information, visit the official website.
