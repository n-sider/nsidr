--- title: "Nintendo to anchor E for All expo" date: 2007-03-29T14:58:41Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- On October 18th through the 21st, the Los Angeles Convention Center will host IDG's E for All expo—and Nintendo has announced today they will be an anchor exhibitor at the show.

E for All was created this year as a consumer-focussed exposition for "people who are passionate about video games and everything related". It will feature hands-on trials of new products before they are available for purchase, and is intended to boost the holiday shopping season.
