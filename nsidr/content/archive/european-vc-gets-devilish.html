--- title: "European VC gets devilish" date: 2007-07-27T13:27:39Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- It's Friday again, which means the European Virtual Console gets another update. This week, Wii owners get to play pinball with devils and challenge friends in Kirby's action puzzler.

  • Kirby's Ghost Trap (SNES, 800 points)
  • Devil's Crush (TurboGrafx16, 600 points)

These two games bring the total number of European VC releases up to 116.
