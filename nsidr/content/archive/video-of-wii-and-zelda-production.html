--- title: "Video of Wii and Zelda production" date: 2006-11-09T10:53:46Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo has released a glimpse into the production of the Wii console and The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess game. Perrin Kaplan, vice president of marketing and corporate affairs, comments on the upcoming launch.


Nintendo will ship over 4 million Wii consoles to stores worldwide by the end of this year. Wii arrives in North America on November 19.
