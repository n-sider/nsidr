--- title: "Mage Knight Destiny's Soldier now available" date: 2006-10-05T22:44:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---
Mage Knight Destiny's Soldier is now available on Nintendo DS for $29.99. The title arrives via publisher Namco Bandai and developer Big Blue Bubble.

The story of Mage Knight Destiny's Soldier takes place in a land that is consumed by war. The strain on the world's magical energy is literally tearing the Land apart. It's up to you to save the world with your customized army. The game currently has a 56 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 2 reviews).

GameSpot says (5.6/10),
"The game itself can indeed be engaging and challenging if you're willing to spend a couple of hours getting your bearings and can look past some serious problems. Since there's a well-thought-out game design underneath the interface, it's certainly possible to get into Mage Knight DS and spend some good, quality time thinking about the best army to bring into each battle and balancing the various trade-offs and risks of doing battle. But unless you're patient, forgiving, and looking for a complicated strategy game, you'd best stay away from this one."

N-Forum wcwislife says (9/10),
"Mage Knight Destinys Soldier for the Nintendo DS nearly flawlessly takes the engine made popular by its WizKids board game brother and integrates a solid RPG esque storyline. The game boasts intense strategy with a shade of luck and a cast of known fantasy characters (all of which can be acquired for your team during your campaign). Mage Knight is poised to be a major sleeper hit that should appeal to fans of Gladius, Mage Knight (Board game) or the smash hit Hero Clix."
