--- title: "A million in the UK" date: 2006-01-20T17:19:11Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

By way of GamesIndustry.biz comes news that the DS has sold a million units in the United Kingdom since its launch earlier this year.

The report states Nintendo's saying several UK retailers are out of stock of the device, driven by demand for titles like Nintendogs, which has sold 1.6 million copies across Europe since its release in October. Of course, this fanned the redesign rumor flames somewhat, leading to a denial from NOE this week.
