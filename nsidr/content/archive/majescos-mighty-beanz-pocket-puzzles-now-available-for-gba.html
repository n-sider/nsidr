--- title: "Majesco's \"Mighty Beanz Pocket Puzzles\" now available for GBA" date: 2004-06-09T16:00:25Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Majesco said it has released its Game Boy Advance puzzle game Mighty Beanz Pocket Puzzles to North American retailers nationwide. The title features an MSRP of $19.99.

More information including screens and features, can be found at its official website.
