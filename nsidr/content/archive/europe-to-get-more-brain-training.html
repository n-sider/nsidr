--- title: "Europe to get More Brain Training" date: 2007-05-16T12:21:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of Europe announced this morning that they will be publishing More Brain Training from Dr Kawashima: How Old Is Your Brain?

The sequel to Brain Age, or as it is known in Europe, Dr Kawashima's Brain Training: How Old Is Your Brain?, will feature brand new exercises including Rock, Paper, Scissors; Memorising 5x5; Speedy Symbols; Correct Change; Missing Symbols; and Masterpiece Recital. 2-16 players via DS Download Play is available for Word Scramble, Memorise 5x5 and Correct Change. Sudoku returns with a hundred new puzzles as well.

More Brain Training will hit European shelves June 29. No word yet on a North American release.
