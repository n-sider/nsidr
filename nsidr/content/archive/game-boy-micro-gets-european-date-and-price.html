--- title: "Game Boy Micro gets European date and price" date: 2005-08-17T10:21:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo Europe said it will release the new Game Boy Micro on November 4 for GBP 69, according to GamesIndustry.biz. The unit will launch in four colors -- silver, blue, pink and green -- with more face-plates likely to be made available in the following months.
