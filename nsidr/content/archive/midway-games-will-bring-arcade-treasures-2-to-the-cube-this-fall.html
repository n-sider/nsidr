--- title: "Midway Games will bring \"Arcade Treasures 2\" to the 'Cube this Fall" date: 2004-04-26T17:28:40Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Midway Games said it will bring Midway Arcade Treasures 2, a compilation of classic Midway arcade games, to the Nintendo GameCube during Fall of 2004. The game will sell for a suggested retail price of $19.99 and contain the following games:

  • A.P.B.
  • Arch Rivals
  • Championship Sprint
  • Cyberball 2072
  • Gauntlet 2
  • Hard Drivin'
  • Mortal Kombat
  • Mortal Kombat II
  • Mortal Kombat III
  • NARC
  • Pit Fighter
  • Primal Rage
  • Rampage World Tour
  • Spy Hunter 2
  • Steel Talons
  • STUN Runner
  • Timber
  • Total Carnage
  • Wizard of Wor
  • Xenophobe
  • Xybots
