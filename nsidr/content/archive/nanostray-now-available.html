--- title: "Nanostray now available" date: 2005-07-19T21:35:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Majesco said it has released Nanostray for the Nintendo DS. Nanostray is a 3D shooter developed by Shinen, creators of the Game Boy Advance Iridion series. The game features touch screen support, local area network play, and several gameplay modes including "adventure", "arcade", "challenge" and "multiplayer". Nanostray is available for a suggested retail price of $29.99. More information can be found at the game's official website.
