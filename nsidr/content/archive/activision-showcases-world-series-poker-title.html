--- title: "Activision showcases World Series Poker title" date: 2005-07-07T20:04:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Activision said it will showcase a game based on the World Series of Poker Tournament at this year's Lifestyle Show in Las Vegas on July 6 - 10. Activision's World Series of Poker video game will be featured in television spots on ESPN during the tournament. The game is being developed for the Nintendo GameCube and will be available in stores later this summer.
