--- title: "E3 2004 - the biggest yet" date: 2004-05-15T01:06:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to the Entertainment Software Association, the 2004 Electronic Entertainment Expo hosted more than 400 exhibitors in 540,000 net square feet of exhibit space - the most ever. Sixty-five thousand industry professionals from a record 87 countries around the globe gathered at the Los Angeles Convention Center.

E3Expo 2005 is scheduled for May 18-20, 2005 in Los Angeles.
