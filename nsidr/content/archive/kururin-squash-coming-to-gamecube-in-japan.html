--- title: "\"Kururin Squash\" coming to GameCube in Japan" date: 2004-07-22T18:15:02Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan said it will bring Kururin Squash to the GameCube later this year. The game will be co-developed with Eighting and is said to be based on the similarly titled Kuru Kuru Kururin that was released for the Game Boy Advance.
