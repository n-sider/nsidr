--- title: "Midway confirms Nintendo Revolution support" date: 2006-03-01T23:26:41Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- GameSpot.com has learned that Midway is developing software for Nintendo's next-generation console codenamed Revolution . "We will have a Revolution title this year," Midway CEO, David Zucker, told GameSpot.
