--- title: "Free Radical needs more power than Wii has" date: 2006-07-31T10:10:53Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Free Radical Design, the studio responsible for TimeSplitters and sequels as well as Second Sight, has revealed in their FAQ that upcoming title Haze won't be appearing on Nintendo's Wii home console. Their explanation is as follows:

There are currently no plans for a Wii version of Haze. Sadly, the cutting-edge technology we're using requires more power than the Wii has available. If we could, we would.

Free Radical was formed several years ago by a group of programmers who had just completed work on GoldenEye 007 at then-Nintendo-owned studio Rare. Their first release, the aforementioned TimeSplitters, was strongly reminiscent of GoldenEye.
