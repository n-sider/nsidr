--- title: "Nintendo Fusion Tour comes back this year for another round" date: 2004-08-13T14:00:54Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America has announced the return of the "Nintendo Fusion Tour". The tour combines music and video games into a single event. It will be hitting 37 cities across the United States and begins September 18 in Phoenix. Bands headlining the tour include Story of The Year with supporting acts lostprophets, My Chemical Romance, Letter Kills, Anberlin and Autopilot Off.

Tickets for most shows will go on sale August 14 at www.nintendofusiontour.com.

The Nintendo DS will make its first playable public showing at each stop on the tour. In addition the latest and upcoming GameCube titles will be available to play.
