--- title: "Screens of Sega's Triforce title \"Virtua Striker 4\"" date: 2004-07-05T02:44:18Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sega has released screens of its Triforce arcade soccer game Virtua Striker 4. The game will play using the Namco-Sega-Nintendo developed Triforce arcade board - which allows easy conversions from arcade to the Nintendo GameCube console. There's currently no word however on whether this title will also make it to the GameCube. Check out the screens here, courtesy of The-MagicBox.
