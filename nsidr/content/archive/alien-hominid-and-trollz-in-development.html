--- title: "Alien Hominid and Trollz in development" date: 2005-07-05T20:40:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Zoo Digital has revealed that Alien Hominid is coming to the Game Boy Advance. The title is currently scheduled for release this November. More information can be found at the official website.

In other 'coming soon' news is Ubisoft's Trollz: Hair Affair for the Game Boy Advance. The title is scheduled for release fall 2005. The title targets the 6-12 year-old girl demographic by featuring the Trollz characters through minigames, dress-up and dancing.
