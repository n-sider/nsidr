--- title: "Kuju Entertainment moving to a larger home" date: 2004-07-13T01:50:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Kuju Entertainment, developers of the Nintendo GameCube title Advance Wars: Under Fire, announced its plans to move its studios to new locations - ones that will provide more space for the company's growing development teams.

Mark Washbrook, head of London's Kuju Studio said, "With the development of both Advance Wars: Under Fire for Nintendo and our PS2/Xbox game for Konami being produced here these new offices meet all our needs for future growth."

Kuju has headquarters in Brighton and London, England. The company said it will be relocating to new buildings in those two areas.
