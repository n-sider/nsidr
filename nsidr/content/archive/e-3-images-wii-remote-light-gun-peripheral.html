--- title: "[E3] Images: Wii remote Light Gun peripheral" date: 2006-05-10T20:48:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGN.com snapped a picture of the Nintendo Wii remote controller "light gun" peripheral. As seen above, the light gun is a plastic shell that will encase the controller and connect to the "device out" port on the bottom of the remote. We hope to learn more details soon.
