--- title: "Tecmo to release golf game for Revolution launch" date: 2006-02-15T22:21:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Tecmo has announced its first Revolution title. The company said it will bring the next edition in its Pangya Golf series from developer Ntreev Soft to Nintendo's next-generation home console. The game has been popular with Japanese and Korean PC players and is known for its online functionality. The Revolution game is tentatively titled Skatto Golf Pangya and scheduled to release alongside the console's launch.
