--- title: "EA getting deeper into next-generation software development" date: 2004-09-10T14:15:17Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

According to the latest issue of the MCV publication, Electronic Arts has gone on record to say that it has 200 people working on next generation game software. No specific titles were announced nor the intended platforms. The company feels it is more prepared than anyone for the transition into the next generation.
