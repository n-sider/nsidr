--- title: "Fire Emblem now available" date: 2005-10-20T19:05:21Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo's Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. The title is on sale for a suggested retail price of $49.99. The game was developed by Intelligent Systems.

Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance currently has a 86 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 15 reviews).
