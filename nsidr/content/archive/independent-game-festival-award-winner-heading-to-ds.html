--- title: "Independent Game Festival award winner heading to DS" date: 2007-05-25T14:44:36Z contributors: - Michael Foster --- Independent game developer Metanet Software, creators of N+, will be bringing their Independent Game Festival Audience Award winner to the Nintendo DS and PSP in the fall.

N+ puts players in the shoes of a ninja hunting for gold. Taking cues from Lode Runner, the physics-heavy platformer will be upgraded for its new homes away from the internet. The original game, N, is Flash-based and is still available to play online. In January, Metanet had previously announced that a version would be coming to Xbox Live Arcade.

N+ is being published by Atari, and development by Silverbirch Studios, who are promising "new features and graphics."
