--- title: "Ultimate Spider-Man now available" date: 2005-09-23T01:20:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Activision has said that Ultimate Spider-Man is now available for the Nintendo GameCube, Game Boy Advance and Nintendo DS. The GameCube version features a suggested retail price of $49.99 while the Game Boy Advance version comes with a $29.99 price tag. Ultimate Spider-Man was developed by Treyarch Invention. More information can be found at the game's official website here.

The Nintendo DS version of Ultimate Spider-Man currently has an 80 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on four reviews).
