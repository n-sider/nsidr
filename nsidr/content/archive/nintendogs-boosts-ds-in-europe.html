--- title: "Nintendogs boosts DS in Europe" date: 2005-11-26T23:12:46Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of Europe is reporting that Nintendo DS juggernaut Nintendogs has done wonders for their sales.

Since its release October 7, Nintendogs has collectively sold over 1 million copies in Europe. Nintendo also credits the title for increasing Nintendo DS weekly sales to over 100,000 weekly, and 120,000 last week.
