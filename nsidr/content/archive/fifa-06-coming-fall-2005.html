--- title: "Fifa 06 coming fall 2005" date: 2005-08-18T22:19:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Electronic Arts said it will release FIFA 06 for the NIntendo DS, Game Boy Advance and Nintendo GameCube fall 2005. Developed at its EA Canada studio, the game will feature cover stars Freddy Adu, Ronaldinho, and Omar Bravo. Electronic Arts further said that 39 songs from countries around the world will be included in the game including tracks from Oasis, Jamiroquai, Royksopp, Carlinhos Brown, Subsonica, Boy, Kinky and Damian "Jr. Gong" Marley. More information can be found at the game's official website.
