--- title: "Reggie: 10+ Balance Board games in the works" date: 2008-02-20T13:59:26Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- In an interview conducted with IGN Wii, NOA president Reggie Fils-Aime let loose a number of revelations about the upcoming North American release of Wii Fit and the included Balance Board peripheral.

  • Release date is May 19
  • Game and Board bundle to go for "under $100"
  • Reggie expects no trouble selling Wii Fit to Americans
  • Big media push; Oprah not yet confirmed
  • Expected to appeal across all Wii consumer sectors
  • "Over 10 games in development" that will be able to use Balance Board

Interview: Reggie Fils-Aime on Wii Fit (IGN)
