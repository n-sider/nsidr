--- title: "Sudoku Mania now available on Nintendo DS" date: 2006-06-30T22:15:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sudoku-Mania is now available on Nintendo DS for $19.99.

Sudoku-Mania, published by UFO Interactive and developed by FrontLine Studios, features an "auto-generating algorithm" that gives it an infinite number of puzzles and customizable difficulty settings. The game makes use of the touch screen for number entry. The title also has a save feature, two-player versus mode and five different themes as well as the ability to switch between numbers or symbols.

Sudoku-Mania accompanies the recent release of several other puzzle games including Brain Age: Train Your Brain in Minutes a Day (77 percent average review), Big Brain Academy (77 percent average review), and Sudoku Gridmaster (63 percent average review).
