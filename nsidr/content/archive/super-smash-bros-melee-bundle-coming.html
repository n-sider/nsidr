--- title: "Super Smash Bros. Melee bundle coming" date: 2005-06-30T21:50:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America will begin selling its Super Smash Bros. Melee GameCube bundle this August. The bundle includes a Platinum Nintendo GameCube console and controller and will likely be on sale for the standard $99 price. So if you don't have a GameCube already, it's about time you go out and get one.
