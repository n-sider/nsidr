--- title: "European VC: break things with Mario!" date: 2007-08-24T13:46:12Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Three new games were added to the European Virtual Console today. Break things with Mario, play pool in space, or save another princess from another demon. Your choice!

  • Lunar Pool (NES, 500 points)
  • Neutopia (Turbografx-16, 600 points)
  • Wrecking Crew (NES, 500 points)

This brings the total number of VC games in Europe up to 128.
