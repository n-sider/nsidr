--- title: "Reggie to present at UBS Global Media" date: 2005-12-07T12:30:39Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo has announced that Reginald Fils-Aime will be presenting at the 2005 UBS Global Media conference which began Monday and runs through tomorrow.

The conference features presentations by execs representing advertising, broadcasting, publishing, multi-channel television distribution, entertainment, video games and new media companies.
