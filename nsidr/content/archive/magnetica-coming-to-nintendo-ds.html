--- title: "Magnetica coming to Nintendo DS?" date: 2006-02-21T16:42:41Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America will publish Magnetica for the Nintendo DS, according to AdvancedMN. Developed by Mitchell, the game was previously released in Japan under the name of Puzzloop. The game is described as a strategic puzzle where players must stop the onslaught of marbles from reaching the end. Players shoot marbles into the descending ones, where matching three colors in a row will make them disappear. The game is said to make good use of the Nintendo DS touch screen feature. Magnetica is scheduled for release on June 5, 2006.
