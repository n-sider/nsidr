--- title: "Spyro Superpack now available" date: 2005-10-20T19:06:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Vivendi Universal's Spyro Superpack is now available for the Game Boy Advance. The title contains the two games "Spyro: Season of Ice" and "Spyro: Season of Flame" for a suggested retail price of $19.99.
