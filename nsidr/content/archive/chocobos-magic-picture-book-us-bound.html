--- title: "Chocobo's Magic Picture Book US Bound" date: 2007-01-10T18:11:21Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- Via Square Enix comes official word that the previously Japanese-only Chocobo and the Magic Picture Book is headed to the US.

The game has received much praise for its graphical style which closely resembles an actual storybook.

wark

Renamed Final Fantasy Fables: Chocobo Tales, the game will hit shelves in April of 2007.
