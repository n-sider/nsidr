--- title: "Super Punch Out!! in EA's Fight Night Round 2" date: 2005-01-26T01:16:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts has confirmed that the Super Nintendo version of Super Punch Out!! will be available in the upcoming release of Fight Night Round 2 for the Nintendo GameCube. Super Punch Out!! will be available to play from the main menu of the game. In addition, Fight Night Round 2 will feature an unlockable fully three-dimensional version of Little Mac, star of the Punch Out!! series. Check out some screens of the game here.
