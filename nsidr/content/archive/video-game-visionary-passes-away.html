--- title: "Video game visionary passes away" date: 2004-07-13T01:18:40Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Shinya Nishigaki, creator and producer of the Sega Dreamcast titles Blue Stinger and Illbleed has passed away, according to GameSpot. Nishigaki, 42, is said to have died at his home from a heart attack. The N-Sider staff would like to wish our condolences to Nishigaki's family and friends.
