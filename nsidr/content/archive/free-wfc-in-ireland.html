--- title: "Free WFC in Ireland" date: 2006-01-24T11:01:11Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Via SiliconRepublic.com comes news of free WFC in Ireland thanks to a recent deal between BT and Nintendo.

The deal gets folks carrying Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection games free online access at existing BT Openzone hotspots in hotels, restaurants, and service stations. In addition, Nintendo is to install additional hotspots in major game retailers such as HMV, Game, Smyths Toys and GamesStop.
