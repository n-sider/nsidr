--- title: "Castlevania: DoS now available" date: 2005-10-11T00:29:12Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Konami's Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow is now available for the Nintendo DS. The title is on sale for a suggested retail price of $34.99.

Castlevania: Dawn of Sorrow currently has a 92 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 14 reviews).
