--- title: "Two more games coming to the DS" date: 2005-03-02T03:14:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Rising Star Games said it will bring Space Invaders Revolution to the Nintendo DS this Spring. The game will make use of the touch screen and feature over 60 levels of gameplay.

Conspiracy Entertainment said it will bring a title inspired by the Jeff Corwin Experience Animal Planet television program to the Nintendo DS. More information is expected to come soon.
