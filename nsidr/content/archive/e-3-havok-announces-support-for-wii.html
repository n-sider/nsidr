--- title: "[E3] Havok Announces Support for Wii" date: 2006-05-10T16:11:56Z contributors: - Brenden Petracek ---

Havok, a developer of middleware solutions used to optimize cross-platform videogame development, has announced support for Nintendo's new console.

Previously, Havok's software had been used in the development of more than fifteen GameCube titles, including, Goldeneye: Rogue Agent, Medal of Honor: European Assault and The Lord of the Rings: The Third Age.
