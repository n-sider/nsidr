--- title: "\"Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Adventures\" now available" date: 2004-06-07T18:13:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Adventures is now available in stores, according to Nintendo of America. Four Swords is a multiplayer competitive and cooperative game and is priced at an MSRP of $49.99.

The title features three modes: a single player quest where you're given control over all four Links, a Game Boy Advance link-up mode where players can cooperatively explore eight massive worlds, and a Shadow Battle mode where players can battle against each other.

Four Swords currently has an 86 percent review rating on GameRankings.com.
