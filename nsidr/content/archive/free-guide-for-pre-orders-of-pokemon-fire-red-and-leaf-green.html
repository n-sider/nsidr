--- title: "Free Guide for pre-orders of \"Pokemon Fire Red and Leaf Green\"" date: 2004-06-20T16:41:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

North American retailers will be providing pre-orders of Pokemon Fire Red or Leaf Green with a free Pokemon Pocket Trainer's Guide beginning August 1. Pokemon Fire Red and Leaf Green are scheduled for release on September 7 for a suggested retail price of $39.99 each. Each game will include a Wireless Adapter that allows players to battle and trade with each other wirelessly.
