--- title: "[E3] Ossu! Tatakae! Ouendan! coming to Nintendo DS?" date: 2006-05-08T16:51:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Unofficial word arrives that says the Japanese-centric Nintendo DS title Ossu! Tatakae! Ouendan! will be localized for a North American release under the name Elite Beat Agents. The game is said to be showcased during E3 this week and contain new scenarios and music.

Considered a sleeper hit in Japan when it released on July 28, 2005, Ouendan's gameplay combines comic-book storytelling with rhythmic gameplay. Stay tuned for the official word from Nintendo.
