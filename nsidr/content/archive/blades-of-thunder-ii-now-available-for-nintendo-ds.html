--- title: "Blades of Thunder II now available for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-04-21T01:31:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Blades of Thunder 2 is now available for the Nintendo DS for $19.99. The title is published by Summitsoft Entertainment and developed by CokeM Interactive. The game currently has a 45 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on one review).

In Blades of Thunder 2, players "take to the skies to battle terrorism in three different heavily-armed helicopter gunships through 17 fast-paced action missions." The game also features a Free-For-All multiplayer with up to six players over wireless LAN. Blades of Thunder 2 was originally announced at the 2005 Electronic Entertainment Expo. Check out official screens from the game on the Nintendo.com game page.
