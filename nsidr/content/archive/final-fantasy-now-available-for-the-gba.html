--- title: "Final Fantasy now available for the GBA" date: 2004-12-02T13:36:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

In case you haven't already heard, Nintendo of America has released Final Fantasy I & II: Dawn of Souls for the Game Boy Advance. The official website which features screens, artwork and more can be found here.

The game currently has an 80 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com.
