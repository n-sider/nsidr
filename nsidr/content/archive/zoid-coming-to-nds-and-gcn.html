--- title: "Zoid coming to NDS and GCN" date: 2005-08-27T17:16:33Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Tomy said it will publish Zoid Metal Crush for the Nintendo GameCube and Zoid Saga DS ~ legend of arcadia ~ for the Nintendo DS, according to IGN.com. The GameCube title is a fighting game and is scheduled for a Japanese release on October 27. The Nintendo DS title fits in the RPG genre and is scheduled for a winter release in Japan. A North American release is not currently planned for either title.
