--- title: "Square Enix's E3 lineup" date: 2007-07-09T13:30:51Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- Square Enix has revealed its E3 lineup for this year, as well as confirmed some release dates. As expected of the Japanese developer, the majority of the games at the show are from its immensely popular Final Fantasy and Dragon Quest franchises. Nintendo platforms, especially the DS, will see a lot of action from Square Enix at this year's E3.

Nintendo DS

  • Dragon Quest Monsters: Joker (November 6, 2007)
  • Final Fantasy Crystal Chronicles: Ring of Fates (Spring 2008)
  • Final Fantasy XII: Revenant Wings (November 20, 2007)
  • Front Mission (October 23, 2007)
  • Heroes of Mana (August 14, 2007)

Wii

  • Dragon Quest Swords: The Masked Queen and the Tower of Mirrors (TBA)

All of the above games will be playable, excepting Ring of Fates and Dragon Quest Swords.

Square-Enix's lineup is certainly not limited just to these announced games; it is entirely possible they will have some surprises in store for fans. The floodgates will open when E3 officially begins Wednesday afternoon.
