--- title: "Nintendo to publish quiz game" date: 2005-10-16T17:16:31Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo will release Legendary Quiz King - Championship for the Nintendo GameCube in Japan on December 8. The title is a four-player question-based quiz game that makes use of the GameCube microphone. The game is said to feature over eight-thousand questions on topics such as sports, movies, and food.
