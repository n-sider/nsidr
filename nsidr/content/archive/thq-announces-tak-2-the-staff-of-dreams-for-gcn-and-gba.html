--- title: "THQ announces \"Tak 2: The Staff of Dreams\" for GCN and GBA" date: 2004-04-22T17:51:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

THQ said it will release Tak 2: The Staff of Dreams, the sequel to Tak and the Power of Juju, on the Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance in October 2004. According to THQ, the title will be shown for the first time at the upcoming Electronic Entertainment Expo.
