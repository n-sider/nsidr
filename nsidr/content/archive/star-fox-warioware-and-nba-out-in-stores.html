--- title: "Star Fox, WarioWare, and NBA out in stores" date: 2005-02-15T20:01:48Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

There have been several releases over the past few weeks. Some of the more notable are mentioned below.

Star Fox: Assault is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. You can check out the official website for screens and other info here. Star Fox: Assault currently has a 75 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.

WarioWare Touched! is now available for the Game Boy Advance. The official website is located here and provides the usual images and info. WarioWare Touched! currently has an 85 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.

Electronic Arts' NBA Street v3 is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. The title, which features Mario, Peach and Luigi as playable characters, currently has a 91 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.
