--- title: "Pokemon XD now available" date: 2005-10-03T16:17:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo's Pokemon XD: Gale of Darkness is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. The title is on sale for the suggested retail price of $49.99. Nintendo has also bundled Pokemon XD: Gale of Darkness with the Nintendo GameCube console for the suggested retail price of $99.99. The game's official website can be found here.

Pokemon XD: Gale of Darkness currently has an 85 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on one review).
