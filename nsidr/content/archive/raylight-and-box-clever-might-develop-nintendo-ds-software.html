--- title: "Raylight and Box Clever might develop Nintendo DS software" date: 2004-06-08T15:20:31Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

UK-based Box Clever Interactive and Italy-based Raylight Studios have both been approved for official Nintendo DS development, according to Spong.com.

Both companies have experience developing for the Game Boy Advance. Raylight is responsible for such titles as Ozzy and Drix and Wing Commander Prophecy while Box Clever has created Reign of Fire and Freestyle Scooter.

No specific Nintendo DS titles have been announced.
