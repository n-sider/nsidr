--- title: "\"Mega Man Anniversary Collection\" now available for the GCN" date: 2004-06-24T03:57:13Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Capcom's Mega Man Anniversary Collection is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. Mega Man Anniversary Collection is a collection of classic Mega Man games including Mega Man 1 through 8 and two never-before-released arcade games "Mega Man the Power Battle" and "Mega Man 2 the Power Fighters".

The collection contains special bonuses such as original artwork, remixed versions of classic Mega Man music, and an exclusive GameCube-only interview with a Capcom producer.
