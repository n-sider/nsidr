--- title: "Ubisoft to bring \"Advance Guardian Heroes\" to the US" date: 2004-07-13T02:02:35Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ubisoft said it would publish Treasure's Advance Guardian Heroes for the Game Boy Advance. Scheduled for a North American release in September, Advance Guardian Heroes is a scrolling "beat 'em up" that features 20 controllable characters, a single player and several multiplayer scenarios.
