--- title: "Pokemon Mystery Dungeon now available" date: 2006-09-20T03:25:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Blue Rescue Team is now available on Nintendo DS and Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Red Rescue Team is now available on Game Boy Advance each for $34.99. The titles arrive via publisher Nintendo and developer Chunsoft. More info can be found at the game's official website.

Pokemon Mystery Dungeon features strategic battles that enhance the adventure and randomly generated dungeons that make every mission unique. Pokemon Mystery Dungeon for DS currently has a 64 percent and for GBA currently has a 85 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 2 and 1 reviews respectively).


GamePro says (2.5/5),
"Like most spin-off titles, Mystery Dungeon fails to capture the spirit and replay incentives that made the core products so irresistible to gamers of all ages. If endless dungeon crawling is your thing, then you should give this title a whirl. However, if monotonous gaming and skimpy Pokemon action aren't your thing, then this is one mystery that's best left unsolved."
