--- title: "Neo Geo heading to Virtual Console at last" date: 2007-09-12T17:47:16Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- SNK Playmore officially announced today their plans to bring classic NeoGeo arcade titles to the American Virtual Console. NeoGeo titles are already on their way to the Japanese VC. Three games have been announced so far:

  • Art of Fighting
  • Fatal Fury
  • World Heroes

They will be priced at 900 points each. No specific release dates were given, but you can expect to see them in the coming months.
