--- title: "Super Monkey Ball now available for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-02-27T22:07:31Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Super Monkey Ball Touch & Roll is now available for Nintendo DS for $29.99. The title is published by Sega. The game currently has a 58 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 8 reviews).

Super Monkey Ball: Touch and Go! utilizes the DS touch screen to control the ball. The game is said to feature over 100 new stages and two new party games for up to four players. The DS Download function allows you to play with other Nintendo DS owners with a single game cartridge. Take a look at the game's official website for more details.
