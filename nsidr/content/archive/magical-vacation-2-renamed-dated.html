--- title: "Magical Vacation 2 renamed, dated" date: 2006-06-22T17:48:19Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo snuck a little change into their release list today—Magical Vacation 2, hinted at before E3 and confirmed at the show is now called Magical Starsign and will ship this coming October 23.

The game, from Sword of Mana creators Brownie Brown, pits rocket-hijacking students against a strange planet in search of their disappeared teacher. It's an entirely stylus-controlled RPG and features cooperative play for up to five players.
