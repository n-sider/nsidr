--- title: "M3 multimedia player for NDS" date: 2005-08-10T01:45:47Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- M3 is preparing its NDS M3 Adapter for release later this month, according to Engadget.com. The cartridge is said to have been developed specifically for the Nintendo DS hardware. It is inserted into your Nintendo DS and allows you to watch movies, listen to music, view images and e-books. No price has been announced. More information can be found here.
