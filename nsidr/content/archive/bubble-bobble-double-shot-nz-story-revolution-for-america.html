--- title: "Bubble Bobble Double Shot, NZ Story Revolution for America" date: 2007-10-11T13:40:59Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Ignition Entertainment today announced their intent to bring Bubble Bobble Double Shot and New Zealand Story Revolution, "cult classic" arcade titles from Marvelous Entertainment, to North American Nintendo DS systems in early 2008.

  • Bubble Bobble Double Shot is "a respectful return to the series' roots"
  • Based on 1986 arcade game
  • Is a "perfect re-creation" of the original's gameplay
  • New Zealand Story Revolution is "an accurate re-telling of" 1988's The New Zealand Story
  • Tiki the Kiwi bird rescues kidnapped friends from a leopard seal
  • Settings unchanged from the original, but levels are remixed
