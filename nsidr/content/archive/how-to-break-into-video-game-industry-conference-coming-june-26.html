--- title: "\"How to Break Into Video Game Industry\" conference coming June 26" date: 2004-06-03T14:14:06Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The Game Initiative said it will hold a "How To Break Into Video Game Industry: Conference and Resume Workshop" on June 26 in Chevy Chase, Maryland at the National 4-H Youth Conference Center.

The event is designed for students and professionals to meet and network with professionals in the industry as well attend workshops to learn more about video game development.

The cost is $45 in advance or $60 at the door. More information can be found here.
