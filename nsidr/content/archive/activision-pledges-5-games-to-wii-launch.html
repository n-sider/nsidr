--- title: "Activision pledges 5 games to Wii launch" date: 2006-09-14T00:55:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Gamespot.com has received word of a five-game launch lineup from Activision, reported during a presentation at the Merrill Lynch Media and Entertainment Conference. These titles will include:

  • Call of Duty 3
  • Marvel Ultimate Alliance
  • Tony Hawk's Downhill Jam
  • Rapala's Tournament Fishing
  • World Series of Poker

Activision CEO Robert Kotick describes it as "the largest launch lineup that we've had in the history of our company."
