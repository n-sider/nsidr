--- title: "More titles released in GBA Video line" date: 2004-07-07T02:56:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Majesco released two new videos for its Game Boy Advance Video line. The first called "Codename: Kids Next Door", will feature "Operation: R.E.P.O.R.T.", "Operation: D.O.G.F.I.G.H.T.", "Operation: N.O. - P.O.W.U.H." and "Operation: B.R.I.E.F." all on a single cartridge. The second video is called "Cartoon Network Collection" and will contain one episode each of The Grim Adventures of Billy & Mandy, Johnny Bravo, Ed, Edd 'n Eddy and Courage the Cowardly Dog in a single Game Boy Advance Video pak. The two videos are on sale for a suggested retail price of $19.99.

More information can be found at the official website GBA-Video.com.
