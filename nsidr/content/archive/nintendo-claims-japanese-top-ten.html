Of particular note are the absolutely mind-boggling to-date sales: New Super Mario Brothers, More DS Brain Training, and Animal Crossing are all directly on the cusp of the 4,000,000 mark, a sales plateau virtually never reached by any game, and yet there are three in the top ten (and FOUR if you count the combined totals of Pokemon Diamond and Pearl, who together have sold over 4,500,000 copies).
