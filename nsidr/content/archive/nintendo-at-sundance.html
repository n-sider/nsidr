--- title: "Nintendo at Sundance" date: 2006-01-20T17:36:44Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

By way of Next Generation comes the news that Nintendo will be exhibiting products at the Sundance Film Festival, which opened yesterday.

Nintendo will be showing off Nintendo DS and GameCube titles as well as the Game Boy micro at the show. Attendees will also be given a chance to get their posteriors handed them by a dirty snaker playing Mario Kart DS.
