--- title: "Nintendo DS Lite European launch date?" date: 2006-02-21T16:34:51Z ---

DS-X2 has noticed that several German online retailers have the Nintendo DS Lite listed for an August 16, 2006 release for 129.99 Euros. It would be surprising considering UK general manager David Yarnton, mentioned in an interview with Eurogamer a few weeks ago that "we haven't got any plans at the moment [for a Nintendo DS redesign]. Nothing I know of." Props to Nintendo Europe if they've ironed out a release plan this quickly. The three launch colors listed by the German websites are the same as those in Japan -- Crystal White, Enamel Navy and Ice Blue. Given this information, it's best to wait for an official announcement from Nintendo Europe.
