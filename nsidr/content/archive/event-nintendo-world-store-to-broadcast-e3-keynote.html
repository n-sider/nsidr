--- title: "[Event] Nintendo World Store to Broadcast E3 Keynote" date: 2006-05-01T15:47:36Z contributors: - Dean Bergmann ---

Nintendo's "Nintendo World Store" in New York City, will be broadcasting the Nintendo E3 2006 Press Conerence/Keynote speech live on May 9th.

Space is extremely limited, and they are requesting that you RSVP at their website, nintendoworldstore.com as soon as possible. Please note that this store is located in New York City, only RSVP if you are able to be there on May 9th, 2006.

As an added bonus, it will be customer appreciation day, so expect some insane deals as Nintendo celebrates the unveiling of Wii. The broadcast starts at 12:30pm EDT, with competitions and prizes to follow at 1:30pm to 5:00pm.
