--- title: "Ubisoft to show Rayman at E3" date: 2006-05-03T16:31:41Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Ubisoft has announced their E3 2006 lineup today, and in addition to previously-announced Wii title Red Steel, they've unveiled the Rayman title they've been working on: Rayman Raving Rabbids.

Ubisoft describes the game as follows:

Developed by famed creative director Michel Ancel, this new installment of the Rayman franchise will be the funniest and zaniest yet. Rayman's world is threatened by a devastating invasion by the most unexpected creatures and Rayman must battle to save his world and its inhabitants.

Of course, we're all just focusing on Rayman's awesome new hairdo for now. With luck, we'll get a chance to get our hands on this title at the upcoming E3 show next week.
