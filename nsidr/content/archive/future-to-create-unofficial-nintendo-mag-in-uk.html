--- title: "Future to create unofficial Nintendo mag in UK" date: 2006-04-24T12:05:30Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Buried in a story about recent editorial shakeups at Edge Magazine is the news that new senior editor Tony Mott will help create a new magazine devoted exclusively to Nintendo — though this one will be unofficial.

Future is already responsible for Official Nintendo Magazine in that territory, having launched it this past February. The official magazine has already gained a bit of notoriety for claiming the Revolution's stand would double as a power stand — a report they later retracted. ONM has very recently been the focus of attention as they had a feature that strongly hinted at a fall release for Revolution in Europe.
