--- title: "Coming next week: Diary of a Hunter" date: 2010-04-23T17:00:00Z contributors: - Amber Ahlborn series: - Diary of a Hunter --- Imagine, if you will, a world inhabited by predatory beasts larger than your house. What would it be like to live in a world of such fantastic creatures?


Capcom's Monster Hunter Tri, one of the most highly anticipated games to hit the Wii yet this year, has finally arrived. To celebrate Tri's release, N-Sider's Amber Ahlborn will step into the world of Monster Hunter Tri, not simply as a player but as the character of a rookie hunter as she explores this harrowing realm of titanic beasts. Join her for the next two weeks as she chronicles her experiences in the Diary of a Hunter.
