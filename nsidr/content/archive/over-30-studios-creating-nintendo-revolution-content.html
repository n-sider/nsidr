--- title: "Over 30 studios creating Nintendo Revolution content" date: 2006-03-22T20:24:54Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

There are over 30 studios confirmed to be working on content for Nintendo's next-generation console codenamed Revolution. This likewise equates to at least 30 games in development for the platform. We've performed an exhaustive update to N-Sider's A Third-Party Revolution piece which lists every developer and publisher currently readying titles for Nintendo's new device.
