--- title: "Konami acquires Marvel Trading Card Game" date: 2005-08-18T22:19:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Konami said it has signed an agreement with Marvel Enterprises and Upper Deck Entertainment to develop games for current and next generation video game systems based on the Marvel Trading Card Game. The agreement further gives Konami the rights to Marvel Trading Card Game video games for both online and offline formats and licenses the company to incorporate Marvel character content into the game -- including content based on The Incredible Hulk, Spider-Man, the X-Men, the Avengers and the Fantastic Four. No platforms or release dates were announced.
