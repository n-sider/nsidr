--- title: "Nintendo: The Cash Card" date: 2006-06-07T11:12:06Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Kotaku has a bit today on Kyoto Shinkin Bank's new Nintendo-adorned cash card.

The cash card has a built-in smart chip and stores biometric fingerprint identification used to verify the person at the ATM is really authorized to empty the bank account the card is linked to.
