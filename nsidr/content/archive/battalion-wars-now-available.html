--- title: "Battalion Wars now available" date: 2005-09-21T20:37:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America said Battalion Wars is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. The title features a suggested retail price of $49.99. The title was developed by Kuju Entertainment. More information can be found at the game's official website here.

Battalion Wars currently has an 84 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 6 reviews).
