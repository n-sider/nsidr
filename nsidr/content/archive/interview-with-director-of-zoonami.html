--- title: "Interview with director of Zoonami" date: 2004-08-30T13:29:38Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

GameCube Heaven has conducted an interview with Martin Hollis, director and head of UK developer Zoonami. The interview is mainly focused on the upcoming music title from the company called "Funkydilla" however Mr. Hollis also mentions the previously-named and mysterious Game Zero title. Commenting on the company's relationship with Nintendo, Hollis said, "We continue to maintain a very close relationship with Nintendo as we always have." Check out the full interview here.
