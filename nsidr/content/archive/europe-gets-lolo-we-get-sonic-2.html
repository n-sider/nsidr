--- title: "Europe gets Lolo, we get Sonic 2" date: 2007-06-11T16:18:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- The Virtual Console service on the Wii Shop Channel has been updated both here and across the Atlantic, and both territories are now home to several new classic re-releases.

For Europe, as of last Friday, we have the following releases, making a total of 96 (we're expecting a "100th release" press release any day now):

  • Balloon Fight (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Adventures of Lolo (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Dead Moon (TurboGrafx16, 600 Wii Points)

And having gone live at 9 a.m. Pacific time this morning, these titles are now available on the North American Virtual Console:

  • Lode Runner (NES, 500 Wii Points)
  • Sonic the Hedgehog 2 (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)
  • Golden Axe II (Sega Genesis, 800 Wii Points)

Again, all these titles are available now, so if you're interested in the ones in your territory, and have the Wii Points to spend, head on over and grab one.
