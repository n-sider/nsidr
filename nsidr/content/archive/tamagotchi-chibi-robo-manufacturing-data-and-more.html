--- title: "Tamagotchi, Chibi Robo, manufacturing data and more" date: 2005-06-22T11:13:42Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Bandai has opened a teaser website for its upcoming Tamagotchi game for the Nintendo DS. Take a stab at navigating the pages here.

An article in Nintendo Dream has said that Chibi Robo for the Nintendo GameCube will utilize the GameCube Mic, according to Nintendojo. The GameCube Mic launched alongside Mario Party 6 last December and is also being used in upcoming titles such as Mario Party 7 and Yoot Saito's Odama.

In other news, there's word from Japan's Nikkei BP that Sony has plans to manufacture only 12 million PSP units for the coming year. In early 2005, Sony had told its component providers to expect orders for about 18 million units. The reason for this change is not known. On the other hand, Nintendo is expected to manufacture over 20 million Nintendo DS units over the next year.

Final news bit. Square Enix has said that it has nearly 50 percent of the online gaming market share in Japan while in US its online userbase has surpassed EverQuest. Online gaming is becoming a significant revenue stream and the company is keeping ahead of market demands.
