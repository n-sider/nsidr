--- title: "Nyko to release the \"Wireless Air Flo\" controller for GameCube" date: 2004-05-11T00:10:54Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nyko will unveil the Wireless Air Flo controller for GameCube this week. The controller is a wireless controller - with a range of 50 feet - that constantly circulates fresh air to player's hands and features the button placement of the Sony PlayStation controller.

More information will be released this week including release date and price.
