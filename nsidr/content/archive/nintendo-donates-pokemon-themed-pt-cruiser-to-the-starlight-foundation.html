--- title: "Nintendo donates Pokemon-themed PT Cruiser to the Starlight Foundation" date: 2004-02-27T11:52:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

To promote the upcoming release of the newest Pokemon e-Reader expansion called Pokemon-e Trading Card Game: EX Team Magma vs Team Aqua, a Special Edition Pokemon-e TCG: EX Team Magma vs Team Aqua PT Cruiser is being auctioned on eBay from Tuesday, February 24 through Friday, March 5.

All proceeds from the auction will go directly to the Starlight Children's Foundation - a non-profit organization that Nintendo has supported for years and of which grants a single wish to sick childen and their family. Along with a variety of cute decals decorating the outside of the car, the interior includes a Nintendo GameCube and a 27" color television.

The winner of the auction will also receive two tickets to the "Pokemon Trading Card Game Presents Frankie Muniz HoopLA! 2004 Celebrity Basketball" game and VIP reception which takes place on Sunday, March 14, at the Los Angeles Sports Arena.
