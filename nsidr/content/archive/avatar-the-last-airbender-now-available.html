--- title: "Avatar: The Last Airbender now available" date: 2006-10-11T02:21:16Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---
Avatar: The Last Airbender is now available on Nintendo DS ($29.99), Game Boy Advance ($29.99) and GameCube ($39.99). The title arrives via publisher THQ. Find more info at the game's official website.

In Avatar: The Last Airbender players will use characters Aang, Katara, Haru and Sokka in an adventure across the universe to discover your untapped bending powers. Players engage in martial arts style combat and use fully customizable and upgradeable bending powers.
