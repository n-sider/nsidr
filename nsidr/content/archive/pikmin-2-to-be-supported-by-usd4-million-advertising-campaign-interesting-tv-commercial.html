--- title: "\"Pikmin 2\" to be supported by $4 million advertising campaign...interesting TV commercial" date: 2004-08-25T01:04:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

IGN had gotten a hold of Nintendo's new advertising campaign for Pikmin 2, which is scheduled for release on August 30. The release of the title will be supported by a $4 million campaign as announced by Nintendo during a teleconference. A portion of that will be devoted to a 30-second comedic television commercial. Check out the interview with Nintendo of America's director of advertising and promotions and the full details on the commercial here.
