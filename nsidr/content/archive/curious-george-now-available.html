--- title: "Curious George now available" date: 2006-02-01T21:43:28Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Namco's Curious George is now available for Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance. The game is based on the six-decade old Curious George book, which follows the adventures of a mischievous monkey and his friend The Man with the Yellow Hat. The series is now being turn into an animated movie as well as series of video games. In this game, players solve puzzles and play mini-games in order to save the financially in-trouble museum where the Man with the Yellow Hat works.

The console versions, priced at $39.99, are developed by Monkey Bar Games. The portable version retails for $19.99 and was created by Torus Games.
