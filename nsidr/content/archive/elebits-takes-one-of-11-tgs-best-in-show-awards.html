--- title: "Elebits takes one of 11 TGS best-in-show awards" date: 2006-09-26T09:58:16Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Coming out of the recently-held Tokyo Game Show, Konami's Wii launch title Elebits has received one of eleven awards given by a committee for "best games showing particular promise", after tallying the votes of show attendees.

The only other Nintendo-platform title recieving the award is Capcom's Gyakuten Saiban 4, better known to English-speakers as Phoenix Wright 4. Nintendo does not have a presence at the show, as is typical for them—this year especially, the company is concentrating on its own Wii press events such as the Wii Preview events held two weeks ago.
