--- title: "Sega to release \"Phantasy Star Online Episode I & II Plus\" in North America?" date: 2004-07-09T00:57:51Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Sega will release Phantasy Star Online Episode I & II Plus for the GameCube in North America, according to GameGossip.com. The title was recently released in Japan. While essentially the same game, it's said to contain new offline quests, Game Boy Advance downloads, and a five-stage challenge mode. A release date is not known for the title. Wait for an official announcement from Sega.
