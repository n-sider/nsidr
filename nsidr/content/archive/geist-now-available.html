--- title: "Geist now available" date: 2005-08-16T00:26:01Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo has released its possession-filled first-person-adventure-shooter title Geist for the Nintendo GameCube. Check out screens and other information regarding the game at its official website.

Geist currently has a 67 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com (based on six reviews).
