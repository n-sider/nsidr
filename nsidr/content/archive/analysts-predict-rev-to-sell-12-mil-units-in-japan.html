--- title: "Analysts predict Rev to sell 12 mil units in Japan" date: 2005-06-28T20:41:11Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The DFC Intelligence has released a short summary of its latest research "Forecasting the Video Game Industry". In this report, the researchers predict the best and worst case scenarios for the three console manufacturers. For instance, one best-case prediction for the Nintendo Revolution is that it will sell over 12 million units in Japan compared to the around 4 million sold of GameCube.
