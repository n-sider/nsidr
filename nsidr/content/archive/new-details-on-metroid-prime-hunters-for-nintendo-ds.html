--- title: "New details on Metroid Prime Hunters for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-02-04T02:26:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- It sounds as though the upcoming Metroid Prime Hunters for Nintendo DS is coming along well according to the latest word out of Nintendo.com. Last Friday, Nintendo of America conducted a Wi-Fi Connection beta test for the game. NOA's Andy recalls the night saying he had a great experience and looks forward to the game's public release on March 20. Andy notes, "The first thing I noticed is that the lobby system in Hunters is great. Unlike Mario Kart DS, you can choose when to stop looking for opponents and start the action."

Be sure to check out the official Metroid Prime Hunters website for an opportunity to meet all the characters you'll play as in the game. Also, head on over to Jeux-France for a selection of new images from the game.
