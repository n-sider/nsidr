--- title: "Race on Nintendo Revolution, says Reggie" date: 2006-04-29T05:06:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

TriGames.net caught up with Reggie Fils-Aime, Nintendo of America VP of Sales and Marketing, at the Nintendo World Store located in New York. Fils-Aime was available for two hours to answer questions and interact with Nintendo fans.

Of note, Fils-Aime let info slip about a racing title in development for Nintendo's Wii console. "I'm going to give one little secret," Fils-Aime said. "Only one little secret, and hopefully I won't lose my job. We're going to show a racing game at E3 where you use the core controller in a very different way. That will answer all the questions to how you'll play a racing game."

Look to Nintendo's E3 press conference on May 9, for more info.
