--- title: "The countdown begins to Nintendo's worldwide briefings" date: 2006-09-13T00:38:54Z ---

Nintendo is scheduled to have three consecutive worldwide media events over the next several days beginning tomorrow in Japan, followed closely by events in North America and Europe.

N-Sider's very own Anthony JC has received an invitation to the New York event on Thursday. He'll be arriving early to hear Nintendo of America president Reggie Fils-Aime discuss Nintendo's upcoming holiday season. Afterwards, he'll hang around and get nearly six hours of hands-on time with upcoming Nintendo games. Wii games specifically will be presented as well as several other pieces of highly-anticipated news. This is expected to be a significant event and so we've created countdowns to the respective show times.

  • Japan (unknown)
  • America (Thursday 10:00AM - 4:00PM EST)
  • Europe (Friday 11:00AM - 5:00PM UTC/GMT +1 hour)

If the countdowns aren't working properly for you and have left you befuddled, don't worry, we'll give you a heads up through a news story an hour in advance of each conference.

We'd also like to invite you to relax and discuss the events in the N-Forums Chat Room. Arrive early and get yourself a good seat.
