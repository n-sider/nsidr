--- title: "Okami port headed to Wii in Spring 2008" date: 2007-10-18T15:03:51Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- After countless unsubstantiated rumors and online petitions heedless of the very history of petitions for games themselves, Capcom has confirmed that PlayStation 2 cult hit Okami is coming to Wii.

  • Slated for a spring 2008 release in Europe; other territories unannounced
  • Wii Remote to be used for the celestial brush mechanic
  • Combat to include "motion-controlled physical attacks"
  • Port being handled by Ready at Dawn, the studio responsible for Jax and God of War: Chains of Olympus for the PSP
  • News follows this year's runaway success of Resident Evil 4 Wii Edition
