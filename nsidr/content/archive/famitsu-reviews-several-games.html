--- title: "Famitsu reviews several games" date: 2005-08-10T18:34:50Z --- The latest issue of Japanese magazine Famitsu has reviewed several upcoming Nintendo DS games. Each game is reviewed by four people and rated on a ten-point scale.
  • Akumajou Dracula: Aoitsuki no Juujika (Konami): 9, 8, 8, 8 - (33/40)
  • Jump! Super Stars (Nintendo): 8, 8, 9, 7 - (32/40)
  • Kyouryuu Ouja Ketteisen: Grand Prix (MTO): 6, 6, 6, 5 - (23/40)
  • Lunar Genesis (Marvelous): 6, 9, 6, 6 - (27/40)
  • Puzzle Bobble DS (Taito): 7, 6, 6, 6 - (25/40)
  • Survival Kids: Lost in Blue (Konami): 8, 8, 7, 8 - (31/40)
In related news, Jump! Super Stars for the Nintendo DS has sold 320,000 copies during its first day of sale in Japan, according to The-MagicBox.com.
