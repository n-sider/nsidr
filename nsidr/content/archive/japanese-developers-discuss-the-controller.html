--- title: "Japanese developers discuss the controller" date: 2005-10-27T14:25:14Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- The Revolution controller is inspiring the imagination of Japanese game developers, according to the latest issue of Nintendo Dream magazine.

Q Entertainment's Tetsuya Mizuguchi said he would like to think of ways to combine the controller with music.

Meanwhile, Killer 7 producer Gouichi Suda said his studio Grasshopper has begun preliminary development of a Project Revolution title. He said the game will focus heavily on direct input through the controller.
