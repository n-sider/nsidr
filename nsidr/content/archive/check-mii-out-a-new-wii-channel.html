--- title: "Check Mii Out, a new Wii channel" date: 2007-07-11T17:00:50Z contributors: - Jon Rust --- During the Nintendo E3 press conference, a new Wii channel was revealed.

This free Wii channel builds on the phenomenon of people creating fun Mii caricatures. Now people can show off Miis they have created either on their own or for Nintendo-suggested competitions.

The channel will be available sometime this year as a free downloadable in the WiiWare shop.
