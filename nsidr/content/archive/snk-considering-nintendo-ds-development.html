--- title: "SNK considering Nintendo DS development" date: 2004-09-10T14:06:21Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Kikizo Games recently interviewed Shinya Morishita, Overseas Marketing Department Manager of SNK NeoGeo Corporation. Morishita said his company was already in development with a title for Sony's PSP and when asked about development for the Nintendo DS, he responded, "Yes, we are also interested in Nintendo DS, but at the moment, we first need to see what the response of the market is to it."
