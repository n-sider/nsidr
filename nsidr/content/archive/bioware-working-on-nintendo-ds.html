--- title: "BioWare working on Nintendo DS" date: 2006-09-19T20:28:28Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Word comes from Daily Game that Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic and Jade Empire developers BioWare are working on Nintendo DS.

Daily Game says the company has formed a new handheld group led by project director Dan Tudge, who has been involved in titles for Nintendo, Microsoft, THQ, and Sega Sports. It is not said what projects the group will be working on.
