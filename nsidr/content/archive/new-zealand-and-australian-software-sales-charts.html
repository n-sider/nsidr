--- title: "New Zealand and Australian software sales charts" date: 2005-07-07T20:45:20Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Gfk has released the New Zealand and Australian software sales charts for the week ending July 3, 2005. In New Zealand, only one Nintendo title makes it into the top ten with Pokemon Emerald for Game Boy Advance at number six. In Australia, Nintendo platforms fare a little better with two titles in the top ten. Pokemon Emerald remains the number one selling title for the week.

    New Zealand
  1. Tekken 5 (PS2)
  2. SingStar Pop Bundle (PS2)
  3. Battlefield 2 (PC/MAC)
  4. Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas (Xbox)
  5. Madagascar (PS2)
  6. Pokemon Emerald (GBA)
  7. Juiced (PS2)
  8. God Of War (PS2)
  9. Medal Of Honor: European Assault (PS2)
  10. Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas (PS2)
