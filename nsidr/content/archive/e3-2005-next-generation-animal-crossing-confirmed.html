--- title: "E3 2005: Next Generation Animal Crossing Confirmed" date: 2005-05-20T19:09:16Z contributors: - James Remington ---

In an interview with Nintendo Now, General Manager Entertainment, Analysis & Development Division Takashi Tezuka and Manager/Producer Entertainment, Analysis & Development Division Katsuya Eguchi confirmed that not only is Animal Crossing in development for the Revolution, but also alluded to some form of compatibility with the Nintendo DS.

NNow: Are you in early development of Animal Crossing for the Nintendo Revolution?
T. Tezuka: Yes.
K. Eguchi: We are trying to make it compatible with the DS version as well.

Source: Nintendo Now
