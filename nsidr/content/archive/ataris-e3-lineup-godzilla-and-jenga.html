--- title: "Atari's E3 lineup: Godzilla and Jenga" date: 2007-07-09T21:17:04Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Atari has revealed its E3 lineup. Although there aren't any surprises, it's clear the company is putting a lot of support behind Nintendo platforms. Atari will have the latest installments in the Dragon Ball Z and Godzilla franchises playable at its booth. It will also debut a new franchise to the press called Jenga, based on the board game of the same name.

Nintendo DS

  • Godzilla: Unleashed (Fall 2007)
  • Jenga (Fall 2007)

Wii

  • Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi 3 (Holiday 2007)
  • Godzilla: Unleashed (Fall 2007)
  • Jenga (Fall 2007)

Please note that Atari's lineup is not limited to these announced titles. It's possible the publisher will reveal new projects under development.
