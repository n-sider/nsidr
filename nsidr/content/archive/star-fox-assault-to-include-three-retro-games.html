--- title: "Star Fox Assault to include three retro games" date: 2005-01-19T02:18:59Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Namco will include three classic arcade games with Star Fox Assault for the Nintendo GameCube, according to IGNCube. The three games which include Xevious, Star Luster, and Battle City will be implimented as unlockable extras. Star Fox Assault is scheduled to release in North America on February 15, 2005.
