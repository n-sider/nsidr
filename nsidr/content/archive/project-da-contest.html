--- title: "\"Project DA\" contest" date: 2004-10-03T22:38:05Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

NeoPong Software, an officially licensed Nintendo DS games developer, is currently holding a contest on its website forums to allow readers to design weapons, quests, or spells for inclusion in the company's upcoming Project DA Nintendo DS RPG. Three winners will be awarded a free copy of the final game and will have their names placed in the credits.

N-Sider forum member Mr. GlubGlub has created a topic showing off some of his artistic creations. Check that out here.
