--- title: "Video: Super Paper Mario for Nintendo GameCube" date: 2006-06-18T13:58:04Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

GamesAreFun.com has acquired video of Super Paper Mario for the Nintendo GameCube from the World Hobby Fair currently taking place in Tokyo, Japan. The World Hobby Fair is an event for companies to showcase to the public upcoming toys, games, and videogames. The video highlights a boss battle between Mario and a ferocious paper-dragon. Take a look at the interesting new gameplay developed by Intelligent Systems and use of the graphic style established by Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door.
