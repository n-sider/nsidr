--- title: "Electroplankton for DS" date: 2005-03-07T17:06:58Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ohhh, what is this? It's a new game for the Nintendo DS being made by Nintendo and Toshio Iwai, a contemporary Japanese electronic artist. The game is being described as "Touchable Media Art". Although it's in Japanese, check out the website to see videos of the game in motion here.
