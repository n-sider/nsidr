--- title: "Miyamoto to visit Nintendo World Store" date: 2005-09-17T02:23:21Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Shigeru Miyamoto will be visiting the Nintendo World Store in New York City in Rockefeller Plaza on September 25, according to 4ColorRebellion.com. Miyamoto will be giving away free Miyamoto DS skins and signing autographs. More information can be found at the store's website.
