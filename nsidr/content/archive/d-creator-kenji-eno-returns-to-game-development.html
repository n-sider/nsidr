--- title: "\"D\" creator Kenji Eno returns to game development" date: 2006-05-24T22:55:35Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Kenji Eno told Gamasutra in an interview that he has returned to game development on "a console with a new control device." Despite the clear hint, he did not go so far as to confirm a Wii title. Eno is known for his "D" series of cinematic-adventure games for the Sega Saturn, Sony PlayStation and Sega Dreamcast.

Eno has been absent from game development for nearly seven years now. His new studio is called From Yellow To Orange. The company's website has only a teaser image with a logo and text that reads "fyto will be on your side..." Stay tuned for more info.
