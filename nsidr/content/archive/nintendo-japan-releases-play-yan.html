--- title: "Nintendo Japan releases \"Play Yan\"" date: 2005-02-22T20:23:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan has released its new Play Yan media player, according to PlanetGameCube.com. The device is said to enable users the ability to play music and movies on their Game Boy Advance or Nintendo DS. Nintendo Japan has opened a section of its website that also allows users to download "Garage Games" to Panasonic SD cards. The first game available is called "Insect", with a new title being available each week for the next 12 weeks. A thirteenth game will be accessible to members of Nintendo Japan's "Club Nintendo".
