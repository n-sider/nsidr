--- title: "SeattlePI says Wii arrives on November 19 for $250" date: 2006-09-14T02:21:45Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

SeattlePI reports that Nintendo "plans to announce today that it will release its new Wii video-game console in North and South America on Nov. 19, just as the holiday shopping season begins, and that the machine will cost $250 in the United States."

The article continues: "Nintendo intends to announce today that every Wii will come with a game compilation called Wii Sports -- including tennis, golf, baseball and bowling -- meant to show off the machine's intuitive controls."

The article also states: "There will be a photo channel that will allow users to use the Wii to display digital photographs on television. There also will be an easy-to-use interactive news channel and weather channel."
