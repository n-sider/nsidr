--- title: "Release forecast this week: Puzzly Planets and Dragon Balls" date: 2007-06-05T15:27:55Z contributors: - Glen Bayer - Mattie Behrens --- Below is a tentative list of games available this week of June 4-10. Please note that all releases are subject to change. Since we've already brought you Virtual Console release news just yesterday (and Europe's last Friday), we won't be repeating it here.

North America

Notable releases this week for North America include, finally, the arrival of the Opera Software-developed web browser, as well as Intelligent Systems' stylish Panel de Pon/Tetris Attack/Puzzle League/etc. remake, Planet Puzzle League.

Nintendo DS

  • Nintendo DS Lite Web Browser (June 4)
  • Planet Puzzle League (June 4)
  • Dragon Ball Z: Harukanaru Densetsu (June 5)

GameCube

  • Surf's Up (June 5)
