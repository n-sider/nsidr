--- title: "More Internet Channel update details" date: 2007-03-26T12:23:15Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo has released a few more details concerning the upcoming final version of Wii's Internet Channel, due in April.

The new channel's feature list includes improvements to zooming and scrolling, including an improved zoomed font; faster access to favorites and startup of the channel itself; the ability to hide the toolbar (useful for full-screen viewing of sites); and buttons to key in common phrases such as "www." and ".com".

As previously mentioned, the final version will be available for download in April as a free update through the end of June. After June, the Internet Channel will be available for 500 Wii Points.
