--- title: "Clover to make Joe for Revolution?" date: 2006-01-24T10:49:46Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Had enough of Viewtiful Joe for awhile? According to a recent Jeux-France interview with Clover's Atsushi Inaba, no, you haven't. He's mulling over how Joe might make an appearance on Revolution.

1UP has a brief mention of the relevant part of the interview for us non-French-speakers. "Now, if the character and the universe of Viewtiful Joe adapt correctly to the characteristics of Nintendo's future console, and we could indeed conceive a Viewtiful Joe on Nintendo Revolution, [we would do it]," Inaba says. "In the contrary case, we will try to make to propose another game on Nintendo Revolution which is as interesting."

Hopefully, Clover will stretch their legs a bit as they are currently doing with the intriguing Okami (for Sony's PlayStation 2), and produce a new franchise for the Revolution.
