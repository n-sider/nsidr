--- title: "Over 500 new WFC hotspots in the UK" date: 2006-03-24T19:05:02Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Back in November, we let you know that Nintendo was partnering with BT Openzone for Wi-Fi Connection access across the UK. This week, they've jumped into phase two of that agreement, installing over 500 new BT Openzone hotspots.

The hotspots are being installed in retail stores such as GAME, Gamestation, HMV, and Toys 'R Us and will offer DS downloads as well as access to the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection. Beyond the recently-installed hotspots, Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection is also available from 7,500 other hotspots in the country.
