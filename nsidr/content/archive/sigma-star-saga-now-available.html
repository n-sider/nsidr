--- title: "Sigma Star Saga now available" date: 2005-08-16T22:50:26Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Namco Hometek Inc. said it has released the Game Boy Advance role-playing game Sigma Star Saga to North American retailers. More information regarding the game including screenshots can be found at the game's official website.

Sigma Star Saga currently has a 82 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on four reviews).
