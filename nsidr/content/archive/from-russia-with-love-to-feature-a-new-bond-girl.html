--- title: "From Russia With Love to feature a new Bond Girl" date: 2005-08-03T23:16:06Z contributors: - Travis Woodside --- Maria Menounos, better known as eye candy for teenage boys as the sexy nurse in Fantastic Four, has signed on to appear alongside the digital Sean Connery in EA's From Russia With Love.

Though her exact role in the game is unknown I theorize that she will be integral to the segments of the game that have nothing to do with the 1963 hit film.

"I am honored to be a Bond girl and be a part of the Bond legacy. To star in a video game with Connery, the original Bond, is incredible," stated Menounos.

Source: Planet GameCube
