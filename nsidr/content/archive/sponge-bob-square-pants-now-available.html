--- title: "Sponge Bob Square Pants now available" date: 2005-10-20T19:07:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- THQ's Sponge Bob Square Pants: Lights, Camera, PANTS! is now available for the Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance.
