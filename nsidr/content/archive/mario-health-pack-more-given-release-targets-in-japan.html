--- title: "Mario, Health Pack, more given release targets in Japan" date: 2007-04-23T11:08:32Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- In the pages of Famitsu, gaming blog Kotaku has unearthed Nintendo's release intentions in Nintendo's homeland for several upcoming titles, including heavy-hitters Super Mario Galaxy and Wii Health Pack. They are as follows:

Wii

  • MushiKing Super Collection (Sega, July)
  • Wii Health Pack (Nintendo, July)
  • Mario Party 8 (Nintendo, July)
  • Super Mario Galaxy (Nintendo, October)
  • Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games (Sega, December)
  • Resident Evil: Umbrella Chronicles (Capcom, 2008)

Nintendo DS

  • Final Fantasy Tactics A2: The Sealed Grimoire (Square Enix, July)
  • Final Fantasy Crystal Chronicles: Ring of Fates (Square Enix, August)
  • Pokemon Mystery Dungeon 2 (Nintendo, November)
  • Dragon Quest IX (Square Enix, November)
  • Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games (Sega, December)

Also revealed: Nintendo intends to bring the reverentially-regarded Wii with DVD playback capabilities to Japan late this year. No news, of course, on such a move for other territories—as usual, we'll let you know if we hear.
