--- title: "Europe moves 7 million Nintendo DS units" date: 2006-11-21T15:57:42Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of Europe announced it has crossed 7 million sold Nintendo DS units in that territory.

In addition, six DS titles are now million-sellers in that territory. Nintendogs has sold over four million, while Dr. Kawashima's Brain Training and Animal Crossing: Wild World have moved a million apiece. Super Mario 64 DS, Mario Kart DS, and New Super Mario Bros. have all sold 1.5 million units apiece.
