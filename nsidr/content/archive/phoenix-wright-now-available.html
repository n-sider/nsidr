--- title: "Phoenix Wright now available" date: 2005-10-11T21:08:35Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Capcom today released Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney for Nintendo DS at a suggested retail price of $29.95. The title is a remake of the original first installment of the Gyakuten Saiban series and marks the first appearance of the series on US shores.

The title currently has a 81% average review ranking based on seven reviews at GameRankings.com.
