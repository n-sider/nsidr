--- title: "Kuju expresses Revolution intent" date: 2006-02-01T19:58:17Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

N-Sider reader nbob89, took the liberty of forwarding us an email conversation he had with Kuju Entertainment, developers of the recent Battalion Wars for Nintendo GameCube. In the email, Kuju reiterates its interest in developing for Nintendo's next-generation console codenamed Revolution.

enbob89 says, "Being pleasantly surprised by their work on Battalion Wars, I decided to contact Kuju and find out if they were planning on developing for Revolution. And like anybody who's ever contacted a developer about their future plans, I didn't expect much. But, here's what happened:

    From: enbob89
    Posted At: 16 January 2006 00:31
    Posted To: Kuju
    Subject: Next Generation?

    Will Kuju be supporting the Nintendo Revolution?


    From: Ian Baverstock
    Date: Jan 23, 2006 5:22 AM
    Subject: RE: Next Generation?

    Hi,
    Kuju expect to be supporting all the next generation platforms in due course but have made no specific statements about dates at this time.

    Ian
While this may not come as a shock to most, since Kuju previously expressed interest in developing for Revolution, there's nothing quite as warm and fuzzy as a confirmation even if it's as ambiguous and autoreply-esque as this one."
