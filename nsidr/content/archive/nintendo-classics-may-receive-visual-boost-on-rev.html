--- title: "Nintendo classics may receive visual boost on Rev?" date: 2005-06-22T00:06:57Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo said it is deciding whether or not it will provide updated visuals to retro game titles offered on the virtual gaming Revolution network.

"We are doing several experiments, including working with the original Super Mario Bros., with the new technology," said Nintendo president Satoru Iwata in a recent interview with Electronic Gaming Monthly. "The game itself and the gameplay shall be identical, but the look will be different; it's possible that with Revolution, we may be able to see the old games with new looks."
