--- title: "Nippon Ichi considering development" date: 2005-08-10T18:32:17Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Gamasutra recently interviewed Nippon Ichi Software America's marketing coordinator Jack Niida. In the interview, Niida revealed the company was considering developing games for the Nintendo Revolution. "We are seriously thinking of working to Xbox 360 and also Revolution," Niida told Gamasutra.

Nippon Ichi is known for titles such as Disgaea for the PlayStation 2. More of the interview can be read here.
