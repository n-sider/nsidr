--- title: "Price drop, new colors coming in Australia" date: 2005-09-14T07:52:38Z --- Nintendo Australia has announced that it will be dropping the price of the Nintendo DS from $199.95 to $179.95 on September 22 to coincide with the release of puppy simulator Nintendogs in that territory. September 22 will also see the system released in two new colors: Cosmic Blue and Mystic Pink.
