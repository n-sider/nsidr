--- title: "Telegames announces the first Nintendo DS software title" date: 2004-05-04T01:40:00Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Telegames will release a compilation from its Ultimate Brain Games and Ultimate Card Games series for the Nintendo DS, according to 1up.com. The currently untitled compilation will feature the following games:

Brain Games

  • Chess
  • Checkers
  • Backgammon
  • Mahjong
  • Dominoes
  • 4-in-a-Row
  • Sink the Ships
  • Reversi
  • Shogi
  • Go

Card Games

  • Hearts
  • Spades
  • Bridge
  • Euchre
  • Cribbage
  • Canasta
  • Gin Rummy
  • Go Fish
  • Crazy Eights
  • Chess
  • Pinochle
  • Draw Poker
  • Blackjack
  • Texas Hold 'Em
  • Skat

The card-game selection will also include some 50 solitaire variations and five versions of video poker. More information, including further uses of the DS features, will be released after E3. This marks the first officially announced title for the Nintendo DS.
