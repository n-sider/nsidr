--- title: "DS moves 10 million, Nintendo lists Q1 releases" date: 2007-01-25T18:06:16Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo sent word today that they have passed the ten million mark for sales of the Nintendo DS in the Americas since the system's launch in November 2004.

Riding along with the announcement was a list of titles headed to store shelves in the first three months of the year, now including third-party offerings. Because the first-party offerings are exactly the same as previously noted (and actually, a little less detailed), we'll only be listing the third-party commitments here.

Without any further ado:

  • Arthur & The Invisibles (Atari, January)
  • Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney: Justice For All (Capcom, January)
  • Fossil League DTC (D3Publisher, January)
  • Lunar Knights (Konami, February)
  • F24 Stealth Fighter (Majesco, February)
  • Disney's Kim Possible: Global Gemini (Buena Vista, February)
  • Meteos: Disney Magic (Buena Vista, February)
  • Winning Eleven Pro Evolution Soccer 2007 (Konami, February)
  • Izuna: Legend of the Unemployed Ninja (Atlus, February)
  • Disney's Herbie: Rescue Rally (Buena Vista, March)
  • Disney's Meet the Robinsons (Buena Vista, March)
  • Spectrobes (Buena Vista, March)
  • Steel Horizon (Konami, March)
  • Cake Mania (Majesco, March)
  • Puzzle Quest: Challenge of the Warlords (D3Publisher, March)
  • TMNT (Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles) (Ubisoft, March)
