--- title: "Electroplankton Finally Given US Release Date" date: 2005-10-19T20:10:18Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker ---

After months of speculation, the gaming community will be glad to know that Nintendo has finally announced a United States release date for the quirky music/visual synethstesia title, Electroplankton.

The game is slated for release on January 9, 2006, according to Nintendo's official website. Just in time to blow your Christmas dough.
