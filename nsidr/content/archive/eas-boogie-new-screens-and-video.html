--- title: "EA's Boogie: new screens and video" date: 2007-05-04T11:37:03Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Vooks has posted brand-new screenshots and video of Electronic Arts' Wii-exclusive dance title, Boogie.

The new media shows off what may be a somewhat different direction artistically for the title, which originally debuted in the form of screenshots showing vegetablish-looking creatures engaging in the titular boogie-ing. It's also possible, since the press release says there are multiple "Boog" characters to choose from, that the new characters showcased are simply additions to the game's repertoire.

Boogie's current claim to fame is that it also lets players sing using a now-confirmed microphone peripheral. The game is slated for a release this year.
