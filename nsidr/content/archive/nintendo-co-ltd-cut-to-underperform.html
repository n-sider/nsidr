--- title: "Nintendo Co., Ltd. cut to \"underperform\"" date: 2005-10-05T21:05:24Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Bloomberg.com is reporting today that Credit Suisse First Boston has cut their rating of Nintendo Co., Ltd. from "neutral" to "underperform" among Japanese exporters. Credit Suisse First Boston cited sales data in their decision suggesting that the Nintendo DS is struggling in the US market.
