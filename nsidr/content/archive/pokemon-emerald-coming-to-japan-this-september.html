--- title: "\"Pokemon Emerald\" coming to Japan this September?" date: 2004-07-13T02:11:24Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan will release its new Pokemon title "Pokemon Emerald" in Japan September 13, according to Game-Science.com.

Japanese magazine Koro Koro Comics revealed that Pokemon Emerald will utilize the wireless adapter. The title will make its first appearance at the Pokemon Festa in Japan on July 17.
