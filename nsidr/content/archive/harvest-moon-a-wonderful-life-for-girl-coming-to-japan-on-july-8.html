--- title: "\"Harvest Moon: A Wonderful Life for Girl\" coming to Japan on July 8" date: 2004-05-20T15:46:03Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Marvelous Interactive will release Harvest Moon: A Wonderful Life for Girl in Japan on July 8 for GameCube, according to The-MagicBox.com. A sound track album will be bundled with the game as a bonus.
