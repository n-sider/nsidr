--- title: "Nihilistic completes its part in the development of \"StarCraft: Ghost\"" date: 2004-06-22T20:17:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

The co-developer of Blizzard's StarCraft: Ghost is no longer involved in the title's development, according to HomeLAN.

"Nihilistic has completed its contribution on StarCraft: Ghost," a Nihilistic spokesperson told HomeLAN. "They are contracted to begin work on another project in the months ahead. Due to the requirements of that obligation and the delay of StarCraft, it was mutually determined that now would be the best time for them to complete their work."

Last week, Blizzard officially announced the delay of StarCraft: Ghost into 2005 noting the time would be used to further polish the game.
