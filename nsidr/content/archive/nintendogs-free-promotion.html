--- title: "Nintendogs free promotion" date: 2005-07-21T23:22:45Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America is holding a special promotion where certain "My Nintendo" users will receive a free copy of Nintendogs. Nintendo has emailed certain users to let them know if they will be receiving a copy. Check this page for more info. For the rest of us who aren't so lucky, we can pick up Nintendogs when it arrives in stores on August 22.
