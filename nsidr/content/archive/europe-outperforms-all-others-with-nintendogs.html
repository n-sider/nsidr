--- title: "Europe outperforms all others with Nintendogs" date: 2006-02-16T11:56:14Z ---

A rather short press release this morning announces that Nintendo DS juggernaut Nintendogs has surpassed all other regions with its European sales.

In Europe, the pups have sold two million copies so far, compared to 1 million in Japan and 1.5 million in the US. Apparently something about the little dogs is clicking with the Europeans, and as the release puts it, with "many female purchasers".
