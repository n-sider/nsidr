--- title: "No Killer 7 for North American PS2 Owners" date: 2004-02-03T05:39:27Z contributors: - Kenneth Kyle Wade ---

Capcom has updated its press release webpage for Killer 7 that now includes one very important new detail: the PlayStation 2 port of Killer 7 will not be coming to North America. It appears, that only PlayStation 2 owners in Japan and Europe will be able to acquire the title.

Killer 7 is a cel-shaded action-adventure title developed by Capcom for GameCube and PlayStation 2. The game centers around a detective named Harman Smith and his seven personalities. Using each character and their unique ablities, you must take to the streets and rid the world of a mysterious crime wave that has plagued the globe.
