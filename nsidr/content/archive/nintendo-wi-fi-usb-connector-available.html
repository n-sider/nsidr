--- title: "Nintendo Wi-Fi USB Connector available" date: 2005-11-12T16:32:39Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America is now offering the Nintendo Wi-Fi USB Connector for purchase in their online store for $34.95.

The connector plugs into the USB port on your PC running Windows XP and shares your PC's Internet connection with your Nintendo DS for playing WFC-compatible games. The connector is not required if you have either a supported Wi-Fi router or wish to play WFC-compatible games at a supported hotspot.
