--- title: "DKC2, Mario & Yoshi hit Europe's Virtual Console" date: 2007-05-18T12:42:22Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of Europe has announced two new releases for their territory's version of the Wii Shop Channel's Virtual Console service, which lets Wii owners spend Wii Points to play emulated versions of classic games.

The new titles are:

  • Donkey Kong Country 2: Diddy Kong's Quest (SNES, 800 Wii Points)
  • Mario & Yoshi (NES, 500 Wii Points)

Europe's Virtual Console service currently sports 87 titles. The above-mentioned titles are available now.
