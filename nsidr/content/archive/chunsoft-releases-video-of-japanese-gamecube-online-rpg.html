--- title: "Chunsoft releases video of Japanese GameCube online RPG" date: 2004-12-23T02:25:47Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Although there has been no word on a North American release, Japanese GameCube owners will have the chance at checking out a new massively-multiplayer online RPG called "Homeland". The title is in development by Chunsoft and is scheduled for a Japanese release in Spring 2005. Take a look at three gameplay videos that have been made available on Chunsoft's Japanese website.
