--- title: "[E3] Namco has three Wii titles planned" date: 2006-05-10T13:47:40Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

N-Sider has learned that Namco has three titles planned for Wii. The first is dubbed Final Haron and categorized under the "racing" genre. The other two lack titles but fall into the genres of Action and RPG. Thanks to N-Sider reader Samer for the heads-up. We'll bring you more as we learn it.
