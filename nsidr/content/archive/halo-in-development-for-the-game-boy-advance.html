--- title: "Halo in development for the Game Boy Advance" date: 2004-06-11T04:15:29Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Eurogamer reported on a rumor that IGN.com has further validated in its mailbag, regarding a title based on the Halo series coming to the Nintendo Game Boy Advance. Although nothing has been officially confirmed, the Eurogamer article goes further and says that the GBA title will be developed by an outside studio under Bungie's (the original developer) supervision.
