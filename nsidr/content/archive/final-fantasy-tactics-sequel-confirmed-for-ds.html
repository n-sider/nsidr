--- title: "Final Fantasy Tactics sequel confirmed for DS" date: 2006-12-18T16:30:42Z contributors: - James Remington --- Today Square Enix has confirmed initial speculation that their previously announced game Final Fantasy Tactics A2: The Sealed Grimoire is being developed for the Nintendo DS. It's also being reported by Japanese media outlets that The Sealed Grimoire, as was speculated earlier, is indeed a sequel to Final Fantasy Tactics Advance for the GameBoy Advance.

Be sure to stay tuned to N-Sider as we'll be reporting on any new updates for this game when they become available.
