--- title: "A sneak peek at Nintendo's GDC offerings" date: 2008-02-20T01:22:54Z contributors: - Dean Bergmann ---
A blurry shot of Nintendo's GDC booth being built
After a bit of choice wordplay and quick footwork, we were able to get a sneak peek at the games Nintendo will have playable when the Game Developers Conference (GDC) officially kicks off tomorrow.

Playable titles for the Nintendo DS:

  • Spore: Creatures
  • Final Fantasy Crystal Chronicles: Ring of Fates
  • Ninja Gaiden: Dragon Sword

Playable titles for the Wii:

  • Super Smash Bros. Brawl
  • WiiFit

There was also a rolling video of Mario Kart Wii, which showed 4-player split screen action and various different levels. Nintendo's booth is relatively small, and all their meeting rooms are named after playable characters found in Super Smash Bros. Brawl (Samus, Meta Knight, etc).

We'll have more GDC coverage as the week progresses. Stay tuned.
