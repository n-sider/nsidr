--- title: "EA's plans for the next-generation of consoles?" date: 2005-01-25T02:00:36Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts has released preliminary details on its software lineup for next generation consoles, according to GameIndustry.biz. According to the article, the company has thirteen titles scheduled to launch on Xbox 2 in late 2005 and early 2006. A list of tentative titles and names are below:

  • Burnout 4
  • Black
  • Battlefield: Modern Combat
  • FIFA 2006
  • FIFA Street 2
  • Tiger Woods PGA Tour 2006
  • SSX 4
  • Harry Potter title
  • James Bond: From Russia With Love
  • The Godfather
  • Need for Speed: Most Wanted
  • The Sims title
  • The Urbz 2

No titles have officially been announced for Nintendo's next-generation console codenamed Revolution, however expect more details at the coming Electronic Entertainment Expo in May
