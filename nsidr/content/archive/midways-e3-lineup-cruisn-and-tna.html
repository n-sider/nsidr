--- title: "Midway's E3 lineup: Cruis'n and TNA" date: 2007-07-09T21:36:48Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Midway plans to display a wealth of games at this week's E3. The company has a few games scheduled for release on Wii and Nintendo DS and has several more allocated to the PS3, Xbox 360 and PC platforms. One of the more notable announced games is perhaps the Wii exclusive Cruis'n, a franchise that was previously very popular in arcades and on Nintendo 64.

Nintendo DS

  • Ultimate Mortal Kombat (November 2007)
  • Foster's Home for Imaginary Friends: Imagination Invaders (November 2007)
  • The Bee Game (November 2007)

Wii

  • Cruis'n (November 2007)
  • Game Party (November 2007)
  • TNA iMPACT! (Spring 2008)

Game Boy Advance

  • The Bee Game (November 2007)

Please note that Midway's lineup is not limited to these announced titles. It's possible the publisher will reveal additional projects in development.
