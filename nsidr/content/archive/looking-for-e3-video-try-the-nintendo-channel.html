--- title: "Looking for E3 video? Try the Nintendo Channel" date: 2009-06-02T18:42:31Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens series: - E3 2009 Coverage --- This is really just a heads-up for you readers more than anything, but an hour ago, I was being asked by folks in our chat if there were any good videos they could watch of things they'd missed at the conference—such as Metroid: Other M—and the answer was no. At the time, not even Nintendo's own press site had the videos in question.

Well, before I closed up for the day, I quickly checked the Nintendo Channel, and lo and behold, there are trailers for everything, from Other M to New Super Mario Bros. Wii and Galaxy 2. The digest video says these will be up all week, so if you've got a Wii with the Nintendo Channel installed, you're set: just dim the lights, turn up the stereo, and enjoy!
