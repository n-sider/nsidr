--- title: "Iwata speaks about Nintendo's future in the Australian market, and moreover the world" date: 2004-06-04T20:45:12Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo president Satoru Iwata spoke with the Herald Sun earlier this week regarding Nintendo's position in Australia and also the future direction of the game industry.

"Nintendo created 20 years ago the standard of how video games were going to be played. There is the TV set, here is the controller, (held) in both hands," he said. "We should not hesitate to crash through the system Nintendo itself created," he says. That's innovation."
