--- title: "Nintendo to sponsor \"Rip Curl GromSearch 2005\"" date: 2005-07-07T01:54:39Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo said it will be the presenting sponsor of the "Rip Curl GromSearch 2005" national surfing competition. Between now and November 7, Nintendo and Rip Curl will partner to bring the competition to nine North American beaches. All events are free and open to the public. For a list of dates and locations visit www.ripcurl.com.
