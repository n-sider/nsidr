--- title: "Rumble free with Hunters" date: 2006-05-02T14:28:32Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo of America announced today that, at "select retailers", and "while supplies last", you'll get a free Nintendo DS Rumble Pak with your purchase of Metroid Prime Hunters.

Hunters was released on March 20 alongside Tetris DS; together, both brought Nintendo's first-party Wi-Fi Connection game count to a total of four.
