--- title: "Future to produce Nintendo Official Magazine" date: 2005-12-01T11:13:16Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Nintendo UK today announced that Future will be producing Nintendo Official Magazine in the UK, starting with the February 2006 issue. Future will be giving the magazine an overhaul from the previous Emap-managed publication, giving it a new look, feel, and editorial team.

Future currently produces the official PlayStation 2, Xbox, and Xbox 360 magazines in the UK. Future is also responsible for Next Generation, Edge, GamesRadar, and CVG.
