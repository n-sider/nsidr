--- title: "Nintendo to exhibit at tomorrow's Comic-Con" date: 2005-07-14T00:42:00Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Nintendo of America will exhibit playable game demonstrations at the San Diego Comic-Con, according to PlanetGameCube.com. The event takes place July 14 through July 17 at the San Diego Convention Center. Admission is open to the public and daily passes are available for $15-30. More information can be found at the Comic-Con website. The list of playable demos will include:
    Nintendo GameCube
  • Battalion Wars
  • Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance
  • Geist
  • The Incredibles 2
  • Killer 7
  • Pokmon XD: Gale of Darkness
  • Spartan: Total Warrior
  • The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess
    Game Boy Advance
  • Batman Begins
  • Dynasty Warriors Advance
  • Mega Man Zero 4
  • Nightmare Before Christmas: The Pumpkin King
    Nintendo DS
  • Advance Wars: Dual Strike
  • Age of Empires
  • Castlevania DS
  • DS Download Station
  • Mario Kart DS
  • Metroid Prime Hunters
  • Nintendogs
  • Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith
