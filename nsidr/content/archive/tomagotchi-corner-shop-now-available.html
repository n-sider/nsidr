--- title: "Tomagotchi Corner Shop now available" date: 2006-01-31T23:51:19Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Bandai's "Tamagotchi: Corner Shop" is now available for the Nintendo DS. Those of you who enjoy raising virtual pets will likely get a kick out of this latest twist in the Tomagotchi series. In Corner Shop players find themselves running one of three businesses, including a dentist office. Player's goal is to treat their customers well so they can earn money and grow their shop. The title currently has a 7.2 average review according to GameStats.com (based on four reviews).
