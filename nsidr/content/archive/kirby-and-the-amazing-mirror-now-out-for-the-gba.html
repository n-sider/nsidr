--- title: "\"Kirby & The Amazing Mirror\" now out for the GBA" date: 2004-10-18T18:54:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo of America announced the release of Kirby & The Amazing Mirror for the Game Boy Advance. Screens and other information on the title can be found at its official website.

Kirby & The Amazing Mirror currently has an 83 percent average review rating at GameRankings.com.
