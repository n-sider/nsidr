--- title: "Engine Software to support the Nintendo DS" date: 2004-06-03T02:13:53Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Netherlands developer Engine Software said it will develop games for the Nintendo DS, according to GCAdvanced.com.

"Personally I am very glad to have expanded our relationship with Nintendo once again," said Ruud van Moosdijk, Head of Development at Engine Software. "No matter how long you work with Nintendo they keep amazing you with new innovative ideas which simply result in an explosion of inspiration. I am confident that the DS will bring new enjoyment to developers and consumers alike."
