--- title: "European publisher/developer shuffle" date: 2004-08-09T16:27:49Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ubisoft, Electronic Arts and THQ are interested in purchasing Eidos, according to Telegraph.co.uk. Electronic Arts has allegedly placed a $300 million offer for the company. Eidos is known for its Lara Croft series of games. Further details will likely come in the coming month.

Meanwhile, Ubisoft is also rumored to be negotiating with developer Codemasters for a buyout.
