--- title: "\"Mario Power Tennis\" now available" date: 2004-11-09T13:54:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Mario Power Tennis should be available at retailers nationwide, according to Nintendo of America. The title has received high reviews thus far and currently holds a 90 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com.

Mario Power Tennis is developed by Camelot Software Planning, the same company that has brought us such memorable titles as Golden Sun and Mario Golf.
