--- title: "Kirby-assured: VC Monday does not suck" date: 2007-02-12T13:21:28Z contributors: - Brandon Daiker --- It's the dawn of a new week once again and you know what that means!

VIRTUAL CONSOLE MONDAY


  • Kirby's Adventure (NES, 1 player, 500 Wii Points)
  • Ice Climber (NES, 1-2 players, 500 Wii Points)
  • Kid Icarus (NES, 1 player, 500 Wii Points)

Some real gems this week, with Kirby's Adventure being entirely worth the dough, and Kid Icarus completely deserving of a spin (if only for the latecomers to see how basic it really is and ponder why we're all clamoring for a sequel for some reason). And hey, that fancy Wii suspend state is always appreciated! Ice Climber... not so much, but two out of three ain't bad, right?
