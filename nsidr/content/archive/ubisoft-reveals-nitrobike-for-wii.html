--- title: "Ubisoft reveals Nitrobike for Wii" date: 2007-07-13T02:59:09Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Ubisoft announced it will publish Nitrobike, a motocross racing game exclusively for Wii. The game is being developed by Left Field Productions, the former Nintendo second-party studio behind the creation of Excitebike 64 and NBA Courtside. Players will use the Wii remote to guide rocket-propelled motorcycles across hilly terrain and huge jumps. Ubisoft said we can expect Nitrobike to speed into stores this holiday.
