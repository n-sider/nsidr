--- title: "Dress up your consoles with Gamer Graffix" date: 2006-03-17T02:43:37Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

If you have the sinking feeling that your Nintendo DS or Nintendo GameCube lack style, GameStop is offering some skins to help brighten your day. DS Pink Fur Skin might go well with your copy of Nintendogs. "No way," you say? Well, you're a hard one to please. How about the fashionable GameCube Sonic Riders Skin? On a more serious note, both of these products were created by Gamer Graffix and are available for $9.99. If these two particular skins don't suit your taste, you might have some better luck at their official website.
