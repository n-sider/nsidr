--- title: "Pokemon Trozei now available for Nintendo DS" date: 2006-03-11T22:31:03Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Pokemon Trozei is now available for the Nintendo DS for $34.99. The title is published by Nintendo of America and developed by Genius Sonority. Pokemon Trozei currently has a 77 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 9 reviews).

It's described as a Tetris-style game with recognizable Pokemon characters. It has both a single and wireless two-player multiplayer mode. Nintendo.com has a preview of the game and tips on how to pronounce its title. Also, check out the game's official website here.
