--- title: "Prism: Light the Way illuminates DS" date: 2007-08-10T11:24:56Z contributors: - Glen Bayer - Jon Rust --- The Nintendo DS is fast becoming a veritable haven for puzzle games, and the latest to join the lineup is Eidos-published Prism: Light the Way, based on a mobile game developed by Morpheme.

In Prism, players use mirrors and crystals to reflect, split, and color a beam of light in order to create an illuminating pattern. The game features over 130 puzzles, and an infinity mode, which randomly generates puzzle after puzzle.

Prism: Light the Way is due to come out next month; a specific date has yet to be announced.
