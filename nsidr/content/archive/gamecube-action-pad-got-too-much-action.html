--- title: "GameCube Action Pad got too much action?" date: 2005-11-10T12:49:32Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Have you already worn out the Dance Dance Revolution: Mario Mix GameCube Action Pad with your sweet moves? Although the Action Pad is only bundled with the game in-store, all you stylish dancers can acquire a replacement or new Pad through Nintendo's toll-free number at 1-800-255-3700. The part number is 59730 and the price is $19.99 (tax and shipping not included - UPS Ground within the U.S. is $5.00).
