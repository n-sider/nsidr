--- title: "\"Fullmetal Alchemist: Omoide no Sonata\" coming to GBA" date: 2004-06-15T19:49:34Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Square Enix said in its Japanese Monthly Gangan magazine that a new title in the Fullmetal Alchemist series is coming to the Game Boy Advance.

The new game called Fullmetal Alchemist: Omoide no Sonata will involve card RPG battles. There is no word on a North American release, however the Japanese release date is set for July 22.

Source: GameSpot
