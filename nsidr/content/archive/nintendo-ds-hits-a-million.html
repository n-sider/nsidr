--- title: "Nintendo DS hits a million" date: 2004-12-08T00:29:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo has said that sales of the Nintendo DS in North America and Japan have reached a million units. Apparently within just four days of the Japanese December 2 launch, 462,000 units have been sold. Nintendo Japan has shipped 570,000 units thus far. More units are expected to arrive in both Japanese and American stores in the following weeks. Nintendo hopes the Nintendo DS will sell five million units worldwide by March 31, 2005.
