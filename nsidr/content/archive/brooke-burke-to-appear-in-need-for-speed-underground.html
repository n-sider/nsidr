--- title: "Brooke Burke to appear in \"Need for Speed Underground\"" date: 2004-06-17T21:58:44Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Electronic Arts said it has signed Brooke Burke to star in Need for Speed Underground 2, which is coming to GameCube and Game Boy Advance. Burke might be familiar to those of you who watched E! Entertainment Television's travel series "Wild On".

In Need for Speed, Burke plays an organizer of the elite underground racing circuit where she will assist you in fine-tuning your skills to become the top racer in the underground.

"I'm an absolute adrenaline junkie and car freak in real life, so starring in Need for Speed Underground 2 is a huge thrill," Burke said. "Need for Speed Underground 2 really captures one of the reasons I love my cars so much - they're a canvas for self-expression."
