--- title: "Ice Age 2 now available for Nintendo platforms" date: 2006-03-17T02:20:40Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Ice Age 2: The Meltdown is now available via VUGames for Game Boy Advance, Nintendo DS, and Nintendo GameCube. The two handheld titles are priced at $29.99 while the console version can be found on store shelves for $39.99. Check out the games' official website for more info and screens.

Ice Age 2 is based on the Twentieth Century Fox animated film of the same name which will be arriving in theaters on March 31. Players can control the familiar main characters of the movie in a variety of mini-games. The GameCube version has a 79 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on one review). Meanwhile, the Nintendo DS version sits at a 55 percent average review ranking (based on one review).
