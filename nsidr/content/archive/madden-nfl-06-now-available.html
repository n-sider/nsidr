--- title: "Madden NFL 06 now available" date: 2005-08-08T22:22:28Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Electronic Arts has said that Madden NFL '06 is now available for the Nintendo DS, Nintendo GameCube and Game Boy Advance. More information on the game including screens can be found at the game's official website.

Madden NFL '06 for GameCube currently has a 90 percent average review ranking at GameRankings.com (based on four reviews).
