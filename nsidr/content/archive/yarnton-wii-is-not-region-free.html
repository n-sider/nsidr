--- title: "Yarnton: Wii is not region-free" date: 2006-09-18T18:07:41Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

After breaking the earlier news that Wii was apparently mostly region-free, Wired News has issued an update indicating the opposite.

Nintendo's official line now is that Wii is not region-free, nor is its own first-party software. We'll know for sure when the console releases in the U.S. November 19.
