--- title: "Digimon World 4 now available" date: 2005-06-02T20:17:22Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Bandai Games said it has released Digimon World 4 on the Nintendo GameCube for a suggested retail price of $29.99. The title features four-player gaming and a real-time battle system. Screens and more info can be found at the game's official website.

Digimon World 4 currently has a 70 percent average review ranking on GameRankings.com.
