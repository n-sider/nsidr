--- title: "Ubisoft to bring Dogz to GBA" date: 2005-10-11T00:51:08Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Ubisoft said it has licensed MTO's Dogz series for release on the Game Boy Advance. The title is described as a puppy simulation game and will be released worldwide this November. The game is said to include 49 different dogs from 18 different breeds. It will also include three mini-games and a visiting mode. More details will likely arrive closer to its release. Until then, check out the game's official website here.
