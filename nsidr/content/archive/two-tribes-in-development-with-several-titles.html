--- title: "Two Tribes in development with several titles" date: 2005-06-29T00:36:15Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Two Tribes has said that it will develop Toki Tori 2 for the Nintendo DS. The title will be a sequel to the 2001 Game Boy Color game published by Capcom and is said to make extensive use of the touch screen. Two Tribes is in development with two additional Nintendo DS titles. ReWind is described as an on-rails shooter with 3D graphics and a rich storyline. Meanwhile, Three Tribes is an adventure game in a 3D environment where players control a group of natives who are aiming to please the village shaman. More information can be found at the company's official website.
