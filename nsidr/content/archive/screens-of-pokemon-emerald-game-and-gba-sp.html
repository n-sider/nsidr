--- title: "Screens of \"Pokemon Emerald\" game and GBA SP" date: 2004-07-21T02:01:35Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---

Nintendo Japan has released new screens of its upcoming Game Boy Advance title Pokemon Emerald. Nintendo will release alongside the game, a special edition Pokemon Emerald Game Boy Advance SP on September 16, which will be available exclusively at the Pokemon Center Online for 12,500 yen.
