--- title: "New Super Mario Bros. hits 500k stateside" date: 2006-06-22T16:07:27Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

GameSpot has word that New Super Mario Bros. has passed the half-million sales mark in US markets since its release this past May 15.

The title has not yet hit European shelves; there, it's slated for release June 30. In Japan, the game has been on the shelves since May 25, and has sold over 1.2 million copies.
