--- title: "Release dates for Nintendo titles through September" date: 2007-05-21T11:52:15Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Nintendo of America has given release dates to a handful of Nintendo-published titles for release through the end of September.

Wii

  • Mario Strikers Charged (July 30)
  • Metroid Prime 3 Corruption (August 20)
  • Battalion Wars 2 (September)

Nintendo DS

  • Picross DS (July 30)
  • Brain Age 2: More Training in Minutes a Day (August 20)
  • DK Jungle Climber (formerly King of Swing 2; September 10)
  • Chibi-Robo: Park Patrol (September 24)

Dates for DS Air, Super Mario Galaxy, Disaster: Day of Crisis, Project H.A.M.M.E.R., and Super Smash Bros. Brawl remain unspecified. The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass, which is headed for Japan next month, is still slated for "holiday 2007".
