--- title: "Capcom: RE4 Wii hits 1 million shipped worldwide" date: 2007-10-30T15:49:12Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Capcom sends word today that Resident Evil 4: Wii Edition has hit the million-shipped mark worldwide.

  • Retails for $29.99, which certainly can't hurt
  • Capcom expects sales to continue into the holidays
  • Upcoming Wii offerings include Resident Evil: The Umbrella Chronicles on November 13, and We Love Golf next year
