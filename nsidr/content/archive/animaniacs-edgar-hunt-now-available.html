--- title: "Animaniacs Edgar Hunt now available" date: 2005-09-21T20:35:52Z contributors: - Glen Bayer --- Ignition Entertainment said Animaniacs: The Great Edgar Hunt is now available for the Nintendo GameCube. The title features a suggested retail price of $19.99. The title was developed by Warthog.

Animaniacs: The Great Edgar Hunt currently has a 56 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 4 reviews).
