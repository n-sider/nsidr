--- title: "1UP takes a look at the Revolution revolution" date: 2005-10-17T12:40:09Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

While not providing any new information, 1UP has published The Revolution Begins Now, a lengthy take on what they expect Nintendo's upcoming console to do to gaming. The debunking of the purported similarities between Revolution's control scheme and Microsoft's XWand/Sony's EyeToy wand is a good read in and of itself.
