--- title: "Merrill Lynch, Sega: Wii for $200 — or less" date: 2006-05-17T10:57:46Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens ---

Continuing the neverending parade of analyst opinion about Nintendo's upcoming Wii console, Merrill Lynch analyst Justin Post predicted this past week that Wii will sell for $200 when it hits stores this coming fourth quarter.

The author of the Forbes story also notes that a Sega exec agrees with this analysis, saying Wii will sell for less than $200. $200 certainly seems like the upper limit for Wii, given all the commentary we've heard since IGN spilled the beans on the rumored system specs — and at the same time, the story of what Nintendo was reportedly telling developers and publishers to expect — $200 or less.
