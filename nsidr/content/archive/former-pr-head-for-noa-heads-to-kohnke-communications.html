--- title: "Former PR head for NOA heads to Kohnke Communications" date: 2007-10-05T13:43:49Z contributors: - Mattie Behrens --- Julia Roether, formerly of GolinHarris and responsible for Nintendo of America's public relations there, has been snapped up by Kohnke Communications.

  • Takes position of vice president
  • Will lead a new Kohnke office in LA
  • With NOA, oversaw launch of Wii, Nintendo DS, and numerous games
  • Prior to GolinHarris tenure, was senior manager of corporate communications with Activision
  • Kohnke currently represents Nyko and has in the past represented Bandai Namco, Disney Interactive, Hudson, and Konami
