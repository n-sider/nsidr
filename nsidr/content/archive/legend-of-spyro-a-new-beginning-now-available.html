--- title: "Legend of Spyro: A New Beginning now available" date: 2006-10-11T02:52:23Z contributors: - Glen Bayer ---
Legend of Spyro: A New Beginning is now available on Nintendo DS ($29.99), Game Boy Advance ($29.99), and GameCube ($39.99). The title arrives via publisher Sierra Entertainment and developer Krome Studios. Find more info at the game's official website.

The Legend of Spyro: A New Beginning is a fresh start for the series that began on the original PlayStation. In the game, players will explore the true origins of Spyro by embarking on a combat-driven quest that will bring you face to face with the Dark Master's ultimate creation, Cynder. Unleash fury attacks, upgradeable breaths & ground to aerial melee combos as you battle with hordes of enemies and bosses. The GameCube version currently has a 70 percent average review ranking according to GameRankings.com (based on 2 reviews).
